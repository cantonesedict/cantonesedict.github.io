《英華分韻撮要》

(From the Chinese title page of the original.)

(lit. "Anglo–Chinese Rime-Divided Handpicked Essentials")

咸豐丙辰年鐫

(lit. Engraved Ping-shan Year of Hám-fung = 1856)

(Remarks:

  • 丙辰 Ping-shan: year 53/60 of the sexagenary cycle
  • 咸豐 Hám-fung: Emperor Hsien-fêng, penultimate of the Tsʻing Dynasty
  • Fun fact: 咸豐年 is now a Cantonese phrase meaning "ages ago"

End of remarks.)
