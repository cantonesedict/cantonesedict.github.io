(From the English title page of the original.)

A Tonic Dictionary of the Chinese Language in the Canton Dialect.

꜀Ying ꜁Wá ꜀Fan Wan[꜅] Tsʻüt꜆ Iú꜄.

(Remarks:

  • This is the Williams romanisation of the Chinese title, 英華分韻撮要 ying1 waa4 fan1 wan6 cyut3 yiu3.
  • Corrected Wan꜄ (wan3) to Wan꜅ (wan6). The reading of 韻 in 陽去 (tone 6) is that according to the 分韻 itself, which has followed the expected reflex from Middle Chinese 王問切.
  • The reading of 韻 preferred by most modern speakers is in 陽上 (tone 5).

End of remarks.)

By S. Wells Williams.

Canton: Printed at the Office of the Chinese Repository.

1856.
