  • 【晨早】 (san4 zou2):
    1. Very early on, ages ago.
      • 晨早去咗 (san4 zou2 heoi3 zo2): "[I] went ages ago."
  • 【一早】 (jat1 zou2):
    1. Very early on, ages ago.
      • 一早話咗 (jat1 zou2 waa6 zo2): "Said [so] ages ago."
  • 【早晨】 (zou2 san4):
    1. Good morning.
      • 早晨啊 (zou2 san4 a3): "Good morning!"

做 Tsò꜅ (zou6)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 9
Unicode code point
U+505A
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 18 做 Tsú꜅) 作事曰做
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 585 做꜅)
    • To do, to make, to act;
    • to be, to perform the duties of;
    • much used for the next (造);
    • tsò꜅ tak꜆ (zou6 dak1 做得) very well, it will do; yes; I can do it, I will do it;
    • tsò꜅ ꜀kún (zou6 gun1 做官), to be an officer;
    • tsò꜅ muk꜇ (zou6 muk6 做⽊) is a carpenter;
    • tsò꜅ ꜂tsau (zou6 zau2 做酒), to prepare a feast;
    • tsò꜅ ꜂hí ꜁yan (zou6 hei2 jan4 做起人), a prosperous man;
    • tsò꜅ ꜀[sháng] í꜄ (zou6 saang1 ji3 做生意) is in business, he trades;
    • ꜃úi tsò꜅ ꜁yan (wui5 zou6 jan4 會做人), he understands the world, popular;
    • ꜁'m tsò꜅ tak꜆ (m4 zou6 dak1 唔做得) it will not do, isn't the thing;
    • ꜃ní tsò꜅ tak꜆ ꜁'m tsò꜅ tak꜆ (nei5 zou6 dak1 m4 zou6 dak1 你做得唔做得) can you undertake this, can you do it?
    • ꜃ní tsò꜅ mat꜆ ꜃yé (nei5 zou6 mat1 je5 你做乜嘢) what are you doing?
    • tsò꜅ tak꜆ ꜁'m ꜁hám ꜁'m [꜃tʻám] (zou6 dak1 m4 haam4 m4 taam5 做得唔鹹唔淡) it's neither salt nor fresh—unsatisfactory;
    • tsò꜅ mat꜆ (zou6 mat1 做乜) wherefore, why?
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "used for the next": I have not seen 做 used for 造.
  • Williams' 做生意: Changed ꜀shang (sang1) to vernacular reading ꜀sháng (saang1).
  • Williams' 你做得唔做得: This is dated syntax. In modern Cantonese, it would be 你做唔做得, with only the verb hugging the negative.
  • Williams' 做得唔鹹唔淡: Corrected tám꜅ (daam6 去聲) to ꜃tʻám (taam5 上聲).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【做】 (zou6):
    1. To work, to be employed.
      • 做嘢未 (zou6 je5 mei6): "[Do you] work yet?"
      • 做廚 (zou6 cyu4-2): "Work [as a] chef."
      • 冇嘢做 (mou5 je5 zou6): "Out of work."
    2. To be airing or broadcasting.
      • 做緊新聞 (zou6 gan2 san1 man4-2): "[The] news [is] on."
      • 做完 (zou6 jyun4): "[The show has] finished airing."
    3. (mahjong) To try for a hand, especially a hand of a numbered suit.
      • 做牌 (zou6 paai4-2): "Going for [a numbered-suit] hand."
      • 做大佢 (zou6 daai6 keoi5): "Make it big" (i.e. don't settle for a mediocre hand).
      • 做索子 (zou6 sok3 zi2): "Going for rods."
      • 做十三幺 (zou6 sap6 saam1 jiu1): "Going for Thirteen Littles."
  • 【做低】 (zou6 dai1):
    1. To kill, to eliminate lethally.
      • 做低咗男主角 (zou6 dai1 zo2 naam4 zyu2 gok3): "[He] took out [the] male main character."
  • 【做乜】 (zou6 mat1):
    1. Synonym of 做乜嘢.
  • 【做乜嘢】 (zou6 mat1 je5):
    1. Interrogative phrase pertaining to action.
      • 做乜嘢啊 (zou6 mat1 je5 a3): "What [are you] doing?"
    2. Interrogative phrase pertaining to something wrong.
      • 做乜嘢啊 (zou6 mat1 je5 a3): "What's [wrong]?"
      • 你做乜嘢啊 (nei5 zou6 mat1 je5 a3): "What's with you?"
    3. Why.
      • 做乜嘢遲到啊 (zou6 mat1 je5 ci4 dou3 a3); "Why [are you] late?"
  • 【做咩】 (zou6 me1):
    1. Short for 做乜嘢.
      • 做咩啊 (zou6 me1 a3): "What [are you] doing?"
      • 做咩啊 (zou6 me1 a3): "What's [wrong]?"
      • 做咩喊啊 (zou6 me1 haam3 a3): "Why [are you] crying?"
