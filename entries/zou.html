租 ꜀Tsò (zou1)

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 9 租 ꜀Tsú) 田賦也納谷納屋錢地稅俱曰租又聚也詩子所蓄租
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 584 ꜀租)
    • Rent in kind;
    • rental, rent;
    • tribute, taxes;
    • to rent;
    • ꜀tsò ꜁ngan (zou1 ngan4 租銀), the rent;
    • ꜀shau ꜀tsò (sau1 zou1 收租), to collect rents;
    • kot꜆ ꜀tsò (got3 zou1 割租), to deduct from the rent;
    • ꜀tsò yam꜅ (zou1 jam6 租賃) to lease;
    • ꜀tsò uk꜆ chü꜅ (zou1 uk1 zyu6 租屋住) to live in a hired honse;
    • shéung꜅ ꜁kʻí ꜀tsò (soeng6 kei4 zou1 上期租), to prepay the rent;
    • ꜀tám ꜀tsò (daam1 zou1 擔租), security for the rent;
    • ꜂hí ꜀tsò (hei2 zou1 起租), to raise the rent.

糟 ꜀Tsò (zou1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽶ + 11
Unicode code point
U+7CDF
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 9 糟 ꜀Tsú) 酒糟又糟粕
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 584 ꜀糟)
    • The sediment;
    • grains left after distilling spirits;
    • dregs;
    • ꜀tsò ꜀hong (zou1 hong1 糟糠), dregs and husks;
    • ꜂tsau ꜀tsò (zau2 zou1 酒糟), distiller's grains;
    • ꜀tsò ꜁ü (zou1 jyu4-2 糟魚), fish laid in grains of glutinous rice;
    • ꜀tsò ꜀hong ꜀chí ꜀tsʻai (zou1 hong1 zi1 cai1 糟糠之妻), a wife who has passed through poverty with one.

遭 ꜀Tsò (zou1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾡ + 11
Unicode code point
U+906D
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 9 遭 ꜀Tsú) 遭逢遭遇
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 584 ꜀遭)
    • To encounter, to meet;
    • to suffer, to endure, to occur, to happen;
    • to cruize, to go about;
    • to make a revolution;
    • an occasion, a time;
    • ꜀tsò tsʻák꜇ kíp꜆ (zou1 caak6 gip3 遭賊劫) to be robbed;
    • ꜀tsò kíp꜆ (zou1 gip3 遭劫) time of a great affliction;
    • ꜀tsò tʻát꜆ mat꜇ kín꜅ (zou1 taat3 mat6 gin6-2 遭塌物件) to waste and misuse things;
    • ꜀tsò tʻát꜆ ꜁yan (zou1 taat3 jan4 遭塌人), to abuse, to treat harshly;
    • pák꜇ ꜂tsau yat꜆ ꜀tsò (baak6 zau2 jat1 zou1 白走一遭), went once for nothing;
    • ꜀tsò wo꜅ (zou1 wo6 遭禍) to suffer calamity;
    • ꜁'m ꜀tsò tʻát꜆ ꜃kʻü (m4 zou1 taat3 keoi5 唔遭塌佢), be patient with him, don't berate him.
