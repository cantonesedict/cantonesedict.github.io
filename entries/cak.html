惻 Chʻak꜆ (cak1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼼ + 9
Unicode code point
U+60FB
Reading variations
  • caak1 (longer syllable)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 32 惻) 隱傷也[痛]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 12 惻꜆)
    • Secret grief, distress;
    • to pity, to commisserate, to sympathize;
    • chʻak꜆ [꜂]yan ꜀chí ꜀sam (cak1 jan2 zi1 sam1 惻隱之心), kind-hearted, humane.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 𭙡 (⿸广甬) to 痛.
  • Williams' 惻隱之心: Corrected ꜁yan (jan4) to ꜂yan (jan2).

測 Chʻak꜆ (cak1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 9
Unicode code point
U+6E2C
Reading variations
  • caak1 (longer syllable)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 32 測) 度也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 12 測꜆)
    • To sound, to fathom;
    • to estimate, to measure;
    • clear, as varnish;
    • chʻak꜆ tok꜇ (cak1 dok6 測度) to comprehend, to conjecture;
    • chʻak꜆ tsz'꜅ (cak1 zi6 測字) to dissect characters, as when divining;
    • pat꜆ chʻak꜆ (bat1 cak1 不測) unexpected;
    • ꜀tʻui chʻak꜆ (teoi1 cak1 推測) to explain, to reason;
    • pat꜆ ꜂ho chʻak꜆ (bat1 ho2 cak1 不可測) incomprehensible.
