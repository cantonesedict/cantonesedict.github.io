惻 Chʻak꜆ (cak1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼼ + 9
Unicode code point
U+60FB
Reading variations
  • caak1 (longer syllable)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 32 惻) 隱傷也[痛]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 12 惻꜆)
    • Secret grief, distress (Kangxi: 痛也);
    • to pity, to commisserate, to sympathize;
    • chʻak꜆ ꜁yan ꜂yan ꜀chí ꜀sam (cak1 jan2 zi1 sam1 惻隱之心), kind-hearted, humane.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 𭙡 to 痛.
  • Williams' 惻隱之心: Corrected ꜁yan (jan4) to ꜂yan (jan2).

測 Chʻak꜆ (cak1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 9
Unicode code point
U+6E2C
Reading variations
  • caak1 (longer syllable)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 32 測) 度也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 12 測꜆)
    • To sound, to fathom (Kangxi: 度深曰測);
    • to estimate, to measure (Kangxi: 凡測度之稱);
    • clear, as varnish (Kangxi: 淸也 etc.);
    • chʻak꜆ tok꜇ (cak1 dok6 測度) to comprehend, to conjecture;
    • chʻak꜆ tsz'꜅ (cak1 zi6 測字) to dissect characters, as when divining;
    • pat꜆ chʻak꜆ (bat1 cak1 不測) unexpected;
    • ꜀tʻui ꜀tʻúi chʻak꜆ (teoi1 cak1 推測) to explain, to reason;
    • pat꜆ ꜂ho chʻak꜆ (bat1 ho2 cak1 不可測) incomprehensible.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 推測: Corrected ꜀tʻui to ꜀tʻúi.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Extreme depth (Kangxi: 深所至也).
  2. Deep.
