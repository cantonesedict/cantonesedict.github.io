Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 51 妾) 次妻曰妾
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 579 妾꜆)
    • A concubine, a wife taken without the legal ceremonies (Kangxi: 接也得接于君子者也), a secondary wife;
    • term by which ladies call themselves;
    • tsʻíp꜆ shí꜅ (cip3 si6 妾侍) or shí꜅ tsʻíp꜆ (si6 cip3 侍妾) a concubine;
    • lap꜇ tsʻíp꜆ (lap6 cip3 立妾) to bring in a concubine;
    • tsʻíp꜆ ꜀shan (cip3 san1 妾身), I, your handmaid;
    • ꜂siú tsʻíp꜆ (siu2 cip3 小妾) my concubine;
    • oi꜄ tsʻíp꜆ (oi3 cip3 愛妾) your concubine;
    • shiú꜄ tsʻíp꜆ (siu3 cip3 少妾) my young concubine.
