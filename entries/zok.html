Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 26* 濯꜇)
    • To rinse, to wash;
    • fat, sleek, bright;
    • to drink;
    • chok꜇ chok꜇ (zok6 zok6 濯濯) sleek, as fat animals;
    • ꜂shá chok꜇ (saa2 zok6 灑濯) to sprinkle (_i. e. cleanse) the heart.
