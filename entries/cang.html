層 ꜁Tsʻang (cang4)

Radical + residual strokes
⼫ + 12
Unicode code point
U+5C64
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 31 層) 重叠也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 557 ꜁層)
    • A story of a house;
    • a layer, a strata, a tier, a stage;
    • a lamina, a plate, or whatever is piled or laid on;
    • still more, added to;
    • a step, a degree above (Kangxi: 級也);
    • a classifier of stories;
    • ꜀kò ꜃kʻü yat꜆ ꜁tsʻang (gou1 keoi5 jat1 cang4 高佢一層), one peg higher than it;
    • ꜁mò ꜁tsʻang (mou4 cang4 無層), no progress, it can't be done;
    • ꜀tsʻín ꜁tsʻang ꜂chí (cin1 cang4 zi2 千層紙), foliated mica;
    • ꜃shéung tai꜅ ꜀sám ꜁tsʻang (soeng5 dai6 saam1 cang4 上第三層), go up to the third story;
    • chung꜅ ꜃chʻung yat꜆ ꜁tsʻang (cung5 jat1 cang4 重一層), one degree heavier;
    • ꜃yau ꜁tsʻang ꜃yau tsʻz'꜄ (jau5 cang4 jau5 ci3 有層有次) there are proper gradations.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 重一層: Corrected chung꜅ (zung6 去聲) to ꜃chʻung (cung5 上聲).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A multi-storey house (Kangxi: 重屋也).
  2. Multi-layered (Kangxi: 凡物之重者通曰層).

曾 ꜁Tsʻang (cang4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽈ + 8
Unicode code point
U+66FE
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 31 曾) 嘗也又不料之詞又反詞
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 557 ꜁曾)
    • An adverb of time;
    • past, already, done (Kangxi: 經也, 嘗也);
    • how?
    • a sign of past time;
    • ꜁'m ꜁tsʻang (m4 cang4 唔曾) or mí꜅ ꜁tsʻang (mei6 cang4 未曾), not yet; often a polite way of saying no;
    • ꜃yá ꜁tsʻang tsò꜅ kwo꜄ (jaa5 cang4 zou6 gwo3 也曾做過) it is already done;
    • ꜁ho ꜁tsʻang (ho4 cang4 何曾), when will it be was it?
    • ꜁tsʻang kín꜄ (cang4 gin3 曾見) I have seen it;
    • tsò꜅ ꜁'m ꜁tsʻang (zou6 m4 cang4 做唔曾), is it done yet?
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 唔曾: Dated.
