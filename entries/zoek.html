Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 着) 被服也又置也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 32 着꜆著꜆)
    • (…);
    • to cover over, to put on, as clothes;
    • (…);
    • ꜂hò chéuk꜆ (hou2 zoek3 好着) sets well, as a garment;
    • (…);
    • chéuk꜆ ꜀í fuk꜇ (zoek3 ji1 fuk6 着衣服) to dress;
    • (…).

    This character is often distinguished into chéuk꜆ (zoek3) & chéuk꜇ (zoek6) (see 着 zoek6).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams (following Fan Wan) does not provide separate entries for 陰入 chéuk꜆ (zoek3) (clothing senses) and 陽入 chéuk꜇ (zoek6) (other senses). I have split things up accordingly.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【着】 (zoek3):
    1. To wear, to put on.
      • 着褲 (zoek3 fu3): "Wearing pants."
      • 唔着衫 (m4 zoek3 saam1): "Not wearing clothes."

Olden attestation for 着 (same as 著) chéuk꜆ (zoek3) meaning "wear":

  • 《廣韻》張略切：著：服衣於身又直略張豫二切

Chéuk꜇ Tséuk꜇ (zoek6 陽入)

  • 着
  • 著

着 Chéuk꜇ (zoek6)

Radical + residual strokes
⽬ + 7
Unicode code point
U+7740
Alternative forms
  • 著
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 着 Chéuk꜆ (zoek3)) 被服也又置也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 32 着꜆著꜆ Chéuk꜆ (zoek3); here corrected to Chéuk꜇ (zoek6))
    • Right, correct;
    • according to;
    • (…);
    • to cause, to order, to send;
    • a particle placed after verbs, showing a transitive and present action, and is like chü꜅ (zyu6 住) in colloquial;
    • before verbs, let, make, permit;
    • ꜁'m chéuk[꜇] (m4 zoek6 唔着) unsuitable;
    • chéuk[꜇] kwo꜄ ꜁tʻau (zoek3 gwo3 tau4 着過頭), just right;
    • (…);
    • shéung꜅ chéuk[꜇] (soeng6 zoek6 上着) best for use;
    • fan꜄ ꜁'m chéuk[꜇] (fan3 m4 zoek6 瞓唔着) I cannot sleep;
    • chéuk[꜇] shat꜇ ꜂tá (zoek6 sat6 daa2 着實打), gave him a flogging;
    • ü꜅ ꜀ká chéuk[꜇] ꜁mong (jyu6 gaa1 zoek6 mong4 愈加着忙), he was still more vexed;
    • chéuk[꜇] shò꜄ (zoek6 sou3 着數) cheap, good for the price;
    • chéuk[꜇] yung꜅ (zoek6 jung6 着用) useful;
    • chéuk[꜇] ꜀shing ꜁'m chéuk[꜇] ꜂tau (zoek6 sing1 m4 zoek6 dau2 着升唔着斗), it will do for a gill if not for a peck, serve for this if not for that;
    • chéuk[꜇] í꜄ (zoek6 ji3 着意) to suit one;
    • chéuk[꜇] lik꜇ (zoek6 lik6 着力) to exert one's self;
    • ꜃yau chéuk[꜇] lok꜇ (jau5 zoek6 lok6 有着落) all right, settled satisfactorily;
    • (…);
    • ꜀shiú chéuk[꜇] (siu1 zoek6 燒着) it is on fire;
    • chéuk[꜇] shat꜇ (zoek6 sat6 着實) entirely right;
    • ꜁tsʻam chéuk[꜇] (cam4 zoek6 尋着) or ꜂wan chéuk[꜇] (wan2 zoek6 搵着) found it;
    • chéuk[꜇] ꜁'m chéuk[꜇] (zoek6 m4 zoek6 着唔着) is it right or not?
    • ꜂tím ü꜅ tak꜆ chéuk[꜇] (dim2 jyu6 dak1 zoek6 點遇得着) how shall I get to him?
    • ꜁mò chéuk[꜇] lok꜇ (mou4 zoek6 lok6 無着落) I [don't] know what to do;
    • púi꜄ chéuk[꜇] ꜁yan tsò꜅ (bui3 zoek6 jan4 zou6 背着人做) hidden, sub rosâ, underhand.

    This character is often distinguished into chéuk꜆ (zoek3) (see 着 zoek3) & chéuk꜇ (zoek6).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams (following Fan Wan) does not provide separate entries for 陰入 chéuk꜆ (zoek3) (clothing senses) and 陽入 chéuk꜇ (zoek6) (other senses). I have split things up accordingly.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【着】 (zoek6):
    1. To turn on.
      • 着燈 (zoek6 dang1): "Turn on [the] light."
      • 部機開着咗 (bou6 gei1 hoi1 zoek6 zo2): "[The] machine [is] turned on."
