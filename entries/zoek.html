嚼 Tséuk꜆ (zoek3)

Unicode code point
U+56BC

Reading variations
  • zoek6 (per 《廣韻》在爵切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 嚼 Tséuk꜇ (zoek6)) 咀嚼咬嚼
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 565 嚼꜇ Tséuk꜇ (zoek6); here normalised to Tséuk꜆ (zoek3))
    • To chew, to bite, to ruminate, to craunch, to mumble, to munch, to masticate (Kangxi: 齧也);
    • a mouthful, a bite;
    • ꜃ngáu tséuk꜇ tséuk꜆ (ngaau5 zoek3 咬嚼) to chew;

爝 Tséuk꜆ (zoek3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽕ + 17
Unicode code point
U+721D
Han unification vigilance
May appear with bottom-left of right component as the bottom of 飠 (+17 assumed here) or 𩙿 (+18), depending on the font.
Reading variations
  • ziu3 (alternative per 《廣韻》子肖切)
  • zoek6 (alternative per 《廣韻》在爵切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 爝 Tséuk꜇ (zoek6)) 炬火也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 565 爝꜇ Tséuk꜇ (zoek6); here normalised to Tséuk꜆ (zoek3))
    • A flambeau, a torch, a link;
    • a lighted match burned at night on an alarm.
Conway's commentary
  • Per 《說文》苣火祓也, a torch specifically for warding off evil.
  • Both the dark-entering reading 即略切 tséuk꜆ (zoek3) and the light-entering reading 在爵切 tséuk꜇ (zoek6) are attested in 《廣韻》, explained 炬火 "torch fire", along with departing 子肖切 tsiú꜄ (ziu3), which is simply explained 火 "fire".

Conway's commentary

Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【着】 (zoek3)
    1. To wear, to put on.
      • 着褲 (zoek3 fu3): "Wearing pants."
      • 唔着衫 (m4 zoek3 saam1): "Not wearing clothes."

Olden attestation for 著 (alternative form 着) chéuk꜆ (zoek3) meaning "wear":

《廣韻》張略切：著：服衣於身又直略張豫二切

雀 Tséuk꜆ (zoek3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾫ + 3
Unicode code point
U+96C0
Alternative forms
  • 爵
Reading variations
  • zoek3-2 (vernacular rising-tone noun)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 雀) 孔雀又小鳥亦曰雀
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 565 雀꜄)
    • A sparrow (Kangxi: 依人小鳥也);
    • a term for small birds (Fan Wan: 小鳥亦曰雀), like the finch or lark;
    • a kind of cap;
    • a kind of wheat (Kangxi: 蘥雀麥, 卽燕麥也);
    • tséuk꜆ lap꜇ (zoek3 lap6 雀立) to skip, to hop, as a sparrow (Kangxi: 雀立踊也);
    • ꜂hung tséuk꜆ (hung2 zoek3-2 孔雀) a peacock;
    • ꜁má tséuk꜆ (ma4 zoek3-2 麻雀) a sparrow;
    • ꜀shán ꜁má tséuk꜆ (saan1 ma4 zoek3-2 山麻雀) a lark;
    • tséuk꜆ kok꜆ (zoek3 gok3 雀角) litigation;
    • ꜁wo ꜀fá tséuk꜆ (wo4 faa1 zoek3-2 禾花雀) rice-birds, ortolans;
    • ꜁múi ꜀fá tséuk꜆ (mui4 faa1 zoek3-2 梅花雀) the avedevat (Fringilla amanduva);
    • ꜁shí ꜁shan tséuk꜆ (si4 san4 zoek3-2 時辰雀) a canary-bird;
    • tséuk꜆ shít꜇ shít꜆ ꜀fá (zoek3 sit3 faa1 雀舌花), the Gardenia radicans.
  • (Page 565 爵꜆)
    • (…);
    • occurs for the preceding (雀);
    • (…).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "a kind of cap": This is a misreading of Kangxi 雀韋弁. The subcommentary makes it clear that 雀 is an adjective describing the colour of the cap (Kangxi: 雀言如雀頭色也).
  • Williams' 雀舌花: Changed shít꜇ (sit6) to common reading shít꜆ (sit3).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【麻雀】 (maa4 zoek3, maa4 zoek3-2)
    1. Mahjong.
      • 打麻雀 (daa2 maa4 zoek3-2): "Play mahjong."
      • 麻雀館 (maa4 zoek3 gun2): "Mahjong parlour."
    2. A mahjong tile.
      • 跌咗隻麻雀 (dit3 zo2 zek3 maa4 zoek3-2): "Dropped [a] mahjong-tile."
  • 【雀局】 (zoek3 guk6)
    1. Mahjong games (bundled with dinner at a restaurant). Erroneously spelt 鵲局.

    • chéuk꜆ chéuk꜇ shat꜇ (zoek6 sat6 着實) entirely right;
    • ꜁tsʻam chéuk꜆ chéuk꜇ (cam4 zoek6 尋着) or ꜂wan chéuk꜆ chéuk꜇ (wan2 zoek6 搵着) found it;
    • chéuk꜆ chéuk꜇ ꜁'m chéuk꜆ chéuk꜇ (zoek6 m4 zoek6 着唔着) is it right or not?
    • ꜂tím ü꜅ tak꜆ chéuk꜆ chéuk꜇ (dim2 jyu6 dak1 zoek6 點遇得着) how shall I get to him?
    • ꜁mò chéuk꜆ chéuk꜇ lok꜇ (mou4 zoek6 lok6 無着落) I dont don't know what to do;
    • púi꜄ chéuk꜆ chéuk꜇ ꜁yan tsò꜅ (bui3 zoek6 jan4 zou6 背着人做) hidden, sub rosâ, underhand.

    This character is often distinguished into chéuk꜆ (zoek3) & chéuk꜇ (zoek6).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams (following Fan Wan) does not provide separate entries for 陰入 chéuk꜆ (zoek3) ("wear" sense) and 陽入 chéuk꜇ (zoek6) (other senses). I have split things up accordingly.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To put (Kangxi 著: 著置也).
  2. To attach (Kangxi 著: 附也).
  3. To contact, to touch (Kangxi 著: 著地無足).
  4. A plan, a stratagem.
  5. To catch fire, to ignite.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【着】 (zoek6)
    1. To turn on.
      • 着燈 (zoek6 dang1): "Turn on [the] light."
      • 部機開着咗 (bou6 gei1 hoi1 zoek6 zo2): "[The] machine [is] turned on."
