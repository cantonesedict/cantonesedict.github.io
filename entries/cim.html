Fan Wan entry

  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 ？) 咸也皆也共也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 571 ꜀僉)
    • All, the whole (Kangxi: 皆也咸也);
    • an unanimous voice, the general opinion (Kangxi: 衆共言之也);
    • a flail (Kangxi: 連枷亦曰僉打穀具也);

Conway's commentary

㔐 ꜀Tsʻím (cim1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼑ + 16
Unicode code point
U+3510
Alternative forms
  • 𠠃
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 ？) [切]也割也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 571 ꜀𠠃; here normalised to 㔐)
    • To cut (Kangxi 𠠃: 切割也);
    • to stick in the throat;
    • ꜀tsʻím ꜃kʻü ꜁hau ꜁lung (cim1 keoi5 hau4 lung4 㔐佢喉嚨), pierce his weasand;
    • ꜀tsʻím ꜀chü (cim1 zyu1 㔐豬), to stick a pig;
    • ꜀tsʻím shü꜅ (cim1 syu6 㔐樹) to ingraft by slips.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 竊 to 切.
  • I have normalised 𠠃 to 㔐 for the better font support.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【㔐】 (cim1):
    1. To cut or sever.
    2. (Chinese chess) To capture a piece on an upper corner of the palace using an advisor.
      • 士㔐 (si6 cim1): "Advisor captures [on the upper corner]."

Olden attestation for 㔐 ꜀tsʻím (cim1) meaning "cut" or "sever":

《廣韻》七廉切：𠠃：𠠃切割也
《康熙字典》𠠃：《五音集韻》七廉切音籤切割也
《康熙字典》㔐：《玉篇》千廉切音僉割也〇按《五音集韻》作𠠃

Alternative forms
  • 簽
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 籖) 神籤騐也又竹籌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 571 ꜀簽; here normalised to 籤)
    • Slips of bamboo written on for lots (…);
    • (…);

    • ꜁ngò ꜀tsʻím (ngou4 cim1 摮籤), to draw lots before idols;
    • ꜀tsʻím ꜁tʻung (cim1 tung4 籤筒), a cup to hold the slips;

諂 ꜂Chʻím (cim2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾔ + 8
Unicode code point
U+8AC2
Alternative forms
  • 讇
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 49 [諂]) 諛媚也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 49 [讇]) 同[上]
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 16* ꜂諂꜂[讇])
    • To flatter (Kangxi: 同讇省諛也), to lie to;
    • to cajole;
    • to give a god or man worship or praise not due to him;
    • to fawn on, to court;
    • gratifying to one's feelings, adulation, sycophancy;
    • ꜂chʻím mí꜅ ꜀chí ꜁yan (cim2 mei6 zi1 jan4 諂媚之人), a flatterer;
    • ꜂chʻím ü꜅ (cim2 jyu6 諂譽) to praise extravagantly;
    • ꜂chʻím siú꜄ (cim2 siu3 諂笑) to cajole and laugh with;
    • ꜁pʻan ꜁í ꜁mò ꜂chʻím (pan4 ji4 mou4 cim2 貧而無諂), poor without being sycophantic.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword 謟 to 諂, headword ⿵門謟 to 讇, and headword self-reference to 上.

撍 ꜁Tsʻím (cim4)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 12
Unicode code point
U+648D
Reading variations

Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 572 ꜁撍)
    • To move with the hand (Kangxi: 手動也), to take up (Kangxi: 掇也);
    • to select (Kangxi: 摘也), to pull out, as from a pile;
    • to draw, as lots;
    • quick, rapid, urgent (Kangxi: 疾也, 速也);
    • ꜁tsʻím ꜁'m ꜂tò (cim4 m4 dou3-2 撍唔到), I drew a blank lot;

  • The reading ꜁tsʻím (cim4) is not unattested for the "select" sense; Kangxi has 又《集韻》慈鹽切《韻會》昨鹽切𠀤音潛摘也.
