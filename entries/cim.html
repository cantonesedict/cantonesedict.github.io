僉 ꜀Tsʻím (cim1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 11
Unicode code point
U+50C9
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 僉) 咸也皆也共也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 ⿳人⿻丅口从) 咸也皆也共也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 571 ꜀僉)
    • All, the whole;
    • an unanimous voice, the general opinion;
    • a flail;
    • ꜀tsʻím [át]꜆ (cim1 aat3 僉押) all signed it.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 僉押: Changed Middle Chinese áp꜆ (aap3) to common reading át꜆ (aat3).

    • to subscribe, to write one's name;

    • ꜀tsʻím ꜁meng (cim1 meng4-2 簽名), to write one's name;
    • ꜀tsʻím ꜁tʻai (cim1 tai4 簽題), to subscribe to;
    • ꜀tsʻím ꜀kwán (cim1 gwaan1 簽關), to join in engaging a teacher;

諂 ꜂Chʻím (cim2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾔ + 8
Unicode code point
U+8AC2
Alternative forms
  • 讇
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 49 [諂]) 諛媚也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 49 [讇]) 同[上]
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 16* ꜂諂꜂[讇])
    • To flatter, to lie to;
    • to cajole;
    • to give a god or man worship or praise not due to him;
    • to fawn on, to court;
    • gratifying to one's feelings, adulation, sycophancy;
    • ꜂chʻím mí꜅ ꜀chí ꜁yan (cim2 mei6 zi1 jan4 諂媚之人), a flatterer;
    • ꜂chʻím ü꜅ (cim2 jyu6 諂譽) to praise extravagantly;
    • ꜂chʻím siú꜄ (cim2 siu3 諂笑) to cajole and laugh with;
    • ꜁pʻan ꜁í ꜁mò ꜂chʻím (pan4 ji4 mou4 cim2 貧而無諂), poor without being sycophantic.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword 謟 to 諂, headword ⿵門謟 to 讇, and headword self-reference to 上.
  • Williams entry: Corrected headword 𨶒 (⿵門舀) to 讇.
