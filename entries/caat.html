刷 Chʻát꜆ (caat3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼑ + 6
Unicode code point
U+5237
Reading variations
  • caat3-2 (vernacular rising-tone noun)
  • saat3 (per 《廣韻》數刮切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 Shát꜆ (saat3)) 成也歸也又振作也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 427 刷꜆ Shát꜆ (saat3); here normalised to Chʻát꜆ (caat3))
    • A brush, a scraper (Kangxi: 刮也);
    • a curry-comb;
    • to brush;
    • to cleanse, to scour, to scrub (Kangxi: 刷淸也);
    • a card for cotton;
    • to print, as the Chinese do;
    • ꜂hon shát꜆ chʻát꜆ (hon2 caat3 刊刷) to cut and print books;
    • shát꜆ chʻát꜆ ꜂chʻí ꜂koi hang꜅ (caat3 ci2 goi2 hang6 刷恥改行) to wipe off disgrace by reforming,

察 Chʻát꜆ (caat3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼧ + 11
Unicode code point
U+5BDF
Alternative forms
  • 詧
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 察) 審也考也省也又察察潔淨貌
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 詧) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 24 察꜆)
    • To examine, to judge, to search out the truth (Kangxi: 覆審也, 諦也知也);
    • to observe closely, to inquire into all particulars (Kangxi: 考也廉視也);
    • ꜁chʻá chʻát꜆ (caa4 caat3 查察) to scrutinize;
    • kám꜄ chʻát꜆ (gaam3 caat3 鑒察) beg you to look at, said by suppliants;
    • ꜀king chʻát꜆ (ging1 caat3 京察) in the censorate;
    • chʻát꜆ tüt꜇ (caat3 dyut6 察奪) examine and decide [respecting the petition.]

擦 Tsʻát꜆ (caat3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 14
Unicode code point
U+64E6
Reading variations
  • caat3-2 (vernacular rising-tone noun)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 [擦]) 粲入聲揩必也磨也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 560 擦꜆)
    • To rub hard, to brush;
    • to feel for;
    • a brush;
    • ꜁ngá tsʻát꜆ (ngaa4 caat3-2 牙擦) a toothbrush;
    • tsʻát꜆ ꜀ní túi꜄ ꜁hái (caat3 ni1 deoi3 haai4 擦呢對鞋) brush this pair of shoes;
    • ꜁mo tsʻát꜆ (mo4 caat3 磨擦) to make smooth;
    • tsʻát꜆ ꜀kon tseng꜅ (caat3 gon1 zeng6 擦乾淨) brush it clean;
    • tsʻát꜆ láp꜇ (caat3 laap6 擦蠟) to wax and brighten.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword 摖 to 擦.
  • The character 擦 is not attested in the Middle Chinese rime books. Kangxi has 《篇海》《字彙》𠀤初戛切音察, which is chʻát꜆ rather than tsʻát꜆.
  • In modern usage, the noun sense of "brush" is spelt 刷; see 刷 caat3.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【擦膠】 (caat3 gaau1):
    1. A rubber or eraser.
  • 【擦鞋】 (caat3 haai4):
    1. To bootlick, to brownnose. (Literally, "polish shoes".)
  • 【擦紙膠】 (caat3 zi2 gaau1):
    1. Synonym of 擦膠.

獺 Chʻát꜆ (caat3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽝ + 16
Unicode code point
U+737A
Reading variations
  • taat3 (per 《廣韻》他達切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 獺) 水狗食魚之物
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 24 獺꜆)
    • An otter (Kangxi: 如小狗 etc.);
    • ꜀shán chʻát꜆ (saan1 caat3 山獺) a beaver, or fresh-water otter;
    • ꜂hoi chʻát꜆ (hoi2 caat3 海獺) a seal;
    • chʻát꜆ ꜃ling ꜃leng (caat3 leng5 獺領), seal skin collars or tippets;
    • chʻát꜆ ꜂tsai (caat3 zai2 獺仔), a tanka woman.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 獺領: Changed ꜃ling (ling5) to vernacular reading ꜃leng (leng5).
