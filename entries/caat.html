  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 察) 審也考也省也又察察潔淨貌

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 24 察꜆)
    • To examine, to judge, to search out the truth;
    • to observe closely, to inquire into all particulars;
    • ꜁chʻá chʻát꜆ (caa4 caat3 查察) to scrutinize;
    • kám꜄ chʻát꜆ (gaam3 caat3 鑒察) beg you to look at, said by suppliants;
    • ꜀king chʻát꜆_ (ging1 caat3 京察), in the censorate;
    • chʻát꜆ tüt꜇ (caat3 dyut6 察奪) examine and decide [respecting the petition.] (these square brackets appear in the original text)
