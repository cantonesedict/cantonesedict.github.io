俎 ꜂Cho (zo2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 7
Unicode code point
U+4FCE
Alternative forms
  • 爼
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 60 爼) 爼豆祭器
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 25* ꜂爼; here normalised to 俎)
    • A basin or bowl like a tripod, used in sacrifice;
    • ꜂cho tau꜅ (zo2 dau6 俎豆) a basin used in worship.
Conway's commentary
  • Also a table or chopping board.

阻 ꜂Cho (zo2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾩ + 5
Unicode code point
U+963B
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 60 阻) 阻滯阻隔險阻
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 25* ꜂阻)
    • To hinder, to impede, to stop, to make delay;
    • grieved;
    • dangerous;
    • an impediment;
    • a defile;
    • to suspect, to doubt;
    • ꜂hím ꜂cho (him2 zo2 險阻), an obstruction in the way;
    • ꜂cho chai꜅ (zo2 zai6 阻滯) prevented from being ready;
    • ꜂cho kák꜆ (zo2 gaak3 阻隔) to prevent going;
    • ꜂cho ꜁nán (zo2 naan4 阻難), difficulties;
    • ꜂cho chü꜅ (zo2 zyu6 阻住) to hinder, blocked up;
    • ꜂cho ꜂tong (zo2 dong2 阻擋), to stand in the way of;
    • ꜂cho ꜁chʻí (zo2 ci4 阻遲), to delay;
    • ꜂cho 'ng꜅ sz'꜅ kon꜄ (zo2 ng6 si6 gon3 阻誤事幹) to retard or interrupt business.

    A colloquial word (here normalised to 咗; see 咗 zo2 for the canonical entry).
