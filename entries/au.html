區 ꜀Au (au1)

Radical + residual strokes
匸 + 9
Unicode code point
U+5340
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 ꜀區)
    • A surname;
    • a measure of four pecks (四豆為區);
    • puk꜇ ꜀au (buk6 au1 蹼區), to conceal.

歐 ꜀Au (au1)

Radical + residual strokes
欠 + 11
Unicode code point
U+6B50
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 ꜀歐)

    To sing; to crow merrily; in which it is like the last (謳).

    Used for the next (嘔), to vomit;

    (Otherwise:)

    • ꜀au ꜀tò (au1 dou1 歐刀), a headsman's sword.

Radical + residual strokes
瓦 + 11
Unicode code point
U+750C
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 ꜀甌)
    • A bowl, a deep cup;
    • ꜃ngá ꜀au (ngaa5 au1 瓦甌), a basin to hold water;

꜂Au (au2 陰上)

  • 嘔
  • 毆

嘔 ꜂Au (au2)

Radical + residual strokes
口 + 11
Unicode code point
U+5614
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 ꜂嘔)

    Interchanged with the last (歐).

    (Otherwise:)

    • To vomit;
    • the noise of retching;
    • to spit out;
    • a child's prattle;
    • to sing, to make a glad noise;
    • ꜂au hüt꜆ (au2 hyut3 嘔血) to spit blood;
    • ꜂au ꜀fán chʻut꜆ ꜁lai (au2 faan1 ceot1 lai4 嘔返出嚟), to disgorge; to pay back;
    • tsok꜆ ꜂au (zok3 au2 作嘔), a disposition to vomit;
    • ꜂au ꜀hiú ꜂kí tám꜅ (au2 hiu1 gei2 daam6 嘔嘵幾啖) threw up several times;
    • ꜂au ꜀á (au2 aa1 嘔啞), children humming;
    • ꜂au tʻò꜄ (au2 tou3 嘔吐) to puke;
    • ꜂au long꜅ (au2 long6 嘔浪) seasick.

毆 ꜂Au (au2)

Radical + residual strokes
殳 + 11
Unicode code point
U+6BC6
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 ꜂毆)
    • To fight, to hit with a stick;
    • to strike, to knock;
    • tau꜄ ꜂au (dau3 au2 鬥毆), to wrangle, to fisticuff;
    • ꜂au yuk꜇ (au2 juk6 毆辱) to disgrace a man by knocking him;
    • ꜂au ꜂tá (au2 daa2 毆打), to beat, to take a stick to.

Au꜄ (au3 陰去)

  • 漚

漚 Au꜄ (au3)

Radical + residual strokes
水 + 11
Unicode code point
U+6F1A
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 漚꜄)
    • To soak, to macerate, to steep;
    • to soften by soaking;
    • au꜄ lán꜅ (au3 laan6 漚爛) soaked and spoiled; to macerate to rags;
    • au꜄ ꜁múi (au3 mui4 漚霉), soaked till moldy;
    • ꜁'m ꜀kʻam au꜄ (m4 kam1 au3 唔𠸐漚) it will spoil if soaked long;
    • au꜄ tò꜄ chʻau꜄ (au3 dou3 cau3 漚到臭) soaked till it stinks;
    • au꜄ nau꜅ (au3 nau6 漚腬) glutted, loathing food;
    • ꜁tʻang [꜃]shín au꜄ ꜀chü yuk꜇ (tang4 sin5 au3 zyu1 juk6 藤鱔漚豬肉) to fry eels in pork fat, met. to add insult to injury.

    Read ꜀au (au1); (see 漚 au1).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 藤鱔漚豬肉: Corrected shín꜅ (sin6) to ꜃shín (sin5).
Conway's vernacular entry
  • 【漚泥】 (au3 nai4):
    1. To rot in mud. Used to express discontent, contempt, etc.
