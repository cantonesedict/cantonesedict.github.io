區 ꜀Au (au1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼖ + 9
Unicode code point
U+5340
Reading variations
  • ngau1 (hypercorrected initial)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 49 區) 人姓又音拘
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 ꜀區)
    • A surname (Kangxi: 姓);
    • a measure of four pecks (Kangxi: 四豆爲區);
    • puk꜇ ꜀au (buk6 au1 蹼區), to conceal.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "four pecks": It is disputed whether one 豆 is 0.4 pecks (豆實四升) or 1 peck (豆當爲斗毛居正曰豆古斗字).

漚 Au꜄ (au3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 11
Unicode code point
U+6F1A
Reading variations
  • ngau3 (hypercorrected initial)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 55 漚) 以水久浸物曰漚
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 漚꜄)
    • To soak, to macerate, to steep (Kangxi: 久漬也);
    • to soften by soaking (Kangxi: 漚柔也);
    • au꜄ lán꜅ (au3 laan6 漚爛) soaked and spoiled; to macerate to rags;
    • au꜄ ꜁múi (au3 mui4 漚霉), soaked till moldy;
    • ꜁'m ꜀kʻam au꜄ (m4 kam1 au3 唔𠸐漚) it will spoil if soaked long;
    • au꜄ tò꜄ chʻau꜄ (au3 dou3 cau3 漚到臭) soaked till it stinks;
    • au꜄ nau꜅ (au3 nau6 漚腬) glutted, loathing food;
    • ꜁tʻang [꜃]shín au꜄ ꜀chü yuk꜇ (tang4 sin5 au3 zyu1 juk6 藤鱔漚豬肉) to fry eels in pork fat, met. to add insult to injury.

    (…)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 藤鱔漚豬肉: Corrected shín꜅ (sin6) to ꜃shín (sin5).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【漚泥】 (au3 nai4):
    1. To rot in mud. Used to express discontent, contempt, etc.
