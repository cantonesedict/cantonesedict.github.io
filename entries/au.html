  • (Page 6 ꜀區)

甌 ꜀Au (au1)

Radical + residual strokes
瓦 + 11
Unicode code point
U+750C
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 ꜀甌)
    • A bowl, a deep cup;
    • ꜃ngá ꜀au (ngaa5 au1 瓦甌), a basin to hold water;
    • ꜁chʻá ꜀au (caa4 au1 茶甌), a tea-cup;
    • ꜀ín ꜀au (jin1 au1 煙甌), a tobacco-cup;
    • pún꜄ ꜀au (bun3 au1 半甌), half a cup, as of wine;
    • muk꜇ ꜀au (muk6 au1 木甌), a wooden bowl.
