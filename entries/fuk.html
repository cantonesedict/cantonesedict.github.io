иҰҶ FukкңҶ (fuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
вҫ‘В +В 12
Unicode code point
U+8986
Reading variations
  • fau3 (per гҖҠе»Јйҹ»гҖӢж•·ж•‘еҲҮ for "overspread" sense)
  • fau6 (per гҖҠе»Јйҹ»гҖӢжү¶еҜҢеҲҮ for "waylay" sense and Fan Wan for "overspread" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol.В 1 ImageВ 56 иҰҶ Fauкң… (fau6)) еӨ©иҰҶең°ијүи‘ўд№ҹ
  • (Vol.В 1 ImageВ 64 иҰҶ) еҸҚиҰҶ
Williams (1856) entry
  • (PageВ 52 иҰҶкң… Fauкң… (fau6); here normalised to FukкңҶ (fuk1))
    • To cover, to overshadow (Kangxi: и“Ӣд№ҹ);
    • to brood, as a hen;
    • to overspread;
    • кңҖtК»Гӯn fauкң… fukкңҶ tГӯкң… tsoiкң„ (tin1 fuk1 dei6 zoi3 еӨ©иҰҶең°ијү) the heaven covers and earth contains carries;
    • кңҖkai кңӮnГЎ fauкң… fukкңҶ кңӮtsai (gai1 naa2 fuk1 zai2 йӣһд№ёиҰҶд»”), the hen covers her chickens.
  • (PageВ 63 иҰҶкңҶ)
    • To and fro, to repeat, back and forth (Kangxi: еҸҚиҰҶд№ҹ);
    • unstable, unsettled;
    • to subvert, to overthrow (Kangxi: еҖ’д№ҹ);
    • to defeat (Kangxi: иҰҶи»Қж•—д№ҹ);
    • to throw down, to upset, to prostrate;
    • to judge (Kangxi: еҜ©д№ҹ, и©іеҜҹжӣ°иҰҶ);
    • кңҖkК»ing fukкңҶ (king1 fuk1 еӮҫиҰҶ) defeated, tumbled down, ruined;
    • кңҖtГӯn fukкңҶ (din1 fuk1 йЎӣиҰҶ) reduced to poverty, reprobate;
    • кңғmГЎ кңҒtК»sГӯn кңҒtsК»Гӯn fukкңҶ кңӮshui (maa5 cin4 fuk1 seoi2 йҰ¬еүҚиҰҶж°ҙ), impossible;
    • fukкңҶ pГЎkкңҮ (fuk1 baak6 иҰҶзҷҪ) to understand clearly.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "to judge": Better rendered "to examine" or "to review".
  • Williams' йЎӣиҰҶ: Or, to subvert, to overthrow.
  • Williams' йҰ¬еүҚиҰҶж°ҙ: Less common than иҰҶж°ҙйӣЈж”¶.
  • Williams' иҰҶзҷҪ: I am not convinced that this means "to understand clearly".
  • гҖҠе»Јйҹ»гҖӢ has four headline readings:
    1. ж•·ж•‘еҲҮ fauкң„ (fau3) for the "overspread" sense;
    2. жү¶еҜҢеҲҮ fauкң… (fau6) for the "waylay" sense;
    3. иҠізҰҸеҲҮ fukкңҶ (fuk1) for the "overturn" sense; and
    4. еҢ№еҢ—еҲҮ pК»akкңҶ (pak1) without explanation.
    The explanations for the first and third readings (i.e. fauкң„ (fau3) and fukкңҶ (fuk1)) refer to each other. It is unclear whether these are intended as mere cross-references or as genuine alternatives for those two senses.
  • Fan Wan fauкң… (fau6) for the "overspread" sense is not strictly correct, and ought to be fauкң„ (fau3) if one insists on the departing tone.
  • According to ж®өзҺүиЈҒ's annotations of гҖҠиӘӘж–ҮгҖӢ, the "overspread" and "overturn" senses have the same olden reading иҠізҰҸеҲҮ fukкңҶ (fuk1), and distinguishing ж•·ж•‘еҲҮ fauкң„ (fau3) for the "overspread" sense is a southern reading (еҚ—йҹі).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To waylay (Kangxi: дјҸе…өжӣ°иҰҶ).
  2. To reply.
  3. Conversely.
Conway's literary rendering
  • гҖҗиҰҶгҖ‘ (bjuwH)
    • (verb-like) [to] waylay
  • гҖҗиҰҶгҖ‘ (phjuwH)
    • (verb-like) [to] overspread
  • гҖҗиҰҶгҖ‘ (phjuwk)
    • (verb-like) [to] overturn, [to] overthrow
    • (verb-like) [to] reply
    • (adverb-like) conversely
    • Used in еҸҚиҰҶ.
  • гҖҗеҸҚиҰҶгҖ‘ (pjonX phjuwk)
    • (adverb-like) back [and] over, (met.) over [and] over, back [and] forth
