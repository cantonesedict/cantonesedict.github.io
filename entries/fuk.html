    • unstable, unsettled;
    • to subvert, to overthrow (Kangxi: еҖ’д№ҹ);

    • кңғmГЎ кңҒtК»sГӯn кңҒtsК»Гӯn fukкңҶ кңӮshui (maa5 cin4 fuk1 seoi2 йҰ¬еүҚиҰҶж°ҙ), impossible;

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "to judge": Better rendered "to examine" or "to review".
  • Williams' йЎӣиҰҶ: Or, to subvert, to overthrow.
  • Williams' йҰ¬еүҚиҰҶж°ҙ: Less common than иҰҶж°ҙйӣЈж”¶.
  • Williams' иҰҶзҷҪ: I am not convinced that this means "to understand clearly".
  • гҖҠе»Јйҹ»гҖӢ has four headline readings:
    1. ж•·ж•‘еҲҮ fauкң„ (fau3) or fГәкң„ (fu3) for the "overspread" sense;
    2. жү¶еҜҢеҲҮ fauкң… (fau6) for the "waylay" sense;
    3. иҠізҰҸеҲҮ fukкңҶ (fuk1) for the "overturn" sense; and
    4. еҢ№еҢ—еҲҮ pК»akкңҶ (pak1) without explanation.
    The explanations for the first and third readings (i.e. fauкң„ (fau3) and fukкңҶ (fuk1)) refer to each other. It is unclear whether these are intended as mere cross-references or as genuine alternatives for those two senses.
  • Fan Wan fauкң… (fau6) for the "overspread" sense is not strictly correct, and ought to be fauкң„ (fau3) if one insists on the departing tone.
  • According to ж®өзҺүиЈҒ's annotations of гҖҠиӘӘж–ҮгҖӢ, the "overspread" and "overturn" senses have the same olden reading иҠізҰҸеҲҮ fukкңҶ (fuk1), and distinguishing ж•·ж•‘еҲҮ fauкң„ (fau3) or fГәкң„ (fu3) for the "overspread" sense is a southern reading (еҚ—йҹі).
  • The sense of "reply" (see below) appears to be modern. For the classical word meaning "reply", see еҫ©В fuk6.

Williams (1856) entry
  • (PageВ 64 еҫ©кңҮ)
    • Again, reiterated, to do the second time (Kangxi: еҸҲд№ҹ, еҶҚд№ҹ);
    • to return (Kangxi: иҝ”д№ҹ, еҫ©йӮ„д№ҹ), to reply (Kangxi: зӯ”д№ҹ);
    • to revenge, to restore (Kangxi: иҲҲеҫ©), to repay;
    • to report to (Kangxi: еҸҚе‘Ҫд№ҹ, еҫ©е ұд№ҹеҸҚд№ҹ), to answer;
    • to recall the spirit of one who died from home (Kangxi: жӢӣйӯӮжӣ°еҫ©);
    • fukкңҮ кңҖyam (fuk6 jam1 еҫ©йҹі), an answer;
    • fukкңҮ sanкң„ sunкң„ (fuk6 seon3 еҫ©дҝЎ) reply a to a letter;
    • fukкңҮ mingкң… (fuk6 ming6 еҫ©е‘Ҫ) to report on a commission;
    • fukкңҮ кңҒГјn (fuk6 jyun4 еҫ©еҺҹ), restored to health;
    • fukкңҮ кңҒchК»au кңҒshau (fuk6 sau4 еҫ©д»Ү), to revenge a wrong.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Fixed incomplete strokes in иҝ”, зӯ”, and йҮҚ.
  • Williams' еҫ©дҝЎ: Corrected sanкң„ (san3) to sunкң„ (seon3).
  • Williams' еҫ©д»Ү: Normalised кңҒchК»au (cau4) to кңҒshau (sau4).
  • For the modern word meaning "reply", see иҰҶВ fuk1.

  • (PageВ 64 жңҚкңҮ)
    • To use (Kangxi: з”Ёд№ҹ) (as a boat);
    • to wait on, to serve;
    • to submit, yield to, accord with;
    • to cause to submit (Kangxi: жҮҫжңҚд№ҹ), to convince;
    • accustomed to, acclimated, habituated (Kangxi: зҝ’д№ҹ);
    • to think (Kangxi: жҖқд№ҹ);
    • to dress;
    • to fold;
    • garments (Kangxi: иЎЈжңҚ), mourning apparel;
    • a carriage cloth;
    • a quiver (Kangxi: зӣӣзҹўеҷЁд№ҹ);
    • an affair;
    • кңҖГӯ fukкңҮ (ji1 fuk6 иЎЈжңҚ) clothes;
    • chГ©ukкңҶ fukкңҮ (zoek3 fuk6 зқҖжңҚ) to go in mourning;
    • кңғ'ng fukкңҮ (ng5 fuk6 дә”жңҚ) five grades of mourning;

    • tК»ГјtкңҮ tК»ГјtкңҶ fukкңҮ (tyut3 fuk6 и„«жңҚ) to lay off mourning;
    • fukкңҮ yГ©ukкңҮ (fuk6 joek6 жңҚи—Ҙ) to take medicine;
    • кңҖkung fukкңҮ (gung1 fuk6 е…¬жңҚ) ceremonial dress;
    • fukкңҮ sz'кң… (fuk6 si6 жңҚдәӢ) to attend, to serve;
    • кңҒ'm fukкңҮ kГЎuкң„ (m4 fuk6 gaau3 е””жңҚж•ҷ) intractable;
    • fukкңҮ кңӮshui кңӮtК»ГІ (fuk6 seoi2 tou2 жңҚж°ҙеңҹ), acclimated;
    • кңҒГјn fukкңҮ (jyun4 fuk6 е…ғжңҚ) an official cap.
