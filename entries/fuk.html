幅 Fuk꜆ (fuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼱ + 9
Unicode code point
U+5E45
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 幅) 布之一片曰一幅又[邊]幅
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 63 幅꜆)
    • A wide strip The width of cloth (Kangxi: 布帛廣也 etc.);
    • a roll of paper or cloth;
    • a selvedge or hem;
    • a border, a frontier;
    • a classifier of maps, pictures, rolls, flags, walls, and cloth;
    • (…);
    • ꜀pín fuk꜆ (bin1 fuk1 邊幅) a frontier; a hem;
    • sz'꜄ fuk꜆ ꜁tʻau (si3 fuk1 tau4-2 四幅頭), a set of four scrolls hung in halls.

    (Otherwise, a reading variation. See 幅 bik1 for the canonical entry.) (TODO)

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected malformed 邊.
  • Williams' 邊幅: Metaphorically, one's appearance.
  • In modern usage, used in the sense of "extent" or "amplitude", as in 大幅, 升幅, 振幅, etc.
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【幅】 (pjuwk)
    • (noun-like) cloth-width, width
    • (noun-like) hem
    • (verb-like) (met.) [to] limit

輻 Fuk꜆ (fuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾞ + 9
Unicode code point
U+8F3B
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 [輻]) [輻]輳
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 63 輻꜆)
    • The spokes of a wheel (Kangxi: 輪轑也, 輻謂輪中木之直指者);
    • fuk꜆ tsʻau꜄ (fuk1 cau3 輻輳) to collect (as at a metropolis), running together.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 輹. See 輹 fuk1 for the canonical entry.) (TODO)

Conway's commentary
  • Corrected malformed headword 輻.
  • In modern usage, used in 輻射 (radiation).
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【輻】 (pjuwk)
    • (noun-like) spoke
