之 ꜀Chí (zi1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼃ + 3
Unicode code point
U+4E4B
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 35 之) 於也往也又語助詞
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 38 ꜀之)
    • A sign of the genitive;
    • when placed between two nouns or verbs, answers to who, which, that;
    • a pronoun, he, she, it, them;
    • often used in the abstract, after a noun, or as a relative particle, in apposition;
    • to progress;
    • to go to;
    • [tʻing]꜄ ꜀chí ꜀chí ꜁yan (ting3 zi1 zi1 jan4 聽之之人), the person who hears him;
    • tʻín ꜀chí ꜁ming ming꜅ (tin1 zi1 ming4 ming6 天之明命) heaven's plain decree;
    • ꜁ü ꜀chí noi꜅ ꜁ho (jyu4 zi1 noi6 ho4 如之奈何) if so, how then?
    • mí꜅ ꜀chí ꜃yau ꜃yá (mei6 zi1 jau5 jaa5 未之有也), there is no such thing;
    • ꜀tséung ꜁ho ꜀chí (zoeng1 ho4 zi1 將何之), where are you going?
    • ꜂kú ꜀chí (gu2 zi1 古之), the ancients;
    • tʻín ming꜅ ꜀chí (tin1 ming6 zi1 天命之), heaven orders it;
    • ꜁mò ꜀chí (mou4 zi1 無之), impossible;
    • ꜁hang ꜁í pat꜆ chí꜄ ꜂ché ꜃yau ꜀chí (hang4 ji4 bat1 zi3 ze2 jau5 zi1 行而不至者有之), to go and not arrive is common;
    • ꜂chü ꜀chí ꜂ché ꜀chí wai꜅ tai꜄ (zyu2 zi1 ze2 zi1 wai6 dai3 主之者之謂帝) that which rules is called supreme.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 聽之之人: Corrected tʻeng꜄ (teng3) to tʻing꜄ (ting3).

知 ꜀Chí (zi1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽮ + 3
Unicode code point
U+77E5
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 35 知) 覺也識也明也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 37 ꜀知)
    • To know, to perceive, to apprehend;
    • to be acquainted with;
    • to tell, to inform;
    • a fellow, a friend;
    • knowledge, wisdom (correctly departing tone chí꜄ (zi3) when used for 智);
    • to remember;
    • ꜀chí kwo꜄ pít꜆ ꜂koi (zi1 gwo3 bit1 goi2 知過必改), reform when you know your wrong;
    • ꜁man ꜀chí (man4 zi1 聞知), I heard so;
    • ꜁shui ꜀chí (seoi4 zi1 誰知), unexpected, who would have thought it?
    • kú꜄ ꜀chí (gu3 zi1 故知), an old friend;
    • ꜀chí ꜀tí (zi1 di1 知啲), I know a little;
    • ꜀chí ꜂kí ꜁pʻang ꜃yau (zi1 gei2 pang4 jau5 知己朋友), an intimate friend;
    • ꜀sín ꜀chí (sin1 zi1 先知), a prophet;
    • ꜀chí tò꜅ (zi1 dou6-3 知道) I know it; the thing is known;
    • ꜀chí ꜂hiú (zi1 hiu2 知曉), to understand;
    • ꜀chí kok꜆ (zi1 gok3 知覺) to notice;
    • ꜀chí tò꜅ hai꜅ ꜂kòm (zi1 dou6-3 hai6 gam2 知道係噉), I know it is so;
    • ꜁ím ꜀chí sz'꜅ (jim4 zi1 si6 鹽知事) a salt commissioner;
    • ꜀chí ꜂fú (zi1 fu2 知府), a prefect;
    • ꜀chí ꜀fung (zi1 fung1 知風), aware of;
    • ꜀chí tsuk꜆ (zi1 zuk1 知足) contented;
    • pat꜆ ꜀chí ꜂tai sai꜄ (bat1 zi1 dai2 sai3 不知底細) I do not know its history.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 知覺: Now "consciousness".

蜘 ꜀Chí (zi1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾍ + 8
Unicode code point
U+8718
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 35 蜘) 蜘蛛
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 38 ꜀蜘)
    • A spider;
    • ꜀chí ꜀chü (zi1 zyu1 蜘蛛), a spider, a general name for all kinds of aranea.
