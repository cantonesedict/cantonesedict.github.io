操 ꜀Tsʻò (cou1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 13
Unicode code point
U+64CD
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 9 操 ꜀Tsʻú) 操持
  • (Vol. 2 Image 9 撡 ꜀Tsʻú) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 586 ꜀操)
    • To take, to hold, to grasp;
    • to manage;
    • to exercise, to drill;
    • expert, used to;
    • to maintain, to restrain one's self, to moderate one's desires;
    • (see 操 cou3 for "integrity" senses);
    • ꜀tsʻò ꜂ín (cou1 jin2 操演) or há[꜅] ꜀tsʻò (haa6 cou1 下操), to drill troops;
    • ꜂tʻai ꜀tsʻò (tai2 cou1 睇操), to see a review;
    • tái꜅ ꜀tsʻò (daai6 cou1 大操), the triennial review;
    • ꜀tsʻò ꜁chʻí (cou1 ci4 操持), resolute, adhering to right;
    • ꜀tsʻò ꜀sam (cou1 sam1 操心), anxious, careworn.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 下操: Corrected ꜃há (haa5) to há꜅ (haa6).

粗 ꜀Tsʻò (cou1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽶ + 5
Unicode code point
U+7C97
Alternative forms
  • 麄
  • 麤
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 9 粗 ꜀Tsʻú) 不精也臭惡也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 9 麄 ꜀Tsʻú) 同上
  • (Vol. 2 Image 9 麤 ꜀Tsʻú) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 586 ꜀粗꜀麤)
    • Coarse, uncleaned, dirty, as rice just thrashed;
    • large, open, coarse;
    • rude, vulgar, boisterous, unpolished;
    • gross, indecent, dirty;
    • harsh, as a voice;
    • partially, rudely;
    • ꜀tsʻò yau꜄ (cou1 jau3 粗幼) coarse or fine, as cloth;
    • ꜀tsʻò tsuk꜇ ꜁yan (cou1 zuk6 jan4 粗俗人), a rough vulgar man;
    • ꜀tsʻò ꜃lò (cou1 lou5 粗魯), rough, unmannerly;
    • ꜀tsʻò ꜀chí (cou1 zi1 粗知), partly knew it;
    • ꜀tsʻò wá꜅ (cou1 waa6-2 粗話) obscene language;
    • ꜀tsʻò shik꜇ (cou1 sik6 粗食) coarse food; a gross eater;
    • ꜀tsʻò ꜁ün (cou1 jun4 粗完), nearly done;
    • ꜂shau ꜀tsʻò (sau2 cou1 手粗), a large hand;
    • ꜀tsʻò ꜀sam ꜁fau hí꜄ (cou1 sam1 fau4 hei3 粗心浮氣) light, inattentive, giddy;
    • ꜀tsʻò ꜂shai tái꜅ yung꜅ (cou1 sai2 daai6 jung6 粗使大用) careless in spending money;
    • ꜀ú ꜀tsʻò (wu1 cou1 污粗), dirty, unwashed.

    (See 麤 cou1 for the alternative form.)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 污粗: Should be 污糟.

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 12 曹 ꜁Tsʻú) 局也衆也軰也又同事曰曹
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 586 ꜁曹)
    • A sort of revising judge;
    • a judge of appeals;
    • a place where officers meet;
    • a company, a class;
    • denotes the plural of persons;
    • luk꜇ ꜁tsʻò (luk6 cou4 六曹), the six Boards;
    • ꜀tʻín ꜁tsʻò (tin1 cou4 天曹) and ꜀yam ꜁tsʻò (jam1 cou4 陰曹), gods and devils;
    • ꜃í ꜁tsʻò (ji5 cou4 爾曹), you;
    • ꜀kún ꜁tsʻò (gun1 cou4 官曹), officers;
    • ꜁Tsʻò ꜀Tsʻò (cou4 cou1 曹操), a famous general A.D. 250.
