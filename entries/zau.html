周 ꜀Chau (zau1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 5
Unicode code point
U+5468
Alternative forms
  • 週
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 49 周) 周密周濟周備周遍又姓
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 25 ꜀周)
    • Universal, complete, entire;
    • to supply, to provide, to assist;
    • plenty, enough;
    • to make a circuit, to environ;
    • a famous dynasty, B. C. 1122–255;
    • secret, fine, subtle;
    • close together, fine;
    • a curve, a bend;
    • to extend everywhere;
    • honest, trustworthy;
    • to the end, entirely;
    • ꜂hò ꜀chau tò꜄ (hou2 zau1 dou3 好周到) all things prepared;
    • ꜀chau ꜂chí (zau1 zi1 周知), let all know;
    • hü꜄ kín꜄ ꜀chau ꜀kung (heoi3 gin3 zau1 gung1 去見周公), he's gone to see Duke Chau—asleep;
    • ꜀chau mat꜇ (zau1 mat6 周密) numerous, close together;
    • ꜀chau pí꜅ (zau1 bei6 周備) everything ready;
    • ꜀chau ꜁wai (zau1 wai4 周圍), everywhere, all around;
    • ꜀chau ꜁sün (zau1 syun4 周旋) to circulate, to treat friends well;
    • ꜀chau ꜁tsʻün (zau1 cyun4 周全), to bring a thing about, to remove ill feeling, to explain;
    • ꜂hò ꜀chau chí[꜄] (hou2 zau1 zi3 好周置), it is excellent.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 好周置: Corrected ꜀chí (zi1) to chí꜄ (zi3).
  • Williams records 周時 under 週 (which is interchangeable); see 週 zau1.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【周身刀冇張利】 (zau1 san1 dou1, mou5 zoeng1 lei6)
    1. Jack of all trades, master of none. (Literally, "Knives all-about the person, [but] not [one] sharp".)
  • 【周時】 (zau1 si4):
    1. All the time.

州 ꜀Chau (zau1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼮ + 3
Unicode code point
U+5DDE
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 49 州) 九州
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 25 ꜀州)
    • A district, less than a prefecture;
    • a continent, a great division;
    • an island;
    • ꜀chí ꜀chau (zi1 zau1 知州), the magistrate over a chau (zau 州) district;
    • ꜀chau ꜁tʻung (zau1 tung4 州同), his deputy;
    • ꜂kau ꜀chau (gau2 zau1 九州), the empire; the whole world;
    • tuk꜇ kéuk꜆ ꜀chau (duk6 goek3 zau1 獨腳州), a district without subdivisions.
Conway's commentary
  • In modern usage, the "continent" and "island" senses are spelt 洲; see 洲 zau1.

週 ꜀Chau (zau1)

Radical + residual strokes

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 49 週) 回也也

  • (Page 25 ꜀週)

    • to inform people;

咒 Chau꜄ (zau3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 5
Unicode code point
U+5492
Alternative forms
  • 呪
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 55 咒) 符咒又咒咀
  • (Vol. 1 Image 55 [呪]) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 26 咒꜄呪꜄)
    • To imprecate, to rail at, to curse;
    • to pray or recite spells;
    • an incantation, an imprecation, a charm, a spell;
    • a prayer;
    • ním꜅ chau꜄ (nim6 zau3 念咒) to recite prayers;
    • ꜂[tʻò] chau꜄ (tou2 zau3 禱咒) to curse;
    • ꜁fú chau꜄ (fu4 zau3 符咒) to prepare and bless charms;
    • chau꜄ cho꜄ (zau3 zo3 咒詛) or chau꜄ má꜅ (zau3 maa6 咒罵) to curse.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected duplicate headword 咒 to 呪.
  • Williams' 念咒: More reciting an incantation than a prayer.
  • Williams' 禱咒: Changed Middle Chinese ꜂tò (dou2) to common reading ꜂tʻò (tou2).

Unicode code point
U+5191
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 55 胄) 后裔也又甲胄
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 26 冑꜅)

    Often confounded with the last (胄).

    • A helmet, a morion;
    • káp꜆ chau꜅ (gaap3 zau6 甲冑) helmets and cuirasses.

宙 Chau꜅ (zau6)

Radical + residual strokes
⼧ + 5
Unicode code point
U+5B99
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 55 宙) 宇宙古[往]今來曰宙
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 26 宙꜅)
    • To contain, as the earth does;
    • including all ages, from ancient times till now.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 行 to 往.
  • Williams' "as the earth does": Not quite. Kangxi quotes 《說文》舟車所極覆也下覆爲宇上奠爲宙, so it is 宇 that is as the earth below, while 宙 is instead as the firmament above.
