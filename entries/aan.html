晏 Án꜄ (aan3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽇ + 6
Unicode code point
U+664F
Reading variations
  • aan3-2 (rising second in vernacular 晏晏地)
  • ngaan3 (hypercorrected initial)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 70 晏) 日[旰]也晚也又安也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 4 晏꜄)
    • Evening, sunset;
    • a serene, clear, sky (Kangxi: 天淸也);
    • tardy, behindhand (Kangxi: 晚也);
    • peaceful, gentle (Kangxi: 和柔也);
    • rich, full, said of furs (Kangxi: 鮮盛也 etc.);
    • a tiffin;
    • ꜂hí ꜀shan án꜄ (hei2 san1 aan3 起身晏) to rise late;
    • shéung꜅ án꜄ (soeng6 aan3 上晏) forenoon;
    • fan꜄ án꜄ káu꜄ (fan3 aan3 gaau3 瞓晏覺) to take a siesta;
    • fong꜄ án꜄ hok꜇ (fong3 aan3 hok6 放晏學) recess in school;
    • shik꜇ án꜄ (sik6 aan3 食晏) to eat lunch;
    • án꜄ chau꜄ (aan3 zau3 晏晝) noon;
    • tsò꜅ án꜄ (zou6 aan3 做晏) to get tiffin;
    • kòm꜄ án꜄ (gam3 aan3 咁晏) so late!
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 旴 (dawn) to 旰 (late).
  • Williams' "tiffin": British for "lunch".
  • Williams' 上晏: Now rarely heard; 上晝 is more common.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A surname (Kangxi: 姓).
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【晏】 ('aenH)
    • (adjective-like) clear, cloudless
    • (adjective-like) serene
    • (adjective-like) late
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【晏】 (aan3)
    1. Late (in the day).
      • 三點咁晏 (saam1 dim2 gam3 aan3): "3 o'clock? that late?"
      • 晏得嚌 (aan3 dak1 zai6): "[That's] too late [in the day]."
    2. Lunch.
      • 食晏未 (sik6 aan3 mei5): "Eaten lunch yet?"
  • 【晏晝】 (aan3 zau3)
    1. Afternoon.
      • 晏晝有戲睇 (aan3 zau3 jau5 hei3 tai2): "There's [a] movie [to] watch [in the] afternoon."

Olden attestation for 晏 án꜄ (aan3) meaning "late":

《廣韻》烏旰切：晏：晚也又於諫切
《廣韻》烏澗切：晏：柔也天淸也又晚也又姓左傳齊有晏氏代爲大夫
《康熙字典》晏：《唐韻》烏㵎切《集韻》《韻會》《正韻》於諫切𠀤音曣[…]又《玉篇》晚也《儀禮士相見禮》問日之早晏《淮南子天文訓》日至于桑野是謂晏食《屈原離騷》及年歲之未晏兮[…]

鷃 Án꜄ (aan3)

Unicode code point
U+9DC3
Alternative forms

Reading variations

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 70 鷃) [鳸]鷃鳥名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 4 鷃꜄)
    • A kind of small (Kangxi: 小雀也) partridge, which breeds on the ground (Kangxi: 鷃不木處), and is said to crow in the morning (Kangxi: 常晨鳴如雞).
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected ⿰斤鳥 to 鳸.
