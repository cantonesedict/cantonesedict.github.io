晏 Án꜄ (aan3)

Radical + residual strokes
日 + 6
Unicode code point
U+664F
Fan Wan entry
    (Vol. 2 Page 70 晏) 日[旰]也晚也又安也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 4 晏꜄)
    • Evening, sunset;
    • a serene, clear, sky;
    • tardy, behindhand;
    • peaceful, gentle;
    • rich, full, said of furs;
    • a tiffin;
    • ꜂hí ꜀shan án꜄ (hei2 san1 aan3 起身晏) to rise late;
    • shéung꜅ án꜄ (soeng6 aan3 上晏) forenoon;
    • fan꜄ án꜄ káu꜄ (fan3 aan3 gaau3 瞓晏覺) to take a siesta;
    • fong꜄ án꜄ hok꜇ (fong3 aan3 hok6 放晏學) recess in school;
    • shik꜇ án꜄ (sik6 aan3 食晏) to eat lunch;
    • án꜄ chau꜄ (aan3 zau3 晏晝) noon;
    • tsò꜅ án꜄ (zou6 aan3 做晏) to get tiffin;
    • kòm꜄án꜄ (gam3 aan3 咁晏) so late!
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 旴 (dawn) to 旰 (late).
  • Williams' "tiffin": British for "lunch".
  • Williams' 上晏: Now rarely heard; 上晝 is more common.

鷃 Án꜄ (aan3)

Radical + residual strokes
鳥 + 10
Unicode code point
U+9DC3
Alternative forms
  • 鴳
Fan Wan entry
    (Vol. 2 Page 70 鷃) [鳸]鷃鳥名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 4 鷃꜄)
    • A kind of small partridge, which breeds on the ground, and is said to crow in the morning.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected ⿰斤鳥 to 鳸.
