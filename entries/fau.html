Radical + residual strokes

Unicode code point

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol.В 1 ImageВ 53 еҗҰ) дёҚз„¶д№Ӣи©һ
Williams (1856) entry
  • (PageВ 52 кңӮеҗҰ)
    • Not so, ought not (Kangxi: дёҚд№ҹ etc.);
    • used at the end of sentences as a negative interrogation;
    • else, whether, otherwise;
    • ГјtкңҮ кңӮfau (jyut6 fau2 пјҹеҗҰ), it is not;
    • кңӮho кңӮfau (ho2 fau2 еҸҜеҗҰ), will or can it do, or not;
    • shГӯкң… кңӮfau (si6 fau2 жҳҜеҗҰ), is it so?
    • кңӮfau tsakкңҶ кңҒwai кңҖwai кңҖchГӯ (fau2 zak1 wai1 zi1 еҗҰеүҮеЁҒд№Ӣ), if it be bad not, then frown it away awe them.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "a negative interrogation": Specifically, to present an alternative in the negative, i.e. "[or] not".
  • Williams' еҗҰеүҮеЁҒд№Ӣ: Corrected кңҒwai (wai4) to кңҖwai (wai1). This is Kangxi's example from гҖҠжӣёзӣҠзЁ·гҖӢ.
Conway's literary rendering
  • гҖҗеҗҰгҖ‘ (pjuwX)
    • (adverb-like) nay
    • (adverb-like) not

жө® кңҒFau (fau4)

Radical + residual strokes
вҪ”В +В 7
Unicode code point
U+6D6E
Reading variations
  • pou4 (vernacular)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol.В 1 ImageВ 52 жө®) жіӣд№ҹжәўд№ҹ
Williams (1856) entry
  • (PageВ 51 кңҒжө®)
    • To float (Kangxi: ж°ҫд№ҹ), to drift (Kangxi: й ҶжөҒжӣ°жө®);
    • to overflow, to exceed (Kangxi: жәўд№ҹйҒҺд№ҹ), to inundate;
    • past time;
    • light, unsteady, fleeting;
    • excessive;
    • кңҒfau fГЎnкң„ (fau4 faan3 жө®жіӣ) floating;
    • vulg. кңҒpГІ кңҒpГІ pГЎnкң„ кңҒpК»ГІ кңҒpК»ГІ pК»ГЎnкң„ (pou4 pou4 paan3 жө®жө®жіӣ);
    • кңҒfau кңҒГӯn (fau4 jin4 жө®иЁҖ), unmeaning words;
    • кңҒfau кңҒtsК»oi (fau4 coi4 жө®иІЎ), floating property;
    • кңҒfau кңҒyau tsoiкң… shaiкң… shaiкң„ (fau4 jau4 zoi6 sai3 жө®йҒҠеңЁдё–) engaged in worldly affairs;
    • кңҒfau shekкңҮ (fau4 sek6 жө®зҹі) pumice;
    • кңӮkГІm кңҒfau (gam2 fau4 ж•ўжө®), heedless, flagitious;
    • кңҒfau кңҒtК»ГІ (fau4 tou4 жө®еұ ), a pagoda;
    • кңҒfau кңҖshang (fau4 sang1 жө®з”ҹ), the living;
    • кңҒfau кңҒfau (fau4 fau4 жө®жө®), vain, much.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "past time": This is a misreading of Kangxi е…ҲжҷӮжӣ°жө®. The example given is гҖҠжӣёзӣӨеәҡгҖӢй®®д»ҘдёҚжө®дәҺеӨ©жҷӮ. Reading жө® as the noun "past time" clearly does not make sense. Instead, жө® should be read as a verb, "preempt".
  • Williams' жө®жө®жіӣ: Corrected кңҒpГІ кңҒpГІ pГЎnкң„ (bou4 bou4 baan3) to кңҒpК»ГІ кңҒpК»ГІ pК»ГЎnкң„ (pou4 pou4 paan3).
  • Williams' жө®жө®еңЁдё–: Corrected shaiкң… (sai6) to shaiкң„ (sai3).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Flippant, levitous (Kangxi: иј•д№ҹ).
  2. Falling heavily (Kangxi: йӣЁйӣӘзӣӣиІҢ).
  3. To punish with drink as forfeit (Kangxi: жө®зҪ°д№ҹи¬ӮзҪ°зҲөд№ҹ).
Conway's literary rendering
  • гҖҗжө®гҖ‘ (bjuw)
    • (verb-like) [to] float
    • (verb-like) [to] penalise
    • (adjective-like) (met.) heavy, strong
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • гҖҗжө®йқўгҖ‘ (pou4 min6-2)

      • жө®йқўрўіӮжІ№ (pou4 min6-2 bat1 jau4): "Skim [the] oil along [the] surface."
  • гҖҗжө®й ӯгҖ‘ (pou4 tau4)
