꜂Fau (fau2 陰上)

  • 缶

缶 ꜂Fau (fau2)

Radical + residual strokes
⽸ + 0
Unicode code point
U+7F36
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 50 缶 ꜀Fau (fau1))
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 51 ꜀缶 ꜀Fau (fau1); here corrected to ꜂Fau (fau2))
    • Earthenware in general (Kangxi: 瓦缶之器);
    • a vessel used to contain wine (Kangxi: 瓦器所以盛酒漿);
    • one on which time was noted beaten (Kangxi: 秦人鼓之以節歌);
    • a jar (Kangxi: 盎謂之缶, 缶卽盎也大腹而斂口);
    • ꜃ngá ꜀fau ꜂fau (ngaa5 fau2 瓦缶), earthenware.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. The 121st radical ("jar").

꜁Fau (fau4 陽平)

  • 罘

罘 ꜁Fau (fau4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽹ + 4
Unicode code point
U+7F58
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 50 [罘] ꜀Fau (fau1)) [罘罳]屏風也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 51 ꜀罘 ꜀Fau (fau1); here corrected to ꜁Fau (fau4))
    • A rabbit net (Kangxi: 兔罟, 罘罝也);
    • a wooden screen before an official residence (Kangxi: 罘罳 etc.).
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised headword ⿱𠔿不 to 罘, and normalised ⿱𠔿思 to 罳.
