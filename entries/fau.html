否 ꜂Fau (fau2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 4
Unicode code point
U+5426
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 53 否) 不然之詞
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 ꜂否)
    • Not so, ought not (Kangxi: 不也 etc.);
    • used at the end of sentences as a negative interrogation;
    • else, whether, otherwise;
    • üt꜇ ꜂fau (jyut6 fau2 ？否), it is not;
    • ꜂ho ꜂fau (ho2 fau2 可否), will or can it do, or not;
    • shí꜅ ꜂fau (si6 fau2 是否), is it so?
    • ꜂fau tsak꜆ ꜁wai ꜀wai ꜀chí (fau2 zak1 wai1 zi1 否則威之), if it be bad not, then frown it away awe them.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 否則威之: Corrected ꜁wai (wai4) to ꜀wai (wai1). This is Kangxi's example from 《書益稷》.

浮 ꜁Fau (fau4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 7
Unicode code point
U+6D6E
Reading variations
  • pou4 (vernacular)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 52 浮) 泛也溢也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 51 ꜁浮)
    • To float (Kangxi: 氾也), to drift (Kangxi: 順流曰浮);
    • to overflow, to exceed (Kangxi: 溢也過也), to inundate;
    • past time;
    • light, unsteady, fleeting;
    • excessive;
    • ꜁fau fán꜄ (fau4 faan3 浮泛) floating;
    • vulg. ꜁pò ꜁pò pán꜄ ꜁pʻò ꜁pʻò pʻán꜄ (pou4 pou4 paan3 浮浮泛);
    • ꜁fau ꜁ín (fau4 jin4 浮言), unmeaning words;
    • ꜁fau ꜁tsʻoi (fau4 coi4 浮財), floating property;
    • ꜁fau ꜁yau tsoi꜅ shai꜅ shai꜄ (fau4 jau4 zoi6 sai3 浮遊在世) engaged in worldly affairs;
    • ꜁fau shek꜇ (fau4 sek6 浮石) pumice;
    • ꜂kòm ꜁fau (gam2 fau4 敢浮), heedless, flagitious;
    • ꜁fau ꜁tʻò (fau4 tou4 浮屠), a pagoda;
    • ꜁fau ꜀shang (fau4 sang1 浮生), the living;
    • ꜁fau ꜁fau (fau4 fau4 浮浮), vain, much.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "past time": This is a misreading of Kangxi 先時曰浮. The example given is 《書盤庚》鮮以不浮于天時. Reading 浮 as the noun "past time" clearly does not make sense. Instead, 浮 should be read as a verb, "preempt".
  • Williams' 浮浮泛: Corrected ꜁pò ꜁pò pán꜄ (bou4 bou4 baan3) to ꜁pʻò ꜁pʻò pʻán꜄ (pou4 pou4 paan3).
  • Williams' 浮浮在世: Corrected shai꜅ (sai6) to shai꜄ (sai3).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Flippant, levitous (Kangxi: 輕也).
  2. Falling heavily (Kangxi: 雨雪盛貌).
  3. To punish with drink as forfeit (Kangxi: 浮罰也謂罰爵也).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【浮面】 (pou4 min6-2)
    1. On the surface.
      • 浮面𢳂油 (pou4 min6-2 bat1 jau4): "Skim [the] oil along [the] surface."
  • 【浮頭】 (pou4 tau4)
    1. To surface.

    • fau꜅ ꜃tʻau ꜁tʻau (fau6 tau4 埠頭), a port;

    • kwo꜄ fau꜅ (gwo3 fau6 過埠) to go from port to port.

阜 Fau꜅ (fau6)

Radical + residual strokes
⾩ + 0
Unicode code point
U+961C
Alternative forms
  • 阝 (as a character component on the left)
  • 𨸏
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 56 阜) 山無石曰阜又曲阜地名又民[豐]物阜
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 阜꜅阝)
    • A great mound or hill of earth (Kangxi: 大陸曰阜, 山無石者);
    • the 170th radical ("mound") of elevations, hills, &c.;
    • great (Kangxi: 大也), large (Kangxi: 肥也), high, abundant (Kangxi: 盛也), numerous (Kangxi: 多也);
    • mat꜇ fau꜅ (mat6 fau6 物阜) plenty of things;
    • ꜀fung fau꜅ (fung1 fau6 豐阜) abundant, as a harvest.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Fixed missing strokes in 豐.
