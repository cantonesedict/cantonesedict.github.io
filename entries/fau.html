浮 ꜁Fau (fau4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 7
Unicode code point
U+6D6E
Reading variations
  • pou4 (vernacular)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 52 浮) 泛也溢也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 51 ꜁浮)
    • To float (Kangxi: 氾也), to drift (Kangxi: 順流曰浮);
    • to overflow, to exceed (Kangxi: 溢也過也), to inundate;
    • past time;
    • light, unsteady, fleeting;
    • excessive;
    • ꜁fau fán꜄ (fau4 faan3 浮泛) floating;
    • vulg. ꜁pò ꜁pò pán꜄ ꜁pʻò ꜁pʻò pʻán꜄ (pou4 pou4 paan3 浮浮泛);
    • ꜁fau ꜁ín (fau4 jin4 浮言), unmeaning words;
    • ꜁fau ꜁tsʻoi (fau4 coi4 浮財), floating property;
    • ꜁fau ꜁yau tsoi꜅ shai꜅ shai꜄ (fau4 jau4 zoi6 sai3 浮遊在世) engaged in worldly affairs;
    • ꜁fau shek꜇ (fau4 sek6 浮石) pumice;
    • ꜂kòm ꜁fau (gam2 fau4 敢浮), heedless, flagitious;
    • ꜁fau ꜁tʻò (fau4 tou4 浮屠), a pagoda;
    • ꜁fau ꜀shang (fau4 sang1 浮生), the living;
    • ꜁fau ꜁fau (fau4 fau4 浮浮), vain, much.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "past time": This is a misreading of Kangxi 先時曰浮. The example given is 《書盤庚》鮮以不浮于天時. Reading 浮 as the noun "past time" clearly does not make sense. Instead, 浮 should be read as a verb, "preempt".
  • Williams' 浮浮泛: Corrected ꜁pò ꜁pò pán꜄ (bou4 bou4 baan3) to ꜁pʻò ꜁pʻò pʻán꜄ (pou4 pou4 paan3).
  • Williams' 浮浮在世: Corrected shai꜅ (sai6) to shai꜄ (sai3).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Flippant, levitous (Kangxi: 輕也).
  2. Falling heavily (Kangxi: 雨雪盛貌).
  3. To punish with drink as forfeit (Kangxi: 浮罰也謂罰爵也).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【浮面】 (pou4 min6-2):
    1. On the surface.
      • 浮面𢳂油 (pou4 min6-2 bat1 jau4): "Skim [the] oil along [the] surface."
  • 【浮頭】 (pou4 tau4):
    1. To surface.

紑 ꜁Fau (fau4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽷ + 4
Unicode code point
U+7D11
Reading variations
  • fau1 (per 《廣韻》匹尤切 and 甫鳩切)
  • fu1 (per 《集韻》芳無切 for "bright-coloured" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 52 紑) 衣鮮[絜]貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 51 ꜁紑)
    • New silken Fresh, clean, as garments (Kangxi: 白鮮衣貌, 紑絜鮮貌);
    • elegant, lustrous, as silk (Kangxi: 繒色鮮).
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected malformed 絜.

罘 ꜁Fau (fau4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽹ + 4
Unicode code point
U+7F58
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 50 [罘] ꜀Fau (fau1)) [罘罳]屏風也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 51 ꜀罘 ꜀Fau (fau1); here corrected to ꜁Fau (fau4))
    • A rabbit net (Kangxi: 兔罟, 罘罝也);
    • a wooden screen before an official residence (Kangxi: 罘罳 etc.).
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised headword ⿱𠔿不 to 罘, and normalised ⿱𠔿思 to 罳.
