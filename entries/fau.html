Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 50 缶 ꜀Fau (fau1))
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 51 ꜀缶 ꜀Fau (fau1); here corrected to ꜂Fau (fau2))
    • Earthenware in general (Kangxi: 瓦缶之器);
    • a vessel used to contain wine (Kangxi: 瓦器所以盛酒漿);
    • one on which time was noted beaten (Kangxi: 秦人鼓之以節歌);
    • a jar (Kangxi: 盎謂之缶, 缶卽盎也大腹而斂口);
    • ꜃ngá ꜀fau ꜂fau (ngaa5 fau2 瓦缶), earthenware.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. The 121st radical ("jar").
