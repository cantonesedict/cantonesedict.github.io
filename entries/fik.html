Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 53 Fik꜆ (fik1) exemplified; here corrected to Fik꜇ (fik6) and assigned to 掝)
    • To brush off (as a fly);
    • fik꜆ fik꜇ ꜀hoi (fik6 hoi1 掝開), brush it off!
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【掝】 (fik6, fak6)
    1. To swing, to flap, especially quickly.
      • 掝甩 (fik6 lat1): "Flap [it] off."
    2. To flick or to brush away quickly.
      • 掝走佢 (fik6 zau2 keoi5): "Flick it away."
