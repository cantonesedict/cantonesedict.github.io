Fan Wan entry

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 42 ꜀𫫃)
    • A final particle, denoting are you willing, if you please, is it not so?
    • ꜃Ngo káu꜄ ꜃ní tuk꜇ ꜀é (ngo5 gaau3 nei5 duk6 e1 我教你讀𫫃), shall I teach you to read?
    • shí꜄ yat꜆ shí꜄ ꜀é (si3 jat1 si3 e1 試一試𫫃), try, will you?
  • (Page 713 ꜀𫫃)
    • A tone, a whine;
    • ꜀é ꜀é ꜀sheng (e1 e1 seng1 𫫃𫫃聲), the whining moan of a sick child.

    Also pronounced ꜀ngé (nge1).
