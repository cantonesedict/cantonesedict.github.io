占 ꜀Chím (zim1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼘ + 3
Unicode code point
U+5360
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 占) 騐也測也候也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 15* ꜀占)
    • To divine, to cast lots;
    • divination, casting lots;
    • to observe, to wait for a verification;
    • to look towards;
    • ꜀chím pat꜆ ꜁ling (zim1 bat1 ling4 占不靈), a false prediction;
    • ꜀chím sün꜄ ꜃há (zim1 syun3 haa5 占算吓), cast a lot about it;
    • ꜀chím kwá꜄ (zim1 gwaa3 占卦) to prognosticate;
    • ꜀chím ím꜅ (zim1 jim6 占驗) a verification of lots;
    • ꜀chím hau꜅ (zim1 hau6 占候) to foretell seasons;
    • ꜀chím [shiú]꜅ (zim1 siu6 占兆) to see signs, to foretell by signs.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 占兆: Changed Middle Chinese chiú꜅ (ziu6) to common reading shiú꜅ (siu6).

沾 ꜀Chím (zim1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 5
Unicode code point
U+6CBE
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 沾) 漬也濡也又沾恩
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 15* ꜀沾)
    • To moisten, to tinge;
    • to receive benefits;
    • to participate in;
    • to be a recipient of;
    • obliged, benefited;
    • imbued with;
    • infected, affected;
    • ꜀chím ꜀kwong (zim1 gwong1 沾光), got it through your favor;
    • ꜂fú lok꜇ ꜀kwan ꜀chím (fu2 lok6 gwan1 zim1 苦樂均沾), sorrow and joy equally bestowed;
    • ꜀chím ꜀yan chák[꜇] (zim1 jan1 zaak6 沾恩澤) received benefits;
    • ꜀chím peng꜅ (zim1 beng6 沾病) to catch a disease;
    • ꜀chím ꜃ím shai꜄ ꜁tsʻing (zim1 jim5 sai3 cing4 沾染世情), to be corrupted by bad influences;
    • ꜃ní ꜀chím chéuk[꜇] ꜃yau tik꜆ ꜁'m ꜂hò ꜁pí hí꜄ (nei5 zim1 zoek6 jau5 dik1 m4 hou2 pei4 hei3 你沾着有滴唔好脾氣) you have contracted a bad temper.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 沾恩澤: Corrected chák꜆ (zaak3) to chák꜇ (zaak6).
  • Williams' 你沾着有滴唔好脾氣: Corrected chéuk꜆ (zoek3) to chéuk꜇ (zoek6).

粘 ꜀Chím (zim1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽶ + 5
Unicode code point
U+7C98
Alternative forms
  • 黏
Reading variations
  • nim4 (expected from 女廉切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 粘) ？也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 秥) 秥米
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 15* ꜀粘)

    (Alternative form of 黏, and reading variation. See 黏 nim4 for the canonical entry.)

  • (Page 16* ꜀黏; here normalised to 粘)
    • Used for the preceding (粘);
    • rice;
    • ꜀chím ꜃mai (zim1 mai5 粘米), white rice, of which there are many kinds, as süt꜆ ꜀chím (syut3 zim1 雪粘), ꜂ai ꜁miú ꜀chím (ai2 miu4 zim1 矮苖粘), ꜀chím ꜂tsai (zim1 zai2 粘仔), &c.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: The first character of the explanation for 粘 is incomprehensible, but appears like ⿰苗月.

覘 ꜀Chím (zim1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾒ + 5
Unicode code point
U+8998
Reading variations
  • cim1 (expected from 丑廉切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 [覘]) 闚視也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 15* ꜀覘)
    • To peep, to spy at, to glance at;
    • ꜀chím hau꜅ (zim1 hau6 覘候) to wait for;
    • ꜀chím kín꜄ (zim1 gin3 覘見) to take a look at.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword ⿰古見 to 覘.

霑 ꜀Chím (zim1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾬ + 8
Unicode code point
U+9711
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 霑) 霑足雨之多也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 15* ꜀霑)

    Interchanged with the last (沾).

    • A drizzling, dripping, pattering, rain;
    • to wet through;
    • soaked, saturated;
    • ꜀chím tsuk꜆ (zim1 zuk1 霑足) wet to the skin;
    • ꜀chím ꜀í (zim1 ji1 霑衣), wet clothes.
