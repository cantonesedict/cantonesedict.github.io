占 ꜀Chím (zim1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼘ + 3
Unicode code point
U+5360
Reading variations
  • zem1 (for "jam" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 47 占) 騐也測也候也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 15* ꜀占)
    • To divine, to cast lots;
    • divination, casting lots (Kangxi: 視兆問也);
    • to observe, to wait for a verification (Kangxi: 𠋫也, 視也);
    • to look towards (Kangxi: 占亦瞻也);
    • ꜀chím pat꜆ ꜁ling (zim1 bat1 ling4 占不靈), a false prediction;
    • ꜀chím sün꜄ ꜃há (zim1 syun3 haa5 占算吓), cast a lot about it;
    • ꜀chím kwá꜄ (zim1 gwaa3 占卦) to prognosticate;
    • ꜀chím ím꜅ (zim1 jim6 占驗) a verification of lots;
    • ꜀chím hau꜅ (zim1 hau6 占候) to foretell seasons;
    • ꜀chím chiú꜅ shiú꜅ (zim1 siu6 占兆) to see signs, to foretell by signs.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 占兆: Changed chiú꜅ (ziu6) to common reading shiú꜅ (siu6).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【占】 (zem1, zim1)
    1. Jam (fruit preserve).
      • 果占 (gwo2 zem1): "Fruit jam."
      • 油占多 (jau4 zim1 do1): "Butter jam toast."

尖 ꜀Tsím (zim1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼩ + 3
Unicode code point
U+5C16
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 48 尖) 小也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 571 ꜀尖)
    • Sharp, pointed, acute, tapering to a peak or point (Kangxi: 末銳也小也);
    • wedge-like, pyramidal, conical, or needle-shaped;
    • clever, ingenious;
    • ꜂ting ꜀tsím (ding2 zim1 頂尖), ready at, smart;
    • yuk꜇ ꜀tsím (juk6 zim1 玉尖), a lady's fingers (Kangxi: 玉尖十尖指也);
    • ꜀tsím lí꜅ (zim1 lei6 尖利) sharp, quick-witted;
    • ꜂tá ꜀tsím (daa2 zim1 打尖), to wedge in, to work one's self in;
    • ꜀pán ꜀tsím ꜁hái (baan1 zim1 haai4 班尖鞋), to dress in women's shoes, to act the female parts.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Harsh or ear-piercing (of sound).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【打尖】 (daa2 zim1)
    1. To push in.
  • 【尖隊】 (zim1 deoi6-2)
    1. To push in line.

占 Chím꜄ (zim3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼘ + 3
Unicode code point
U+5360
Alternative forms
  • 佔
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 50 占) 霸占擅[據]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 16* 占꜄佔꜄)
    • To usurp, to seize, to take forcibly (Kangxi: 固有也, 擅據也著位也);
    • to assume, to arrogate, to take the liberty;
    • to possess (Kangxi: 有也);
    • pá꜄ chím꜄ (baa3 zim3 霸占) to take possession of;
    • chím꜄ ꜀sín hü꜄ (zim3 sin1 heoi3 占先去) to assume precedence;
    • chím꜄ ꜂siú shín꜅ (zim3 siu2 sin6 占小善) to praise one's self;
    • chím꜄ ꜁pʻín ꜁í (zim3 pin4 ji4-2 占便宜), to take what one likes, to assume more for one's self;
    • chím꜄ ꜀shan fan꜅ (zim3 san1 fan6 占身份) to regard one's place, to exercise one's rights.

    The second character (佔) is commonly but incorrectly used in this sense.

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected illegible character to 據.
  • Williams' "to praise one's self": This is a misreading of 占小善 from Kangxi's example 《韓愈進學解》占小善者率以錄, which should be rendered "they that possess [even a] little quality [are] all employed".

    • tsím꜅ yap꜇ ꜀kái ꜂king (zim6 jap6 gaai1 ging2 漸入佳境), opens up a fine prospect; gradually improving, as one's prospects;
    • tsím꜅ tsʻz'꜄ (zim6 ci3 漸次) many times, gradually;
    • ꜁tsʻun tsü꜅ tsím꜅ tsun꜄ (ceon4 zeoi6 zim6 zeon3 循序漸進) to follow on regularly, to advance in order.
