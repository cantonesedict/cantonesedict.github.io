且 ꜂Chʻé (ce2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼀ + 4
Unicode code point
U+4E14
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 84 且) 語詞
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 31 ꜂且)
    • A conjunctive particle;
    • and, further, moreover, still;
    • also, and also;
    • thus, so;
    • still, yet;
    • a word of doubt, if, should;
    • ꜁hang ꜂chʻé (hang4 ce2 行且), on the point of being;
    • ꜂chʻé [sun]꜄ ꜂chʻé ꜁í (ce2 seon3 ce2 ji4 且信且疑), half believing, half doubting;
    • fú꜄ ꜂chʻé kwai꜄ (fu3 ce2 gwai3 富且貴) rich and so honored;
    • ꜂kau ꜂chʻé ꜃liú sz'꜅ (gau2 ce2 liu5 si6 苟且了事) slovenly way of doing things;
    • fong꜄ ꜂chʻé (fong3 ce2 況且), still further;
    • ꜂chʻé mán꜅ (ce2 maan6 且慢) by and by, presently;
    • ꜀kú ꜂chʻé (gu1 ce2 姑且) or tsám꜅ ꜂chʻé (zaam6 ce2 暫且), for a time.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 且信且疑: Corrected san꜄ (san3) to sun꜄ (seon3).
  • Williams' 且慢: More an imperative "slow down" or "hang on" than adverbial "soon" or "shortly".
  • Strictly, the reading is ꜂tsʻé (ce2) from 七也切, but Fan Wan groups it with 扯 (same as 撦), whose reading is ꜂chʻé (ce2) from 昌者切. Notwithstanding, this is all academic given the initials tsʻ and chʻ are now considered merged.

扯 ꜂Chʻé (ce2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 4
Unicode code point
U+626F
Alternative forms
  • 撦
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 84 抯) 止也拽也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 84 扯) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 31 ꜂撦꜂抯꜂扯)
    • To tear open, to pull apart;
    • to tear away;
    • to pull up or on;
    • to haul, to drag;
    • ꜂chʻé ꜃shéung (ce2 soeng5 扯上), haul up;
    • ꜀lái ꜂chʻé (laai1 ce2 拉扯), to pull and haul, to borrow and lend;
    • ꜀lái ꜀lái ꜂chʻé ꜂chʻé (laai1 laai1 ce2 ce2 拉拉扯扯), borrowing here and there; on the average, lumping the whole;
    • ꜂chʻé ꜀hoi (ce2 hoi1 扯開), to pull apart;
    • ꜂chʻé ꜁mái (ce2 maai4 扯埋), to pull up, to close, as a skylight;
    • ꜂chʻé chü꜅ (ce2 zyu6 扯住) to stop from going.

    A colloquial word.

    • To clear out, to go off, to abscond;
    • to send off;
    • to go away, to take one's self off;
    • ꜂chʻé ꜀lo (ce2 lo1 扯囉), go! let us go;
    • ꜃ngo ꜂chʻé lok꜆ (ngo5 ce2 lok3 我扯咯) I'm off.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【扯】 (ce2):
    1. To leave.
      • 你扯啦 (nei5 ce2 laa1): "Leave!"

  • (Page 31 ꜂撦꜂抯꜂扯)

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan (and Williams following it) considers 抯 the same as 扯. Strictly, this is erroneous; the three readings in Middle Chinese are:
    1. ꜀chá (zaa1) from 側加切, explained 挹也 (quoting 說文);
    2. tsé꜅ (ze6) from 徐野切, explained 取也 (or ꜃tsʻé (ce5) if insisting on rising tone); and
    3. ꜂tsé (ze2) from 兹野切, also explained 取也.
    None of these are 陰上 ꜂chʻé (ce2).
