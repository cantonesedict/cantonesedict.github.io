Am (am)

꜂Am (am2 陰上)

唵 (U+5535) ꜂am (am2)

Williams (1856) entry
  • To feed with the hands;
  • to hold in the mouth;
  • a word used by Budhists.
Conway's remarks
  • "[A] word used by Budhists": Namely, a transliteration of the Sanskrit ॐ (Om).
