鵪 ꜀Òm (am1)

Radical + residual strokes
鳥 + 8
Unicode code point
U+9D6A
Alternative forms
  • 䳺
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Page 87 䳺) 䳺鶉鳥名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 341 ꜀䳺; here normalised to 鵪)
    • A quail;
    • ꜀òm [꜀chʻun] (am1 ceon1 鵪鶉), a quail, under which two or three species of Coturnix are included;

揞 ꜂Òm (am2)

Radical + residual strokes
手 + 9
Unicode code point
U+63DE
Alternative forms
  • 掩
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Page 87 揞) 藏[也]覆之也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 3 ꜂掩; here normalised to 揞)

    A colloquial word.

    • To cover with the hand;
    • to conceal from;
    • ꜂am ꜁mái ꜀shéung ꜃ngán, (am2 maai4 soeng1 ngaan5 揞埋雙眼) to cover both eyes;
    • ꜂am chü꜅ (am2 zyu6 揞住) hide it;
    • ꜂am tik꜆ púi꜅ ꜂shau, (am2 dik1 bui6 sau2 揞滴背手) give him a little sop, cover his palm.
  • (Page 341 ꜀掩꜂揞)
    • To cover, to hide, to screen, by putting something on;
    • to, extinguish, to suppress;
    • ꜂òm chü꜅ ꜂hau (am2 zyu6 hau2 揞住口), to put the hand over the mouth;
    • ꜀ché ꜂òm pat꜆ chü꜅ (ze1 am2 bat1 zyu6 遮揞不住) can not be hid;
    • ꜂òm chü꜅ ꜁tʻau hok꜆ (am2 zyu6 tau4 hok3 揞住頭殼) to lean the head on the hand;
    • ꜂pí ꜂shau ꜂òm chü꜅ (bei2 sau2 am2 zyu6 畀手揞住) cover it with your hand;
    • ꜂òm mat꜇ ꜁lo ꜂kú (am2 mat6 lo4 gu2 揞密鑼鼓), "muffle the gong," i. e. keep it quiet;
    • ꜂òm ꜀fung ꜁kʻam (am2 fung1 kam4 揞風琴), to play a piano;
    • ꜂òm mit꜇ (am2 mit6 揞滅) to put out, as fire; to quash;
    • ꜂òm pák[꜇] ꜁mai ꜁mung (am2 baak6 mai4 mung4 揞白迷濛), blindman's buff;
    • ꜂òm chü꜅ ꜃kʻü ꜀shéung ꜃ngán (am2 zyu6 keoi5 soeng1 ngaan5 揞住佢雙眼), blindfold his eyes;
    • ꜂òm mak꜇ (am2 mak6 揞脈) to feel the pulse;
    • ꜂òm púi꜅ ꜂shau (am2 bui6 sau2 揞背手) to give hush-money, to bribe.
    • The first character (掩) is read ꜂ím (jim2), but also used with this meaning in the pʻing shing (平聲) (am1).
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 藏𡈼 to 藏也.
  • Williams' 揞白迷濛: Corrected pák꜆ (baak3) to pák꜇ (baak6).
  • Williams' 揞背手: Now rarely heard; 揞口費 is more common for "hush money".
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【揞】 (am2):
    1. To cover with the hand.
      • 揞口費 (am2 hau2 fai3): "Hush money."

諳 ꜂Òm (am2)

Radical + residual strokes
言 + 9
Unicode code point
U+8AF3
Reading variations
  • am1 (expected from 烏含切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Page 87 諳) 諳練曉也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 342 ꜂諳)
    • Accustomed, skilled in, versed;
    • to know, fully acquainted with;
    • knowing thoroughly, to commit to memory, to recite, to chant or repeat;
    • ꜂òm lín꜅ (am2 lin6 諳練) well practiced in, mature;
    • ꜁'m ꜂òm shai꜄ ꜁tsʻing (m4 am2 sai3 cing4 唔諳世情), unacquainted with the world;
    • ꜂òm tsung꜅ (am2 zung6 諳誦) to repeat exactly.

暗 Òm꜄ (am3)

Radical + residual strokes
日 + 9
Unicode code point
U+6697
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Page 88 暗) 不明也又目不明貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 342 暗꜄)
    • Sun obscured by clouds or otherwise;
    • dark, dimly lighted, sombre;
    • gloomy, not shining bright;
    • clandestine, unobserved, secret, underhand;
    • dull, not perspicacious;
    • private, mental;
    • hak꜆ òm꜄ (hak1 am3 黑暗) dark;
    • ꜀tʻín shik꜆ òm꜄ (tin1 sik1 am3 天色暗) a dark day;
    • ꜀tang òm꜄ (dang1 am3 燈暗) a dim lamp;
    • òm꜄ ꜂séung (am3 soeng2 暗想), to think over;
    • ꜂tá òm꜄ hò꜅ (daa2 am3 hou6 打暗號) to give a hint;
    • òm꜄ ꜀chung tsò꜅ sz'꜅ (am3 zung1 zou6 si6 暗中做事) to do things in the dark;
    • òm꜄ múi꜅ (am3 mui6 暗昧) dull of perception;
    • ꜁ho ꜀páu òm꜄ ꜃nam (ho4 baau1 am3 nam5 荷包暗稔), a full purse, well supplied with cash;
    • òm꜄ ꜀kán (am3 gaan1 暗間), in the dark;
    • òm꜄ ꜃liú (am3 liu5 暗了), it is now night;
    • òm꜄ hoi꜅ (am3 hoi6 暗害) to privily injure.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【暗算】 (am3 syun3):
    1. To wound (usually mortally) with a hidden weapon.
