Ái (aai)

꜀Ái (陰平; aai1)

挨 (U+6328) ꜀ái (aai1)

Williams (1856) entry
(Page 2)
  • To lean upon, to trust to;
  • to push away, to carry on the back;
  • to place alongside;
  • to strike;
  • to force, to crowd, as with the elbows;
  • to graft;
  • next, near;
  • passable;
  • to lie down, to recline;
  • ꜀ái ꜁mái ꜀ní pín꜅ (挨埋呢便; aai1 maai4 ni1 bin6) lean it against here; help here a little;
  • ꜃yau tak꜆ ꜀ái pang꜅ (有得挨憑; jau4 dak1 aai1 bang6) has some dependance or resource;
  • ꜃yau tái꜅ ꜀shán ꜀ái (有大山挨; jau5 daai6 saan1 aai1), he has powerful friends;
  • ꜀ái ꜃há tik꜆ (挨吓滴; aai1 haa5 dik1) lie down a little;
  • ꜀ái tak꜆ hü꜄ (挨得去; aai1 dak1 heoi3) it will answer;
  • ꜀ái ꜁mún ꜀ái ú꜅ (挨門挨戶; aai1 mun4 aai1 wu6) to gad and stare, as a virago; to go from door to door to examine;
  • ꜀ái ꜂í (挨倚; aai1 ji2), to mutually rely upon;
  • ꜀ái lái꜅ (挨賴; aai1 laai6) to trust for help;
  • ꜀ái ꜃mán (挨晚; aai1 maan5-1), towards evening.

Ái꜄ (陰去; aai3)

嗌 (U+55CC) ái꜄ (aai3)

Williams (1856) entry
(Page 3)
A colloquial word;
  • to call after, to bawl;
  • to quarrel;
  • crowded, thronged;
  • ái꜄ ꜀káu (嗌交; aai3 gaau1), to wrangle, to dispute;
  • ái꜄ pʻo꜄ ꜁hau ꜁lung (嗌破喉嚨; aai3 po3 hau4 lung4), hallooed till he split his throat;
  • ái꜄ náu꜅ (嗌鬧; aai3 naau6) thronged; brawling;
  • ái꜄ mat꜆ ꜃yé (嗌乜嘢; aai3 mat1 je5), what are you scolding about?
  • shap꜇ ái꜄ ꜂kau ꜀cháng ꜁ngan (十嗌九爭銀; sap6 aai3 gau2 zaang1 ngan4), ten to one it is a quarrel about money.
Conway's remarks
  • Among the readings attested in Kangxi's Dictionary are:
    1. yik1 (伊昔切), meaning "throat" (咽也);
    2. aai3 (烏懈切音隘), meaning "choke" (噎也).
    Williams writes "A colloquial word" because the meaning attested under aai3 ("choke") does not match the vernacular Cantonese meaning.
  • "Ten to one" is better rendered as "nine out of ten".

隘 (U+9698) ái꜄ (aai3)

Williams (1856) entry
(Page 3)
  • A pass, a defile;
  • narrow, confined, straitened; distressed, urgent;
  • narrowminded, mean;
  • impeded, stopped up;
  • ꜁yan kòm꜄ ái꜄ (人咁隘; jan4 gam3 aai3) way is all crowded, a jam;
  • háp꜇ ái꜄ (狹隘; haap6 aai3) narrow as a pass; a contracted mind.
