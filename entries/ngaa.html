Ngá. (ngaa)

꜁Ngá. (ngaa4 陽平)

呀 (U+5440) ꜁ngá (ngaa4)

Williams (1856) entry
(Page 1)
  • the wrangling of children;
  • to open the mouth wide;
  • ꜁ngá ꜀lán ꜃mai (ngaa4 laan1 mai5 呀囒米), cochineal.
Conway's remarks
  • Williams has this entry within that for ꜀á (aa1); see aa#呀1.
