Ngá. (ngaa)

꜁Ngá. (ngaa4 陽平)

呀 ꜁ngá (ngaa4)

Unicode code point
(U+5440)
Williams (1856) entry
(Page 1, within the entry for 呀 ꜀á (aa1))
  • the wrangling of children;
  • to open the mouth wide;
  • ꜁ngá ꜀lán ꜃mai (ngaa4 laan1 mai5 呀囒米), cochineal.
