呀 ꜁Ngá (ngaa4)

Radical + residual strokes
口 + 4
Unicode code point
U+5440
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 1 ꜀呀)

    Read ꜁ngá (ngaa4):

    • the wrangling of children;
    • to open the mouth wide;
    • ꜁ngá ꜀lán ꜃mai (ngaa4 laan1 mai5 呀囒米), cochineal.
