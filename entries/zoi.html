哉 ꜀Tsoi (zoi1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 6
Unicode code point
U+54C9
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 43 哉) 語助詞又始也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 588 ꜀哉)
    • An interjection (Kangxi: 言之閒也) of praise or surprise;
    • an interrogative particle, intimating doubt or conviction, according to the scope (Kangxi: 疑辭也);
    • a superlative;
    • a particle placed after important words to draw attention (Kangxi: 語助), but the regimen alters its meaning much;
    • to begin (Kangxi: 哉始也);
    • to burst forth, as plants in spring;
    • ꜁kʻí kú꜄ ꜁ho ꜀tsoi (kei4 gu3 ho4 zoi1 其故何哉), on what account, pray?
    • ꜁ho ꜃yau ꜀ü ꜃ngo ꜀tsoi (ho4 jau5 jyu1 ngo5 zoi1 何有於我哉), what is this to me?
    • ꜂hí ꜂ho sik꜆ ꜀tsoi (hei2 ho2 sik1 zoi1 豈可惜哉), would you pity him!
    • ꜁yan ꜃ün ꜁ú ꜁fú ꜀tsoi (jan4 jyun5 fu4 zoi1 仁遠乎哉), has humanity so far gone!
    • ꜀on pat꜆ ꜀pí ꜀tsoi (on1 bat1 bei1 zoi1 安不悲哉), how could I forbear pity?
    • sik꜆ ꜀tsoi (sik1 zoi1 惜哉), sad indeed!
    • tái꜅ ꜀tsoi man꜅ (daai6 zoi1 man6 大哉問) ah! truly an important question;
    • tái꜅ ꜀tsoi ꜂Hung ꜂tsz' (daai6 zoi1 hung2 zi2 大哉孔子) Confucius, oh, how great!
    • ꜀ú ꜀fú ꜀oi ꜀tsoi (wu1 fu1 oi1 zoi1 嗚呼哀哉), alas, sad indeed!
    • ꜃yé ꜀tsoi ꜁Yau yá꜅ ꜃yá (je5 zoi1 jau4 jaa5 野哉由也) what a clown is Yau!
    • ꜀kú ꜀chí ꜀tsoi, ꜀kú ꜀chí ꜀tsoi (gu1 zi1 zoi1, gu1 zi1 zoi1 沽之哉沽之哉), sell it! sell it!
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "to burst forth, as plants in spring": This is a misreading of Kangxi 說文云才草木之初也以聲近借爲哉始之哉, which says that the "begin" sense of 哉 was sometimes spelt 才 due to the phonetic nearness and the (now considered incorrect) explanation of 才 as a sprouting plant in 《說文》.
  • Williams' 仁遠乎哉: Changed ꜁ú (wu4) to common reading ꜁fú (fu4).
  • Williams' 野哉由也: Corrected yá꜅ (jaa6) to ꜃yá (jaa5).

栽 ꜀Tsoi (zoi1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽊ + 6
Unicode code point
U+683D
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 43 栽) 種植也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 588 ꜀栽)
    • To plant seed (Kangxi: 種也);
    • to set out saplings, to put suckers in the ground (Kangxi: 草木之殖曰栽);
    • to hill, to heap up earth at the roots;
    • a sapling (Kangxi: 穉曰栽長曰樹);
    • to assist;
    • ꜀fá ꜀tsoi (faa1 zoi1 花栽), flower suckers;
    • ꜀tsoi ꜁pʻúi (zoi1 pui4 栽培), to put earth to, as to roots;
    • ꜂ho ꜃í ꜀tsoi ꜁pʻúi (ho2 ji5 zoi1 pui4 可以栽培), he is worthy of your care.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To tumble, to fall.

災 ꜀Tsoi (zoi1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽕ + 3
Unicode code point
U+707D
Alternative forms
  • 灾
  • 烖
  • 甾
  • 菑
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 43 災) 災禍災害
  • (Vol. 2 Image 43 灾) 同上
  • (Vol. 2 Image 43 烖) 同上
  • (Vol. 2 Image 43 菑) 古灾字又音[兹]
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 588 ꜀災꜀灾꜀烖)
    • Natural calamities (Kangxi 災: 天火也), as floods or drought;
    • divine judgments, plagues;
    • misfortunes, miseries, dangers (Kangxi 災: 害也);
    • adverse, calamitous, unfortunate;
    • ꜀tsoi hoi꜅ (zoi1 hoi6 災害) injurious, afflictive;
    • ꜀tsoi wo꜅ (zoi1 wo6 災禍) miserable; afflictions;
    • kau꜄ ꜀tsoi (gau3 zoi1 救災), to relieve sufferings;
    • ꜀chiú ꜀tsoi (ziu1 zoi1 招災), to bring evil on one's self;
    • ꜀tʻín ꜀tsoi ꜁wáng wo꜅ (tin1 zoi1 waang4 wo6 天災橫禍) divine and unforeseen judgments;
    • sung꜄ ꜂fo ꜀tsoi (sung3 fo2 zoi1 送火災), to dismiss all risk of fire, as is done at a ꜂ta ꜂tá tsiú꜄ (daa2 ziu3 打醮);
    • ꜂shui ꜀tsoi (seoi2 zoi1 水災), a flood;
    • ꜀tʻín ꜀tsoi ꜁lau ꜁hang (tin1 zoi1 lau4 hang4 天災流行), an epidemic;
    • ꜀tsoi ꜀yéung kok꜆ tí꜅ ꜁tʻau (zoi1 joeng1 gok3 dei6 tau4 災殃各地頭), each place has its own calamity.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Added (hypothesised) homophone 兹 for 菑.
  • Williams' 打醮: Corrected ꜂ta to ꜂tá.

宰 ꜂Tsoi (zoi2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼧ + 7
Unicode code point
U+5BB0
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 44 宰) 主宰烹宰又邑宰家宰
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 588 ꜂宰)
    • To govern, to rule, to superintend (Kangxi: 治也, 主也, 制也, 爲事物主也);
    • to fashion to one's liking;
    • a ruler, a headman, a governor (Kangxi: 官稱);
    • to slaughter animals, to butcher, to skin and dress animals (Kangxi: 屠也烹也);
    • to cook;
    • ꜂tsoi séung꜄ (zoi2 soeng3 宰相) a prime-minister;
    • ꜂chü ꜂tsoi (zyu2 zoi2 主宰), the ruler of all, the chief governor; the mind;
    • ꜀ká ꜂tsoi (gaa1 zoi2 家宰), a steward (Kangxi: 家臣之長曰宰, 諸宰家宰也);
    • kam꜄ ꜂chí ꜁tʻò ꜂tsoi (gam3 zi2 tou4 zoi2 禁止屠宰), prohibited the butchering of animals, as in time of drought;
    • ün꜅ ꜂tsoi (jyun6 zoi2 縣宰), a district magistrate;
    • ꜂tsoi ꜁ngau (zoi2 ngau4 宰牛), to kill an ox.

載 ꜂Tsoi (zoi2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾞ + 6
Unicode code point
U+8F09
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 44 載) 年也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 589 ꜂載)
    • A year (Kangxi: 年也);
    • to commence, as after a full revolution (Kangxi: 載始也取物終更始之義);
    • pún꜄ ꜂tsoi (bun3 zoi2 半載), half a year;
    • ꜀tsʻín ꜂tsoi há꜅ (cin1 zoi2 haa6 千載下) handed down a thousand years;
    • ꜂kí ꜂tsoi (gei2 zoi2 幾載), several years.
  • (Page 589 載꜄ Tsoi꜄ (zoi3); here corrected to ꜂Tsoi (zoi2))
    • (…);
    • an incalculably high number;
    • (…).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "incalculably high number": Based on the progression 億兆京垓秭壤溝澗正載, one of the following:
    • 1014 (starting from 億 = 105 and going up by factors of 10);
    • 1017 (starting from 億 = 108 and going up by factors of 10);
    • 1044 (starting from 億 = 108 and going up by factors of 104);
    • 104096 (starting from 億 = 108 and going up by squares).
    It is an intuitive feeling that has led me to put this sense under rising-tone ꜂tsoi (zoi2) rather than departing-tone tsoi꜄ (zoi3), noting I have not seen solid evidence to back up my decision. I am happy to be proven wrong, and for Williams to be vindicated.
See also

再 Tsoi꜄ (zoi3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼌ + 4
Unicode code point
U+518D
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 46 再 Tsoi꜅ (zoi6)) 又也乃也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 589 再꜄)
    • Again;
    • doubled, repeated (Kangxi: 兩也, 重也);
    • a second time (Kangxi: 一舉而二也);
    • then, further, continued, longer (Kangxi: 仍也);
    • to repeat;
    • ꜂tsʻing tsoi꜄ ꜂kong (cing2 zoi3 gong2 請再講), please say it again;
    • tsoi꜄ iú꜄ ꜀tʻím (zoi3 jiu3 tim1 再要添), I shall want some more;
    • ꜁ming yat꜇ tsoi꜄ ꜁loi (ming4 jat6 zoi3 loi4 明日再來), come back to-morrow;
    • tsoi꜄ kwo꜄ ꜂kí yat꜇ (zoi3 gwo3 gei2 jat6 再過幾日) again some days elapsed;
    • tsoi꜄ ꜀shang (zoi3 sang1 再生) another life;
    • tsoi꜄ ꜁'m tsoi꜄ sz'꜄ (zoi3 saam1 zoi3 si3 再三再四) again and again [I ordered him];
    • tsoi꜄ ꜁'m ꜂kú tò꜄ (zoi3 m4 gu2 dou3-2 再唔估到) I should not have guessed it;
    • tsoi꜄ hai꜅ ꜂kòm ꜁'m tak꜆ (zoi3 hai6 gam2 m4 dak1 再係噉唔得) it will not do for you to do so again;
    • tsoi꜄ ꜂ché (zoi3 ze2 再者), further;
    • tsoi꜄ tuk꜇ kwo꜄ (zoi3 duk6 gwo3 再讀過) read it again;
    • tsoi꜄ pat꜆ ꜁loi (zoi3 bat1 loi4 再不來), I shall not return.

載 Tsoi꜄ (zoi3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾞ + 6
Unicode code point
U+8F09
Reading variations
  • zoi6 (per 《廣韻》昨代切 for "transport" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 45 載) 裝載又載籍
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 589 載꜄)
    • To contain, to hold, as a carriage or ship (Kangxi: 乗也);
    • to fill in, to load on, to bear, to sustain (Kangxi: 承也勝也);
    • to act, to do (Kangxi: 行也), to fullfil fulfil (Kangxi: 成也), to effect;
    • to record (Kangxi: 記載也);
    • to adorn, to beautify (Kangxi: 飾也);
    • to load in and transport (Kangxi: 舟車運物也);
    • written, recorded;
    • filled, loaded (Kangxi: 滿也);
    • an auxiliary word, then, thereupon (Kangxi: 則也助語辭);
    • (…);
    • ꜀chong tsoi꜄ (zong1 zoi3 裝載) to store, to load, as a carriage;
    • tsoi꜄ ꜃mún (zoi3 mun5 載滿), loaded full;
    • tsoi꜄ lok꜇ ꜁ho ꜀shü (zoi3 lok6 ho4 syu1 載落何書), in what book is it recorded?
    • ꜁'m kau꜄ tsoi꜄ (m4 gau3 zoi3 唔夠載) not enough to load it;
    • ꜃mò ꜃yé tsoi꜄ chü꜄ chü꜅ (mou5 je5 zoi3 zyu6 冇嘢載住) I've nothing to put it in;
    • ꜂pí ko꜄ ꜃tʻò tsoi꜄ (bei2 go3 tou5 zoi3 畀個肚載) he lays it up in his mind, he'll remember it;
    • ꜁nang tsoi꜄ ꜂kí tám꜄ (nang4 zoi3 gei2 daam3 能載幾擔) she carries several peculs.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 冇嘢載住: Corrected chü꜄ (zyu3) to chü꜅ (zyu6).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Deeds, works (Kangxi: 事也).

儎 Tsoi꜅ (zoi6)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 13
Unicode code point
U+510E
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 589 儎꜅)

    A colloquial word.

    • The cargo, the loading of a boat;
    • the tonnage or capacity of a vessel;
    • ꜂kí shap꜇ tsoi꜅ (gei2 sap6 zoi6 幾十儎) several scores of cargoes;
    • ꜁shün ꜃mún tsoi꜅ (syun4 mun5 zoi6 船滿儎) the ship is loaded;
    • chák꜆ tsoi꜅ shek꜇ (zaak3 zoi6 sek6 矺儎石) stone ballast;
    • tsoi꜅ kéuk꜆ (zoi6 goek3 儎腳) freight money;
    • ꜀chong mán꜅ pát꜆ tsoi꜅ (zong1 maan6 baat3 zoi6 裝萬八儎) she carries 18,000 catties.

在 Tsoi꜅ (zoi6)

Radical + residual strokes
⼟ + 3
Unicode code point
U+5728
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 46 在) 居也存也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 589 在꜅)
    • To be in, to pertain to, to be at;
    • to reside, to remain, to live, to dwell at (Kangxi 𡉈: 居也);
    • to belong to, to consist in;
    • involved in, depending on;
    • to be preserved or continue;
    • existing, being, alive (Kangxi 𡉈: 存也);
    • is in, in, at;
    • to examine (Kangxi 𡉈: 察也);
    • a place, a home;
    • ꜁'m tsoi꜅ uk꜆ (m4 zoi6 uk1 唔在屋) not at home;
    • ꜁'m tsoi꜅ chʻü꜄ (m4 zoi6 cyu3 唔在處) not in, not here;
    • ín꜅ tsoi꜅ (jin6 zoi6 現在) at this moment, now;
    • ꜁wán tsoi꜅ (waan4 zoi6 還在) still here;
    • tsoi꜅ hok꜇ ꜁tʻong (zoi6 hok6 tong4-2 在學堂), at school;
    • tsoi꜅ wai꜅ ꜂kí noi꜅ ꜀ní ꜀né (zoi6 wai6 gei2 noi6 ne1 在位幾耐呢), how long has he reigned?
    • fú꜅ ꜃mò tsoi꜅ (fu6 mou5 zoi6 父母在) parents are alive;
    • tsoi꜅ há꜅ ꜂tai ꜀tai (zoi6 haa6 dai1 在下低), is down stairs;
    • ꜁'m tsoi꜅ (m4 zoi6 唔在) he's not in; dead; no occasion, no need, — a polite phrase;
    • tsoi꜅ ꜀ká ꜀to (zoi6 gaa1 do1 在家多), remains much at home;
    • ꜃ní tsoi꜅ ꜀sam (nei5 zoi6 sam1 你在心), remember it;
    • ꜁'m tsz'꜅ tsoi꜅ (m4 zi6 zoi6 唔自在) not feeling well;
    • tsoi꜅ ꜃ngo tsau꜅ ꜂kòm yéung꜅ (zoi6 ngo5 zau6 gam2 joeng6-2 在我就噉樣) I've made up my mind;
    • ꜁nín ꜃mán tsoi꜅ tsik꜆ (nin4 maan5 zoi6 zik1 年晚在即) new year's eve is at hand;
    • ꜂sho tsoi꜅ pít꜆ ꜃yau (so2 zoi6 bit1 jau5 所在必有), it is everywhere, they are in all places;
    • tsʻo꜄ tsoi꜅ ꜃ngo (co3 zoi6 ngo5 錯在我), it is my fault;
    • ꜂sz' tsoi꜅ ꜂tsò ꜃mán (sei2 zoi6 zou2 maan5 死在早晚), his ailment is imminent; he does things blunderingly;
    • pat꜆ tsoi꜅ ꜁ú ꜁fú (bat1 zoi6 fu4 不在乎), does not consist in;
    • tsoi꜅ ꜀pín chʻü꜄ (zoi6 bin1 cyu3 在邊處) where is it?
    • tsoi꜅ ꜃ní (zoi6 nei5 在你), it's your affair.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 在位幾耐呢: Changed ꜀ní (ni1) to modern ꜀né (ne1).
  • Williams' 在下低: Corrected ꜂tai (dai2) to ꜀tai (dai1).
  • Williams' 不在乎: Changed ꜁ú (wu4) to common reading ꜁fú (fu4).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Whereabouts (Kangxi: 所在也).
