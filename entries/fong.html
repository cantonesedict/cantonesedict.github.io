Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 96 方) 方正方[術]方法[量]也矩也[類]也且也有也又四方又方圓
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 55 ꜀方)
    • Square (Kangxi: […]方者中矩);
    • cornered, angular;
    • a place, a region, a side, a direction (Kangxi: 方名如東西也);
    • manner, art, rule, means (Kangxi: 道也, 術也法也);
    • uprightness, correctness (Kangxi: 義之宜也), regularity (Kangxi: 常也);
    • then, thereupon, in that case, in consequence of;
    • towards, to;
    • like;
    • to compare (Kangxi: 比方也), to lay together;
    • to possess (Kangxi: 有之也);
    • a prescription (Kangxi: 醫方);
    • a thin board or tablet (Kangxi: 板也);
    • a written list of vassals or retainers;
    • sz'꜄ ꜀fong (sei3 fong1 四方), square, the four points, everywhere, all around;
    • ꜀fong ching꜄ (fong1 zing3 方正) upright, correct;
    • ꜀fong ꜂ho (fong1 ho2 方可), proper, suitable;
    • ꜃yau ꜀fong (jau5 fong1 有方), there are rules;
    • ꜀fong pín꜅ (fong1 bin6 方便) convenient, all ready, to do good to, to oblige, to bestow charity;
    • ꜁léung ꜀fong (loeng4 fong1 良方), a good prescription;
    • ꜀fong wai꜅ (fong1 wai6-2 方位) a seat, residence, location;
    • ꜀fong fát꜆ (fong1 faat3 方法) means or way of doing;
    • tái꜅ ꜀fong (daai6 fong1 大方), liberal, generous in feeling;
    • ꜀fong ꜁tsʻoi (fong1 coi4 方纔), then, just now;
    • ꜀fong ꜂chʻé (fong1 ce2 方且), about to do, just then:;
    • ꜀fong ꜀kam (fong1 gam1 方今), now;
    • ꜀fong tsʻün꜄ (fong1 cyun3 方寸) the heart;
    • ꜀fong léuk꜆ léuk꜇ (fong1 loek6 方略) a stratagem;
    • ꜀hoi ꜀fong (hoi1 fong1 開方), to commence a new business.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Fixed missing dot in 術, corrected malformed 量, and normalised 𩔖 to 類.
  • Williams' "a written list of vassals or retainers": This is probably a misreading of Kangxi 《儀禮聘禮》不及百名書於方 as a definition, when it is actually an example.
  • Williams' 方略: Corrected léuk꜆ (loek3) to léuk꜇ (loek6).
  • Williams' 開方: Also to take the square root.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Boats abreast (Kangxi: 倂船也).
  2. A raft (Kangxi: 方泭也).
  3. Just grown (Kangxi: 穀始生未實也).
  4. Just now.
  5. Now (Kangxi: 今也).
  6. A cube, a power.
  7. The 70th radical ("square").
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【方】 (pjang)
    • (noun-like) square, cube, (met.) block
    • (noun-like) direction, side
    • (noun-like) place, (met.) [to] occupy
    • (suffix)
    • (noun-like) regularity, norm, prescription
    • (adjective-like) upright
    • (noun-like) method
    • (adverb-like) abreast, (met.) [to] compare, raft
    • (adverb-like) just, (just) now, [only] then, when
    • (adverb-like) about to
