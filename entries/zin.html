氈 ꜀Chín (zin1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽑ + 13
Unicode code point
U+6C08
Alternative forms
  • 毡
  • 氊
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 20 氊) 以毛爲物曰氊
  • (Vol. 1 Image 20 毡) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 16* ꜀毡꜀氊; here normalised to 氊)
    • Hair or wool worked into a felt;
    • coarse woolens, as blankets, carpets, rugs;
    • tí꜅ ꜀chín (dei6 zin1 地氈), a carpet;
    • ꜀chín mò꜅ (zin1 mou6-2 氈帽) a felt hat;
    • pák꜇ ꜀chín (baak6 zin1 白氈), a blanket;
    • ꜁hái ꜀chín (haai4 zin1 鞋氈), felt for soles;
    • chʻéuk꜆ ꜀chín (coek3 zin1 桌氈), a table-cover;
    • ꜀chín ꜀páu (zin1 baau1 氈包), a carpet-bag;
    • ꜀chín ꜂chí (zin1 zi2 氈紙), coarse rough paper.
