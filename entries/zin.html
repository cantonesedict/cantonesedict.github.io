  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 栽 to 裁 and ⿰糸殳 to 殺.

  • 【鉸剪】 (gaau3 zin2):
    1. Scissors. Also spelt 較剪 (per Fan Wan).
      • 把鉸剪鈍 (baa2 gaau3 zin2 deon6): "[The] pair of scissors [is] blunt."

    • yat꜆ tsín꜄ ꜀chí tí꜅ (jat1 zin3 zi1 dei6 一箭之地) a bowshot, or 120 chʻek꜆ (cek3 尺).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 火箭: Now "rocket".
  • Williams' 放冷箭: Corrected ꜃lang (lang5) to ꜃láng (laang5).

Reading variations

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 23 薦) 舉薦祭亦曰薦
  • (Vol. 1 Image 23 荐) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 573 荐꜄)
    • Interchanged with the last (薦);
    • also to continue;
    • some;
    • to succeed, to recur repeatedly, as a famine;
    • repeatedly, again.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 墊 (for "padding" senses). See 墊 zin3 for the canonical entry.)

    • Grass eaten by animals;

    • to lay out food;
    • to adore, to worship without a victim;

    • tsín꜄ tsʻoi꜄ (zin3 coi3 薦菜) take a little greens—with your wine; said to a guest;
    • tsín꜄ ꜂hí (zin3 hei2 薦起) raise it up.
