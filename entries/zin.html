煎 ꜀Tsín (zin1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽕ + 9
Unicode code point
U+714E
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 20 煎) 熬煎
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 572 ꜀煎)
    • To fry, to roast in a pan;
    • to vex, to harass;
    • ꜀tsín ꜀kò (zin1 gou1 煎膏), to express lard;
    • yung꜅ ꜁yau ꜀tsín (jung6 jau4 zin1 用油煎), use fat in frying it;
    • mán꜅ ꜂fo ꜀tsín ꜁ü (maan6 fo2 zin1 jyu4-2 慢火煎魚), a slow fire tries fish; i. e. small bets will erelong drain the purse;
    • ꜀tsín [꜀]nung (zin1 nung1 煎燶), to fry brown;
    • [꜁]ngò ꜀tsín (ngou4 zin1 熬煎), to simmer, to decoct; vexed, irritated.

    Read tsín꜄ (zin3); (see 煎 zin3).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 煎燶: Corrected ꜁nung (nung4) to ꜀nung (nung1).
  • Williams' 熬煎: Corrected ngò꜅ (ngou6) to ꜁ngò (ngou4).

展 ꜂Chín (zin2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼫ + 7
Unicode code point
U+5C55
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 22 展) 舒展
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 17* ꜂展)
    • To open out, to unroll, and inspect;
    • to exhibit, to disentangle;
    • to stretch out at one's ease, to expand;
    • to judge of, to look into;
    • true, sincere;
    • cheerful, pleasant;
    • gratified;
    • yuk꜇ ꜂chín (juk6 zin2 玉展), may you open this;
    • ꜂chín hon꜄ (zin2 hon3 展看) to open and look at;
    • ꜂chín ꜀hoi (zin2 hoi1 展開), to spread out, to open (as a letter);
    • ꜂chín ꜁mí (zin2 mei4 展眉), a pleasant face;
    • ꜂chín ún꜅ (zin2 wun6 展緩) to defer, to put off to get time;
    • ꜂chín hán꜅ (zin2 haan6 展限) to extend the time.

戰 Chín꜄ (zin3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼽ + 12
Unicode code point
U+6230
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 25 戰) 閩戰又恐懼貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 17* 戰꜄)
    • To fight;
    • to be alarmed, struck with dread;
    • fearful, anxious;
    • a battle, an engagement;
    • war, hostilities;
    • military, pertaining to war;
    • hüt꜆ chín꜄ (hyut3 zin3 血戰) a bloody fight;
    • chín꜄ shing꜄ (zin3 sing3 戰勝) a victory;
    • ꜂tá chín꜄ (daa2 zin3 打戰) terrified;
    • chín꜄ tau꜄ (zin3 dau3 戰鬥) or ꜀káu chín꜄ (gaau1 zin3 交戰) to join battle;
    • chín꜄ [chan꜅] (zin3 zan6 戰陳), troops drawn up for battle;
    • chín꜄ pái[꜅] (zin3 baai6 戰敗) to be discomfited;
    • chín꜄ chín꜄ ꜀king ꜀king (zin3 zin3 ging1 ging1 戰戰兢兢), frightened, afraid of;
    • chín꜄ ꜁shün (zin3 syun4 戰船), a war-junk, a ship of war;
    • chín꜄ ꜀shü (zin3 syu1 戰書), a challenge to fight.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: 閩 is probably a typo for 鬪 or similar.
  • Williams' 戰陳: Corrected ꜁chʻan (can4) to chan꜅ (zan6).
  • Williams' 戰敗: Corrected pái꜄ (baai3) to pái꜅ (baai6).
