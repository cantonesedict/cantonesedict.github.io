Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 65 ꜀風)
    • Breath, spirit, passion;
    • air-gusts, gale, wind (Kangxi: 風以動萬物也 etc.);
    • haste, fleetness;
    • manners, deportment, etiquette;
    • administration, policy;
    • fame, example, fashion (Kangxi: 風俗);
    • instruction, reformation (Kangxi: 敎也, 風是諸侯政敎也);
    • institutes, influence;
    • disposition, temper;
    • to affect by example;
    • heat in animals (Kangxi: 佚也, […]風放也牝牡相誘謂之風[…]);
    • tái꜅ ꜀fung (daai6 fung1 大風), a gale, a tyfoon;
    • ꜀fung ꜃máng (fung1 maang5 風猛), the wind is violent;
    • shun꜅ ꜀fung (seon6 fung1 順風), a fair wind;
    • ꜂shím ꜀shán ꜀fung (sim2 saan1 fung1 閃山風), a gust;
    • ngák꜇ ꜀fung (ngaak6 fung1 額風), a head wind;
    • ꜁wáng ꜀fung (waang4 fung1 橫風), a side wind;
    • ꜀fung ꜀sheng (fung1 seng1 風聲), or ꜁lau ꜀fung (lau4 fung1 流風), a rumor;
    • ꜀fung tsuk꜇ (fung1 zuk6 風俗) usage;
    • ꜀fung fá꜄ (fung1 faa3 風化) influence of example;
    • ꜁man ꜀fung (man4 fung1 民風), character of a people;
    • ꜀fung üt꜇ (fung1 jyut6 風月) dissipation;
    • ꜀fung ꜁lau (fung1 lau4 風流), gaiety, pleasure;
    • ꜁man ꜀fung (man4 fung1 文風), a literary spirit;
    • ꜀fung ꜂shui (fung1 seoi2 風水), geomancy;
    • ꜀fung ꜀séung (fung1 soeng1 風箱), a bellows;
    • ꜂kwai ꜁tʻau ꜀fung (gwai2 tau4 fung1 鬼頭風), an adverse wind;
    • ꜁man ꜀fung chuk꜆ ꜂ying (man4 fung1 zuk1 jing2 聞風捉影), a newsmonger, an idle talebearer;
    • ꜀kòm pái꜄ há꜄ há꜅ ꜀fung (gam1 baai3 haa6 fung1 甘拜下風), I willingly acknowledge your superiority;
    • ꜀fung ꜁tsʻing (fung1 cing4 風情) lascivious looks.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 瘋. See 瘋 fung1 for the canonical entry.) (TODO)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 大風: In modern usage, it is 颱風 that is a typhoon.
  • Williams' 甘拜下風: Corrected há꜄ (haa3) to há꜅ (haa6).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. News.
  2. Scenery.
    • 放風 (fong3 fung1): "Release [the] news."
    • 收到風 (sau1 dou3-2 fung1): "Received news."
  3. "Wind" in Chinese medicine.
    • 傷風 (soeng1 fung1): "[A] cold."
    • 中風 (zung3 fung1): "Stroke."
  4. The 182nd radical ("wind").
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【風】 (pjuwng)
    • (noun-like) wind
    • (noun-like) style, manner
    • (noun-like) (met.) air
    • (noun-like) (met.) [in] heat
