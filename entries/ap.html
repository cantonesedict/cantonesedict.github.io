Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 4 洽꜆)

    (…)

    A colloquial word.

    • To cover, over, to keep from the air;
    • to cover, as with a poultice;
    • poor, inferior;
    • slovenly, neglected;
    • ap꜆ wái꜅ (ap1 waai6 洽壞) spoiled by mold;
    • ap꜆ ap꜆ tsʻui꜄ (ap1 ap1 ceoi3 洽洽脆) crinkling, as light pastry;
    • ap꜆ chap꜆ pʻò꜄ (ap1 zap1 pou3 洽汁舖) a miserable, dirty shop;
    • ap꜆ ꜀chʻong (ap1 cong1 洽瘡), to poultice;
    • ꜀úi ꜁shü ap꜆ ú꜅ (wui1 syu4 ap1 wu6 煨薯洽芋) to roast a potato or taro in ashes; met. to enjoy one's self secretly.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【洽】 (ap1)
    1. To cover and keep wet (when undesirable to do so).
