塊 Fái꜄ (faai3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼟ + 10
Unicode code point
U+584A
Alternative forms
  • 凷
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 28 塊) 片也片也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 44 塊꜄)
    • A clod (Kangxi: 墣也), a lump;
    • a piece of, a fraction;
    • a classifier of small things which are flattish and usually shapeless, as boards, panes, slices, &c.;
    • doltish;
    • yat꜆ fái꜄ yuk꜇ (jat1 faai3 juk6 一塊肉) a slice of meat;
    • yat꜆ fái꜄ tí꜅ (jat1 faai3 dei6 一塊地) a piece of land;
    • hòm꜅ fái꜄ (ham6 faai3 冚塊) the whole space;
    • tái꜅ fái꜄ (daai6 faai3 大塊) the globe, nature (Kangxi: 造物之名曰大塊);
    • fái꜄ ꜁ín ꜁mò ꜀chí (faai3 jin4 mou4 zi1 塊然無知), stupid.

    Used for the pronoun I (Kangxi: 一曰楚人謂我曰塊).

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: The headword 塊 is printed with the top dot missing in the component 鬼.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Solitary (Kangxi: 孑也).

快 Fái꜄ (faai3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼼ + 4
Unicode code point
U+5FEB
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 28 快) 稱意也來也喜也又疾速也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 44 快꜄)
    • Contented (Kangxi: 稱心也可也), glad (Kangxi: 喜也);
    • pleasure, cheerfulness;
    • quick, hasty (Kangxi: 爽快也急疾也);
    • alacrity;
    • prompt;
    • fái꜄ lok꜇ (faai3 lok6 快樂) happy;
    • pat꜆ ꜂shong fái꜄ (bat1 song2 faai3 不爽快) indisposed, out of sorts;
    • fái꜄ út꜇ (faai3 jyut6 快悅) delighted, good spirits;
    • (…);
    • fái꜄ fái꜄ (faai3 faai3 快快) quick!
    • fái꜄ ꜂tsz' ꜂shau (faai3 zi2 sau2 快子手), a headsman;
    • fái꜄ ꜃tʻeng (faai3 teng5 快艇), a fast-boat;
    • fái꜄ ꜃má ꜁lai (faai3 maa5 lai4 快馬嚟), bring it quickly;
    • fái꜄ ꜀pán (faai3 baan1 快班), a lictor in a magistrate's office;
    • fái꜄ tik꜆ ꜀fán ꜁loi (faai3 dik1 faan1 loi4 快滴返來), come back quick;
    • fái꜄ ꜃má (faai3 maa5 快馬), a courier;
    • fái꜄ tsʻui꜄ (faai3 ceoi3 快脆) speedy.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 筷. See 筷 faai3 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 快子手: Probably related to 劊子手.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Frank, straightforward.
  2. Sharp.
  3. Soon.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【快脆】 (faai3 ceoi3):
    1. Quick.
      • 咁快脆 (gam3 faai3 ceoi3): "That quick?"
      • 行快脆啲 (haang4 faai3 ceoi3 di1): "Walk quicker."

  • (Page 44 快꜄; here normalised to 筷)
    • (…);
    • fái꜄ ꜂tsz' (faai3 zi2 筷子) chopsticks;
