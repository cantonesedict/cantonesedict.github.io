快 Fái꜄ (faai3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼼ + 4
Unicode code point
U+5FEB
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 28 快) 稱意也來也喜也又疾速也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 44 快꜄)
    • Contented (Kangxi: 稱心也可也), glad (Kangxi: 喜也);
    • pleasure, cheerfulness;
    • quick, hasty (Kangxi: 爽快也急疾也);
    • alacrity;
    • prompt;
    • fái꜄ lok꜇ (faai3 lok6 快樂) happy;
    • pat꜆ ꜂shong fái꜄ (bat1 song2 faai3 不爽快) indisposed, out of sorts;
    • fái꜄ út꜇ (faai3 jyut6 快悅) delighted, good spirits;
    • fái꜄ ꜂tsz' (faai3 zi2 快子) chopsticks;
    • fái꜄ fái꜄ (faai3 faai3 快快) quick!
    • fái꜄ ꜂tsz' ꜂shau (faai3 zi2 sau2 快子手), a headsman;
    • fái꜄ ꜃tʻeng (faai3 teng5 快艇), a fast-boat;
    • fái꜄ ꜃má ꜁lai (faai3 maa5 lai4 快馬嚟), bring it quickly;
    • fái꜄ ꜀pán (faai3 baan1 快班), a lictor in a magistrate's office;
    • fái꜄ tik꜆ ꜀fán ꜁loi (faai3 dik1 faan1 loi4 快滴返來), come back quick;
    • fái꜄ ꜃má (faai3 maa5 快馬), a courier;
    • fái꜄ tsʻui꜄ (faai3 ceoi3 快脆) speedy.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 快子手: Probably related to 劊子手.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Frank, straightforward.
  2. Sharp.
  3. Soon.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【快脆】 (faai3 ceoi3):
    1. Quick.
      • 咁快脆 (gam3 faai3 ceoi3): "That quick?"
      • 行快脆啲 (haang4 faai3 ceoi3 di1): "Walk quicker."
