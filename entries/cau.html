Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 50 抽) 收也援引也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 26 ꜀抽)
    • To take from, to take out;
    • to take a dividend;
    • to expand, as spring does;
    • to lift;
    • to levy, to assess, as duty;
    • to select, to draw out;
    • to utter aloud;
    • to whip;
    • to receive one's portion;
    • to raise water;
    • a tenth, a tithe;
    • ꜀chʻau ꜂shui (cau1 seoi2 抽水), to take the fee at gaming;
    • ꜀chʻau ꜀kan (cau1 gan1 抽筋), spasms of the tendons;
    • ꜀chʻau ꜂hí (cau1 hei2 抽起), lift it out;
    • ꜀chʻau chʻing꜄ ꜃kʻü ꜁lai (cau1 cing3 keoi5 lai4 抽秤), bring him along;
    • ꜂tá ꜀chʻau ꜀fung (daa2 cau1 fung1 打抽風), to beg aid;
    • ꜂pí ꜂pín ꜀chʻau ꜃kʻü (bei2 bin1 cau1 keoi5 畀鞭抽佢), drive him off with a whip;
    • ꜀chʻau ꜃kʻü ꜃léung ꜃há (cau1 keoi5 loeng5 haa5 抽佢兩吓), whipped him twice;
    • ꜀chʻau ꜀shau (cau1 sau1 抽收), to levy duty on;
    • ꜀chʻau ꜀shan pat꜆ há꜅ (cau1 san1 bat1 haa6 抽身不暇) no leisure at all;
    • ꜀chʻau shat꜇ (cau1 sat6 抽實) lift it up firmly;
    • ꜀chʻau mok[꜆] (cau1 mok1 抽剝) to extort, to exact a high fee;
    • ꜀chʻau ꜁chʻau (cau1 cau4 抽籌), or ꜀chʻau ꜁tʻau (cau1 tau4 抽頭), to divide winnings;
    • ꜀chʻau ꜁hán (cau1 haan4 抽閒), to take a leisure hour;
    • ꜀chʻau ꜀fan (cau1 fan1 抽分), to take a commission, to reserve a part;
    • ꜀ká yat꜆ ꜀chʻau (gaa1 jat1 cau1 加一抽), take a tenth.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 抽筋: Medically, these are skeletal muscle contractions (i.e. cramps).
  • Williams' 抽剝: Corrected mok꜇ (mok6) to mok꜆ (mok1).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【抽】 (cau1):
    1. Classifier for things in a bunch that will fall together when lifted.
      • 大抽鎖匙 (daai6 cau1 so2 si4): "Big bunch of keys."
    2. To fight.
    3. (Chinese chess) To win a piece with check, either by skewering, forking, or discovery.
      • 將軍抽車 (zoeng1 gwan1 cau1 geoi1): "Check, skewering [a] chariot."
      • 抽炮 (cau1 paau3): "[Check], winning [a] cannon."
  • 【抽氣扇】 (cau1 hei3 sin3):
    1. A ventilating fan.
  • 【老抽】 (lou5 cau1):
    1. Old soy sauce (dark coloured but less salty).
  • 【生抽】 (saang1 cau1):
    1. Young soy sauce (light coloured but more salty).
  • 【隻抽】 (zek3 cau1):
    1. To fight one-on-one.
