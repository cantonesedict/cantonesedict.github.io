創 ꜀Chʻong (cong1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼑ + 10
Unicode code point
U+5275
Alternative forms
  • 刅
  • 戧
Reading variations
  • cong3 (common)
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28* ꜀創꜀刅)
    • To wound, to cut and injure;
    • to butt, to gore;
    • a wound, a cut;
    • wounded, gashed;
    • ꜀shan pí꜅ ꜀to ꜀chʻong (san1 bei6 do1 cong1 身被多創) he received many wounds.
Conway's commentary
  • Whilst the classical reading for the "wound" sense is level-tone ꜀chʻong (cong1), the common reading departing-tone chʻong꜄ (cong3) is not unattested; Kangxi has 又《唐韻》初亮切瘡去聲始也造也[…]又傷也《前漢匈奴傳》人民創艾戰鬭.

瘡 ꜀Chʻong (cong1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽧ + 10
Unicode code point
U+7621
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 96 瘡) 瘡疾傷也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28* ꜀瘡)
    • A sore, ulcer, boil, or eruption;
    • ꜀chʻong ꜀ná (cong1 naa1 瘡𤶸), a scar;
    • ꜀chʻong ꜀chʻün (cong1 cyun1 瘡穿), to open a boil;
    • ꜀chʻong lau꜅ (cong1 lau6 瘡瘻) the piles;
    • ꜀[sháng] ꜀chʻong (saang1 cong1 生瘡), to have a sore;
    • ꜂fo ꜀[teng] ꜀chʻong (fo2 deng1 cong1 火釘瘡), a boil;
    • wát꜆ yuk꜇ ꜂lo ꜀chʻong ꜀[sháng], (waat3 juk6 lo2 cong1 saang1 挖肉攞瘡生) he scrapes the flesh to make a sore — he trumps up a cause against me;
    • kí꜄ ꜀chʻong (gei3 cong1 寄瘡), to give away a boil.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 生瘡: Changed ꜀shang (sang1) to vernacular reading ꜀sháng (saang1).
  • Williams' 火釘瘡: Changed ꜀ting (ding1) to vernacular reading ꜀teng (deng1).
  • Williams' 挖肉攞瘡生: Changed ꜀shang (sang1) to vernacular reading ꜀sháng (saang1). Not "he trumps up a cause against me", but "he brings unnecessary trouble onto himself".

Radical + residual strokes
⼴ + 4
Unicode code point
U+5E8A
Alternative forms
  • 牀
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 97 床) 臥[榻]
  • (Vol. 1 Image 97 牀) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28* ꜁床꜁牀)
    • A bed, a couch;
    • a settee;
    • a suit of bed clothes;
    • the boards which make a bed;
    • a well-curb;
    • a measure of 8 cubits;
    • yat꜆ ꜁chʻong ꜃pʻí yuk꜇ (jat1 cong4 pei5 juk6 一床被褥) mattrass, sheets, all complete;
    • ꜀pʻò ꜁chʻong (pou1 cong4 鋪床), make the bed;
    • yat꜆ ꜀chéung ꜁chʻong (jat1 zoeng1 cong4 一張床), a bed;
    • tái꜅ ꜁chʻong (daai6 cong4 大床), a double bedstead;
    • kʻong꜄ ꜁chʻong (kong3 cong4 匟床), the couch of honor in a hall;
    • ꜁chʻong ꜀pʻò (cong4 pou1 床鋪), bedding;
    • kwai꜄ ꜀fí ꜁chʻong (gwai3 fei1 cong4 貴妃床), a couch with rolling ends;
    • ꜃shéung ꜁chʻong (soeng5 cong4 上床), to go to bed.

  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 㮼 to 榻.
