創 ꜀Chʻong (cong1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼑ + 10
Unicode code point
U+5275
Alternative forms
  • 刅
  • 戧
Reading variations
  • cong3 (common)
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28* ꜀創꜀刅)
    • To wound, to cut and injure;
    • to butt, to gore;
    • a wound, a cut;
    • wounded, gashed;
    • ꜀shan pí꜅ ꜀to ꜀chʻong (san1 bei6 do1 cong1 身被多創) he received many wounds.
Conway's commentary
  • Whilst the classical reading for the "wound" sense is level-tone ꜀chʻong (cong1), the common reading departing-tone chʻong꜄ (cong3) is not unattested; Kangxi has 又《唐韻》初亮切瘡去聲始也造也[…]又傷也《前漢匈奴傳》人民創艾戰鬭.
See also
  • 創 cong3

瘡 ꜀Chʻong (cong1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽧ + 10
Unicode code point
U+7621
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 96 瘡) 瘡疾傷也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28* ꜀瘡)
    • A sore, ulcer, boil, or eruption;
    • ꜀chʻong ꜀ná (cong1 naa1 瘡𤶸), a scar;
    • ꜀chʻong ꜀chʻün (cong1 cyun1 瘡穿), to open a boil;
    • ꜀chʻong lau꜅ (cong1 lau6 瘡瘻) the piles;
    • ꜀[sháng] ꜀chʻong (saang1 cong1 生瘡), to have a sore;
    • ꜂fo ꜀[teng] ꜀chʻong (fo2 deng1 cong1 火釘瘡), a boil;
    • wát꜆ yuk꜇ ꜂lo ꜀chʻong ꜀[sháng], (waat3 juk6 lo2 cong1 saang1 挖肉攞瘡生) he scrapes the flesh to make a sore — he trumps up a cause against me;
    • kí꜄ ꜀chʻong (gei3 cong1 寄瘡), to give away a boil.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 生瘡: Changed ꜀shang (sang1) to vernacular reading ꜀sháng (saang1).
  • Williams' 火釘瘡: Changed ꜀ting (ding1) to vernacular reading ꜀teng (deng1).
  • Williams' 挖肉攞瘡生: Changed ꜀shang (sang1) to vernacular reading ꜀sháng (saang1). Not "he trumps up a cause against me", but "he brings unnecessary trouble onto himself".

廠 ꜂Chʻong (cong2)

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 98 [厰]) 屋無壁
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 29* ꜂厰; here normalised to 廠)
    • An open shed;
    • a covered place not walled in;
    • a place to receive taxes, broad, spacious;
    • a depository;
    • a large workshop;
    • ꜁múi tʻán꜄ ꜂chʻong (mui4 taan3 cong2 煤炭廠), a coal dépôt;
    • ꜀siú ꜂chʻong (siu1 cong2 硝廠), a saltpetre gabel;
    • ꜁pʻung ꜂chʻong (pung4 cong2 篷廠) a mat shed;
    • [꜂]ko [꜀]kán uk꜆ ꜂ho ꜂chʻong (go2 gaan1 uk1 hou2 cong2 嗰間屋好廠), that's a large house;
    • ꜂chʻong ꜀tʻeng (cong2 teng1 廠廳), a summer arbor.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword ⿸厂敝 to 厰.
  • Williams' 嗰間屋好廠: Corrected ko꜄ (go3) to ꜂ko (go2) and corrected ꜁kán (gaan4) to ꜀kán (gaan1).

Conway's commentary
  • Strictly, the reading ꜂chʻong (cong2) in rising tone is only for the "disappointed" sense in 愴恍 (which is probably just a different spelling of 惝怳, see 惝 cong2). The "grief" sense is correctly departing-tone chʻong꜄ (cong3).

闖 ꜂Chʻong (cong2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾨ + 10
Unicode code point
U+95D6
Reading variations
  • cam3 (per 《廣韻》丑禁切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 98 闖) 闖進出頭貌又音滲
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 29* ꜂闖)
    • A horse going out of a door;
    • to thrust out the head, to appear;
    • to bolt out;
    • suddenly, forcibly, abruptly;
    • ꜂chʻong wo꜅ (cong2 wo6 闖禍) to induce calamities;
    • ꜂chʻong ꜀kwán (cong2 gwaan1 闖關), to slip by the custom-house;
    • ꜂chʻong [tsʻák]꜇ ꜁loi (cong2 caak6 loi4 闖賊來), the rover Chʻong (闖) is on us!

    Also read ꜀sham (sam1).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 闖賊來: Corrected tsák꜇ (zaak6) to tsʻák꜇ (caak6).
  • Williams' alternative reading: Per 《說文》讀若郴.

Radical + residual strokes

  • (Vol. 1 Image 100 創) 創造創始又懲創儆也

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28* 創꜄刱꜄)
    • To begin, to invent;
    • to create, to found, to lay the foundation of;
    • to take measures for;
    • to reprove;
    • the commencement, first;

    • ꜀tʻín tí꜅ ꜀hoi chʻong꜄ (tin1 dei6 hoi1 cong3 天地開創) the creation;
    • chʻong꜄ lap꜇ ꜃má ꜁tʻau (cong3 lap6 maa5 tau4 創立碼頭), to build a ferry or jetty;
    • ꜁chʻing chʻong꜄ (cing4 cong3 懲創) to blame.
