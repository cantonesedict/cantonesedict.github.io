倉 ꜀Tsʻong (cong1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5009
Alternative forms
  • 蒼 (in 倉皇 spelt 蒼黃)
Reading variations
  • cong2 (common in 倉卒)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 95 倉) 倉廪藏谷之所又倉皇言懼也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 593 ꜀倉)
    • A granary (Kangxi: 穀藏也) of the government;
    • a storehouse;
    • a box, a bin, a compartment, a pigeon-hole;
    • kuk꜆ ꜀tsʻong (guk1 cong1 穀倉), a rice storehouse;
    • ꜀tsʻong ꜀séung (cong1 soeng1 倉箱), bins or partitions in a granary;
    • fo꜄ ꜀tsʻong (fo3 cong1 貨倉), a pack-house, a go-down;
    • ꜀hoi ꜀tsʻong (hoi1 cong1 開倉), to dispense rice from the granaries;
    • yat꜆ ꜀tsʻong ꜀tsʻong (jat1 cong1 cong1 一倉倉), one bin or stall;
    • ꜀tsʻong tsʻüt꜆ (cong1 cyut3 倉卒) flurried, perturbed, alarmed.
  • (Page 593 ꜀蒼; here normalised to 倉)
    • Occurs used for the last (倉), hastily;
    • (…);
    • ꜀tsʻong ꜁wong (cong1 wong4 倉皇), hurried, flighty (Kangxi 蒼: 蒼黃急遽貌);
    • (…).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 一倉倉: Better rendered "individual bins".
  • Williams' 倉卒: Not perturbed or alarmed. More hurried or hasty (Kangxi: 倉卒悤遽貌) than flurried.

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28* ꜀創꜀刅)
    • To wound, to cut and injure (Kangxi: 傷也);
    • to butt, to gore;
    • a wound, a cut;
    • wounded, gashed;
    • ꜀shan pí꜅ ꜀to ꜀chʻong (san1 bei6 do1 cong1 身被多創) he received many wounds.

Conway's commentary
  • Whilst the classical reading for the "wound" sense is level-tone ꜀chʻong (cong1), the common reading departing-tone chʻong꜄ (cong3) is not unattested; Kangxi has 又《唐韻》初亮切瘡去聲始也造也[…]又傷也《前漢匈奴傳》人民創艾戰鬭.

䱽 ꜀Tsʻong (cong1)

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 594 ꜀鯧꜀䱽)

    The second character (䱽) is unauthorized.

    • The pomfret;
    • pák꜇ ꜀tsʻong ꜁ü (baak6 cong1 jyu4-2 白䱽魚), the silver pomfret (Stromateus argenteus);
    • hak꜆ ꜀tsʻong (hak1 cong1 黑䱽), the black pomfret (Stromateus niger);
    • ꜁wong [láp]꜇ ꜀tsʻong (wong4 laap6 cong1 黃鱲䱽), the yellow pomfret (Trachinotus auratus);
    • ꜀fá ꜀tsʻong (faa1 cong1 花䱽), small pomfret (Caranx malabaricus);
    • ꜀kwá ꜂tsz' ꜀tsʻong (gwaa1 zi2 cong1 瓜子䱽), long-finned pomfret (Trachinotus asper).
Conway's commentary
  • Effectively, 鯧 and 䱽 are the same semantic word "pomfret", but:
    • 鯧 is classical with reading ꜀chʻéung (coeng1) (see 鯧 coeng1); and
    • 䱽 is vernacular with reading ꜀tsʻong (cong1).
  • Williams' 黃鱲䱽: Changed lap꜇ (lap6) to longer syllable láp꜇ (laap6).

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 594 ꜁藏)
    • To store away (Kangxi: 蓄也), to put in safety;
    • to contain;
    • to hide, to conceal (Kangxi: 匿也);
    • to accumulate;
    • to gather knowledge;
    • ꜁tsʻong ꜁mái (cong4 maai4 藏埋), to lay by;
    • ꜀ká ꜁tsʻong (gaa1 cong4 家藏), heirlooms laid by, family treasures;
    • ꜃mò ꜁tsʻong ꜁tʻau (mou5 cong4 tau4-2 冇藏頭), no chance for profit;
    • ꜁tsʻong ꜂pán (cong4 baan2 藏版), to keep the blocks, to publish a book;
    • ꜀páu ꜁tsʻong (baau1 cong4 包藏), to keep from, not to divulge;
    • ꜀shau ꜁tsʻong (sau1 cong4 收藏), to receive, as in trust;
    • ꜁'m hai꜅ ꜁tsʻong ꜁lung ꜀chí ꜂sho (m4 hai6 cong4 lung4 zi1 so2 唔係藏龍之所), I'm not to be cribbed in this place;
    • ꜂yan ꜁tsʻong (jan2 cong4 隱藏), to conceal;
    • ꜁tsʻong ꜁tʻau lò꜅ ꜃mí (cong4 tau4 lou6 mei5 藏頭露尾), to give a partial account of, an inkling.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 慝 to 匿, and corrected malformed 蓄.
