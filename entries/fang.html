Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【揈】 (fang4, fang6):
    1. To hit hard.
      • 揈佢兩錘 (fang4 keoi5 loeng5 ceoi4): "Hit him hard [with] two blows."

Olden attestation for 揈 ꜁fang (fang4) meaning "hit":

《集韻》乎萌切：揈：擊也
