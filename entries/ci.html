Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10* ꜀癡꜀痴)
    • Foolish, silly;
    • stupid, inapt;
    • idiotic;
    • doting after, lustful, hankering;
    • out of his head, wandering;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁ngoi (ci1 ngoi4 癡呆), foolish stolid;
    • ꜀chʻí chüt꜆ [꜂]kòm ꜀chʻün lün꜅ chʻü꜄ (ci zyut3 gam2 cyun1 lyun6 cyu3 癡拙噉穿亂處) running in and out, making such a rumpus;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁tsʻing (ci1 cing4 癡情), salacious;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁chʻín (ci1 cin4 癡纏) bound up in, loving;
    • pat꜆ ꜀chʻí pat꜆ ꜁lung (bat1 ci1 bat1 lung4 不癡不聾), if I am not foolish and deaf [I cannot reign]; (these square brackets appear in the original text)
    • ꜀shü ꜀chʻí (syu1 ci1 書癡), doting on books;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁[mai] pat꜆ ꜂sing (ci1 mai4 bat1 sing2 癡迷不醒) mad after.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 土 to 上.
  • Williams' 癡拙噉穿亂處: Corrected kòm꜄ (gam3) to ꜂kòm (gam2).
  • Williams' 癡迷不醒: Corrected ꜁mái (maai4) to ꜁mai (mai4).

黐 ꜀Chʻí (ci1)

Radical + residual strokes
⿉ + 11
Unicode code point
U+9ED0
Alternative forms
  • 𥼝 (⿰米禽)
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10* ꜀𥼝 (⿰米禽)꜀黐)
    • To attach to, to stick on;
    • glutinous, sticky, pasty;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜀káu (ci1 gaau1 黐膠), birdlime;
    • ꜀chʻí kéuk꜆ (ci1 goek3 黐腳) to sorn, to sponge for meals;
    • ꜁'m ꜀chʻí ꜁mái (m4 ci1 maai4 唔黐埋), it don't stick;
    • ꜀chʻí tak꜆ ꜂hò shat꜇ (ci1 dak1 hou2 sat6 黐得好實) sticks very tightly;
    • ꜀chʻí mak꜇ ꜀hong (ci1 mak6 hong1 黐麥糠), a sponger, a sorner.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【黐】 (ci1):
    1. To stick, to be sticky, to cling.
      • 黐住咗 (ci1 zyu6 zo2): "[It's] stuck [onto it]."
      • 好黐牙 (hou2 ci1 ngaa4): "Really clingy to [the] teeth."
      • 黐底 (ci1 dai2): "Stuck to [the] bottom [of the pot]."
      • 黐鑊 (ci1 wok6): "Stuck to [the] wok."
      • 黐𣲷𣲷 (ci1 nap6 nap6): "Sticky [and] wet."
    2. To freeload, to impose on another (for food etc.).
      • 黐餐 (ci1 caan1): "Sponge [a] meal."
      • 黐車坐 (ci1 ce1 co5): "Freeload for [a] car ride."
    3. Short for 黐線.
      • 做到黐咗 (zou6 dou3 ci1 zo2): "Worked till [he's] crazy."
  • 【黐肺】 (ci1 fai3):
    1. To be exhausted. (Literally, "stuck-together lungs".)
      • 熱到黐肺 (jit6 dou3 ci1 fai3): "So hot [I'm] knackered."
  • 【黐脷根】 (ci1 lei6 gan1):
    1. To have a lisp. (Literally, "sticky tongue-root".)
  • 【黐孖筋】 (ci1 maa1 gan1):
    1. Crazy, insane.
  • 【黐線】 (ci1 sin6):
    1. Crazy, insane.
      • 黐咗線咩 (ci1 zo2 sin3 me1): "[Have you] gone crazy?"
      • 黐線㗎 (ci1 sin3 ga3): "[It's] crazy!"

Olden attestation for 黐 ꜀chʻí (ci1) meaning "glue", hence "sticky":

  • 《廣韻》吕支切：黐：黏也又丑知切
  • 《廣韻》丑知切：黐：所以粘鳥又吕支切
  • 《康熙字典》黐：《廣韻》丑知切《集韻》《正韻》抽知切𠀤音摛《玉篇》黏也《廣韻》黐膠所以黏鳥　又《廣韻》呂支切《集韻》鄰知切𠀤音離義同

Radical + residual strokes

Fan Wan entry

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10* ꜀呞꜀𪗪 (⿰齒司); here normalised to 齝)
    • To ruminate, to chew the cud, vulgarly called ꜁ngau tʻò꜄ ꜂tsʻò (ngau4 tou3 cou2 牛吐草).
