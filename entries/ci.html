Conway's Cantonese entry

Cantonese prefers classical дјј over еғҸ.

  • гҖҗдјјгҖ‘ (ci5)
    1. To resemble, to be like.
      • дјјдҪўйҳҝеӘҪ (ci5 keoi5 aa3 maa1): "[She] resembles her mum."
      • еҘҪдјјеҫ—е’— (hou2 ci5 dak1 zo2): "Seems like [it] worked."
  • гҖҗдјјжЁЈгҖ‘ (ci5 joeng6-2)
    1. To resemble in appearance.

    2. To appear acceptable, to look the part.
      • еҷүдјјжЁЈе•І (gam2 ci5 joeng6-2 di1): "[It] looks more acceptable like this."
