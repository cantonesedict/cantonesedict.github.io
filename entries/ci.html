癡 ꜀Chʻí (ci1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽧ + 14
Unicode code point
U+7661
Alternative forms
  • 痴
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 36 痴) 痴呆
  • (Vol. 1 Image 36 癡) 同[上]愚也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10* ꜀癡꜀痴)
    • Foolish, silly;
    • stupid, inapt;
    • idiotic;
    • doting after, lustful, hankering;
    • out of his head, wandering;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁ngoi (ci1 ngoi4 癡呆), foolish stolid;
    • ꜀chʻí chüt꜆ [꜂]kòm ꜀chʻün lün꜅ chʻü꜄ (ci zyut3 gam2 cyun1 lyun6 cyu3 癡拙噉穿亂處) running in and out, making such a rumpus;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁tsʻing (ci1 cing4 癡情), salacious;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁chʻín (ci1 cin4 癡纏) bound up in, loving;
    • pat꜆ ꜀chʻí pat꜆ ꜁lung (bat1 ci1 bat1 lung4 不癡不聾), if I am not foolish and deaf [I cannot reign]; (these square brackets appear in the original text)
    • ꜀shü ꜀chʻí (syu1 ci1 書癡), doting on books;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁[mai] pat꜆ ꜂sing (ci1 mai4 bat1 sing2 癡迷不醒) mad after.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 土 to 上.
  • Williams' 癡拙噉穿亂處: Corrected kòm꜄ (gam3) to ꜂kòm (gam2).
  • Williams' 癡迷不醒: Corrected ꜁mái (maai4) to ꜁mai (mai4).

笞 ꜀Chʻí (ci1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽵ + 5
Unicode code point
U+7B1E
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 36 笞) 捶擊也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10* ꜀笞)
    • To punish with a bamboo, to bastinado;
    • to correct, so as to reform;
    • to scourge, to flog, to bamboo;
    • ꜀chʻí chéung꜅ (ci1 zoeng6 笞杖) the stick used in a yámun (衙門) to punish;
    • ꜀chʻí chák꜆ (ci1 zaak3 笞責) to chastise culprits, vulgo, ꜂tá ꜂pán ꜂tsz' (daa2 baan2 zi2 打板子).

鴟 ꜀Chʻí (ci1)

Radical + residual strokes
⿃ + 5
Unicode code point
U+9D1F
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 36 鴟) 鴟鴞惡鳥[攫]鳥子而食者
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10* ꜀鴟)
    • A kind of owl, or a sort of hawk;
    • kok꜆ ꜀chʻí (gok3 ci1 角鴟) or ꜀chʻí ꜀hiú (ci1 hiu1 鴟鴞), the horned owl;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜀chéung (ci1 zoeng1 鴟張), to act unscrupulously, as this bird does.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 欔 to 攫.

Alternative forms

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 36 呞) 口張開也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10* ꜀呞꜀𪗪 (⿰齒司); here normalised to 齝)
    • To ruminate, to chew the cud, vulgarly called ꜁ngau tʻò꜄ ꜂tsʻò (ngau4 tou3 cou2 牛吐草).

    The Fan Wan defines it (呞) to gape.
