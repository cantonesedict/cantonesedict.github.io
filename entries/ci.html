癡 ꜀Chʻí (ci1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽧ + 14
Unicode code point
U+7661
Alternative forms
  • 痴
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 36 痴) 痴呆
  • (Vol. 1 Image 36 癡) 同[上]愚也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10* ꜀癡꜀痴)
    • Foolish, silly;
    • stupid, inapt;
    • idiotic;
    • doting after, lustful, hankering;
    • out of his head, wandering;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁ngoi (ci1 ngoi4 癡呆), foolish stolid;
    • ꜀chʻí chüt꜆ [꜂]kòm ꜀chʻün lün꜅ chʻü꜄ (ci zyut3 gam2 cyun1 lyun6 cyu3 癡拙噉穿亂處) running in and out, making such a rumpus;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁tsʻing (ci1 cing4 癡情), salacious;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁chʻín (ci1 cin4 癡纏) bound up in, loving;
    • pat꜆ ꜀chʻí pat꜆ ꜁lung (bat1 ci1 bat1 lung4 不癡不聾), if I am not foolish and deaf [I cannot reign]; (these square brackets appear in the original text)
    • ꜀shü ꜀chʻí (syu1 ci1 書癡), doting on books;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜁[mai] pat꜆ ꜂sing (ci1 mai4 bat1 sing2 癡迷不醒) mad after.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 土 to 上.
  • Williams' 癡拙噉穿亂處: Corrected kòm꜄ (gam3) to ꜂kòm (gam2).
  • Williams' 癡迷不醒: Corrected ꜁mái (maai4) to ꜁mai (mai4).
