黐 ꜀Chʻí (ci1)

Radical + residual strokes
⿉ + 11
Unicode code point
U+9ED0
Alternative forms
  • 𥼝 (⿰米禽)
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10* ꜀𥼝 (⿰米禽)꜀黐)
    • To attach to, to stick on;
    • glutinous, sticky, pasty;
    • ꜀chʻí ꜀káu (ci1 gaau1 黐膠), birdlime;
    • ꜀chʻí kéuk꜆ (ci1 goek3 黐腳) to sorn, to sponge for meals;
    • ꜁'m ꜀chʻí ꜁mái (m4 ci1 maai4 唔黐埋), it don't stick;
    • ꜀chʻí tak꜆ ꜂hò shat꜇ (ci1 dak1 hou2 sat6 黐得好實) sticks very tightly;
    • ꜀chʻí mak꜇ ꜀hong (ci1 mak6 hong1 黐麥糠), a sponger, a sorner.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【黐】 (ci1):
    1. To stick, to be sticky, to cling.
      • 黐住咗 (ci1 zyu6 zo2): "[It's] stuck [onto it]."
      • 好黐牙 (hou2 ci1 ngaa4): "Really clingy to [the] teeth."
      • 黐底 (ci1 dai2): "Stuck to [the] bottom [of the pot]."
      • 黐鑊 (ci1 wok6): "Stuck to [the] wok."
      • 黐𣲷𣲷 (ci1 nap6 nap6): "Sticky [and] wet."
      • 黐手 (ci1 sau2): "Sticky hands" (a training technique in Wing Chʻun).
    2. To freeload, to impose on another (for food etc.).
      • 黐餐 (ci1 caan1): "Sponge [a] meal."
      • 黐車坐 (ci1 ce1 co5): "Freeload for [a] car ride."
    3. Short for 黐線.
      • 做到黐咗 (zou6 dou3 ci1 zo2): "Worked till [he's] crazy."
  • 【黐肺】 (ci1 fai3):
    1. To be exhausted. (Literally, "stuck-together lungs".)
      • 熱到黐肺 (jit6 dou3 ci1 fai3): "So hot [I'm] knackered."
  • 【黐脷根】 (ci1 lei6 gan1):
    1. To have a lisp. (Literally, "sticky tongue-root".)
  • 【黐孖筋】 (ci1 maa1 gan1):
    1. Crazy, insane.
  • 【黐線】 (ci1 sin6):
    1. Crazy, insane.
      • 黐咗線咩 (ci1 zo2 sin3 me1): "[Have you] gone crazy?"
      • 黐線㗎 (ci1 sin3 ga3): "[It's] crazy!"

Olden attestation for 黐 ꜀chʻí (ci1) meaning "glue", hence "sticky":

  • 《廣韻》吕支切：黐：黏也又丑知切
  • 《廣韻》丑知切：黐：所以粘鳥又吕支切
  • 《康熙字典》黐：《廣韻》丑知切《集韻》《正韻》抽知切𠀤音摛《玉篇》黏也《廣韻》黐膠所以黏鳥　又《廣韻》呂支切《集韻》鄰知切𠀤音離義同

    • 杝 read ꜁tʻo (to4) is probably a mistaken parsing by Williams of 託何切音佗 ꜀tʻo (to1), which is the only reading having the same meaning of "splitting with the grain" (Kangxi quotes 《詩小雅》伐木掎矣析薪杝矣 for this reading).

    • To gallop, to ride fast, as a courier;
    • a fleet horse;
    • to go fast;
    • fast;
    • far, spread abroad;
    • ꜁chʻí tò꜅ (ci4 dou6 馳道) the middle road for the emperor;
    • ngoi꜅ mò꜅ ꜀fan ꜁chʻí (ngoi6 mou6 fan1 ci4 外騖分馳), harrassed with cares;
    • ꜁chʻí ꜃má (ci4 maa5 馳馬), to gallop a horse;
    • ꜁chʻí yik꜇ (ci4 jik6 馳驛) to ride post;
    • ꜁chʻí ꜀kʻü (ci4 keoi1 馳驅), to ride fast.
