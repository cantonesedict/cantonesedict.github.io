齋 ꜀Chái (zaai1)

Radical + residual strokes
齊 + 3
Unicode code point
U+9F4B
Alternative forms
  • 斋
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10 ꜀齋꜀斋)
    • To respect, to reverence;
    • to abstain from;
    • pure, reverential, serious;
    • a retiring room, a closet;
    • ꜂tá ꜀chái (daa2 zaai1 打齋), to get a soul out of tartarus;
    • ꜂tá ꜀chái hok꜇ (daa2 zaai1 hok6-2 打齋鶴) storks for the soul to ride to heaven; met. a pander;
    • tái꜅ sz'꜅ ꜀chái (daai6 si6 zaai1 大士齋), offerings before the purveyor of hungry spirits;
    • ꜁hán ꜀chái (haan4 zaai1 閒齋), a closet;
    • ꜀shü ꜀chái (syu1 zaai1 書齋), a library;
    • ꜀tsʻing ꜀chái (cing1 zaai1 清齋), an entire fast;
    • ꜂lo ꜀chái (lo2 zaai1 攞齋), to beg vegetables;
    • ꜀chái kái꜄ (zaai1 gaai3 齋戒) to fast from animal food;
    • ꜀chái tsiú[꜄] (zaai1 ziu3 齋醮) to fast at All-souls;
    • shik꜇ ꜀chái (sik6 zaai1 食齋), to fast on vegetables;
    • ꜁lo hon꜄ ꜀chái (lo4 hon3 zaai1 羅漢齋), all sorts of vegetables;
    • ꜀chái ꜀kü (zaai1 geoi1 齋居), to live at ease;
    • ꜀chái ꜀chong (zaai1 zong1 齋莊), respectful, reverential.

    A colloquial word.

    • A final particle denoting that it was said, that it is so;
    • mat꜆ ꜁yan wá꜅ ꜀chái (mat1 jan4 waa6 zaai1 乜人話齋), somebody has said;
    • ꜂hò ꜃tsʻz' ꜃kʻü wá꜅ ꜀chái (hou2 ci5 keoi5 waa6 zaai1 好似佢話齋), just as he said.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 齋戒: Properly, abstinence from all pleasures (meat, alcohol, sex).
  • Williams' 齋醮: Corrected tsiú꜅ (ziu6) to tsiú꜄ (ziu3).
  • Williams' 羅漢齋: The name of a dish, commonly called Buddha's Delight.
Conway's entry
  • 【齋】 (zaai1):
    1. Vegetarian food.
      • 初一十五食齋 (co1 jat1 sap6 ng5 sik6 zaai1): "Eating vegetarian [on the] first [and] fifteenth [of the lunar month]."
      • 齋口唔齋心 (zaai1 hau2 m4 zaai1 sam1): "Vegetarian [in] mouth [but] not vegetarian [at] heart" (said of vegetarians that eat imitation meat).
    2. Plain, pure, etc.
      • 齋啡 (zaai1 fe1): "Straight coffee."
      • 齋腸 (zaai1 coeng4-2): "Plain [rice-noodle] roll."
      • 齋噏 (zaai1 ngap1): "Purely blabbering."
  • 【話齋】 (waa6 zaai1):
    1. Emphatic form of "[as he] said".
      • 好似佢話齋 (hou2 ci5 keoi5 waa6 zaai1): "As he said."
