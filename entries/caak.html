冊 Chʻák꜆ (caak3)

Radical + residual strokes
冂 + 3
Unicode code point
U+518A
Alternative forms
  • 册
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 14 册꜆; here normalised to 冊)
    • A slip;
    • an inventory, a register, a list;
    • a patent or commission of nobility;
    • a census book;
    • to choose;
    • to plan, to make a plan or scheme;
    • ꜁tʻín chʻák꜆ (tin4 caak3 填冊) to enrol, to write a list;
    • ú꜅ ꜂hau chʻák꜆ (wu6 hau2 caak3 戶口冊) a register, a census of people;
    • ꜁pʻái ꜁mún chʻák꜆ (paai4 mun4 caak3 排門冊) the register of families;
    • ꜁wong chʻák꜆ (wong4 caak3 皇冊) imperial census.

策 Chʻák꜆ (caak3)

Radical + residual strokes
竹 + 6
Unicode code point
U+7B56
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 13 策꜆)
    • A bamboo slip, such as books were once engraved on;
    • a book, writings;
    • a stratagem, a plan, a means, an expedient, a scheme;
    • a switch, a whip;
    • a divining slip;
    • to switch;
    • ꜂kán chʻák꜆ (gaan2 caak3 簡策) books, chapters, &c.;
    • ꜁mò chʻák꜆ (mou4 caak3 無策) without plan, schemeless;
    • ꜁chʻau chʻák꜆ (cau4 caak3 籌策) to contrive;
    • chʻák꜆ ꜃má (caak3 maa5 策馬), to whip a horse.

    Used for the next (茦).

Williams (1856) entry

Conway's commentary
  • An entry for the reading tsʻák꜇ (caak6) is included here for completeness.
    1. The cuttlefish, as in 鰞鰂 ꜀ú tsʻák꜇ (wu1 caak6), also written 烏賊. Note that in Cantonese, it is usually called 墨魚, "inkfish".
