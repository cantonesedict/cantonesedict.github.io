鴨 áp꜆ (aap3)

Radical + residual strokes
鳥 + 5
Unicode code point
U+9D28
Reading variations
  • aap3-2 (colloquial rising-tone noun)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 5 鴨꜆)
    • A duck, a mallard;
    • ꜂hín áp꜆ (hin2 aap3-2 蜆鴨) a wild duck;
    • láp꜇ áp꜆ (laap6 aap3-2 臘鴨) dried ducks;
    • ꜂chʻáu áp꜆ pʻin꜄ (caau2 aap3 pin3-2 炒鴨片) fried duck cutlet;
    • áp꜆ ꜁miú (aap3 miu4 鴨苗), ducklings;
    • púi꜅ áp꜆ ꜂tsai (bui6 aap3 zai2 焙鴨仔), to hatch ducks artificially.
Conway's commentary
