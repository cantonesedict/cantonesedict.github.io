꜀Chʻáu (caau1 陰平)

  • 抄

抄 ꜀Chʻáu (caau1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 4
Unicode code point
U+6284
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 41 抄) 抄寫
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 29 ꜀抄)
    • To take, to seize;
    • to search;
    • to engross, to transcribe;
    • to lade out, to spoon out;
    • to confiscate, to escheat, to sequestrate, to resume;
    • ꜂shau ꜀chʻáu (sau2 caau1 手抄), a MS. copy;
    • ꜀chʻáu ꜀tsʻing ꜀ká (caau1 cing1 gaa1 抄清家), all his property is taken;
    • chiú꜄ ꜂pún ꜀chʻáu ꜁tʻang (ziu3 bun2 caau1 tang4 照本抄謄), copied exactly;
    • ꜀chʻáu ꜂sé (caau1 se2 抄寫), to copy;
    • ꜀chʻáu pák꜇ (caau1 baak6 抄白) it is copied clearly;
    • ꜀chʻáu sái꜄ (caau1 saai3 抄嗮) it is all copied.

巢 ꜁Chʻáu (caau4)

Radical + residual strokes
⼮ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5DE2
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 41 巢) 鳥在木上曰巢
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 29 ꜁巢)
    • A nest on a tree;
    • a den, a retreat;
    • a lurking place;
    • to nestle, to make a nest;
    • ꜁chʻáu üt꜇ (caau4 jyut6 巢穴) to skulk in, as a brigand;
    • ꜁chʻáu ꜀wo (caau4 wo1 巢窩), or tséuk꜆ ꜁chʻáu (zoek3 caau4 雀巢), a nest;
    • tsʻák꜇ ꜁chʻáu (caak6 caau4 賊巢), a den or resort of robbers;
    • pák꜆ ꜃niú ꜀kwai ꜁chʻáu (baak3 niu5 gwai1 caau4 百鳥歸巢), the birds have gone to roost.
  • (Page 30 ꜁窠; here normalised to 巢)

    (…)

    A colloquial word.

    • Crumpled, wrinkled, corrugated, rough;
    • shriveled, as dried fruit;
    • ꜂hò [꜃]tsʻz' á꜄ ꜁pʻo ꜁pʻí kòm꜄ ꜁chʻáu (hou2 ci5 aa3 po4 pei4 gam3 caau4 好似阿婆皮咁巢), wrinkled as a granny's face;
    • ꜁chʻáu ꜀mang ꜀mang (caau4 mang1 mang1 巢𠵼𠵼), creased, rumpled.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 好似阿婆皮咁巢: Corrected ꜂tsʻz' (ci2) to ꜃tsʻz' (ci5).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【巢】 (caau4):
    1. Wrinkled.
      • 巢𠵼𠵼 (caau4 mang1 mang1): "Wrinkly."
      • 巢皮 (caau4 pei4): "Wrinkled skin."

漅 ꜁Chʻáu (caau4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 11
Unicode code point
U+6F05
Reading variations
  • ziu2 (expected from 子小切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 41 漅) 湖名在合肥亦作巢
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 30 ꜁漅)
    • Name of a large lake in the south of the province of Ngánhwui (安徽).

    Also read ꜂tsiú (ziu2). The last (巢) is usually used for this.

Conway's commentary
  • The reading caau4 is not unattested; Kangxi quotes 又《集韻》《韻會》《正韻》𠀤鋤交切音巢義同.
