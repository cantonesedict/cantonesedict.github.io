꜀Tsʻoi (coi1 陰平)

  • 啋

啋 ꜀Tsʻoi (coi1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 8
Unicode code point
U+554B
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 590 ꜀啋)

    A colloquial word.

    • An interjection of contempt, tush! pish! to pish at;
    • ꜀tsʻoi, fát꜆ ngám꜅ wá꜅ ꜀mé (coi1, faat3 ngaam6 waa6-2 me1 啋、發譫話咩), pshaw, what a lie! said by women;
    • ꜀tsʻoi, ꜀wan ꜃ní (coi1 wan1 nei5 啋瘟你), pox take you!

    Read ꜀hiú (hiu1); (see 啋 hiu1).

Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【啋】 (coi1):
    1. An interjection of rebuke for mentioning a bad or inauspicious outcome.
      • 啋 (coi1): "Pish!"
      • 啋過你 (coi1 gwo3 nei5): "Pish unto you [for saying that]!"

採 ꜂Tsʻoi (coi2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 8
Unicode code point
U+63A1
Alternative forms
  • 采
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 44 採) 取也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 591 ꜂採)
    • To select, to choose, to pick, to pluck, as leaves;
    • to take with the hand;
    • ꜂tsʻoi ꜀song (coi2 song1 採桑), to pick mulberry leaves;
    • ꜂tsʻoi ꜃mái (coi2 maai5 採買), to buy the choicest;
    • ꜂tsʻoi ꜀fá (coi2 faa1 採花), to gather flowers.

采 ꜂Tsʻoi (coi2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾤ + 1
Unicode code point
U+91C7
Reading variations
  • coi3 (alternative for "fief" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 44 采) 文采又采取
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 590 ꜂采)

    To be distinguished from 釆 pín꜅ (bin6) to divide.

    • To pluck, to choose, to take with the hand;
    • variegated, adorned with colors;
    • cities allotted to officers for revenue;
    • in these senses the three next (採, 寀, 彩) are used;
    • a business, an employment;
    • ꜂tsʻoi yap[꜆] (coi2 jap1 采邑), territory set apart for the support of grandees, an ancient usage;
    • náp꜇ ꜂tsʻoi (naap6 coi2 納采), to choose and send the betrothal presents.

才 ꜁Tsʻoi (coi4)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 0
Unicode code point
U+624D
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 44 才) 能也功也畧也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 590 ꜁才)
    • Materials, stuff, or the substance of things, for which the next (材) is used;
    • talents, capacity, ability, powers;
    • endowments, genius, gifts;
    • a person, a graduate;
    • ꜁tsʻoi ꜂tsz' (coi4 zi2 才子) a genius;
    • ꜁man ꜁tsʻoi (man4 coi4 文才), literary talents;
    • ꜀sám ꜁tsʻoi (saam1 coi4 三才), the three powers—are heaven, earth, man, who rule all things;
    • yat꜆ ꜂piú ꜁yan ꜁tsʻoi (jat1 biu2 jan4 coi4 一表人才), a fine looking man;
    • ꜁nò ꜁tsʻoi (nou4 coi4 奴才), a slave;
    • ꜁tsʻoi kü꜅ (coi4 geoi6 才具) parts, talents;
    • ꜁tsʻoi ꜁nang (coi4 nang4 才能), ability;
    • ꜁léung ꜁tsʻoi (loeng4 coi4 良才), goodness, rectitude;
    • ꜁tsʻoi chí꜄ (coi4 zi3 才智) talents and knowledge;
    • ꜂chʻun ꜁tsʻoi (ceon2 coi4 蠢才), you goose!
    • ꜁wai ꜁tsʻoi (wai4 coi4 遺才), the unsuccessful siútsʻái (秀才);
    • ꜂hò ꜂hau ꜁tsʻoi (hou2 hau2 coi4 好口才), eloquent.
Conway's commentary
  • Also used for 纔 coi4.

材 ꜁Tsʻoi (coi4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽊ + 3
Unicode code point
U+6750
Alternative forms
  • 才
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 44 材) 人之可用者曰賢材木之可用者曰材料
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 590 ꜁材)
    • Materials of which things are made;
    • nature, qualities;
    • timber, lumber, wood;
    • materièl, stuff;
    • ꜁tsʻoi liú꜅ (coi4 liu6-2 材料) materials, as wood, stone, &c.; ingredients;
    • ꜁léung ꜁tsʻoi (loeng4 coi4 良材), good stuff; met. upright ministers;
    • ꜂hò ꜀shan ꜁tsʻoi (hou2 san1 coi4 好身材), likely, well formed;
    • ꜃'ng ꜁tsʻoi (ng5 coi4 五材), the five elements.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "materièl": Probably a misspelling of "matériel".

纔 ꜁Tsʻoi (coi4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽷ + 17
Unicode code point
U+7E94
Alternative forms
  • 才
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 44 纔) 暫也[僅]也又剛纔方纔
  • (Vol. 2 Image 44 ⿰糸⿱㲋二) 同[上]
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 590 ꜁纔)
    • An adverb of time;
    • near to, at hand, nearly reaching to;
    • in a short time, thereupon, presently, then, just then, just now, scarcely;
    • ꜀fong ꜁tsʻoi (fong1 coi4 方纔), then, a little while ago;
    • ꜁tsʻoi ꜂hò (coi4 hou2 纔好), then it will do;
    • ꜁tsʻoi hü꜄ (coi4 heoi3 纔去) just gone.

    Read ꜀shám (saam1); (see 纔 saam1).

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 謹 to 僅, and corrected 土 to 上.

裁 ꜁Tsʻoi (coi4)

Radical + residual strokes
⾐ + 6
Unicode code point
U+88C1
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 44 裁) 裁衣又裁度裁製
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 590 ꜁裁)
    • To make clothes, to cut out garments;
    • to tear, to cut off;
    • to moderate, to reduce, to deny, to diminish;
    • to guess, to plan;
    • to regulate;
    • ꜁tsʻoi ꜁fung ꜂lò (coi4 fung4-2 lou2 裁縫佬), a tailor;
    • tsz'꜅ ꜁tsʻoi (zi6 coi4 自裁), suicide;
    • ꜁tsʻoi ꜀í (coi4 ji1 裁衣), to cut out clothes;
    • ꜁tsʻoi ꜂tsín (coi4 zin2 裁剪), to cut off with shears;
    • ꜂siú ꜁tsʻoi (siu2 coi4 小裁) and tái꜅ ꜁tsʻoi (daai6 coi4 大裁), a scant pattern, a full pattern.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 自裁: Literally, to "self-resolve".

財 ꜁Tsʻoi (coi4)

Radical + residual strokes
⾙ + 3
Unicode code point
U+8CA1
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 44 財) 寶也又貨也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 590 ꜁財)
    • Wealth, riches, valuables, property, possessions, goods, whatever men can use;
    • profits;
    • bribes;
    • presents;
    • ꜁tsʻoi ꜂chü ꜂lò (coi4 zyu2 lou2 財主佬), a rich man;
    • ꜁tsʻoi ꜀tung (coi4 dung1 財東), a capitalist, a moneyed man;
    • kʻap꜆ ꜁tsʻoi (kap1 coi4 吸財), quick at money making;
    • fát꜆ ꜁tsʻoi (faat3 coi4 發財), to get rich, to make money;
    • ꜀tʻín ꜁tsʻoi (tin1 coi4 天財), good luck, as a prize in a lottery;
    • ꜁tsʻoi pák꜇ ꜀sing ꜀kwan (coi4 baak6 sing1 gwan1 財帛星君), or ꜁tsʻoi ꜁shan (coi4 san4 財神), god of wealth, Plutus, Mammon;
    • ꜀tʻau ꜀ká ꜁tsʻoi (tau1 gaa1 coi4 偷家財), a thief of a cook;
    • ꜁tsʻoi ꜃lai (coi4 lai5 財禮), presents, complimentary gifts;
    • pʻo꜄ ꜁tsʻoi ꜂tong ꜀tsoi (po3 coi4 dong2 zoi1 破財擋災), to lavish money to ward off (or remedy) calamity;
    • ꜁tsʻoi ꜂ho [꜃]í ꜀tʻung ꜁shan (coi4 ho2 ji5 tung1 san4 財可以通神), money can move the gods;
    • ꜃ní ꜁'m hai꜅ ꜁tsʻoi ꜁shan (nei5 m4 hai6 coi4 san4 你唔係財神), you've bought nothing of me, you've given me no custom.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 財可以通神: Corrected ꜂í (ji2) to ꜃í (ji5).
