Chʻek꜆ Tsʻek꜆ (cek3 低陰入)

  • 尺
  • 赤

(Page 15*, before the lone entry for chʻik꜇ (cik6)) (The next character (尺) is usually pronounced chʻek.) (Here, Williams has been misled by Fan Wan's placement of 尺 (chʻik) in its own series after 劇屐 (kʻik꜇) and 溺 (nik꜇), which are 陽入; see Vol. 1 Image 78. Based on this ordering, Williams has inferred that the character 尺 is read 陽入 chʻik꜇ (cik6). However, since Fan Wan has a separate series for 勅勑敕斥叱螫⿰敕鳥赤 (chʻik꜆), it appears that its authors have attempted to distinguish the vernacular rime ek from the literary rime ik, notwithstanding all series appear under 第七英影應益. I have corrected Williams by placing 尺 under cek3 here.)

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 15* 尺꜆ Chʻek꜇ (cek6); here corrected to Chʻek꜆ (cek3))
    • A cubit, or 10 tsʻün꜄ (寸);
    • the Chinese foot;
    • chʻek[꜆] tsʻün꜄ (cek3 cyun3 尺寸) regular, proportioned;
    • ꜂kí ꜀to chʻek[꜆] tsʻün꜄ (gei2 do1 cek3 cyun3 幾多尺寸) what are the dimensions?
    • ꜁'m kau꜄ chʻek[꜆] tsʻün꜄ (m4 gau3 cek3 cyun3 唔夠尺寸) not of full stature;
    • ꜀sám chʻek[꜆] fát꜆ (saam1 cek3 faat3 三尺法) laws of the land, prohibitions;
    • ꜁pʻái ꜁tsʻín chʻek[꜆] (paai4 cin4 cek3 排錢尺) the full length cubit;
    • kán꜄ chʻek[꜆] (gaan3 cek3-2 間尺) a ruler;
    • ꜁léung ꜀tʻín chʻek[꜆] (loeng4 tin1 cek3 量天尺) a quadrant;
    • chʻek[꜆] wok[꜇] (cek3 wok6 尺蠖) a geometrical worm;
    • chʻek[꜆] yat꜆ ꜀chí ꜀shü (cek3 jat1 zi1 syu1 尺一之書), an epistle, a note;
    • chʻek[꜆] tò꜅ (cek3 dou6 尺度) to measure, measurable;
    • luk꜇ chʻek[꜆] ꜀chí ꜀kú (luk6 cek3 zi1 gu1 六尺之孤), a minor reigning;
    • ꜀sám chʻek[꜆] pák꜇ (saam1 cek3 baak6 三尺帛) a halter, a short rope.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams has been misled by Fan Wan into placing this under Chʻik꜇ (cik6) / Chʻek꜇ (cek6). See remarks under cek3.
  • Williams' 尺蠖: Changed Middle Chinese wok꜆ (wok3) to common reading wok꜇ (wok6).
  • Williams' 尺一之書: From the length of 1.1 chʻek for the writing-boards used for imperial decrees.
  • Williams' 尺度: More correctly the noun "measure", as 度 is in departing tone.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【間尺】 (gaan3 cek3-2):
    1. A ruler.
