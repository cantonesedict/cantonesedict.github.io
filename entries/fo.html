Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 53 ꜀科)
    • A root, the hollow culm of grain;
    • a vacuum, empty a hollow in a tree (Kangxi: 木中空也);
    • a rule, a regulation, a clause (Kangxi: 條也);
    • practice (in medicine), an area or field of study;
    • a class, series, order, rank, gradation (Kangxi: 等也);
    • classifier of herbs;
    • a literary examination (Kangxi: 取人條格曰科第);
    • luk꜆ luk꜇ ꜀fo (luk6 fo1 六科), six departments in official establishments;
    • ꜀fo káp꜆ (fo1 gaap3 科甲) a high literary degree;
    • ꜀hoi ꜀fo (hoi1 fo1 開科), to open the küyan (舉人) examination;
    • ꜀fo ꜀shau (fo1 sau1 科收), to receive taxes;
    • ꜀fo pái꜄ pʻái꜄ (fo1 paai3 科派) compulsory service contributions;
    • ꜀fo muk꜇ (fo1 muk6 科目) an index, series, rank;
    • ꜀fo ꜁lám (fo1 laam4-2 科籃), basket carried by candidates into the hall;
    • ching꜄ ꜀fo (zing3 fo1 正科), the alternate third years when examinations occur;
    • noi꜅ ꜀fo (noi6 fo1 內科), medical practice;
    • ꜀fo ꜁tʻau (fo1 tau4 科頭), bareheaded, head unarmed (Kangxi: 謂不著[兜]鍪入敵).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 六科: Corrected luk꜆ (luk1) to luk꜇ (luk6).
  • Williams' 科派: Corrected pái꜄ (baai3) to pʻái꜄ (paai3).
  • Williams' 科目: Now a subject or area of study.
  • Williams' 內科: Specifically internal medicine, as opposed to 外科, surgery.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A department, a section.
  2. Taxation.
  3. A pit, a hole (Kangxi: 坎也).
  4. To sentence, to punish (Kangxi: 科斷也 etc.).
