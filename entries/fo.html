科 ꜀Fo (fo1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽲ + 4
⽃ + 5
Unicode code point
U+79D1
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 58 科) 科坎科等又科甲
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 53 ꜀科)
    • A root, the hollow culm of grain;
    • a vacuum, empty a hollow in a tree (Kangxi: 木中空也);
    • a rule, a regulation, a clause (Kangxi: 條也);
    • practice (in medicine), an area or field of study;
    • a class, series, order, rank, gradation (Kangxi: 等也);
    • classifier of herbs;
    • a literary examination (Kangxi: 取人條格曰科第);
    • luk꜆ luk꜇ ꜀fo (luk6 fo1 六科), six departments in official establishments;
    • ꜀fo káp꜆ (fo1 gaap3 科甲) a high literary degree;
    • ꜀hoi ꜀fo (hoi1 fo1 開科), to open the küyan (舉人) examination;
    • ꜀fo ꜀shau (fo1 sau1 科收), to receive taxes;
    • ꜀fo pái꜄ pʻái꜄ (fo1 paai3 科派) compulsory service contributions;
    • ꜀fo muk꜇ (fo1 muk6 科目) an index, series, rank;
    • ꜀fo ꜁lám (fo1 laam4-2 科籃), basket carried by candidates into the hall;
    • ching꜄ ꜀fo (zing3 fo1 正科), the alternate third years when examinations occur;
    • noi꜅ ꜀fo (noi6 fo1 內科), medical practice;
    • ꜀fo ꜁tʻau (fo1 tau4 科頭), bareheaded, head unarmed (Kangxi: 謂不著[兜]鍪入敵).

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 蝌. See 蝌 fo1 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 六科: Corrected luk꜆ (luk1) to luk꜇ (luk6).
  • Williams' 科派: Corrected pái꜄ (baai3) to pʻái꜄ (paai3).
  • Williams' 科目: Now a subject or area of study.
  • Williams' 內科: Specifically internal medicine, as opposed to 外科, surgery.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A department, a section.
  2. Taxation.
  3. A pit, a hole (Kangxi: 坎也).
  4. To sentence, to punish (Kangxi: 科斷也 etc.).

伙 ꜂Fo (fo2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 4
Unicode code point
U+4F19
Alternative forms
  • 夥 (for "companion/company" senses)
  • 火
Reading variations
  • fo2-1 (in interrogative intensifier 家伙)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 60 夥) 夥伴
  • (Vol. 2 Image 60 伙) 家用物件曰家伙
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 54 ꜂火; here normalised to 伙)
    • (…);
    • ꜀tsun tsun꜄ ꜂fo (zeon3 fo2 進伙), to move to a new house;
    • (…).
  • (Page 54 ꜂伙)
    • A company of ten in the army (Kangxi 火: 府兵十人爲火 etc.);
    • ꜀ká ꜂fo (gaa1 fo2 家伙), household furniture;
    • ꜂fo ꜁tʻau (fo2 tau4-2 伙頭), a cook;
    • ꜂fo ꜂tsai (fo2 zai2 伙仔), a scullion;
    • ꜂fo ꜂chéung (fo2 zoeng2 伙長), chief mate;
    • í꜅ ꜂fo (ji6 fo2 二伙) second mate;
    • ꜂hò ꜀ká ꜂fo (hou2 gaa1 fo2 好家伙), well, pleasing, handsome; also used in irony, as detestable, badly, done.
  • (Page 54 ꜂夥; here normalised to 伙)
    • A colleague, comrade, accomplice, associate;
    • (…);
    • a company, party, band, society;
    • ꜂fo pún꜅ (fo2 bun6 伙伴) a partner;
    • hòp꜇ ꜂fo (hap6 fo2 合伙), joint partner;
    • ꜂kí ꜂fo (gei2 fo2 幾伙), how many? as men;
    • ꜂fo kí꜄ (fo2 gei3 伙記) (or kai꜄ (gai3 計)) a fellow; the word Fokee applied to the Chinese by foreigners.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 進伙: Corrected ꜀tsun (zeon1) to tsun꜄ (zeon3). Note that the preferred term in Cantonese is 入伙.
  • Williams' "A company of ten in the army": This sense should probably be spelt 火 rather than 伙.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Meals, especially communal meals.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【弊家伙】 (bai6 gaa1 fo2):
    1. An interjectory expression used in response to something going wrong.
  • 【伙記】 (fo2 gei3):
    1. An employee who serves customers, especially a waiter.
  • 【家伙】 (gaa1 fo2, gaa1 fo2-1):
    1. A mild intensifier for interrogatives.
      • 做乜家伙 (zou6 mat1 gaa1 fo2): "What the crap [are you] doing?"
      • 咩家伙啊 (me1 gaa1 fo2-1 a3): "What on earth [is this]?"

火 ꜂Fo (fo2)

Radical + residual strokes
⽕ + 0
Unicode code point
U+706B
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 60 火) 水火五行之一[燒]炙物也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 54 ꜂火)
    • Fire, flame (Kangxi: 火燬也南方之行炎而上);
    • to burn, to consume (Kangxi: 火化也消化物也 etc.);
    • ꜂fo chuk꜆ (fo2 zuk1 火燭) a conflagration;
    • ꜂fo ꜀fá (fo2 faa1 火花), a spark;
    • ꜂fo ꜀ím ím꜅ (fo2 jim6 火焰), a flame;
    • kau꜄ ꜂fo (gau3 fo2 救火), to put out conflagration;
    • shat꜆ ꜂fo (sat1 fo2 失火), to catch fire;
    • ꜂tʻai ꜂fo shik꜆ (tai2 fo2 sik1 睇火色) watch how the fire is, see the blaze!
    • ꜂fo ꜀tsʻéung (fo2 coeng1 火鎗), fire-arms;
    • (…);
    • tsé꜄ ꜂fo (ze3 fo2 借火), trouble you for a light;
    • ꜃mò ꜂fo ꜂chung (mou5 fo2 zung2 冇火種), 'no fire-seed,' poor;
    • ꜂fo shek꜇ ꜂fan, (fo2 sek6 fan2 火石粉) chalk;
    • ꜂fo ꜁shün (fo2 syun4 火船), a steamer;
    • ꜂fo sik꜇ sik꜆ (fo2 sik1 火熄) the fire is out;
    • ꜂fo pʻiú꜄ (fo2 piu3 火票) an urgent warrant;
    • ꜃mò ꜁mò ꜁ming ꜂fo (mou4 ming4 fo2 無名火), causeless anger;
    • ꜂fo ꜂keng (fo2 geng2 火頸), testy;
    • ꜂fo ꜂pá (fo2 baa2 火把), a torch;
    • ꜂fo ꜀lò (fo2 lou1 火撈), soot;
    • ꜂fo ꜃yan (fo2 jan5 火引), a match;
    • ꜂fo hí꜄ (fo2 hei3 火氣) fiery, feverish ;
    • ꜂tá ꜂fo (daa2 fo2 打火), to strike a fire;
    • ꜂fo ꜂fá fá꜄ (fo2 faa3 火化), burned up;
    • ꜃mai ꜃shai ꜂shai ꜂fo hí꜄ (mai5 sai2 fo2 hei3 咪使火氣) do n't don't be so hasty;
    • ꜂fo ꜀kü tò꜅ sz'꜅ (fo2 geoi1 dou6 si6-2 火居道士) a sect among the Táuists.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 伙. See 伙 fo2 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Fixed missing strokes in 燒.
  • Williams' 火焰: Corrected ꜀ím (jim1) to ím꜅ (jim6).
  • Williams' 火船: Dated.
  • Williams' 火熄: Corrected sik꜇ (sik6) to sik꜆ (sik1).
  • Williams' 無名火: Corrected ꜃mò (mou5) to ꜁mò (mou4).
  • Williams' 火引: Perhaps better rendered "a kindler".
  • Williams' 火化: Corrected ꜂fá (faa2) to fá꜄ (faa3). Cremation.
  • Williams' 咪使火氣: Corrected ꜃shai (sai5) to ꜂shai (sai2).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. The 86th radical ("fire").
  2. Internal "fire" (or "heat") in Chinese medicine.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【火】 (fo2):
    1. A watt (of power).
      • 幾多火 (gei2 do1 fo2): "How many watts?"
      • 六十火 (luk6 sap6 fo2): "Sixty watts."

顆 ꜂Fo (fo2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾴ + 8
Unicode code point
U+9846
Alternative forms
  • 堁 (for "clod" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 60 顆) 小頭物也一顆猶一個一粒也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 54 ꜂顆)
    • A little head (Kangxi: 小頭也) or clod;
    • a clod (Kangxi: 與堁同土塊 etc.);
    • classifier of beads, shot, pearls, and round things (Kangxi: 今言物一顆猶一頭也, 凡圜物以顆計又珠琲曰顆);
    • ꜂kí ꜀to ꜂fo (gei2 do1 fo2 幾多顆), how many of them?
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "clod": This is a significantly separate sense. It should be separated from "A little head" by a semicolon.

貨 Fo꜄ (fo3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾙ + 4
Unicode code point
U+8CA8
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 61 貨) 貨財凡物可交易皆曰貨
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 54 貨꜄)
    • Goods, merchandise, commodities, things of value or utility (Kangxi: 財也, 貨寶用物, etc.);
    • to trade, to buy, to sell (Kangxi: 貨賣也);
    • to bribe (Kangxi: 賂也);
    • fo꜄ mat꜇ (fo3 mat6 貨物) goods;
    • fo꜄ ꜁tsʻoi (fo3 coi4 貨財), property, capital;
    • áp꜆ át꜆ fo꜄ (aat3 fo3 押貨) to go with cargo to a ship;
    • fo꜄ ꜁tʻau (fo3 tau4 貨頭), prime goods;
    • fo꜄ lò꜅ (fo3 lou6 貨賂) to bribe;
    • fo꜄ shik꜆ (fo3 sik1 貨色) quality of goods.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 押貨: Normalised áp꜆ (aap3) to át꜆ (aat3).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Currency.
