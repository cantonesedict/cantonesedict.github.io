Conway's Cantonese entry
  • гҖҗд№ӢеҳӣгҖ‘ (za1 maa3)
    1. A final particle of limitation, especially used defensively.
      • и¬ӣз¬‘д№Ӣеҳӣ (gong2 siu3 za1 maa3): "Just kidding!"
      • зӯүе…©ж—Ҙд№Ӣеҳӣ (dang2 loeng5 jat6 za1 maa3): "[It's] just [a] two-day wait."

The spelling д№Ӣеҳӣ is arbitrary (but well established).

Modern Cantonese dictionaries indicate a reading of zi1 maa3 for д№Ӣеҳӣ. I disagree with this reading for three reasons:

  1. I have only heard za1 maa3.
  2. I believe that д№Ӣеҳӣ (za1 maa3) is a contraction of е•« (ze1) + еҗ–еҳӣ (a1 maa3), in the same way that р әўеҳӣ (ga1 maa3) is shortened from еҳ… (ge3) + еҗ–еҳӣ (a1 maa3).
  3. I believe that any insistence on the reading of zi1 maa3 is a case of incorrectly letting the spelling dictate the pronunciation. It would be more helpful if za1 maa3 were written р ҫөеҳӣ instead, but unfortunately the spelling д№Ӣеҳӣ is already well established.
