樝 ꜀Chá (zaa1)

Radical + residual strokes
木 + 11
Unicode code point
U+6A1D
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 7 ꜀樝)
    • A sour red berry as large as a cherry, called ꜀shán ꜀chá (saan1 zaa1 山樝), used for its acid.

渣 ꜀Chá (zaa1)

Radical + residual strokes
水 + 9
Unicode code point
U+6E23
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 7 ꜀渣)
    • Name of a river;
    • dregs, grounds, residuum;
    • sediment, settlings, refuse after expressing juice;
    • ꜂tsz' ꜂kang ꜀chá (zi2 gang2 zaa1 紫梗渣), shell lac;
    • ꜃mò ꜀chá (mou5 zaa1 冇渣), there's no refuse left.

    Often used for the two next (樝, 皻).

Conway's commentary
  • 【油渣】 (jau4 zaa1): Diesel.
  • 【渣】 (zaa1): Unswallowable remains after chewing untender meat or stringy vegetables.
  • 【渣都冇】 (zaa1 dou1 mou5): "Even the scraps are gone".

皻 ꜀Chá (zaa1)

Radical + residual strokes
皮 + 11
Unicode code point
U+76BB
Alternative forms
  • 皶
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 7 ꜀皻)
    • Pimples on the nose, wine blotches;
    • pimples on the skin;
    • ꜂tsau ꜀chá (zau2 zaa1 酒皻), wine blossoms.

蹅 ꜀Chá (zaa1)

Radical + residual strokes
足 + 9
Unicode code point
U+8E45
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 7 ꜀皻)
    • To tread upon;
    • to put the foot on;
    • ꜀chá táp꜇ (zaa1 daap6 蹅踏) to stamp.
