扉 ꜀Fí (fei1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼾ + 8
Unicode code point
U+6249
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 35 扉) 戸也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 ꜀扉)
    • A wooden door with two leaves door-leaf (Kangxi: 戶扇也, 闔謂之扉, 以木曰扉以葦曰扇);
    • met. a rustic house.

緋 ꜀Fí (fei1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽷ + 8
Unicode code point
U+7DCB
Reading variations
  • fei2 (common in 緋聞)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 35 緋) [絳]色也一曰赤練
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 ꜀緋)
    • Red or crimson silk (Kangxi: 帛赤色, 絳色又赤練);
    • ꜀fí ꜁pʻò (fei1 pou4 緋袍), carnation robes.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 終 to 絳.

非 ꜀Fí (fei1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾮ + 0
Unicode code point
U+975E
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 35 非) 不是也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 ꜀非)
    • Not right, not good (Kangxi: 不是也);
    • not so, opposite (Kangxi: 違也);
    • false, bad, wrong;
    • shameless, low (Kangxi: 下也);
    • to slander (Kangxi: 訾也), to reproach (Kangxi: 責也);
    • ꜁'m ꜀chí shí꜅ ꜀fí (m4 zi1 si6 fei1 唔知是非), I don't know whether it is so or not;
    • ꜂kong shí꜅ ꜀fí (gong2 si6 fei1 講是非), to asperse, tittle-tattle;
    • ꜀fí ꜀heng ꜀yung ꜁yung í꜅ (fei1 heng1 jung4 ji6 非輕容易) this is not so easy;
    • ꜀fí ꜃lai (fei1 lai5 非禮), improper, immodest;
    • ꜂hí ꜀fí (hei2 fei1 豈非), is it not so! i.e. it is true;
    • ꜀fí ꜀shéung ꜁shéung (fei1 soeng4 非常), uncommon, superior;
    • ꜀fí ꜃yá (fei1 jaa5 非也), not so.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 非輕容易: Corrected ꜀yung (jung1) to ꜁yung (jung4).
  • Williams' 非禮: Now to molest.
  • Williams' 非常: Corrected ꜀shéung (soeng1) to ꜁shéung (soeng4).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. The 175th radical ("wrong").

騑 ꜀Fí (fei1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾺ + 8
Unicode code point
U+9A11
Alternative forms
  • 匪 (for "continuously walking" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 35 騑) 馬行不止
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 ꜀騑)
    • The two outside of a team of four horses (Kangxi: 車有一轅四馬中兩馬夾轅名服馬兩邊名騑馬亦名驂馬);
    • ꜀fí ꜀fí (fei1 fei1 騑騑), the regular continuous march of horses (Kangxi: 騑騑馬行不止貌).

  • 蜚

蜚 ꜂Fí (fei2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾍ + 8
Unicode code point
U+871A
Reading variations
  • fai3 (alternative per 《正韻牋》芳未切 for "grasshopper" sense)
  • fai6 (per 《廣韻》扶沸切 for "stinking insect" and "ominous animal" senses)
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 ꜀蜚 ꜀Fí (fei1); here corrected to ꜂Fí (fei2))
    • A stinking bug which eats clothes and grain (Kangxi: […]然則蜚是臭惡之蟲害人衣物[…]);
    • a fabulous animal ominous of pestilence (Kangxi: 獸名, […]見則天下大疫).

    (…)

Conway's commentary
  • The reading ꜂fí (fei2) is not unattested; 《正韻牋》 has the reading 敷尾切, encompassing the "grasshopper" and "animal" senses.
  • 《廣韻》府尾切 ꜂fí (fei2) is unhelpfully explained 蟲名咸蜚.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. The grasshopper (Kangxi: 蟲名負蠜也).
