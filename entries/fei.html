？妃 ꜀Fí (fei1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼥ + 3
Unicode code point
U+5983
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 35 妃) [妃]嬪
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 ꜀？妃)
    • Royal concubines (Kangxi: 女與己身儔也古[嬪]御之貴次于后者曰妃);
    • ꜀wong ꜁wong ꜀fí (wong4 fei1 王妃), wife of the heir apparent;
    • ching꜄ ꜀fí (zing3 fei1 正妃), the empress;
    • ꜀tʻín ꜀fí (tin1 fei1 天妃), the Amphitrite of the Chinese (Kangxi: 天妃水神);
    • ꜀fí ꜂tsz' siú꜄ (fei1 zi2 siu3 妃子笑) the best sort of laichí (荔枝).
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised headword ⿰女巳 to 妃.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A spouse (Kangxi: 匹也).

非 ꜀Fí (fei1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾮ + 0
Unicode code point
U+975E
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 35 非) 不是也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 ꜀非)
    • Not right, not good (Kangxi: 不是也);
    • not so, opposite (Kangxi: 違也);
    • false, bad, wrong;
    • shameless, low (Kangxi: 下也);
    • to slander (Kangxi: 訾也), to reproach (Kangxi: 責也);
    • ꜁'m ꜀chí shí꜅ ꜀fí (m4 zi1 si6 fei1 唔知是非), I don't know whether it is so or not;
    • ꜂kong shí꜅ ꜀fí (gong2 si6 fei1 講是非), to asperse, tittle-tattle;
    • ꜀fí ꜀heng ꜀hing ꜀yung ꜁yung í꜅ (fei1 hing1 jung4 ji6 非輕容易) this is not so easy;
    • ꜀fí ꜃lai (fei1 lai5 非禮), improper, immodest;
    • ꜂hí ꜀fí (hei2 fei1 豈非), is it not so! i.e. it is true;
    • ꜀fí ꜀shéung ꜁shéung (fei1 soeng4 非常), uncommon, superior;
    • ꜀fí ꜃yá (fei1 jaa5 非也), not so.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 非輕容易: Corrected ꜀heng (heng1) to ꜀hing (hing1), and corrected ꜀yung (jung1) to ꜁yung (jung4).
  • Williams' 非禮: Now to molest.
  • Williams' 非常: Corrected ꜀shéung (soeng1) to ꜁shéung (soeng4).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. The 175th radical ("wrong").

飛 ꜀Fí (fei1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾶ + 0
Unicode code point
U+98DB
Alternative forms
  • 蜚
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 35 飛) 飛鳥
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 ꜀飛)
    • To fly (Kangxi: 鳥翥, 飛翔);
    • to flit;
    • flying;
    • to go swiftly;
    • lofty;
    • quick;
    • ꜀fí hü꜄ (fei1 heoi3 飛去) to fly away;
    • ꜀fí ꜃niú (fei1 niu5 飛鳥), or ꜀fí ꜁kʻam (fei1 kam4 飛禽), birds;
    • ꜀fí ꜀kam (fei1 gam1 飛金), to put on gold leaf;
    • ꜀fí ꜁ím ꜁shím ꜂tsau pik꜆ (fei1 sim4 zau2 bik3 飛檐走壁) an expert thief;
    • ꜀fí ꜂shü (fei1 syu2 飛鼠), a bat;
    • ꜀fí pò꜄ (fei1 bou3 飛報) to haste to tell;
    • ꜀fí ꜁tʻang (fei1 tang4 飛騰), to rise rapidly in office;
    • ꜀fí ꜀tʻín ꜂pún sz'꜅ (fei1 tin1 bun2 si6 飛天本事) incomparably clever;
    • ꜀Fí-꜁lím (fei1 lim4 飛廉), Boreas, the god of wind (Kangxi: […]世因以飛廉爲風伯之名[…]).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 飛檐走壁: Changed ꜁ím (jim4) to common reading ꜁shím (sim4).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. The 183rd radical ("fly").
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【飛】 (fei1):
    1. To cut off hair.
      • 飛髮 (fei1 faat3): "Get [a] haircut."
      • 飛短啲 (fei1 dyun2 di1): "Cut [it] shorter."
      • 飛光佢 (fei1 gwong1 keoi5): "Cut it all off" (i.e. go bald).
    2. To skip.
      • 巴士飛站 (baa1 si2 fei1 zaam6): "[A] bus skipping [a] stop."
    3. A ticket.
      • 買飛 (maai5 fei1): "Buy [a] ticket."
      • 黃牛飛 (wong4 ngau4 fei1): "Scalped tickets."
  • 【飛起】 (fei1 hei2):
    1. Used after 到 to indicate an extreme degree. Similar to "to death" in English after a verb.
      • 熱到飛起 (jit6 dou3 fei1 hei2): "[It's] so hot [I could] fly."
      • 鬧到飛起 (naau6 dou3 fei1 hei2): "Scolded so [badly I could] fly."

蜚 ꜂Fí (fei2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾍ + 8
Unicode code point
U+871A
Reading variations
  • fai3 (alternative per 《正韻牋》芳未切 for "grasshopper" sense)
  • fai6 (per 《廣韻》扶沸切 for "stinking insect" and "ominous animal" senses)
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 52 ꜀蜚 ꜀Fí (fei1); here corrected to ꜂Fí (fei2))
    • A stinking bug which eats clothes and grain (Kangxi: […]然則蜚是臭惡之蟲害人衣物[…]);
    • a fabulous animal ominous of pestilence (Kangxi: 獸名, […]見則天下大疫).

    (…)

Conway's commentary
  • The reading ꜂fí (fei2) is not unattested; 《正韻牋》 has the reading 敷尾切, encompassing the "grasshopper" and "animal" senses.
  • 《廣韻》府尾切 ꜂fí (fei2) is unhelpfully explained 蟲名咸蜚.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. The grasshopper (Kangxi: 蟲名負蠜也).

肥 ꜁Fí (fei4)

Radical + residual strokes
⾁ + 4
Unicode code point
U+80A5
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 39 肥) 不[瘦]也[潤]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 53 ꜁肥)
    • Fat, oily, fleshy (Kangxi: 多肉也), rich (Kangxi: 饒裕也), fertile (Kangxi: 田有肥瘠);
    • ꜁fí chong꜄ (fei4 zong3 肥壯) robust;
    • ꜁fí tái꜅ (fei4 daai6 肥大) corpulent;
    • ꜁fí ꜂lò (fei4 lou2 肥佬) or á꜄ ꜁fí (aa3 fei4 阿肥), a fat man;
    • ꜁fí yun꜅ (fei4 jeon6 肥潤) fertile;
    • ꜁fí tun꜅ tun꜅ tat꜆ tat꜆ (fei4 dat1 dat1 肥腯腯) obese, fat as a pig;
    • ꜁fí ꜃mí (fei4 mei5 肥美), plump, good looking.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 淝. See 淝 fei4 for the canonical entry.)

  • (Page 714 ꜁肥)

    As a verb.

    • To fatten;
    • fattened;
    • to advantage one;
    • chat꜆ kú꜄ ꜁fí ꜂kí (zat1 gu3 fei4 gei2 只顧肥己), he only thinks of benefiting himself.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected ⿸疒央 to 瘦, and corrected ⿰冫閏 to 潤.
  • Williams' 肥腯腯: Normalised tun꜅ (deon6) to tat꜆ (dat1).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A horse (Kangxi: 馬亦稱肥).
  2. Fertiliser.
Conway's Cantonese entry

Cantonese prefers 肥 over 胖, even when referring to fatness in humans.

  • 【肥腯腯】 (fei4 dat1 dat1):
    1. Chubby.
  • 【肥嘟嘟】 (fei4 dyut1 dyut1):
    1. Synonym of 肥腯腯.
  • 【肥揗揗】 (fei4 tan4 tan4):
    1. Fat, in a somewhat clumsy or bumbling way.
    2. Fatty, greasy.
