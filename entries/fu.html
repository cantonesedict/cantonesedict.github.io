Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "To help, one who can assist": Possibly an attempt to render Kangxi гҖҠи©©иЁ»гҖӢеӨ«жңүеӮ…зӣёд№Ӣеҫ·иҖҢеҸҜеҖҡд»—и¬Ӯд№ӢдёҲеӨ«, which suggests that a дёҲеӨ« is so-called because he can be relied upon (д»—) and is assistive (еӮ…). This should not be taken as a definition of еӨ«.

  • Williams' "an exalted lady": Only in the term еӨ«дәә. More generally, any important man's wife or concubine (Kangxi: еӨ©еӯҗжңүеҗҺжңүеӨ«дәә, еҰҫжӣ°еҰӮеӨ«дәә).
