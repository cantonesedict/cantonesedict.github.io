妻 ꜀Tsʻai (cai1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼥ + 5
Unicode code point
U+59BB
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 28 妻) 夫妻
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 549 ꜀妻)
    • A wife, a partner (Kangxi: 妻與己齊者也), a spouse, a consort who is taken with legal ceremonies;
    • ꜀tsʻai ꜂tsz' (cai1 zi2 妻子) a wife;
    • ling꜅ ꜀tsʻai (ling6 cai1 令妻), your wife;
    • hák꜆ ꜀tsʻai (haak3 cai1 客妻), a courtesan;
    • lò꜅ ꜁tʻau ꜀tsʻai (lou6 tau4 cai1 路頭妻), a wayside wife, one taken while sojourning elsewhere;
    • ꜁pʻing ꜀tsʻai (ping4 cai1 平妻), equal to a wife, i. e. a concubine;
    • tsʻü꜄ ꜂tsʻü ꜀tsʻai (ceoi2 cai1 娶妻), to take a wife;
    • ꜁ín ꜀tsʻai (jin4 cai1 賢妻) my good wife, a virtuous wife;
    • ꜀kiú ꜀tsʻai ꜃mí tsʻíp꜆ (giu1 cai1 mei5 cip3 嬌妻美妾) an accomplished wife and handsome concubine.

    (…)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 娶妻: Changed tsʻü꜄ (ceoi3) to common reading ꜂tsʻü (ceoi2).
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【妻】 (tshej)
    • (noun-like) wife

棲 ꜀Tsʻai (cai1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽊ + 8
Unicode code point
U+68F2
Alternative forms
  • 栖
Reading variations
  • sai1 (per 《廣韻》先稽切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 28 棲) 棲息又牀也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 550 ꜀棲꜀梄栖)
    • To roost, to perch, to nestle (Kangxi 栖: 日在西方而鳥棲 etc.) (Kangxi 棲: 同栖鳥棲也);
    • to sojourn, to stay at, to reside;
    • to settle down, to rest, to repose (Kangxi 栖: 凡物止息皆曰栖);
    • at peace;
    • a perch, a roost;
    • a sleeping-place (Kangxi 棲: 棲謂之牀);
    • a lodging-place;
    • ꜀kai ꜀tsʻai (gai1 cai1 雞棲), a hen-roost;
    • tsám꜅ ꜁shí ꜀tsʻai chü꜅ (zaam6 si4 cai1 zyu6 暫時棲住) to live here for a while;
    • ꜁mò teng꜅ ꜀tsʻai ꜀shan (mou4 deng6 cai1 san1 無埞棲身), no place to stay at;
    • ꜀tsʻai sít꜆ (cai1 sit3 棲屑) to and fro, like a stranger.
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【棲】 (sej)
    • (verb-like) [to] roost

淒 ꜀Tsʻai (cai1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 8
Unicode code point
U+6DD2
Alternative forms
  • 凄
  • 悽 (for "grieved/afflicted" senses)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 28 淒) 寒也又淒凉
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 549 ꜀凄꜀淒)
    • Intense cold (Kangxi 淒: 寒涼也);
    • bleak, cloudy, wintry (Kangxi 淒: 雲雨起貌);
    • a cold wind;
    • shivering, freezing;
    • calamitous, afflicted, grievous;
    • ꜀fung ꜃ü ꜀tsʻai ꜀tsʻai (fung1 jyu5 cai1 cai1 風雨淒淒), sleety rain and wind;
    • ꜀tsʻai tsʻít꜆ (cai1 cit3 淒切) bitter suffering;
    • ꜀tsʻai ꜁léung (cai1 loeng4 淒涼), grievous;
    • kòm꜄ ꜀tsʻai ꜁léung (gam3 cai1 loeng4 咁淒涼), so sad; so serious, as consequences;
    • ꜀tsʻai ꜂tsʻám (cai1 caam2 淒慘), oppressed, borne down;
    • ꜀tsʻai ꜂chʻo sham꜅ (cai1 co2 sam6 淒楚甚) in urgent case, needy.

    The second (淒) also means gathering clouds.

齊 ꜁Tsʻai (cai4)

Radical + residual strokes
⿑ + 0
Unicode code point
U+9F4A
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 30 齊) 整齊不參差也又國名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 550 ꜁齊)
    • Even (Kangxi: 整也), equal (Kangxi: 等也), uniform;
    • to equalize, to tranquillize;
    • to adjust, to classify, to arrange methodically or according to rank;
    • at once, all, together, alike;
    • quick, smart;
    • good;
    • to discriminate (Kangxi: 辨也);
    • an ancient feudal state comprising the part of Shántung (山東) east of Tʻái shán (泰山);
    • the 210th radical ("even");
    • ꜂ching ꜁tsʻai (zing2 cai4 整齊), well arranged, properly placed;
    • ꜁tsʻai ꜀chau (cai4 zau1 齊州), an old name for China;
    • yat꜆ ꜁tsʻai hü꜄ (jat1 cai4 heoi3 一齊去) all go at once;
    • ꜀shau ꜁tsʻai chéung꜄ muk꜇ (sau1 cai4 zoeng3 muk6 收齊賬目) to collect all the accounts;
    • ꜁tsʻai ꜁mí (cai4 mei4 齊眉), an old couple;
    • ꜁'m pín꜄ tak꜆ ꜁yan ꜁tsʻai (m4 bin3 dak1 jan4 cai4 唔變得人齊), he was not turned out a full man — said of a young scapegrace;
    • ꜀tò tsʻít꜆ kòm꜄ ꜁tsʻai (dou1 cit3 gam3 cai4 刀切咁齊), trimmed even, all of a size;
    • ꜁tsʻai ꜀sam (cai4 sam1 齊心), of one mind;
    • ꜂tang ꜁tsʻai (dang2 cai4 等齊), wait till all come;
    • ꜁tsʻai pí꜅ (cai4 bei6 齊備) all are ready, fully prepared;
    • pat꜆ ꜁tsʻai (bat1 cai4 不齊), incomplete, unequal;
    • ꜁tsʻai ꜀ká (cai4 gaa1 齊家), to govern a family.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "good": Kangxi has 又《廣韻》好也, but it is unclear whether 好 is rising-tone "good" or departing-tone "fond".
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A surname (Kangxi: 姓).
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【齊】 (dzej)
    • (adjective-like) equal, uniform
    • (verb-like) [to] equal, [to] uniform, (met.) [to] put in order
    • (adjective-like) together, complete
