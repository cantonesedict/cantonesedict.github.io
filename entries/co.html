Williams (1856) entry
  • (PageВ 583 еүүкң„)

    (вҖҰ)

    Often used for the next (йҠј).

  • (PageВ 583 йҠјкң„)
    • An iron pan or boiler (Kangxi: йҚ‘д№ҹ);
    • a file, a rasp;
    • to rasp, to file, to smooth off the sharp or rough points;
    • to rub down;
    • yatкңҶ кңӮpГЎ tsК»oкң„ (jat1 baa2 co3 дёҖжҠҠйҠј) one file;
    • tsК»oкң„ wГЎtкңҮ (co3 waat6 йҠјж»‘) file it smooth;
    • кңҖkai кңҖchК»un tsК»oкң„ (gai1 ceon1 co3 йӣһиҶҘйҠј) to file eggs, or кңҖchГӯ кңҒmГЎ tsК»oкң„ (zi1 maa4 co3 иҠқйә»йҠј) to scrape sesamum seeds, both mean a skinflint;
    • tsК»oкң„ kokкңҶ (co3 gok3 йҠји§’) to file off corners.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' йӣһиҶҘйҠј, иҠқйә»йҠј: More accurately rendered as nouns, i.e. "chicken-egg file" and "sesame file".

Williams (1856) entry
  • (PageВ 583 йҢҜкң„)
    • In disorder, confused;
    • to mistake, to err, to do wrong;
    • to slip;
    • erroneous, mixed, wrong;
    • кңғmГІ tsК»oкң„ (mou5 co3 еҶҮйҢҜ) no mistake, right;
    • tsК»oкң„ кңғngГЎm кңҖngГЎm (co3 ngaam1 йҢҜе•ұ), a lucky hit, an error which is fortunate;
    • кңҒ'm yingкң… tsК»oкң„ (m4 jing6 co3 е””иӘҚйҢҜ) will not confess the wrong;
    • tsК»oкң„ kГ©ukкңҶ кңҒnГЎn кңҖfГЎn (co3 goek3 naan4 faan1 йҢҜи…ійӣЈзҝ») a slip may be hard to retrieve;
    • tsК»oкң„ kwoкң„ pГӯtкңҶ кңӮkoi (co3 gwo3 bit1 goi2 йҢҜйҒҺеҝ…ж”№), error must be corrected;
    • кңӮsГ© tsК»oкң„ (se2 co3 еҜ«йҢҜ) written wrong.
Conway's commentary
  • The classical reading of the "erroneous" and "mixed" senses is entering-tone tsК»okкңҶ (cok3).
  • Williams' йҢҜе•ұ: Corrected кңғngГЎm (ngaam5) to кңҖngГЎm (ngaam1).
