初 ꜀Chʻo (co1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼑ + 5
Unicode code point
U+521D
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 58 初) 始也先也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 26* ꜀初)
    • To cut out garments;
    • to begin, to commence;
    • the first, the beginning;
    • incipient, at first;
    • á꜄ ꜀chʻo ꜀ko (aa3 co1 go1 阿初哥), a beginner, raw;
    • ꜀chʻo ꜀kán (co1 gaan1 初間), during the first decade;
    • ꜀chʻo ꜀chʻo (co1 co1 初初), at first;
    • ꜀chʻo ꜁tʻau fát꜆ [꜃shí] (co1 tau4 faat3 sai3 初頭發市) a great show at starting;
    • [꜀]chʻo ꜁loi pò꜅ tò꜄ (co1 loi4 bou6 dou3 初來步到) unacquainted with things;
    • ꜀chʻo tsò꜅ (co1 zou6 初做) to begin;
    • ꜀chʻo ꜀shang (co1 sang1 初生), a first-born;
    • ꜀chʻo ꜁tsʻun (co1 ceon4 初旬), the first decade;
    • ꜀chʻo ꜀hoi fau꜅ (co1 hoi1 fau6 初開埠) to found a mart;
    • ꜀chʻo ꜂kí (co1 gei2 初幾), which day of the first decade is it?
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 初間, 初旬, 初幾: Here, "decade" means ten days.
  • Williams' 初頭發市: Corrected shai꜄ (sai3) to ꜃shí (si5).
  • Williams' 初來步到: Corrected ꜁chʻo (co4) to ꜀chʻo (co1).
  • Williams' 初生: More newborn than firstborn.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【初初】 (co1 co1):
    1. At first, initially.
  • 【初哥】 (co1 go1):
    1. A beginner, a novice.

唨 ꜀Chʻo (co1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5528
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 26* ꜀唨)

    A colloquial, final particle.

    • To stop, to desist;
    • ꜃ní ꜃mai hü꜄ tsz'꜅ ꜀chʻo (nei5 mai5 heoi3 zi6 co1 你咪去？唨), don't you go!
    • ꜃ní ꜂tang ꜃há ꜀chʻo (nei5 dang2 haa5 co1 你等吓唨), stop a little!

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 58 芻) 草也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 26* ꜀芻)
    • To cut grass;
    • hay, fodder, grass for animals;
    • yat꜆ chʻuk꜆ ꜀chʻo (jat1 cuk1 co1 一束芻), a bundle of hay;
    • ꜀chʻo ꜁iú (co1 jiu4 芻蕘), grass; met. the people.
