Chʻéuk꜆ Tsʻéuk꜆ (coek3 低陰入)

  • 倬
  • 卓

倬 Chʻéuk꜆ (coek3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 8
Unicode code point
U+502C
Reading variations
  • zoek3 (uncommon)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 倬) 著也大也詩倬彼雲漢
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 倬꜆)
    • Extensive, distant;
    • large;
    • to show, to manifest;
    • ꜁ming chʻéuk꜆ ꜃ü tsúi꜅ (ming4 coek3 jyu5 zeoi6 明倬汝罪) to make your crime known.
Conway's commentary
  • The uncommon reading zoek3 is perhaps following the unaspirated initial in Middle Chinese 竹角切 chok꜆ (zok3).

卓 Chʻéuk꜆ (coek3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼗ + 6
Unicode code point
U+5353
Reading variations
  • zoek3 (uncommon)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 卓) 特立也高也遠也望也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 卓꜆)
    • Firm, stable;
    • lofty and profound;
    • distant;
    • to establish;
    • to expect;
    • to surpass;
    • eminent in, raised above others;
    • at, reached, as a time;
    • ꜀chʻiú chʻéuk꜆ (ciu1 coek3 超卓) preëminent, superior to common;
    • chʻéuk꜆ üt꜇ ꜁yan ꜁kwʻan (coek3 jyut6 jan4 kwan4 卓越人群), excelling other men;
    • chʻéuk꜆ lap꜇ (coek3 lap6 卓立) to erect, upright;
    • chʻéuk꜆ í꜅ (coek3 ji6 卓異) meritorious, said of officers at the quinqennial trial.
Conway's commentary
  • The uncommon reading zoek3 is perhaps following the unaspirated initial in Middle Chinese 竹角切 chok꜆ (zok3).
