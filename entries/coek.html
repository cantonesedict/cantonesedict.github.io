倬 Chʻéuk꜆ (coek3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 8
Unicode code point
U+502C
Reading variations
  • zoek3 (per initial of 《廣韻》竹角切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 倬) 著也大也詩倬彼雲漢
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 倬꜆)
    • Extensive, distant;
    • large (Kangxi: 大也);
    • to show, to manifest (Kangxi: 著也, 明貌);
    • ꜁ming chʻéuk꜆ ꜃ü tsúi꜅ (ming4 coek3 jyu5 zeoi6 明倬汝罪) to make your crime known.

卓 Chʻéuk꜆ (coek3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼗ + 6
Unicode code point
U+5353
Reading variations
  • zoek3 (per initial of 《廣韻》竹角切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 卓) 特立也高也遠也望也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 卓꜆)
    • Firm, stable;
    • lofty (Kangxi: 高也) and profound;
    • distant;
    • to establish;
    • to expect;
    • to surpass (Kangxi: 超卓也);
    • eminent in, raised above others;
    • at, reached, as a time;
    • ꜀chʻiú chʻéuk꜆ (ciu1 coek3 超卓) preëminent, superior to common;
    • chʻéuk꜆ üt꜇ ꜁yan ꜁kwʻan (coek3 jyut6 jan4 kwan4 卓越人群), excelling other men;
    • chʻéuk꜆ lap꜇ (coek3 lap6 卓立) to erect, upright;
    • chʻéuk꜆ í꜅ (coek3 ji6 卓異) meritorious, said of officers at the quinqennial trial.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A surname (Kangxi: 姓).

灼 Chʻéuk꜆ (coek3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽕ + 3
Unicode code point
U+707C
Reading variations
  • zoek3 (per 《廣韻》之若切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 灼 Chéuk꜆ (zoek3)) 燒也炙也昭也又灼灼華盛貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 32 灼꜆ Chéuk꜆ (zoek3); here normalised to Chʻéuk꜆ (coek3))
    • To burn (Kangxi: 炙也, 熱也);
    • to singe, to cauterize with moxa (Kangxi: 熱也);
    • alarmed (Kangxi: 灼驚也);
    • clear, distinct (Kangxi: 明也);
    • luxuriant, as flowers (Kangxi: 灼灼花盛貌);
    • chéuk꜆ chʻéuk꜆ ꜀kwai (coek3 gwai1 灼龜), to singe a terrapin's shell;
    • chéuk꜆ chʻéuk꜆ lán꜅ (coek3 laan6 灼爛) to raise a blister;
    • chéuk꜆ chʻéuk꜆ ngái꜅ (coek3 ngaai6 灼艾) to burn the moxa;
    • chéuk꜆ chéuk꜆ chʻéuk꜆ chʻéuk꜆ (coek3 coek3 灼灼) splendid, as flowers.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【灼】 (coek3)
    1. To cook (briefly) in boiling water, to blanch.
      • 白灼蝦 (baak6 coek3 haa1): "Plain boiled prawns."
      • 灼一灼 (coek3 jat1 coek3): "[Give it a] blanch."

綽 Chʻéuk꜆ (coek3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽷ + 8
Unicode code point
U+7DBD
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 綽) 寬也緩也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 綽꜆)
    • Large, ample (Kangxi: 緩也, 綽綽寬也, 綽猶多也);
    • slow, leisurely;
    • chʻéuk꜆ yéuk꜆ (coek3 joek3 綽約) delicate, as a girl;
    • ꜀fún chʻéuk꜆ (fun1 coek3 寬綽) broad, liberal;
    • chʻéuk꜆ chʻéuk꜆ ꜃yau ꜁ü (coek3 coek3 jau5 jyu4 綽綽有餘), an overplus, more than enough.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "slow, leisurely": This is a misreading of Kangxi 緩也.
