卓 Chʻéuk꜆ (coek3)

Radical + residual strokes

Unicode code point

Reading variations
  • zoek3 (expected from initial of 竹角切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 卓) 特立也高也遠也望也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 卓꜆)
    • Firm, stable;
    • lofty and profound;
    • distant;
    • to establish;
    • to expect;
    • to surpass;
    • eminent in, raised above others;
    • at, reached, as a time;
    • ꜀chʻiú chʻéuk꜆ (ciu1 coek3 超卓) preëminent, superior to common;
    • chʻéuk꜆ üt꜇ ꜁yan ꜁kwʻan (coek3 jyut6 jan4 kwan4 卓越人群), excelling other men;
    • chʻéuk꜆ lap꜇ (coek3 lap6 卓立) to erect, upright;
    • chʻéuk꜆ í꜅ (coek3 ji6 卓異) meritorious, said of officers at the quinqennial trial.

Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 棹꜆)
    • (See 桌 coek3 for table senses);
    • a kind of tree;
    • (…).
Conway's commentary
  • The character 棹 is only attested in the Middle Chinese rime books under the reading 直敎切 cháu꜅ (zaau6), explained 檝也 "oar". The "name of a tree" sense appears in Kangxi, quoting 《類篇》直角切[…]又木名; while this is in entering tone, the expected reading is chok꜇ (zok6).

Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【灼】 (coek3):
    1. To cook (briefly) in boiling water, to blanch.
      • 白灼蝦 (baak6 coek3 haa1): "Plain boiled prawns."
      • 灼一灼 (coek3 jat1 coek3): "[Give it a] blanch."
