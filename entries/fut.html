Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 79 濶) 廣也間濶[疎]濶
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 67 闊꜆)
    • Broad, wide (Kangxi: 廣也), large;
    • ample;
    • distant (Kangxi: 疏也一曰遠也, 闊遠也), separated (Kangxi: 乖闊);
    • liberal;
    • diligent (Kangxi: 契闊勤苦也);
    • to widen, to enlarge, to relax (Kangxi: 寬也);
    • fút꜆ tái꜅ (fut3 daai6 闊大) ample, capacious;
    • ꜁wáng fút꜆ (waang4 fut3 橫闊) broad across;
    • tím꜅ fút꜆ (dim6 fut3 掂闊) across the length;
    • ꜀sho fút꜆ (so1 fut3 疏闊) long separated (as friends);
    • kán꜄ fút꜆ (gaan3 fut3 間闊) widely sundered, long separated (Kangxi: 凡久不相見曰閒闊);
    • fút꜆ lok꜇ (fut3 lok6 闊落) agreeable, spacious.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected ⿰𤴔朿 to 疎.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Impractical (Kangxi: 迂闊).
  2. Sparse.
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【契闊】 (khet khwat)
    • (adjective-like) widely separated
    • (adjective-like) hard working
  • 【闊】 (khwat)
    • (adjective-like) widely separated
    • (adjective-like) wide
    • (verb-like) [to] widen, (met.) [to] ease
    • Used in 契闊.
