川 ꜀Chʻün (cyun1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼮ + 0
Unicode code point
U+5DDD
Alternative forms
  • 巛
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 19 川) 山川衆流也又川穿也謂穿地而流也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 38* ꜀川)
    • Mountain streams, perennial brooks (Kangxi: 穿也穿地而流也);
    • ꜀chʻün ꜁lau pat꜆ sik꜆ (cyun1 lau4 bat1 sik1 川流不息) perennial, unceasing;
    • ꜀chʻün ꜁[ngái] (cyun1 ngaai4 川崖), a cascade.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 川崖: Corrected ꜁ngai (ngai4) to ꜁ngái (ngaai4).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Short for 四川.

穿 ꜀Chʻün (cyun1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽳ + 4
Unicode code point
U+7A7F
Reading variations
  • cyun3 (per 《廣韻》尺絹切 for "thorough" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 19 穿) 貫也[鑿]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • To bore through (Kangxi: 委曲入也鑽也鑿也), to perforate (Kangxi: 通也穴也);
  • to clothe, to put on;
  • to string;
  • ꜀chʻün tsok꜇ (cyun1 zok6 穿鑿) to open a hole;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜁fong ꜂shai [wún꜅] (cyun1 fong4 sai2 wun6 穿房使喚) a servant of all work;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀shá ꜀pò (cyun1 saa1 bou1 穿砂煲), to show the falsity of;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀kam tái꜄ ꜁ngan (cyun1 gam1 daai3 ngan4 穿金戴銀), she wears gold and robes in silver;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀chü (cyun1 zyu1 穿珠), to string beads;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜁hái (cyun1 haai4 穿鞋), to pull on shoes;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀shán káp꜆ (cyun1 saan1 gaap3 穿山甲) the manis.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised ⿱⿰凿殳金 (with a stroke missing) to 鑿.
  • Williams' 穿房使喚: Changed fún꜄ (fun3) to modern wún꜅ (wun6).
  • Williams' 穿砂煲: Now simply 穿煲.
  • Williams' 穿山甲: Here, "manis" means pangolin.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Thorough (in understanding) (Kangxi: 貫也).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【穿煲】 (cyun1 bou1):
    1. To have a secret or embarrassing information exposed.
  • 【穿窿】 (cyun1 lung4-1):
    1. To develop a hole.

喘 ꜂Chʻün (cyun2)

Alternative forms

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 21 喘) 氣[緊]
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 38* ꜂喘)
    • To pant, to gasp (Kangxi: 疾息也, 喘息);
    • the breath, the life;
    • ꜁tsʻán ꜂chʻün (caan4 cyun2 殘喘), my poor life, ready to perish;

Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To speak quietly or subtly (Kangxi: 微言).

舛 ꜂Chʻün (cyun2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾇ + 0
Unicode code point
U+821B
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 21 舛) 差謬也[錯亂]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 38* ꜂舛)
    • Erroneous (Kangxi: 舛錯也, 錯亂也), opposed (Kangxi: 舛偝也 etc.);
    • to contradict;
    • the 136th radical ("oppose")[;]
    • ꜂chʻün tsʻok꜆ (cyun2 cok3 舛錯) wrong;
    • ꜂chʻün mau꜅ (cyun2 mau6 舛謬) to deceive.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected missing stroke in 錯, and corrected malformed 亂.
  • Williams' 舛錯: Now more commonly read ꜂chʻün tsʻo꜄ (cyun2 co3).
  • Williams' 舛謬: Better rendered "absurd".
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Difficult (as a situation).

攛 Tsʻün꜄ (cyun3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 18
Unicode code point
U+651B
Alternative forms
  • 竄 (for "tempt" sense)
Reading variations
  • cyun1 (per Kangxi 七丸切 for "tempt" sense)
  • cyun2 (per Fan Wan)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 21 [攛] ꜂Chʻün (cyun2)) 擲也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 38* ꜂竄 ꜂Chʻün (cyun2); here normalised to 攛 Tsʻün꜄ (cyun3))
    • (…);
    • to seduce, to beguile (Kangxi 竄: 誘人爲惡曰竄俗曰攛掇);
    • (…).
  • (Page 38* ꜂攛 ꜂Chʻün (cyun2); here normalised to Tsʻün꜄ (cyun3))
    • To fling away (Kangxi 攛: 擲也);
    • to tempt, to seduce to evil (Kangxi 攛: 俗謂誘人爲非曰攛掇);
    • to get one to consent;
    • [tsʻün꜄] chüt꜆ (cyun3 zyut3 攛掇) to entice, to inveigle.

竄 Tsʻün꜄ (cyun3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽳ + 13
Unicode code point
U+7AC4
Reading variations
  • cyun2 (per Fan Wan)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 21 [竄] ꜂Chʻün (cyun2)) 也[竄]驅逐也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 38* ꜂竄 ꜂Chʻün (cyun2); here normalised to Tsʻün꜄ (cyun3))
    • To sneak away, to hide, to skulk (Kangxi: 匿也逃也);
    • to kill;
    • (…);
    • to fumigate (Kangxi: 竄以藥薰之也);
    • to secrete (Kangxi: 藏也隱也);
    • petty, weak, pusillanimous;
    • [tsʻün꜄] nik꜆ (cyun3 nik1 竄匿) to hide away;
    • ꜁tʻò [tsʻün꜄] (tou4 cyun3 逃竄), to run off;
    • [tsʻün꜄] sít꜆ (cyun3 sit3 竄竊) to pilfer.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 攛 (for the "tempt" sense). See 攛 cyun3 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected malformed headword. The first two characters 也[竄] of the explanation are erroneous.
  • Williams' "to kill": This is a misreading of Kangxi 放也誅也, which should be rendered "[to] exile, [to] punish".
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To exile (Kangxi: 放也誅也).
  2. In secret (Kangxi: 竄微也).
  3. To alter (Kangxi: 改易也).
  4. To put, to place.
See also
  • 竄 cyun2

傳 ꜁Chʻün (cyun4)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 11
Unicode code point
U+50B3
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 21 傳) 傳[授]傳布傳[遞]又續也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 38* ꜁傳)
    • To transfer (Kangxi: 轉也), to transmit, to hand down, to perpetuate (Kangxi: 授也續也);
    • to deliver an order;
    • to propagate (Kangxi: 布也);
    • to carry over, as a balance;
    • to narrate, to record;
    • ꜁chʻün shau꜅ (cyun4 sau6 傳授) to deliver to;
    • ꜁chʻün káu꜄ (cyun4 gaau3 傳教) to instruct in;
    • ꜁chʻün ꜀san ꜁man (cyun4 san1 man4 傳新聞), to tell the news;
    • ꜁chʻün ꜁ín (cyun4 jin4 傳言), to send a message;
    • ꜁chʻün ꜀kung (cyun4 gung1 傳供), a local interpreter in a government office;
    • ꜁chʻün shüt꜆ (cyun4 syut3 傳說) a rumor.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected missing strokes in 授 and 遞.
  • Williams' 傳教: Now to proselytise (especially Christianity).
  • Williams' 傳言: Also a rumour.
  • Williams' 傳說: Also a legend, a tale.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To shift (Kangxi: 移也).
  2. To summon, to call.
