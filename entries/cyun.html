穿 ꜀Chʻün (cyun1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽳ + 4
Unicode code point
U+7A7F
Reading variations
  • cyun3 (per 《廣韻》尺絹切 for "thorough" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 19 穿) 貫也[鑿]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • To bore through (Kangxi: 委曲入也鑽也鑿也), to perforate (Kangxi: 通也穴也);
  • to clothe, to put on;
  • to string;
  • ꜀chʻün tsok꜇ (cyun1 zok6 穿鑿) to open a hole;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜁fong ꜂shai [wún꜅] (cyun1 fong4 sai2 wun6 穿房使喚) a servant of all work;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀shá ꜀pò (cyun1 saa1 bou1 穿砂煲), to show the falsity of;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀kam tái꜄ ꜁ngan (cyun1 gam1 daai3 ngan4 穿金戴銀), she wears gold and robes in silver;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀chü (cyun1 zyu1 穿珠), to string beads;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜁hái (cyun1 haai4 穿鞋), to pull on shoes;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀shán káp꜆ (cyun1 saan1 gaap3 穿山甲) the manis.
Conway's commentary
  • Note that the (now obsolete) departing-tone reading is cognate with 串 cyun3.
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised ⿱⿰凿殳金 (with a stroke missing) to 鑿.
  • Williams' 穿房使喚: Changed fún꜄ (fun3) to modern wún꜅ (wun6).
  • Williams' 穿砂煲: Now simply 穿煲.
  • Williams' 穿山甲: Here, "manis" means pangolin.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Thorough (in understanding) (Kangxi: 貫也).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【穿煲】 (cyun1 bou1):
    1. To have a secret or embarrassing information exposed.
  • 【穿窿】 (cyun1 lung4-1):
    1. To develop a hole.

揣 ꜂Chʻün (cyun2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 9
Unicode code point
U+63E3
Reading variations
  • ceoi2 (per 《廣韻》初委切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 56 揣 ꜂Chʻui (ceoi2)) 吹上聲揣度揣摩
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 34* ꜂揣)

    Also read ꜂chʻün (cyun2).

    • To estimate, to calculate, to reckon (Kangxi: 量也, 量度也, 凡稱量忖度皆曰揣);

    • pat꜆ ꜂[chʻün] (bat1 cyun2 不揣), unable to detect.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "Also read […]": This reading ꜂chʻün (cyun2) is the canonical reading today. I have normalised ꜂chʻui (ceoi2) in the examples to it.

    • To string together (Kangxi: 物相連貫也與穿讀去聲通穿亦作串);
    • to connect;
    • leagued or banded;

  • (Page 38* ꜂竄 ꜂Chʻün (cyun2); here normalised to Tsʻün꜄ (cyun3))
    • To sneak away, to hide, to skulk (Kangxi: 匿也逃也);

  1. To exile (Kangxi: 放也誅也).
  2. In secret (Kangxi: 竄微也).
  3. To alter (Kangxi: 改易也).
  4. To put, to place.

遄 ꜁Chʻün (cyun4)

Radical + residual strokes
⾡ + 9
Unicode code point
U+9044
Reading variations
  • cyun2 (per Williams)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 56 遄 ꜂Chʻui (ceoi2)) 速也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 38* ꜂遄 ꜂Chʻün (cyun2); here normalised to ꜁Chʻün (cyun4))
    • To go quickly (Kangxi: 往來數也, 疾也速也);
    • haste, speed;
    • to go and come.

    Also read chui꜄ (zeoi3) with the same meaning.

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "to go and come": This is a misreading of Kangxi 往來數也. Here, 數 is to be read in entering tone, shok꜆ (sok3), meaning "frequently". Thus, 往來數也 should be rendered "[to] go [and] come frequently", and defines the same sense as 疾也速也.
