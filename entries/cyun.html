川 ꜀Chʻün (cyun1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼮ + 0
Unicode code point
U+5DDD
Alternative forms
  • 巛
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 19 川) 山川衆流也又川穿也謂穿地而流也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 38* ꜀川)
    • Mountain streams, perennial brooks (Kangxi: 穿也穿地而流也);
    • ꜀chʻün ꜁lau pat꜆ sik꜆ (cyun1 lau4 bat1 sik1 川流不息) perennial, unceasing;
    • ꜀chʻün ꜁[ngái] (cyun1 ngaai4 川崖), a cascade.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 川崖: Corrected ꜁ngai (ngai4) to ꜁ngái (ngaai4).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Short for 四川.

穿 ꜀Chʻün (cyun1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽳ + 4
Unicode code point
U+7A7F
Reading variations
  • cyun3 (per 《廣韻》尺絹切 for "thorough" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 19 穿) 貫也[鑿]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • To bore through (Kangxi: 委曲入也鑽也鑿也), to perforate (Kangxi: 通也穴也);
  • to clothe, to put on;
  • to string;
  • ꜀chʻün tsok꜇ (cyun1 zok6 穿鑿) to open a hole;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜁fong ꜂shai [wún꜅] (cyun1 fong4 sai2 wun6 穿房使喚) a servant of all work;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀shá ꜀pò (cyun1 saa1 bou1 穿砂煲), to show the falsity of;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀kam tái꜄ ꜁ngan (cyun1 gam1 daai3 ngan4 穿金戴銀), she wears gold and robes in silver;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀chü (cyun1 zyu1 穿珠), to string beads;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜁hái (cyun1 haai4 穿鞋), to pull on shoes;
  • ꜀chʻün ꜀shán káp꜆ (cyun1 saan1 gaap3 穿山甲) the manis.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised ⿱⿰凿殳金 (with a stroke missing) to 鑿.
  • Williams' 穿房使喚: Changed fún꜄ (fun3) to modern wún꜅ (wun6).
  • Williams' 穿砂煲: Now simply 穿煲.
  • Williams' 穿山甲: Here, "manis" means pangolin.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Thorough (in understanding) (Kangxi: 貫也).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【穿煲】 (cyun1 bou1):
    1. To have a secret or embarrassing information exposed.
  • 【穿窿】 (cyun1 lung4-1):
    1. To develop a hole.
