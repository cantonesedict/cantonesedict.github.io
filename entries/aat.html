Conway's commentary
  • Williams has the non-pawn senses under ГҒpкңҶ (aap3) and the pawn senses under ГҒtкңҶ (aat3). Whilst ГЎpкңҶ (aap3) is expected from Middle Chinese зғҸз”ІеҲҮ, the common reading of жҠј is ГЎtкңҶ (aat3). Note that жҠј is (probably) cognate with еЈ“, and while еЈ“ also has a Middle Chinese reading of зғҸз”ІеҲҮ, its modern reading as aat3 is uncontroversial.
  • Williams' жҠјйҹ»: Changed wanкң… (wan6) to common reading кңғwan (wan5).
