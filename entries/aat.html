押 Át꜆ (aat3)

Radical + residual strokes
手 + 5
Unicode code point
U+62BC
Reading variations
  • aap3 (expected from 烏甲切)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 5 押꜆ Áp꜆ (aap3); here normalised to Át꜆ (aat3))
    • To sign, to affix a seal, to stamp;
    • to control, to guard;
    • to arrest, to seize, to detain;
    • to compel, to force;
    • to escort;
    • to suppress;
    • a lockup or detention room in a yámun ();
    • wák꜇ ꜀fá [át]꜆ (waak6 faa1 aat3) to make one's mark, to write a device;
    • [át]꜆ fo꜄ (aat3 fo3) to escort goods;
    • [át]꜆ ꜃kʻü tsò꜅ (aat3 keoi5 zou6) make him do it;
    • [át]꜆ sung꜄ ꜁yan (aat3 sung3 yan4), an escort;
    • ꜁'m ꜀kám [át]꜆ ꜃ní (m4 gaam1 aat3 nei5), I'll not keep you; not detain you;
    • [át]꜆ [꜃]wan (aat3 wan5) to rhyme;
    • [át]꜆ ꜁shün (aat3 syun4), custom-house boats, guard-boats;
    • [át]꜆ chan꜅ (aat3 zan6) to go into battle;
    • ꜀tsʻím [át]꜆ ꜁fong (cim1 aat3 fong1), a lockup;
    • ꜀tsʻím [át]꜆ (cim1 aat3) to endorse an edict, in red ink;
    • [át]꜆ ꜀pong (aat3 bong1) to look after, to escort;
    • [át]꜆ ꜀tung (aat3 dung1), kept over winter; left over the season, as teas.
  • (Page 5 押꜆ Át꜆ (aat3))
    • To pawn for a time;
    • to reserve, to keep back;
    • át꜆ kwai꜅ (aat3 gwai6) deposit money;
    • ꜂héung át꜆ (hoeng2 aat3) recognized pawn-shops;
    • kai꜄ át꜆ (gai3 aat3) a hair band-pin;
    • át꜆ pʻiú꜄ (aat3 piu3) to pledge or shave a pawn ticket;
    • ꜂kau ꜃'ng át꜆ (gau2 ng5 aat3) a five per cent. discount pawnbroker;
    • ꜂siú át꜆ (siu2 aat3) a little pawn-shop;
    • tong꜄ át꜆ (dong3 aat3) to pawn.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams has the non-pawn senses under Áp꜆ (aap3) and the pawn senses under Át꜆ (aat3). Whilst aap3 is expected from Middle Chinese 烏甲切, the common reading of 押 is aat3. Note that 押 is (probably) cognate with 壓, and while 壓 also has a Middle Chinese reading of 烏甲切, its modern reading as aat3 is uncontroversial.

遏 Át꜆ (aat3)

Radical + residual strokes
辵 + 9
Unicode code point
U+904F
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 5 遏꜆)
    • To stop, to bring to a standstill;
    • unforeseen obstacle;
    • to reach, to arrive;
    • to cut off, to put a stop to;
    • ꜂cho át꜆ (zo2 aat3 阻遏) to stop one doing, to restrain;
    • át꜆ tek꜇ (aat3 dek6 遏糴) to hold on to rice;
    • át꜆ yuk꜇ ꜁nán (aat3 juk6 naan4 遏慾難), hard to curb one's lusts.
