遏 Át꜆ (aat3)

Radical + residual strokes
辵 + 9
Unicode code point
U+904F
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 5 遏꜆)
    • To stop, to bring to a standstill;
    • unforeseen obstacle;
    • to reach, to arrive;
    • to cut off, to put a stop to;
    • ꜂cho át꜆ (zo2 aat3 阻遏) to stop one doing, to restrain;
    • át꜆ tek꜇ (aat3 dek6 遏糴) to hold on to rice;
    • át꜆ yuk꜇ ꜁nán (aat3 juk6 naan4 遏慾難), hard to curb one's lusts.
