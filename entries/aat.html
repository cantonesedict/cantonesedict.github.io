壓 Át꜆ (aat3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼟ + 14
Unicode code point
U+58D3
Reading variations
  • ngaat3 (hypercorrected initial)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 壓) 鎮也覆也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 壓꜆)
    • To overthrow, as a wall (Kangxi: 壞也);
    • to supply a want (Kangxi: 塞補也);
    • to press down, to steady, to settle (Kangxi: 笮也);
    • to bend;
    • to subject, to suppress, to conquer (Kangxi: 一曰鎭壓);
    • to injure, to oppress;
    • to repress, as alarm;
    • to intimidate;
    • át꜆ wái꜅ (aat3 waai6 壓壞) to crush, to hurt by laying on;
    • át꜆ pák꜆ sing꜄ (aat3 baak3 sing3 壓百姓) to vex the people;
    • ꜁tʻán át꜆ (taan4 aat3 彈壓) to subdue, to put a stop to, as a row;
    • át꜆ ꜀king (aat3 ging1 壓驚), to quiet one's fears;
    • át꜆ chü꜅ (aat3 zyu6 壓住) to steady;
    • chan꜄ át꜆ (zan3 aat3 鎮壓) to repress (a revolt); to remove evils;
    • át꜆ shat꜇ (aat3 sat6 壓實) to press solid.

押 Át꜆ (aat3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 5
Unicode code point
U+62BC
Reading variations
  • aap3 (per 《廣韻》烏甲切)
  • ngaat3 (hypercorrected initial)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 65 押 Áp꜆ (aap3)) 管押簽押
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 5 押꜆ Áp꜆ (aap3); here normalised to Át꜆ (aat3))
    • To sign, to affix a seal, to stamp (Kangxi: 署也 etc.);
    • to control, to guard (Kangxi: 按也一曰管拘也);
    • to arrest, to seize, to detain;
    • to compel, to force;
    • to escort;
    • to suppress;
    • a lockup or detention room in a yámun (衙門);
    • wák꜇ ꜀fá áp꜆ át꜆ (waak6 faa1 aat3 畫花押) to make one's mark, to write a device;
    • áp꜆ át꜆ fo꜄ (aat3 fo3 押貨) to escort goods;
    • áp꜆ át꜆ ꜃kʻü tsò꜅ (aat3 keoi5 zou6 押佢做) make him do it;
    • áp꜆ át꜆ sung꜄ ꜁yan (aat3 sung3 jan4 押送人), an escort;
    • ꜁'m ꜀kám áp꜆ át꜆ ꜃ní (m4 gaam1 aat3 nei5 唔監押你), I'll not keep you; not detain you;
    • áp꜆ át꜆ wan꜅ ꜃wan (aat3 wan5 押韻) to rhyme;
    • áp꜆ át꜆ ꜁shün (aat3 syun4 押船), custom-house boats, guard-boats;
    • áp꜆ át꜆ chan꜅ (aat3 zan6 押陣) to go into battle;
    • ꜀tsʻím áp꜆ át꜆ ꜁fong (cim1 aat3 fong4 簽押房), a lockup;
    • ꜀tsʻím áp꜆ át꜆ (cim1 aat3 簽押) to endorse an edict, in red ink;
    • áp꜆ át꜆ ꜀pong (aat3 bong1 押幫) to look after, to escort;
    • áp꜆ át꜆ ꜀tung (aat3 dung1 押冬), kept over winter; left over the season, as teas.
  • (Page 5 押꜆ Át꜆ (aat3))
    • To pawn for a time;
    • to reserve, to keep back;
    • át꜆ kwai꜅ (aat3 gwai6 押櫃) deposit money;
    • ꜂héung át꜆ (hoeng2 aat3 餉押) recognized pawn-shops;
    • kai꜄ át꜆ (gai3 aat3 髻押) a hair band-pin;
    • át꜆ pʻiú꜄ (aat3 piu3 押票) to pledge or shave a pawn ticket;
    • ꜂kau ꜃'ng át꜆ (gau2 ng5 aat3 九五押) a five per cent. discount pawnbroker;
    • ꜂siú át꜆ (siu2 aat3 小押) a little pawn-shop;
    • tong꜄ át꜆ (dong3 aat3 當押) to pawn.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams has the non-pawn senses under Áp꜆ (aap3) and the pawn senses under Át꜆ (aat3). Whilst áp꜆ (aap3) is expected from Middle Chinese 烏甲切, the common reading of 押 is át꜆ (aat3). Note that 押 is (probably) cognate with 壓, and while 壓 also has a Middle Chinese reading of 烏甲切, its modern reading as aat3 is uncontroversial.
  • Williams' 押韻: Changed wan꜅ (wan6) to common reading ꜃wan (wan5).

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 遏) 止也絕也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 5 遏꜆)
    • To stop, to bring to a standstill (Kangxi: 遏止也);
    • unforeseen obstacle;
    • to reach, to arrive (Kangxi: 逮也);
    • to cut off, to put a stop to (Kangxi: 絕也);
    • ꜂cho át꜆ (zo2 aat3 阻遏) to stop one doing, to restrain;
    • át꜆ tek꜇ (aat3 dek6 遏糴) to hold on to rice;
    • át꜆ yuk꜇ ꜁nán (aat3 juk6 naan4 遏慾難), hard to curb one's lusts.
