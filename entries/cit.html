切 Tsʻít꜆ (cit3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼑ + 2
Unicode code point
U+5207
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 26 切) 近也要也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 580 切꜆)
    • To cut, to carve, to mince, to slice;
    • to urge, to press;
    • a superlative, very, vehement, earnest, eager, pressing, important, serious, momentous;
    • the important parts, a resumé;
    • sincere;
    • to feel, as the pulse;
    • to spell, to express a sound by joining initials and finals;
    • tsʻít꜆ iú꜄ (cit3 jiu3 切要) important;
    • tʻau꜄ tsʻít꜆ (tau3 cit3 透切) plain, lucid;
    • tsʻít꜆ yuk꜇ (cit3 juk6 切肉) minced meat;
    • tsʻít꜆ mat꜇ hü꜄ (cit3 mat6 heoi3 切勿去) stay, don't go;
    • tsʻít꜆ mak꜇ (cit3 mak6 切脈) to feel the pulse;
    • tsʻít꜆ oi꜄ (cit3 oi3 切愛) warm love;
    • tsʻít꜆ tò꜄ sui꜄ (cit3 dou6 seoi3 切到碎) cut to pieces, cut fine;
    • tsʻít꜆ ꜀tsʻo (cit3 co1 切磋), to turn in a lathe; to work and carve; to dehort, as a friend;
    • tsʻít꜆ ꜀yam (cit3 jam1 切音), to spell, to join an initial and a final;
    • kʻok꜆ tsʻít꜆ pat꜆ ꜁í (kok3 cit3 bat1 ji4 確切不移), unchangeably fixed.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 切磋: Not so much to dehort as to learn from one another through an activity (e.g. sparring, a game, etc.).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【切】 (cit3):
    1. In time.
      • 嚟唔切 (lai4 m4 cit3): "Can't make [it] in time."
      • 趕得切 (gon2 dak1 cit3): "Rushed [there] in time."
    2. Short for 白切雞 (plain cut chicken).
      • 叉切飯 (caa1 cit3 faan6): "Char [shiu], [plain] cut [chicken] rice."
  • 【切雞】 (cit3 gai1):
    1. Short for 白切雞 (plain cut chicken).
      • 切雞飯 (cit3 gai1 faan6): "[Plain] cut chicken rice."

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 26 徹) 通明也透也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23* 徹꜆)
    • Pervious, perspicacious, discerning;
    • to penetrate, to pass through;
    • to remove, to take away;
    • a road, a thoroughfare;
    • to skin, to peel off, to destroy;
    • to cultivate;
    • chʻít꜆ chán꜅ (cit3 zaan6 徹饌) to remove food;
    • chʻít꜆ ꜂tai (cit3 dai2 徹底), to understand thoroughly;
    • chʻít꜆ tʻau꜄ (cit3 tau3 徹透) fully known.

設 Chʻít꜆ (cit3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾔ + 4
Unicode code point
U+8A2D
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 26 設) 置主也陳設也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23* 設꜆)
    • To institute, to establish;
    • to set forth;
    • to spread, as a net;
    • to arrange, to place, to set up;
    • to suppose;
    • a band of soldiers, or its officer;
    • large, said of a sword;
    • ꜁'m ꜂shai chʻít꜆ ꜀kún (m4 sai2 cit3 gun1 唔使設官), not to care anything for the magistrates, or there's no need of them;
    • chʻít꜆ lap꜇ (cit3 lap6 設立) to establish;
    • chʻít꜆ ꜁ín (cit3 jin4 設筵), to make a feast;
    • chʻít꜆ fát꜆ (cit3 faat3 設法) to devise means;
    • chʻít꜆ kai꜄ (cit3 gai3 設計) to plot against;
    • chʻít꜆ wák꜇ (cit3 waak6 設或) or chʻít꜆ ꜂sz' (cit3 si2 設使) suppose that;
    • ꜀pʻò chʻít꜆ (pou1 cit3 鋪設) to set in order, to prepare;
    • chʻít꜆ káu꜄ (cit3 gaau3 設教) or chʻít꜆ chéung꜄ (cit3 zoeng3 設帳) to open a school.

    A colloquial word.

    • A very little, a bit, not half enough;
    • ꜂tün chʻít꜆ kòm꜄ ꜂tün (dyun2 cit3 gam3 dyun2 短設咁短), not half long enough.
