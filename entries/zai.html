꜀Chai ꜀Tsai (zai1 陰平)

  • 擠

擠 ꜀Chai ꜀Tsai (zai1)

Radical + residual strokes
手 + 14
Unicode code point
U+64E0
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 9 ꜀擠 ꜀Chai (zai1))

    A colloquial word.

    • To place, to put down, to put by;
    • ꜀chai ꜁mái (zai1 maai4 擠埋), put it aside;
    • ꜀chai ꜀pín chʻü꜄ (zai1 bin1 cyu3 擠邊處) where shall I put it?
    • ꜀chai ꜀pín chʻü꜄ ꜀tò ꜂hò (zai1 bin1 cyu3 dou1 hou2 擠邊處都好), put [it] down anywhere.
  • (Page 548 ꜀擠 ꜀Tsai (zai1))
    • To push, to push over, to upset;
    • to press upon, to crowd;
    • ꜀tsai ꜂yung (zai1 jung2 擠擁), to crowd and press upon.
Conway's commentary
  • 【擠】 (zai1):
    1. To put, or put down.
      • 擠喺度 (zai1 hai2 dou6): "Put [it] over here."
    2. Crowded.
      • 人多擠迫 (jan4 do1 zai1 bik1): "Lots of people [and] crowded."

Chai꜄ Tsai꜄ (zai3 陰去)

制 Chai꜄ (zai3)

Radical + residual strokes
刀 + 6
Unicode code point
U+5236
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 9 制꜄)
    • To adjust, to cut, to form;
    • to govern, to regulate, to rule;
    • to limit, to hinder, to prevent;
    • to invent, to make;
    • a law, a rule, a regulation;
    • tsoi꜅ chai꜄ (zoi6 zai3 在制) or ꜂shau chai꜄ (sau2 zai3 守制) to wear mourning for parents;
    • ꜃yau tsít꜆ chai꜄ (jau5 zit3 zai3 有節制) I can do so much;
    • chai꜄ ꜁tʻoi (zai3 toi4 制臺), a governor-general;
    • ꜀kám chai꜄ (gaam1 zai3 監制) to restrain, to set limits;
    • chai꜄ ꜂kan kòm꜄ ꜀to (zai3 gan2 gam3 do1 制緊咁多), to limit one;
    • chai꜄ ꜂tò (zai3 dou2 制倒), to tame, to subdue;
    • tsz'꜅ chai꜄ (zi6 zai3 自制) self-restraint;
    • chai꜄ ꜀shü (zai3 syu1 制書), an imperial dispatch;
    • chai꜄ tò꜅ (zai3 dou6 制度) a pattern, laws;
    • kam꜄ chai꜄ (gam3 zai3 禁制) a prohibition;
    • chai꜄ tí꜅ (zai3 dei6 制地) the court;
    • sui꜄ chai꜄ (seoi3 zai3 歲制) a sexagenary;
    • chai꜄ fok꜆ (zai3 fok3 制縛) a close surveillance.

製 Chai꜄ (zai3)

Radical + residual strokes
衣 + 8
Unicode code point
U+88FD
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 9 製꜄)

    Interchanged with the last (制).

    • To invent, to make;
    • to cut out clothes;
    • a fashion, a mode;
    • to compound, as medicines;
    • a pattern;
    • a fur cloak;
    • ꜃mí chai꜄ (mei5 zai3) well made;
    • ü꜅ chai꜄ (jyu6 zai3) done by his Majesty;
    • ꜂hò chai꜄ fát꜆ (hou2 zai3 faat3) well compounded;
    • fát꜆ chai꜄ ꜃yé (faat3 zai3 je5), useless things;
    • chai꜄ tsò꜅ (zai3 zou6) to invent;
    • chai꜄ ꜀í ꜁shéung (zai3 ji1 soeng4), to cut out clothes;
    • ꜂háu chai꜄ (haau2 zai3) skillfully made.
