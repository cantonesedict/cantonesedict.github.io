꜀Chai ꜀Tsai (zai1 陰平)

  • 劑

劑 ꜀Tsai (zai1)

Radical + residual strokes
刀 + 14
Unicode code point
U+5291
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 548 ꜀劑)
    • To pare, to cut even, to trim;
    • to equalize, to portion, to adjust;
    • to compound, as pills;
    • a dose, a recipe;
    • yat꜆ ꜀tsai yéuk꜇ (jat1 zai1 joek6 一劑藥) a dose of medicine;
    • ꜁tʻiú ꜀tsai (tiu4 zai1 調劑), to arrange amicably;
    • chat꜆ ꜀tsai (zat1 zai1 質劑), a check, a bond, made so as to cut in twain for evidence.

擠 ꜀Chai ꜀Tsai (zai1)

Radical + residual strokes
手 + 14
Unicode code point
U+64E0
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 9 ꜀擠 ꜀Chai (zai1))

    A colloquial word.

    • To place, to put down, to put by;
    • ꜀chai ꜁mái (zai1 maai4 擠埋), put it aside;
    • ꜀chai ꜀pín chʻü꜄ (zai1 bin1 cyu3 擠邊處) where shall I put it?
    • ꜀chai ꜀pín chʻü꜄ ꜀tò ꜂hò (zai1 bin1 cyu3 dou1 hou2 擠邊處都好), put [it] down anywhere.
  • (Page 548 ꜀擠 ꜀Tsai (zai1))
    • To push, to push over, to upset;
    • to press upon, to crowd;
    • ꜀tsai ꜂yung (zai1 jung2 擠擁), to crowd and press upon.
Conway's entry
  • 【擠】 (zai1):
    1. To put, or put down.
      • 擠喺度 (zai1 hai2 dou6): "Put [it] over here."
    2. Crowded.
      • 人多擠迫 (jan4 do1 zai1 bik1): "Lots of people [and] crowded."

制 Chai꜄ (zai3)

Radical + residual strokes
刀 + 6
Unicode code point
U+5236
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 9 制꜄)
    • To adjust, to cut, to form;
    • to govern, to regulate, to rule;
    • to limit, to hinder, to prevent;
    • to invent, to make;
    • a law, a rule, a regulation;
    • tsoi꜅ chai꜄ (zoi6 zai3 在制) or ꜂shau chai꜄ (sau2 zai3 守制) to wear mourning for parents;
    • ꜃yau tsít꜆ chai꜄ (jau5 zit3 zai3 有節制) I can do so much;
    • chai꜄ ꜁tʻoi (zai3 toi4 制臺), a governor-general;
    • ꜀kám chai꜄ (gaam1 zai3 監制) to restrain, to set limits;
    • chai꜄ ꜂kan kòm꜄ ꜀to (zai3 gan2 gam3 do1 制緊咁多), to limit one;
    • chai꜄ ꜂tò (zai3 dou2 制倒), to tame, to subdue;
    • tsz'꜅ chai꜄ (zi6 zai3 自制) self-restraint;
    • chai꜄ ꜀shü (zai3 syu1 制書), an imperial dispatch;
    • chai꜄ tò꜅ (zai3 dou6 制度) a pattern, laws;
    • kam꜄ chai꜄ (gam3 zai3 禁制) a prohibition;
    • chai꜄ tí꜅ (zai3 dei6 制地) the court;
    • sui꜄ chai꜄ (seoi3 zai3 歲制) a sexagenary;
    • chai꜄ fok꜆ (zai3 fok3 制縛) a close surveillance.

掣 Chai꜄ (zai3)

Radical + residual strokes
手 + 8
Unicode code point
U+63A3
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10 掣꜄)
    • To obstruct, to hinder from entering;
    • to take, to choose out, to raise up;
    • to select;
    • to pull, to draw;
    • chai꜄ ꜀tsʻím (zai3 cim1 掣籤), to draw lots;
    • ꜀hín chai꜄ (hin1 zai3 牽掣) to compel, to force;
    • chá꜄ chai꜄ chá꜄ tsung꜄ (zaa3 zai3 zaa3 zung3 乍掣乍縱) a twitching, as of children in convulsions;
    • chai꜄ tín꜅ (zai3 din6 掣電) like lightning, as an electrical machine acts;
    • híp꜇ chai꜄ (hip6 zai3 挾掣) to restrain;
    • chai꜄ ꜂[cháu] (zai3 zaau2 掣肘), to impede, to embarrass.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 掣肘: Changed Middle Chinese ꜂chau (zau2) to common reading ꜂cháu (zaau2).
Conway's entry
  • 【掣】 (zai3):
    1. A push-button, switch, pedal, etc.
      • 手掣 (sau2 zai3): "Handbrake"
      • 撳掣 (gam6 zai3): "Press [the] button"
      • 煞掣 (saat3 zai3): "Slam [the] brakes"
      • 總掣 (zung2 zai3): "[The] main switch"
      • 跳掣 (tiu3 zai3): "Trip [the] breaker"
      • 閂掣 (saan1 zai3): "Turn off [the] switch"
      • 開掣 (hoi1 zai3): "Turn on [the] switch"
      • 電掣 (din6 zai3): "[An] electrical switch"

製 Chai꜄ (zai3)

Radical + residual strokes
衣 + 8
Unicode code point
U+88FD
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 9 製꜄)

    Interchanged with the last (制).

    • To invent, to make;
    • to cut out clothes;
    • a fashion, a mode;
    • to compound, as medicines;
    • a pattern;
    • a fur cloak;
    • ꜃mí chai꜄ (mei5 zai3 美製) well made;
    • ü꜅ chai꜄ (jyu6 zai3 御製) done by his Majesty;
    • ꜂hò chai꜄ fát꜆ (hou2 zai3 faat3 好製法) well compounded;
    • fát꜆ chai꜄ ꜃yé (faat3 zai3 je5 法製嘢), useless things;
    • chai꜄ tsò꜅ (zai3 zou6 製造) to invent;
    • chai꜄ ꜀í ꜁shéung (zai3 ji1 soeng4 製衣裳), to cut out clothes;
    • ꜂háu chai꜄ (haau2 zai3 巧製) skillfully made.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 製造: Now more "create" than "invent".

滯 Chai꜅ (zai6)

Radical + residual strokes
水 + 11
Unicode code point
U+6EEF
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 10 滯꜅)
    • Water impeded;
    • condensed, congealed;
    • to obstruct, to stop;
    • indigestible, disagreeing, piled up;
    • shik꜇ chai꜅ (sik6 zai6 食滯) indigestible;
    • chai꜅ chü꜅ (zai6 zyu6 滯住) to restrain; impeded; an obstruction, stoppage;
    • ai꜄ chai꜅ (ai3 zai6 翳滯) fullness in the stomach; hindering; slow progress;
    • fúi꜄ chai꜅ (fui3 zai6 晦滯) stupid looking;
    • ꜂pán chai꜅ (baan2 zai6 板滯) an old fogy; adhering to the old way;
    • ꜂cho chai꜅ (zo2 zai6 阻滯) hindered, let, detained;
    • chai꜅ hí꜄ (zai6 hei3 滯氣) a stoppage of secretions.
