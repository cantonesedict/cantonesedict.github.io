除 ꜁Chʻü (ceoi4)

Radical + residual strokes
⾩ + 7
Unicode code point
U+9664
Reading variations
  • cyu4 (uncommon)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 45 除) 消除
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 32* ꜁除)
    • Steps to a palace;
    • a vestibule;
    • to exclude, to remove;
    • to deduct, to subtract;
    • to except from;
    • to cure;
    • to exchange;
    • to vacate;
    • to divide;
    • besides, exclusive of;
    • ꜁chʻü lat꜆ (ceoi4 lat1 除甩) throw it away as useless;
    • ꜁chʻü ꜀fí (ceoi4 fei1 除非), if, when, thenceafter, premising, not reckoning;
    • ꜁chʻü chʻut꜆ (ceoi4 ceot1 除出) to exclude, to lay by;
    • ꜁chʻü fuk꜇ (ceoi4 fuk6 除服) to lay aside mourning;
    • ꜀kwai ꜁chʻü (gwai1 ceoi4 歸除), to multiply or divide;
    • ꜁chʻü ꜁pʻí ꜂kí ꜀to ꜀kan (ceoi4 pei4 gei2 do1 gan1 除皮幾多斤), how many pounds beside the tare?
    • ꜁chʻü chʻák꜆ ꜁mái shing꜅ ꜀ní tik꜆ (ceoi4 caak3 maai4 sing6 ni1 dik1 除拆埋剩呢滴) so much remains after deducting;
    • ꜁chʻü ꜀kún (ceoi4 gun1 除官), to exchange office; to promote to a post;
    • ꜁chʻü ꜀hiú ꜃ní ꜁mò ꜁yan tsò꜅ tak꜆ (ceoi4 hiu1 nei5 mou4 jan4 zou6 dak1 除嘵你無人做得) nobody can do it besides you;
    • ꜁chʻü hü꜄ (ceoi4 heoi3 除去) to push away, to eject.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【除】 (ceoi4):
    1. To remove clothing, accessories, footwear, etc.
      • 除衫 (ceoi4 saam1): "Take off clothes."
      • 除褲 (ceoi4 fu3): "Take off pants."
      • 除帽 (ceoi4 mou6-2): "Take off [a] hat."
      • 除鞋 (ceoi4 haai4): "Take off shoes."
