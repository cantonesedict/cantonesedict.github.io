寬 ꜀Fún (fun1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼧ + 12
Unicode code point
U+5BEC
Alternative forms
  • 寛
  • 宽 (Mainland simplified)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 77 寛) 寛大寛[裕]
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 64 ꜀寛; here normalised to 寬)
    • Large, spacious (Kangxi 寬: 屋寬大也), wide, ample (Kangxi 寬: 裕也), broad;
    • forgiving (Kangxi 寬: 宥也), easy (Kangxi 寬: 舒也), benignant, clement (Kangxi 寬: 不猛也)., kind, indulgent;
    • slow;
    • to widen, to enlarge, to relax (Kangxi 寬: 緩也);
    • to forbear;
    • to unrobe;
    • ꜀fún fút꜆ (fun1 fut3 寬闊) wide, ample;
    • ꜀fún tái꜅ (fun1 daai6 寬大) liberal, indulgent magnanimous;
    • ꜀fún ꜀yung ꜁yung (fun1 jung4 寬容), pleasant, a jolly countenance;
    • ꜀fún ꜁ü ü꜅ (fun1 jyu6 寬裕), an abundance, overplus;
    • ꜀fún hán꜅ (fun1 haan6 寬限) to allow a longer time;
    • ꜀fún shü꜄ (fun1 syu3 寬恕) to forgive;
    • ꜀fún út꜇ (fun1 wut6 寬活) in easy circumstances;
    • sz'꜅ ꜀fún (si6 fun1 事寬), not urgent;
    • ꜀fún tsung꜄ (fun1 zung3 寬縱) over-indulgent, heedless of.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Fixed incompletely printed character 裕.
  • Williams' 寬大: Better rendered "magnanimous". This is a positive trait.
  • Williams' 寬容: Corrected ꜀yung (jung1) to ꜁yung (jung4). Better rendered "lenient" or "permissive".
  • Williams' 寬裕: Corrected ꜁ü (jyu4) to ü꜅ (jyu6).
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【寬】 (khwan)
    • (adjective-like) wide, spacious
    • (adjective-like) magnanimous
    • (adjective-like) clement, lenient
    • (verb-like) [to] clemence, (met.) [to] pardon

  2. A male wolf (Kangxi: 狼牡貛牝狼).

款 ꜂Fún (fun2)

Radical + residual strokes
⽋ + 8
Unicode code point
U+6B3E
Alternative forms
  • 欵
  • 歀
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 78 欵) 衷曲也至也[留]也又條件也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 65 ꜂欵; here normalised to 款)
    • Something desired Desiring (Kangxi 款: 意有所欲也 etc.);
    • sincere, single-hearted;
    • true (Kangxi 款: 誠也, 款款忠誠也), to love (Kangxi 款: 愛也), to venerate, to respect;
    • to treat well;
    • to knock at (Kangxi 款: 叩也求通也);
    • to reach (Kangxi 款: 至也);
    • to repay;
    • a memorandum of an inscription (Kangxi 款: 誌也, 師古曰款刻也識記也, etc.);
    • to enumerate;
    • a classifier of articles of different sorts, classes, affairs;
    • ꜂fún toi꜅ (fun2 doi6 款待) to treat politely;
    • ꜂fún ꜁tʻau tái꜅ (fun2 tau4 daai6 款頭大) haughty, difficult of access;
    • náu꜅ ꜂fún (naau6 fun2 鬧款), pompous;
    • ꜁tʻai ꜂fún (tai4 fun2 題款), to write an autograph;
    • ꜂fún shik꜆ (fun2 sik1 款式) a sort;
    • kok꜆ ꜂fún (gok3 fun2 各款), every kind;
    • ꜀kwai ꜂fún (gwai1 fun2 歸款) to repay a loan;
    • yat꜆ ꜂fún sz'꜅ (jat1 fun2 si6 一款事) an affair;
    • ꜂fún ꜁lau (fun2 lau4 款留), to detain as a guest.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected malformed 留.
  • Williams' "Something desired": This is a misreading of Kangxi 意有所欲也. This sense is an adjective ("desiring", as a hole unfilled), not a noun.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Void, hollow (Kangxi 款: 空也). (Alternative form of 窾. See 窾 fun2.) (Kangxi 款: 與窾同)
  2. A clause (Kangxi 款: 科也今章疏言列款謂科條列之), a provision.
  3. Funds.
  4. To detain, to keep (Kangxi 款: 留也).
  5. Slowly, tardily (Kangxi 款: 緩也).
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【款】 (khwanX)
    • (adjective-like) void, hollow
    • (adjective-like) true, staunch
    • (verb-like) [to] knock
