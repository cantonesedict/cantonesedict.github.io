專 ꜀Chün (zyun1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼨ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5C08
Alternative forms
  • 剸
  • 耑
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 19 專) 一也獨也擅也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 19 耑) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 37* ꜀專꜀耑)
    • One, single, alone (Kangxi 專: 獨也), undivided;
    • bent on, to turn to one thing (Kangxi 專: 壹也誠也, 純篤也);
    • to take upon one, to presume, to assume (Kangxi 專: 擅也自是也);
    • self willed;
    • ꜀chün ꜀sam (zyun1 sam1 專心), undivided attention;
    • ꜀chün yat꜇ (zyun1 jat6 專日) daily;
    • tsz'꜅ ꜀chün (zi6 zyun1 自專), by one's self, to assume;
    • ꜀chün ꜀tang ꜁lai (zyun1 dang1 lai4 專登嚟), came for that purpose;
    • ꜀chün ꜁loi (zyun1 loi4 專來), only came here;
    • ꜀chün yat꜆ (zyun1 jat1 專一) to apply to one thing;
    • ꜀chün [꜁]fong (zyun1 fong4 專房), to engross a husband's company.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 專房: Corrected ꜀fong (fong1) to ꜁fong (fong4).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Autocratic.
  2. Specially, exclusively.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【專登】 (zyun1 dang1):
    1. Specially.
      • 專登買㗎 (zyun1 dang1 maai5 gaa3): "[I] bought [it] especially!"
    2. Deliberately, on purpose.
      • 專登輸畀佢 (zyun1 dang1 syu1 bei2 keoi5): "Lost to him on purpose."

磚 ꜀Chün (zyun1)

Fan Wan entry

  • (Vol. 2 Image 19 甎) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 37* ꜀磚꜀甎)
    • A brick, a tile (Kangxi 甎: 㼾甎, 甎瓦, 燒墼也, 甓也);
    • to cover with brick;
    • ꜀tsʻing ꜀chün (cing1 zyun1 青磚), a burnt blue brick;
    • ꜁nai ꜀chün (nai4 zyun1 泥磚), adobie bricks;
    • kái ꜀chün (gaai1 zyun1 階磚), red tiles;
    • tái꜅ ꜁nai ꜀chün (daai6 nai4 zyun1 大泥磚), a great dolt, a villain;
    • ꜀chün tí꜅ (zyun1 dei6 磚地) to tile a floor.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【階磚】 (gaai1 zyun1):
    1. Ceramic floor tiles.
    2. Diamonds (card suit).
      • 階磚三 (gaai1 zyun1 saam1): "Three of diamonds."
