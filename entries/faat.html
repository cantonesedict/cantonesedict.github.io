法 Fát꜆ (faat3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 5
Unicode code point
U+6CD5
Alternative forms
  • 灋
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 法) 法度法則又刑法方法
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 [灋]) 古法字
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 51 法꜆)
    • A rule, a law, usage (Kangxi: 法常也, 法偪也偪而使有所限也, etc.);
    • regulations, precepts;
    • a punishment (Kangxi: 𠛬法也) or legal infliction;
    • an art;
    • skill, industry;
    • sect;
    • fát꜆ tò꜅ (faat3 dou6 法度) rule, regulation;
    • ꜁ying fát꜆ (jing4 faat3 刑法) penal laws;
    • ꜁fú fát꜆ (fu4 faat3 符法) to lay a spell;
    • fát꜆ tsak꜆ (faat3 zak1 法則) a guide;
    • fát꜆ ꜁mún (faat3 mun4 法門), the Budhists Dharmic gateway of Budhism;
    • chʻák꜆ fát꜆ (caak3 faat3 拆法) mode of concluding or repairing;
    • ꜀tò fát꜆ (dou1 faat3 刀法) rules for carving.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected malformed headword (with 虫 instead of ⿱灬去) to 灋.
  • Williams' 刀法: Also (single-edged) swordsmanship.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To emulate (Kangxi: 效法也).
  2. A method.
  3. Religious, supernatural, or sorcerous practices.
  4. A divisor.

發 Fát꜆ (faat3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽨ + 7
⼸ + 9
Unicode code point
U+767C
Alternative forms
  • 彂
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 發) 起也[揚]也舒也遣也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 72 彂) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 50 發꜆)
    • To issue, to appear, to send forth, to germinate;
    • to cause to go, to dispatch (Kangxi: 遣也), throw out;
    • to ferment;
    • to show forth (Kangxi: 舒也揚也, 發越也, 宣發也, 見也), to manifest (Kangxi: 明也);
    • the spring and summer (Kangxi: 春夏曰發秋冬曰斂);
    • fát꜆ ꜁tsʻoi (faat3 coi4 發財), to make money;
    • fát꜆ ꜃shí (faat3 si5 發市), to have customers;
    • fát꜆ hák꜆ (faat3 haak3 發客) or fát꜆ mái꜅ (faat3 maai6 發賣) for sale;
    • fát꜆ ꜀sam (faat3 sam1 發心), to give in charity;
    • fát꜆ shai꜅ (faat3 sai6 發誓) to take an oath;
    • fát꜆ ꜀tín (faat3 din1 發癲), crazed, beside himself;
    • ꜃ní fát꜆ ꜀wan ꜀mé? (nei5 faat3 wan1 me1 你發瘟咩) are you crazy?
    • fát꜆ ꜂hí ꜁loi (faat3 hei2 loi4 發起來), to rise (as dough), to succeed, to get on;
    • fát꜆ ꜀fán ꜀ün tsik꜆ (faat3 faan1 jyun1 zik1 發？？？) to dismiss home;
    • fát꜆ ꜀tán (faat3 daan1 發單), to issue advertisements an invoice;
    • fát꜆ ꜁chʻiú (faat3 ciu4 發潮), to get damp;
    • ꜂tá fát꜆ (daa2 faat3 打發) to send away;
    • fát꜆ hí꜄ (faat3 hei3 發氣) angry;
    • fát꜆ ꜀fo (faat3 fo1 發科), to become a küyan (舉人);
    • fát꜆ káp꜆ (faat3 gaap3 發甲) to become a tsunsz' (進士);
    • fát꜆ hò꜅ (faat3 hou6 發號) to make a signal;
    • fát꜆ pʻúi꜄ (faat3 pui3 發配) banished;
    • fát꜆ ꜀fung (faat3 fung1 發瘋), leprous;
    • fát꜆ ꜀ting (faat3 ding1 發丁), to have descendants;
    • fát꜆ ꜂chí (faat3 zi2 發紙), to wet paper.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 楊 to 揚.
  • Williams' 發客: Very dated.
  • Williams' 發丁: Specifically, male descendants.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To shoot (Kangxi: 䠶發也 etc.).
  2. To rise (Kangxi: 起也).
  3. To open (Kangxi: 開也).
  4. To go, to depart (Kangxi: 進也行也, 去也, 謂起屨也).
  5. To raise (Kangxi: 舉也, 興也).
  6. To till, to hoe (Kangxi: 伐也, 以耜擊伐).
