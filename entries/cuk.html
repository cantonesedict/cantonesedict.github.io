慉 Chʻuk꜆ (cuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼼ + 10
Unicode code point
U+6149
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 慉) 亦養也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 36* 慉꜆)
    • To sustain;
    • to encourage;
    • chʻuk꜆ ꜀kiú (cuk1 giu1 慉驕), to excite pride;
    • chʻuk꜆ nò꜅ (cuk1 nou6 慉怒) to stir up anger.

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 搐) 宰制也

  • (Page 36* 搐꜆)
    • To drag along, to pull by force, as one drags an ox;
    • to shake rudely.

束 Chʻuk꜆ (cuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽊ + 3
Unicode code point
U+675F
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 [束]) [束]縛約[束]又[束]脩[束]帶
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 36* 束꜆)
    • To bind or bundle a number of things;
    • to tie up in a sheaf;
    • to coerce, to restrain;
    • a bundle, a sheaf;
    • chʻuk꜆ [pok]꜆ (cuk1 bok3 束縛) to cord up;
    • ꜂kún chʻuk꜆ (gun2 cuk1 管束) to restrain those under one;
    • chʻuk꜆ ꜀sau (cuk1 sau1 束修), a teacher's pay;
    • chʻuk꜆ ꜂shau toi꜅ pai꜅ (cuk1 sau2 doi6 bai6 束手待斃) in the utmost distress, no work.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword 朿 to 束.
  • Williams' 束縛: Changed fok꜆ (fok3) to common reading pok꜆ (bok3).

畜 Chʻuk꜆ (cuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽥ + 5
Unicode code point
U+755C
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 畜) 畜養
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 36* 畜꜆)
    • To rear, to feed, to raise;
    • to domesticate, to herd;
    • to cultivate;
    • to restrain;
    • to gather;
    • to bear with;
    • to obey;
    • domestic animals;
    • chʻuk꜆ ꜀shang (cuk1 sang1 畜牲), you brute!
    • luk꜇ chʻuk꜆ (luk6 cuk1 六畜) the six domestic animals;
    • chʻuk꜆ ꜃yéung (cuk1 joeng5 畜養), to raise cattle.

矗 Chʻuk꜆ (cuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽬ + 19
Unicode code point
U+77D7
Han unification vigilance
May appear with components as the right component of 値 or 值 (assumed here), depending on the font.
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 矗) 聳上也高起也又直也齊也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 36* 矗꜆)
    • High, eminent;
    • equal, even, straight;
    • to raise;
    • luxuriant growth.

Radical + residual strokes
⾋ + 10
Unicode code point

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 蓄) 積也藏也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 36* 蓄꜆)
    • To collect, to accumulate, to hoard;
    • to nourish, to rear;
    • chʻuk꜆ tsik꜆ (cuk1 zik1 蓄積) to collect together;
    • chʻuk꜆ ꜀á ꜁tʻau (cuk1 aa1 tau4 蓄丫頭), to bring up slave girls.
