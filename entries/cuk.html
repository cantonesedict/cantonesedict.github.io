促 Tsʻuk꜆ (cuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 7
Unicode code point
U+4FC3
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 600 促꜆)
    • To constrain;
    • near, short, close;
    • urgent, pressed upon;
    • small, shortened;
    • hampered;
    • kuk꜇ tsʻuk꜆ (guk6 cuk1 侷促) a narrow, small space;
    • tsʻuk꜆ sat꜆ ꜁tʻám ꜀sam (cuk1 sat1 taam4 sam1 促膝談心), to draw together and talk, as long absent friends.

束 Chʻuk꜆ (cuk1)

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 36* 束꜆)
    • To bind or bundle a number of things;
    • to tie up in a sheaf;
    • to coerce, to restrain;
    • a bundle, a sheaf;
    • chʻuk꜆ [pok]꜆ (cuk1 bok3 束縛) to cord up;

    • chʻuk꜆ ꜂shau toi꜅ pai꜅ (cuk1 sau2 doi6 bai6 束手待斃) in the utmost distress, no work.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword 朿 to 束.
  • Williams' 束縛: Changed fok꜆ (fok3) to common reading pok꜆ (bok3).

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 600 𫂙꜆ (⿱𥫗敕); here normalised to 簌)
    • To sift, and cause to shower down;

Fan Wan entry

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 599 蔟꜇簇꜇ Tsuk꜇ (zuk6); here normalised to Tsʻuk꜆ (cuk1))

    These two (蔟, 簇) are used interchangeably.

    • (See 簇 cuk1 for "small-bamboo" and other senses);
    • a nest;
    • a framework or whisk on which silk-worms spin coccoons;
    • (…).

    Also read tsʻau꜄ (cau3) (see 蔟 cau3).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams does not provide separate entries for 簇 and 蔟. I have split things up accordingly.

蹙 Tsʻuk꜆ (cuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾜ + 11
Unicode code point
U+8E59
Alternative forms
  • 顣
Reading variations
  • zuk1 (per 《廣韻》子六切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 蹙) 頻蹙愁貌又急也廹也
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 顣) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 600 蹙꜆)
    • Pressed upon, urged, impelled;
    • cramped, cribbed;
    • embarrassed;
    • to wrinkle, to rumple, to draw in;
    • retractile;
    • disquieted, uneasy, reduced to extremity;
    • pik꜆ tsʻuk꜆ (bik1 cuk1 迫蹙) to press or urge vehemently;
    • tsʻuk꜆ tsʻuk꜆ (cuk1 cuk1 蹙蹙) contracted.
  • (Page 600 顣꜆; here normalised to 蹙)
    • Interchanged with the last (蹙), to frown;
    • to wrinkle the forehead;
    • anxious, careworn.
Conway's commentary
  • Otherwise, an alternative form of 蹴. See 蹴 cuk1 for the canonical entry.

蹴 Tsʻuk꜆ (cuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾜ + 12
Unicode code point
U+8E74
Alternative forms
  • 蹙
  • 蹵
Reading variations
  • zuk1 (alternative per 《廣韻》子六切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 65 蹴) 踐踏也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 600 蹴꜆)

    Similar to the last two (蹙, 顣).

    • To tread on, to press with the foot;
    • to kick;
    • tsʻuk꜆ ꜁ín (cuk1 jin4 蹴然), reverential.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "Similar to the last two": Not quite. It is 蹙 that is used for 蹴.

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 600 速꜆)
    • Hasty, quick, hurried;

    • tsik꜆ tsʻuk꜆ (zik1 cuk1 即速) instantly, presently;

    • ꜂fo tsʻuk꜆ (fo2 cuk1 火速) as quick as possible.
