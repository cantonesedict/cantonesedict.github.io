攉 Fok꜆ (fok3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 16
Unicode code point
U+6509
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 103 攉) 手反覆也搖手曰揮反手曰攉
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 55 攉꜆)
    • To recall one with flip over the hand (Kangxi: 手反覆也);
    • to motion back with the hand (Kangxi: 搖手曰揮反手曰攉);
    • to fan;
    • hí꜄ fok꜆ (hei3 fok3 戲攉) to make a fool of;
    • fok꜆ ꜂shau (fok3 sau2 攉手), to shake the hand.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 攉手: More specifically, to shake about the axis of the arm (in the same way that shaking the head is about the axis of the neck).

攫 Fok꜆ (fok3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 20
Unicode code point
U+652B
Reading variations
  • gwok3 (per 《廣韻》居縛切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 103 攫) [爪]持也[撲]取也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 54 攫꜆)
    • To grasp with the talons or claws (Kangxi: 扟也, 撲取也, 以腳取之謂之攫以翼擊之謂之搏, 一曰持也);
    • fok꜆ ꜂tsʻü (fok3 ceoi2 攫取), to seize by force, to get anyhow.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected incomplete 瓜 to 爪, and fixed missing stroke in 撲.

矍 Fok꜆ (fok3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽬ + 15
Unicode code point
U+77CD
Reading variations
  • gwok3 (per 《廣韻》居縛切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 103 矍) 矍鑠輕徤貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 54 矍꜆)
    • The fluttering of a bird when caught (Kangxi: 隹欲逸走也);
    • looking right and left in fear (Kangxi: 左右驚顧也);
    • to glance the eyes about quickly (Kangxi: 一曰視遽貌, 不專視之容, 目不正也);
    • fok꜆ yéuk꜆ shéuk꜆ (fok3 soek3 矍鑠) active; said of an old man (Kangxi: 矍鑠輕健貌).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 矍鑠: Corrected yéuk꜆ (joek3) to shéuk꜆ (soek3).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A surname (Kangxi: 姓).

藿 Fok꜆ (fok3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾋ + 16
Unicode code point
U+85FF
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 103 藿) 藿香草名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 55 藿꜆)
    • Leaves or young sprouts of pulse (Kangxi: 尗之少也, 藿豆葉), used as food;

躩 Fok꜆ (fok3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾜ + 20
Unicode code point
U+8EA9
Reading variations
  • gwok3 (per 《廣韻》居縛切)
  • kwok3 (per 《廣韻》丘縛切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 103 躩) 盤辟貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 54 躩꜆)
    • A short, quick, stately step, as if going up stairs turning on the spot, deemed respectful to superiors (Kangxi: 足盤辟而爲敬也);
    • fok꜆ pó꜅ pò꜅ (fok3 bou6 躩步) a quick, regular walk (Kangxi: 躩步疾行).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 躩步: Corrected pó꜅ to pò꜅.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To jump (Kangxi: 躩跳也).

霍 Fok꜆ (fok3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾬ + 8
Unicode code point
U+970D
Alternative forms
  • 靃
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 103 霍) 姓
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 55 霍꜆)
    • Speed, celerity, agility (揮霍, 揮霍猝遽也, 揮霍疾貌);
    • a surname (Kangxi: 姓);
    • a range of mountains (Kangxi: 山名 etc.) in Húpeh (湖北), the Atlas of China (Kangxi: 卽天柱山);
    • fok꜆ lün꜅ ching꜄ (fok3 lyun6 zing3 霍亂症) the cholera or bilious colic;
    • fok꜆ ꜁ín (fok3 jin4 霍然), quick, like flying clouds.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "in Húpeh": This is dubious. Kangxi has an extended note discussing whether there are multiple mountains called 霍. Of the candidates, 衡山 is in 湖南, while 天柱山 is in 安徽.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To dissipate, to disperse (Kangxi: 霍焉猶渙焉也).
