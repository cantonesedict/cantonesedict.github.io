  • (Vol. 1 Image 75 戚) 親戚憂戚又戚戚心動貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 570 戚꜆)
    • A sort of pole-ax (Kangxi: 戉類, 戚斧也);
    • to compassionate, to pity;
    • mournful, sorry (Kangxi: 哀也);
    • angry (Kangxi: 憤也), aroused, vexed (Kangxi: 憂也, 惱也);
    • deformed, marked in any way (Kangxi: 醜疾人曰戚施);
    • near, related to, dear to (Kangxi: 戚近也);
    • relatives (Kangxi: 親也);
    • ꜀tsʻan tsʻik꜆ (can1 cik1 親戚) one's kindred;
    • ꜀yau tsʻik꜆ ꜀séung ꜀kwán (jau1 cik1 soeng1 gwaan1 休戚相關), to feel for other's woes, mutually effected;
    • tsʻik꜆ tsʻik꜆ (cik1 cik1 戚戚) melancholy.
  • (Page 571 慼꜆)

    (…)

    Used for the preceding (戚) (for the "grief" sense) (Kangxi 慼: 又通作戚).

敕 Chʻik꜆ (cik1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽁ + 7
Unicode code point
U+6555
Alternative forms
  • 勅
  • 勑
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 74 勅) 天子制書曰勅命
  • (Vol. 1 Image 74 勑) 同上
  • (Vol. 1 Image 74 敕) 同上又戒謹
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 14* 勅꜆敕꜆)
    • An order, a prohibition or command from the emperor;
    • an ordinance, a special precept (Kangxi: [誡]也);
    • a charter, a commission;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜀shü (cik1 syu1 敕書), credentials; letters-patent;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜀fung (cik1 fung1 敕封), a special perferment;
    • chʻik꜆ ming꜅ (cik1 ming6 敕命) an imperial order;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜁fú (cik1 fu4 敕符) to make a charm efficacious by spurting water on it;
    • chʻik꜆ shau꜅ (cik1 sau6 敕受) to receive commands;
    • chʻik꜆ tsang꜅ (cik1 zang6 敕贈) to bestow honors on one's dead parents.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【得敕】 (dak1 cik1):
    1. Smug.

斥 Chʻik꜆ (cik1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽄ + 1
Unicode code point
U+65A5
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 74 斥) 開拒也又斥逐擯斥又斥候又斥鹵鹹地曰斥鹵
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 14* 斥꜆)
    • To exclude, to expel, to send off, to drive off (Kangxi: 逐也遠也);
    • to scold;
    • to pry into (Kangxi: 斥度也𠋫視也望也);
    • reaching far, extending to, extensive (Kangxi: 斥廣也), plenty (Kangxi: […]言其多);
    • chʻik꜆ chuk꜇ (cik1 zuk6 斥逐) to order away;
    • mín꜅ chʻik꜆ (min6 cik1 面斥) to reprimand, to speak rudely to;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜃lò (cik1 lou5 斥鹵), to let salt-water into the pans;
    • ꜂chí chʻik꜆ (zi2 cik1 指斥) to point out faults;
    • chʻik꜆ fong꜄ (cik1 fong3 斥放) to dismiss and banish;
    • chʻik꜆ chʻut꜆ (cik1 ceot1 斥出) to remove, to expel;
    • chʻik꜆ pák꜇ ꜃kʻü (cik1 baak6 keoi5 斥白佢), rate him plainly;
    • chʻik꜆ fát꜆ (cik1 faat3 斥法) to juggle.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "to juggle": In the (now) archaic sense of performing magical trickery.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Salty earth (Kangxi: 斥謂地鹹).
  2. To open up (Kangxi: 斥開也).
  3. To allocate or spend.

赤 Chʻik꜆ (cik3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾚ + 0
Unicode code point
U+8D64
Reading variations
  • cek3 (alternative)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 74 赤) 赤色
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 14* 赤꜆)
    • Carnation, purplish or light red (Kangxi: 朱色也);
    • flesh color (Kangxi: […]肉色也);
    • naked, destitute, barren, poor (Kangxi: 空盡無物曰赤);
    • to redden;
    • to denude (Kangxi: 裸裎曰赤體見肉色也);
    • the 155th radical ("red") of characters referring to red;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜂tám (cik3 daam2 赤膽), pureminded;
    • chʻik꜆ tò꜅ (cik3 dou6 赤道) the equator;
    • chʻik꜆ yat꜇ (cik3 jat6 赤日) full day; heat of the day;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜃mai (cik3 mai5 赤米), red rice;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜂tsz' (cik3 zi2 赤子) an infant; we, the people;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜀shan (cik3 san1 赤身), naked;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜀sam (cik3 sam1 赤心), guileless, sincere;
    • chʻik꜆ pák꜇ ꜁nán ꜀fan (cik3 baak6 naan4 fan1 赤白難分), hard to distinguish;
    • ꜂tá chʻik꜆ kéuk꜆ (daa2 cik3 goek3 打赤腳) to bare the feet;
    • chʻik꜆ tí꜅ (cik3 dei6 赤地) barren wastes.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 𤷫. See 𤷫 cek3 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Ardent, loyal.
