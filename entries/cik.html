Chʻik꜆ Tsʻik꜆ (cik3 低陰入)

  • 刺
  • 𤷫
  • 赤

(Page 15*, before the lone entry for chʻik꜇ (cik6))
(The next character (尺) is usually pronounced chʻek.)
(Here, Williams has been misled by Fan Wan's placement of 尺 (chʻik) in its own series after 劇屐 (kʻik꜇) and 溺 (nik꜇), which are 陽入; see Vol. 1 Image 78. Based on this ordering, Williams has inferred that the character 尺 is read 陽入 chʻik꜇ (cik6). However, since Fan Wan has a separate series for 勅勑敕斥叱螫𪅙赤 (chʻik꜆), it appears that its authors have attempted to distinguish the vernacular rime ek from the literary rime ik, notwithstanding all series appear under 第七英影應益. I have corrected Williams by placing 尺 under cek3.)

赤 Chʻik꜆ (cik3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾚ + 0
Unicode code point
U+8D64
Reading variations
  • cek3 (alternative)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 74 赤) 赤色
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 14* 赤꜆)
    • Carnation, purplish or light red (Kangxi: 朱色也);
    • flesh color (Kangxi: […]肉色也);
    • naked, destitute, barren, poor (Kangxi: 空盡無物曰赤);
    • to redden;
    • to denude (Kangxi: 裸裎曰赤體見肉色也);
    • the 155th radical of characters referring to red;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜂tám (cik3 daam2 赤膽), pureminded;
    • chʻik꜆ tò꜅ (cik3 dou6 赤道) the equator;
    • chʻik꜆ yat꜇ (cik3 jat6 赤日) full day; heat of the day;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜃mai (cik3 mai5 赤米), red rice;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜂tsz' (cik3 zi2 赤子) an infant; we, the people;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜀shan (cik3 san1 赤身), naked;
    • chʻik꜆ ꜀sam (cik3 sam1 赤心), guileless, sincere;
    • chʻik꜆ pák꜇ ꜁nán ꜀fan (cik3 baak6 naan4 fan1 赤白難分), hard to distinguish;
    • ꜂tá chʻik꜆ kéuk꜆ (daa2 cik3 goek3 打赤腳) to bare the feet;
    • chʻik꜆ tí꜅ (cik3 dei6 赤地) barren wastes.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 𤷫. See 𤷫 cek3 for the canonical entry.)
