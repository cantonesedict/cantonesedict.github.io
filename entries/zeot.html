怵 ꜀Chut (zeot1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼼ + 5
Unicode code point
U+6035
Reading variations
  • ceot1 (per 《廣韻》丑律切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 55 怵) 怵惕恐懼悽愴
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 41 怵꜆)
    • Fear, terror (Kangxi: 恐也);
    • to dread;
    • (…);
    • chut꜆ [tʻik]꜆ (zeot1 tik1 怵惕) timorous.

    Also pronounced tut (deot).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 怵惕: Corrected tik꜆ (dik1) to tʻik꜆ (tik1).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Pitiful, sorrowful (Kangxi: 悽愴也).

黜 ꜀Chut (zeot1)

Radical + residual strokes
⿊ + 5
Unicode code point
U+9EDC
Alternative forms
  • 絀
Reading variations
  • ceot1 (per 《廣韻》丑律切)
  • zyut3 (common)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 55 黜) 諄入聲貶斥也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 55 絀) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 41 黜꜆)
    • To blame;
    • to degrade, to dismiss, to put out of office (Kangxi: 貶下也, 退也貶也下也去也放絕也減也);
    • to expel;
    • chut꜆ tʻúi꜄ (zeot1 teoi3 黜退) to cashier;
    • sám[꜄] chut꜆ (saam3 zeot1 三黜) a third reprimand;
    • yuk꜇ chut꜆ (juk6 zeot1 辱黜) degraded and ashamed.
  • (Page 41 絀꜆; here normalised to 黜)
    • (…);
    • used for the preceding (黜) (Kangxi 絀: 同黜).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 三黜: Corrected ꜀sám (saam1) to sám꜄ (saam3) per 《論語集注》三去聲. Not a third reprimand, but thrice dismissed.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To reject.
  2. To abolish.
  3. To rebuke.
