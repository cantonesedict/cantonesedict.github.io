  • (PageВ 609 еҚ’кңҶ)
    • Those who execute orders (Kangxi: йҡёдәәзөҰдәӢиҖ…);
    • lictors, soldiers, underlings, menials;
    • to conclude, to finish (Kangxi: зӣЎд№ҹ, зөӮзӣЎд№ҹ);
    • to die, to come to an end (Kangxi: еӨ§еӨ«жӯ»жӣ°еҚ’);

    • pengкң… tsutкңҶ (beng6 zeot1 з—…еҚ’) disease is mortal died of disease;
    • tsutкңҶ кңҒwai shГӯnкң… sz'кң… (zeot1 wai4 sin6 si6 еҚ’зӮәе–„еЈ«) to the last he was a good man.

    • (noun-like) troops

  • (Vol.В 2 ImageВ 55 зөҖ) еҗҢдёҠ
Williams (1856) entry
  • (PageВ 41 й»ңкңҶ)
    • To blame;
    • to degrade, to dismiss, to put out of office (Kangxi: иІ¶дёӢд№ҹ, йҖҖд№ҹиІ¶д№ҹдёӢд№ҹеҺ»д№ҹж”ҫзө•д№ҹжёӣд№ҹ);
    • to expel;
    • chutкңҶ tК»Гәiкң„ (zeot1 teoi3 й»ңйҖҖ) to cashier;
    • кңҖsГЎm sГЎmкң„ chutкңҶ (saam3 zeot1 дёүй»ң) a third reprimand thrice dismissed;
    • yukкңҮ chutкңҶ (juk6 zeot1 иҫұй»ң) degraded and ashamed.
  • (PageВ 41 зөҖкңҶ; here normalised to й»ң)
    • (вҖҰ);
    • used for the preceding (й»ң) (Kangxi зөҖ: еҗҢй»ң).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' дёүй»ң: Corrected кңҖsГЎm (saam1) to sГЎmкң„ (saam3) per гҖҠи«–иӘһйӣҶжіЁгҖӢдёүеҺ»иҒІ. Here, й»ң refers to dismissal, not a reprimand.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To reject.
  2. To abolish.
  3. To rebuke.
Conway's literary rendering
  • гҖҗй»ңгҖ‘ (trhwit)
    • (verb-like) [to] expel, [to] reject, [to] dismiss, [to] degrade
