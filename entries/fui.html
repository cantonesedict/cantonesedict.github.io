恢 ꜀Fúi (fui1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼼ + 6
Unicode code point
U+6062
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 80 恢) 大也又恢復
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 62 ꜀恢)
    • Great (Kangxi: 大也);
    • liberal;
    • extensive;
    • to enlarge (Kangxi: 大之也);
    • ꜀fúi fuk꜇ (fui1 fuk6 恢復) to regain possession of.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Complete.
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【恢】 (khwoj)
    • (verb-like) [to] extend, [to] vasten
    • (adjective-like) extensive, vast
    • (adjective-like) complete

灰 ꜀Fúi (fui1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽕ + 2
Unicode code point
U+7070
Han unification vigilance
May appear as either ⿸𠂇火 (assumed here) or ⿸厂火, depending on the font.
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 80 灰) 火灰石灰
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 62 ꜀灰)
    • Ashes (Kangxi: 死火餘㶳也, 火之滅者爲灰), ashy;
    • lime;
    • dust;
    • to plaster;
    • ꜂fo ꜀fúi (fo2 fui1 火灰), wood-ashes;
    • tʻán꜄ ꜀fúi (taan3 fui1 炭灰), coal-ash;
    • shek꜇ ꜀fúi (sek6 fui1 石灰), stone lime;
    • ꜀pan ꜁long ꜀fúi (ban1 long4 fui1 檳榔灰), betel lime;
    • ꜀fúi ꜂tsun (fui1 zeon2 灰燼), embers;
    • ꜁ngá ꜀fúi (ngaa4 fui1 牙灰), tooth-powder;
    • ꜀fúi ꜁tsʻéung (fui1 coeng4 灰牆), to plaster a wall.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams prints the headword as ⿸厂火, which is compatibility ideograph U+2F835.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Charcoal.
  2. Grey.
  3. Disheartened.
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【灰】 (xwoj)
    • (noun-like) ash
    • (noun-like) charcoal

魁 ꜀Fúi (fui1)

Radical + residual strokes
⿁ + 4
⽃ + 10
Unicode code point
U+9B41
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 80 魁) 凡爲首者皆曰魁
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 62 ꜀魁)
    • A star (Kangxi: 星名);
    • the head, chief (Kangxi: 魁帥也, 魁猶首也);
    • the highest or first of a class;
    • monstrous great (Kangxi: 大也);
    • ꜁ün ꜀fúi (jyun4 fui1 元魁), the chief;
    • ꜀fúi ꜂shau (fui1 sau2 魁首), the first küjin (舉人) graduate;
    • á꜄ ꜀fúi (aa3 fui1 亞魁), the second;
    • ꜀fúi ꜀sing (fui1 sing1 魁星), the north star, the attendant of the god of Literature;
    • ꜁man ꜀fúi (man4 fui1 文魁), the sign over the door of a küjin (舉人) graduate.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "A star": Specifically, the first (or first four) of the Big Dipper (Kangxi: 魁北斗第一星也, 春秋緯曰瑤光第一至第四爲魁).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A (small) mound (Kangxi: 小阜).
  2. A large ladle or dipper.
  3. A type of mollusc (Kangxi: 蜃蛤也).
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【魁】 (khwoj)
    • (noun-like) dipper, dipper-[mollusc]
    • (noun-like) mound
    • (adjective-like) chief
    • (adjective-like) great

悔 Fúi꜄ (fui3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼼ + 7
Unicode code point
U+6094
Han unification vigilance
May appear as either ⿰忄每 (+7 assumed here) or ⿰忄毎 (+6), depending on the font.
Alternative forms
  • 𠧩
Reading variations
  • fui2 (per 《廣韻》呼罪切 for "regret" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 82 悔) 心恨也又反悔
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 63 悔꜄)
    • To repent, to change (Kangxi: 悔恨也, 改也恨也);
    • to regret (Kangxi: 悔吝也), vexed with one's self;
    • to explain the diagrams;
    • fúi꜄ han꜅ (fui3 han6 悔恨) remorse, contrition;
    • fúi꜄ tsúi꜅ (fui3 zeoi6 悔罪) to repent of sin;
    • fúi꜄ ꜂koi (fui3 goi2 悔改), to reform;
    • ꜁mò fúi꜄ (mou4 fui3 無悔) stubborn;
    • fúi꜄ ꜀sam (fui3 sam1 悔心), compunction.
Conway's commentary
  • The form ⿰忄毎 is compatibility ideograph U+2F8A3.
  • Williams' "to explain the diagrams": This is a misreading of Kangxi 外卦曰𠧩 under 𠧩. Here, 外 means "outer", not "explain".
  • Williams' 無悔: Or "without regret".
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. The outer (i.e. upper) trigram of a hexagram (Kangxi 𠧩: 外卦曰𠧩內卦曰貞).
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【悔】 (xwojX)
    • (verb-like) [to] regret
  • 【悔】 (xwojH)
    • (verb-like) [to] repent
    • (noun-like) outer-trigram

晦 Fúi꜄ (fui3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽇ + 7
Unicode code point
U+6666
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 82 晦) 不明也又月[盡]謂之晦
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 63 晦꜄)
    • Obscure (Kangxi: 晦昧也), dark (Kangxi: 晦昏也);
    • night (Kangxi: 晦夜也), the close of light (Kangxi: 晦晏也);
    • the last day of the month (Kangxi: 月盡也 etc.);
    • misty (Kangxi: 霧謂之晦), dim (Kangxi: 晦者何冥也);
    • fúi꜄ hí꜄ (fui3 hei3 晦氣) lugubrious;
    • fúi꜄ hí꜄ ꜀tau (fui3 hei3 dau1 晦氣兜), how unlucky!
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Fixed missing stroke in 盡.
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【晦】 (xwojH)
    • (noun-like) month's-end
    • (adjective-like) dark, obscure
