織 Chik꜆ (zik1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽷ + 12
Unicode code point
U+7E54
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 76 織) 扣結布帛之總名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 13* 織꜆)
    • To weave;
    • woven;
    • chik꜆ ꜁ü (zik1 jyu4 織餘), a thrum left in the web;
    • tsʻuk꜆ chik꜆ (cuk1 zik1 促織) a cricket;
    • chik꜆ ꜀kí (zik1 gei1 織機), a loom;
    • chik꜆ ꜃nü (zik1 neoi5-2 織女), the star Wega α in Lyra;
    • chik꜆ pò[꜄] (zik1 bou3 織布) to weave cloth.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 織布: Corrected pò꜅ (bou6) to pò꜄ (bou3).

職 Chik꜆ (zik1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽿ + 12
Unicode code point
U+8077
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 76 職) 官職品秩也職任職掌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 13* 職꜆)
    • To govern, to rule;
    • to attend to;
    • to record;
    • to act officially;
    • having the direction of;
    • official duty;
    • title, office;
    • tribute, offerings or presents of other states;
    • chik꜆ ꜂shau (zik1 sau2 職守), to direct;
    • chik꜆ yam꜅ (zik1 jam6 職任) in office, the duties of an office;
    • ꜀ká chik꜆ (gaa1 zik1 加職) to confer a title;
    • ꜀pí chik꜆ (bei1 zik1 卑職) my office;
    • chik꜆ ꜁ün (zik1 jyun4 職員), honorary title;
    • ꜀kún chik꜆ (gun1 zik1 官職) official title;
    • chik꜆ ꜂chéung (zik1 zoeng2 職掌), to superintend;
    • kák꜆ chik꜆ (gaak3 zik1 革職) to deprive of a title;
    • lok꜇ chik꜆ (lok6 zik1 落職) to deprive of office;
    • shau꜅ chik꜆ (sau6 zik1 受職) to hold an office;
    • chik꜆ chik꜆ (zik1 zik1 職職) many;
    • chik꜆ fan꜅ (zik1 fan6 職分) a title, official duties.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 職員: Now simply "employee".

陟 Chik꜆ (zik1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾩ + 7
Unicode code point
U+965F
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 76 [陟]) 我也進也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 13* 陟꜆)
    • To ascend, to enter on a higher office;
    • promoted, advanced;
    • to mount, to go up;
    • to advance, to proceed to;
    • chik꜆ ꜁lam (zik1 lam4 陟臨), to behold from on high, as God does;
    • chik꜆ kong꜄ (zik1 gong3 陟降) promotion and degradation.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised headword ⿰阝歩 to 陟. The explanation 我也 is erroneous.
