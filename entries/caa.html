The spelling жҗҪ is arbitrary (but attested in Eitel (1877)).

Wiktionary as of 2026-01-01 suggests дөҰ as the original character, but I am not convinced of this. While the classical meaning (to dirty) is reasonable, the reading per гҖҠе»Јйҹ»гҖӢеҫҗйҮҺеҲҮ is кңғtsК»ГЎ, not кңғchК»ГЎ. The second is required if it is cognate with level-tone жҗҪВ caa4 (which I believe to be the case).
