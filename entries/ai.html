Williams (1856) entry
  • (PageВ 2 кңҖр««ғ; here normalised to е“Һ)
    • A particle of surprise or pain;
    • to beg, to ask;
    • кңҖai кңҖyГЎ, (ai1 jaa1 е“Һеҗ”) whew! halloo! oh, dear!
    • кңҖai, matкңҶ кңғnГӯ кңӮkГІm tsГІкң… (ai1 mat1 nei5 gam2 zou6 е“Һд№ңдҪ еҷүеҒҡ) oh! why did you do so?
    • кңҖai кңҖkК»au кңҒkК»au кңғnГӯ (ai1 kau4 nei5 е“ҺжұӮдҪ ), I intreat you.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' е“ҺжұӮдҪ : Corrected кңҖkК»au (kau1) to кңҒkК»au (kau4).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • гҖҗе“Һеҗ”гҖ‘ (ai1 ja4)
    1. An interjection of pain, mistake, etc.
      • е“Һеҗ” (ai1 ja4): "Ouch", "Whoops", etc.
  • гҖҗе“Һеҗ”гҖ‘ (ai1 jaa1)
    1. An interjection of surprise, shock, indignation, etc.
      • е“Һеҗ” (ai1 jaa1): "Oh dear!", "How dare [you]!", etc.
    2. So-called, or nominal.
      • е“Һеҗ”з¶“зҗҶ (ai1 jaa1 ging1 lei5): "So-called manager."

The spelling е“Һеҗ” is arbitrary.

Williams (1856) entry
  • (PageВ 2 зҝікң„)
    • A feather fan or screen, a flabellum (Kangxi: иҸҜи“Ӣд№ҹ);
    • to screen, to intercept (Kangxi: зҝізҢ¶еұӣд№ҹ);
    • to seclude from observation (Kangxi: зҝійҡңд№ҹ);
    • to keep close, to repress (Kangxi: йҡұд№ҹи”Ҫд№ҹ, д•ғд№ҹ);
    • to destroy (Kangxi: дёҖжӣ°ж»…д№ҹ);
    • anything that intercepts;
    • an obscurity in the eyes;
    • trees dying dead and not standing (Kangxi: жңЁз«Ӣжӯ»жӣ°и‘ҳиҮӘжӯ»зҲІзҝі);
    • a fabulous sort of pheasant (Kangxi: йіҘеҗҚд№ҹдјјйіі).

    A colloquial word.

    • Feverish;
    • sultry, hot;
    • hurried breathing, a stricture in the breast;
    • aiкң„ hГӯкң„ (ai3 hei3 зҝіж°Ј) difficulty of breathing;
    • aiкң„ ГӯtкңҮ (ai3 jit6 зҝізҶұ) hot weather;
    • кңҖshan кңӮtsz' aiкң„ ГӯtкңҮ (san1 zi2 ai3 jit6 иә«еӯҗзҝізҶұ) feverish;
    • aiкң„ faiкң„ (ai3 fai3 зҝіиӮә) to feel grieved;
    • aiкң„ chaiкң… (ai3 zai6 зҝіж»Ҝ) indigestible.
Conway's literary rendering
  • гҖҗзҝігҖ‘ ('ejH, 'ej)
    • (noun-like) screen
    • (verb-like) [to] screen, [to] cover (up)
    • (noun-like) fallen-tree
