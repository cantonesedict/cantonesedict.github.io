Ai. (ai)

꜀Ai. (ai1 陰平)

哎 (U+54CE) ꜀ai (ai1)

Alternative forms
𫫃 = ⿰口挨; (唉)
Reading variations
(aai1: longer syllable for particle sense)
Williams (1856) entry
(Page 2: entry for 𫫃 = ⿰口挨)
  • A particle of surprise or pain;
  • to beg, to ask;
  • ꜀ai ꜀yá, (ai1 jaa1 哎吔) whew! halloo! oh, dear!
  • ꜀ai, mat꜆ ꜃ní ꜂kòm tsò꜅ (ai1 mat1 nei5 gam2 zou6 哎乜你噉做) oh! why did you do so?
  • ꜀ai [꜁kʻau] ꜃ní (ai1 kau4 nei5 哎求你), I intreat you.
Conway's remarks
  • Williams has U+2BAC3 𫫃 = ⿰口挨, which has poor font support. I have grafted its entry to 哎, which does not appear in Williams.
  • 哎求你: Corrected ꜀kʻau (kau1) to ꜁kʻau (kau4).
  • Jyutping suggests using 哎 as the canonical character for ai1 or aai1.

꜂Ai. (ai2 陰上)

矮 (U+77EE) ꜂ai (ai2)

Williams (1856) entry
(Page 2)
  • Low;
  • diminutive, short;
  • ꜂ai ꜃lò ꜂tsai (ai2 lou5 zai2 矮老仔), a pigmy, a little old man;
  • ꜂pí ꜀chéung ꜂ai tang꜄ ꜂tsai ꜃ní [꜃tsʻo] (bei2 zoeng1 ai2 dang3 zai2 nei5 co5 畀張矮凳仔你坐) get a low stool for you to sit on—to delude one;
  • ꜂ai tak꜆ tsai꜅ (ai2 dak1 zai6 矮得嚌) too low.
Conway's remarks
  • 畀張矮凳仔你坐: Corrected tso꜅ (zo6 去聲) to ꜃tsʻo (co5 上聲).

Ai꜄. (ai3 陰去)

屭 (U+5C6D) ai꜄ (ai3)

Reading variations
(hei3: for literary senses)
Williams (1856) entry
(Page 2)
  • Strong, herculean, athletic.
A colloquial word;
  • grief, sorrow;
  • pai꜄ ai꜄ (bai3 ai3 贔屭) trouble, resulting from affliction or poverty.
Conway's remarks
  • The expected Cantonese reflex is hei3 from the olden rime books (which have 虛器切 and 許意切).
See also
  • hei#屭3

曀 (U+66C0) ai꜄ (ai3)

Williams (1856) entry
(Page 2)
  • Cloudy and windy;
  • the sun obscured and nearly hid by the clouds.

縊 (U+7E0A) ai꜄ (ai3)

Williams (1856) entry
(Page 2)
  • To hang, to strangle one's self;
  • the warp of cloth;
  • ai꜄ ꜂sz' ꜂kwai (ai3 sei2 gwai2 縊死鬼), ghost of a suicide;
  • ai꜄ ꜂keng (ai3 geng2 縊頸), hung by the neck.

翳 (U+7FF3) ai꜄ (ai3)

Williams (1856) entry
(Page 2)
  • A feather fan or screen, a flabellum;
  • to screen, to intercept;
  • to seclude from observation;
  • to keep close, to repress;
  • to destroy;
  • anything that intercepts;
  • an obscurity in the eyes;
  • trees dying;
  • a fabulous sort of pheasant.
A colloquial word.
  • Feverish;
  • sultry, hot;
  • hurried breathing, a stricture in the breast;
  • ai꜄ hí꜄ (ai3 hei3 翳氣) difficulty of breathing;
  • ai꜄ ít꜇ (ai3 jit6 翳熱) hot weather;
  • ꜀shan ꜂tsz' ai꜄ ít꜇ (san1 zi2 ai3 jit6 身子翳熱) feverish;
  • ai꜄ fai꜄ (ai3 fai3 翳肺) to feel grieved;
  • ai꜄ chai꜅ (ai3 zai6 翳滯) indigestible.

𦠉 = ⿰⺼壹 (U+26809) ai꜄ (ai3)

Williams (1856) entry
(Page 2)
  • Lean, cadaverous, meager, poor.
