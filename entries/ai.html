Ai (ai)

꜀Ai (陰平; ai1)

哎 (U+54CE) ꜀ai (ai1)

Alternative forms
𫫃 = ⿰口挨; (唉)
Reading variations
(aai1: longer syllable for particle sense)
Williams (1856) entry
(Page 2: entry for 𫫃 = ⿰口挨)
  • A particle of surprise or pain;
  • to beg, to ask;
  • ꜀ai ꜀yá, (哎吔; ai1 yaa1) whew! halloo! oh, dear!
  • ꜀ai, mat꜆ ꜃ní ꜂kòm tsò꜅ (哎、乜你噉做; ai1, mat1 nei5 gam2 zou6) oh! why did you do so?
  • ꜀ai [꜁kʻau] ꜃ní (哎求你; ai1 kau4 nei5), I intreat you.
Conway's remarks
  • Williams has U+2BAC3 𫫃 = ⿰口挨, which has poor font support. I have grafted its entry to 哎, which does not appear in Williams.
  • Corrected ꜀kʻau (kau1) to ꜁kʻau (kau4) in the "I intreat you" example.
  • Jyutping suggests using 哎 as the canonical character for ai1 or aai1.

꜂Ai (陰上; ai2)

矮 (U+77EE) ꜂ai (ai2)

Williams (1856) entry
(Page 2)
  • Low;
  • diminutive, short;
  • ꜂ai ꜃lò ꜂tsai (矮老仔; ai2 lou5 zai2), a pigmy, a little old man;
  • ꜂pí ꜀chéung ꜂ai tang꜄ ꜂tsai ꜃ní [꜃tsʻo] (畀張矮凳仔你坐; bei2 zoeng1 ai2 dang3 zai2 nei5 co5) get a low stool for you to sit on—to delude one;
  • ꜂ai tak꜆ tsai꜅ (矮得嚌; ai2 dak1 zai6) too low.
Conway's remarks
  • Corrected tso꜅ (去聲; zo6) to ꜃tsʻo (上聲; co5) in the "low stool" example.
