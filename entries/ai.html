哎 ꜀Ai (ai1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 6
Unicode code point
U+54CE
Alternative forms
  • 𫫃
  • 唉
Reading variations
  • ngai1 (common for "beg" sense)
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 2 ꜀𫫃; here normalised to 哎)
    • A particle of surprise or pain;
    • to beg, to ask;
    • ꜀ai ꜀yá, (ai1 jaa1 哎吔) whew! halloo! oh, dear!
    • ꜀ai, mat꜆ ꜃ní ꜂kòm tsò꜅ (ai1 mat1 nei5 gam2 zou6 哎乜你噉做) oh! why did you do so?
    • ꜀ai ꜀kʻau ꜁kʻau ꜃ní (ai1 kau4 nei5 哎求你), I intreat you.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 哎求你: Corrected ꜀kʻau (kau1) to ꜁kʻau (kau4).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【哎吔】 (ai1 ja4):
    1. An interjection of pain, mistake, etc.
      • 哎吔 (ai1 ja4): "Ouch", "Whoops", etc.
  • 【哎吔】 (ai1 jaa1):
    1. An interjection of surprise, shock, indignation, etc.
      • 哎吔 (ai1 jaa1): "Oh dear!", "How dare [you]!", etc.
    2. So-called, or nominal.
      • 哎吔經理 (ai1 jaa1 ging1 lei5): "So-called manager."

꜂Ai (ai2 陰上)

矮 ꜂Ai (ai2)

Radical + residual strokes
⽮ + 8
Unicode code point
U+77EE
Reading variations
  • ngai2 (hypercorrected initial)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 31 矮) 不高也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 2 ꜂矮)
    • Low;
    • diminutive, short (Kangxi: 短人也, 矬也);
    • ꜂ai ꜃lò ꜂lò ꜂tsai (ai2 lou2 zai2 矮佬仔), a pigmy, a little old man;
    • ꜂pí ꜀chéung ꜂ai tang꜄ ꜂tsai ꜃ní tso꜅ ꜃tsʻo (bei2 zoeng1 ai2 dang3 zai2 nei5 co5 畀張矮凳仔你坐) get a low stool for you to sit on—to delude one;
    • ꜂ai tak꜆ tsai꜅ (ai2 dak1 zai6 矮得嚌) too low.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 矮佬仔: Corrected ꜃lò (lou5) to ꜂lò (lou2).
  • Williams' 畀張矮凳仔你坐: Corrected tso꜅ (zo6 去聲) to ꜃tsʻo (co5 上聲).

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 32 縊) 懸樑自縊也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 2 縊꜄)
    • To hang, to strangle one's self (Kangxi: 經也, 懸繩曰縊);
    • the warp of cloth;
    • ai꜄ ꜂sz' ꜂kwai (ai3 sei2 gwai2 縊死鬼), ghost of a suicide;

  • Williams' "the warp of cloth": This is a misreading of Kangxi 經也.

翳 Ai꜄ (ai3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽻ + 11
Unicode code point
U+7FF3
Reading variations
  • ngai3 (hypercorrected initial)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 32 翳) 障翳
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 2 翳꜄)
    • A feather fan or screen, a flabellum (Kangxi: 華蓋也);
    • to screen, to intercept (Kangxi: 翳猶屛也);
    • to seclude from observation (Kangxi: 翳障也);
    • to keep close, to repress (Kangxi: 隱也蔽也, 䕃也);
    • to destroy (Kangxi: 一曰滅也);
    • anything that intercepts;
    • an obscurity in the eyes;
    • trees dying (Kangxi: 木立死曰葘自死爲翳);
    • a fabulous sort of pheasant (Kangxi: 鳥名也似鳳).

    A colloquial word.

    • Feverish;
    • sultry, hot;
    • hurried breathing, a stricture in the breast;
    • ai꜄ hí꜄ (ai3 hei3 翳氣) difficulty of breathing;
    • ai꜄ ít꜇ (ai3 jit6 翳熱) hot weather;
    • ꜀shan ꜂tsz' ai꜄ ít꜇ (san1 zi2 ai3 jit6 身子翳熱) feverish;
    • ai꜄ fai꜄ (ai3 fai3 翳肺) to feel grieved;
    • ai꜄ chai꜅ (ai3 zai6 翳滯) indigestible.
