啍 ꜀Chun (zeon1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 8
Unicode code point
U+554D
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 52 啍) 啍啍重語貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 36* ꜀啍)
    • Garrulous;
    • ꜀chun ꜀chun (zeon1 zeon1 啍啍), to reiterate, to say over and over.

    Read ꜀[tʻun] (teon1); (see 啍 teon1).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' alternative reading: Corrected ꜀tun (deon1) to ꜀tʻun (teon1).

准 ꜂Chun (zeon2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼎ + 8
Unicode code point
U+51C6
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 53 准) 信也實也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 36* ꜂准)
    • To permit, to approve;
    • to allow, to decide;
    • to receive;
    • on, upon, as a day;
    • ꜂chun chong꜅ (zeon2 zong6 准狀) grant the petition;
    • (see 準 zeon2 for "prepare" sense);
    • ꜂chun ꜁hang (zeon2 hang4 准行), allowed to be done;
    • ꜂chun sun꜄ (zeon2 seon3 准信) to believe;
    • ꜂chun ꜃í shap꜇ yat꜇ (zeon2 ji5 sap6 jat6 准以十日) permission for ten days;
    • ꜂chun shat꜇ kòm꜄ ꜀to (zeon2 sat6 gam3 do1 准實咁多), worth just that.

    Used for the following (凖).

  • (Page 36* ꜂凖; here normalised to 準)

    • ꜂chun ꜁'m ꜂chun (zeon2 m4 zeon2 準唔準), is it exact?
