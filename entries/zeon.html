啍 ꜀Chun (zeon1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 8
Unicode code point
U+554D
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 52 啍) 啍啍重語貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 36* ꜀啍)
    • Garrulous;
    • ꜀chun ꜀chun (zeon1 zeon1 啍啍), to reiterate, to say over and over.

    Read ꜀[tʻun] (teon1); (see 啍 teon1).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' alternative reading: Corrected ꜀tun (deon1) to ꜀tʻun (teon1).

樽 ꜀Tsun (zeon1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽊ + 12
Unicode code point
U+6A3D
Alternative forms
  • 尊
  • 罇
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 51 樽) 酒樽
  • (Vol. 2 Image 51 鐏) 同上
  • (Vol. 2 Image 51 [罇]) 同上
  • (Vol. 2 Image 51 尊) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 600 ꜀樽꜀罇꜀尊)
    • A vase for libations;
    • a vase for wine;
    • a bottle, a decanter;
    • a vase, a jar;
    • full, flourishing foliage;
    • to stop;
    • yat꜆ ꜀tsun ꜂tsau (jat1 zeon1 zau2 一樽酒), a bottle of spirits;
    • ꜀fá ꜀tsun (faa1 zeon1 花樽), a flower-vase;
    • ꜀hoi ꜀tsun (hoi1 zeon1 開樽), open the bottle.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised ⿰𦈢尊 to 罇.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【樽】 (zeon1):
    1. A bottle.
      • 樽豉油 (zeon1 si6 jau4): "[The] bottle of soy sauce."
      • 樽水 (zeon1 seoi2): "[The] bottle of water."
      • 樽裝水 (zeon1 zong1 seoi2): "Bottled water."
      • 水樽 (seoi2 zeon1): "Water bottle."
      • 膠樽 (gaau1 zeon1): "Plastic bottle."
      • 酒樽 (zau2 zeon1): "Wine bottle."
      • 玻璃樽 (bo1 lei4-1 zeon1): "Glass bottle."
    2. A vase.
      • 花樽 (faa1 zeon1): "[A] flower vase."

津 ꜀Tsun (zeon1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 6
Unicode code point
U+6D25
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 51 津) 濟渡處又津液
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 600 ꜀津)
    • A ford, a ferry;
    • a landing-place, a ghaut;
    • to ford, to cross a stream;
    • to imbue, to moisten, to soften by soaking;
    • to split wood by the hands;
    • spittle;
    • ꜀tsun ꜁léung (zeon1 loeng5 津梁), ends of a bridge;
    • ꜀tsun yat꜇ (zeon1 jat6 津溢) saliva;
    • ꜀tsun ꜂hau (zeon1 hau2 津口), a ferry;
    • ꜀kwán ꜀tsun (gwaan1 zeon1 關津), excise stations, guard-houses;
    • man꜅ ꜀tsun (man6 zeon1 問津), to ask the way;
    • ꜀shang ꜀tsun (sang1 zeon1 生津), to produce saliva;
    • _꜀tʻín ꜀tsun (tin1 zeon1 天津), the constellation Cygnus; the port of Peking (北京);
    • ꜀tsun ꜀tsun ꜃yau mí꜅ (zeon1 zeon1 jau5 mei6 津津有味) I relish it more and more.

准 ꜂Chun (zeon2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼎ + 8
Unicode code point
U+51C6
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 53 准) 信也實也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 36* ꜂准)
    • To permit, to approve;
    • to allow, to decide;
    • to receive;
    • on, upon, as a day;
    • ꜂chun chong꜅ (zeon2 zong6 准狀) grant the petition;
    • (see 準 zeon2 for "prepare" sense);
    • ꜂chun ꜁hang (zeon2 hang4 准行), allowed to be done;
    • ꜂chun sun꜄ (zeon2 seon3 准信) to believe;
    • ꜂chun ꜃í shap꜇ yat꜇ (zeon2 ji5 sap6 jat6 准以十日) permission for ten days;
    • ꜂chun shat꜇ kòm꜄ ꜀to (zeon2 sat6 gam3 do1 准實咁多), worth just that.

    Used for the following (凖).
