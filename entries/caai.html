差 ꜀Chʻái (caai1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼯ + 7
Unicode code point
U+5DEE
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 25 差) 使也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 11 ꜀差)
    • To commission, to send on a message;
    • to manage, to act;
    • a messenger, an envoy;
    • a legate, a commissioner;
    • ꜀chʻái sz'꜄ (caai1 si3 差使) a bailiff;
    • ꜀chʻái yik꜇ (caai1 jik6 差役) a policeman, a lictor;
    • ꜀chʻái sz'꜅ (caai1 si6 差事) an errand;
    • kái꜄ ꜀chʻái (gaai3 caai1 解差), a criminal's guard;
    • ꜂fú ꜀chʻái (fu2 caai1 苦差), an unpleasant duty;
    • chʻut꜆ ꜀chʻái (ceot1 caai1 出差), sent as legate;
    • ꜂pʻáu ꜀king ꜀chʻái (paau2 ging1 caai1 跑京差), a courier with dispatches.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【差】 (caai1):
    1. An officer.
      • 差人 (caai1 jan4): "[Police] officer."
      • 差佬 (caai1 lou2): "Copper."
      • 差婆 (caai1 po4): "Copperess."
      • 當差 (dong1 caai1): "Serve as [a] copper."
      • 郵差 (jau4 caai1): "Post officer."

猜 ꜀Chʻái (caai1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽝ + 8
Unicode code point
U+731C
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 25 猜) 疑也度也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 11 ꜀猜)
    • To guess; to doubt, to conjecture;
    • to dislike;
    • to abhor;
    • to fear, to apprehend, lest;
    • ꜀chʻái yat꜆ kuk꜇ ꜀chung (caai1 jat1 guk6 zung1 猜一焗盅), to bet a glass of wine;
    • ꜀chʻái kwat꜆ ꜁pʻái (caai1 gwat1 paai4-2 猜骨牌), to guess on dominoes;
    • ꜀chʻái tok꜇ (caai1 dok6 猜度) an opinion or idea, to guess;
    • ꜀chʻái ꜁múi (caai1 mui4-2 猜枚), or ꜀chʻái ꜃má (caai1 maa5 猜馬), morra, to guess at the fingers—a game;
    • ꜀chʻái ꜁í (caai1 ji4 猜疑), to suspect, to doubt of;
    • ꜀chʻái ꜁ím (caai jim4 猜嫌), to suspect and dislike;
    • ꜀chʻái ꜀ngám (caai1 ngaam1 猜啱) guessed right;
    • ꜀chʻái yat꜆ ꜀chʻái (caai1 jat1 caai1 猜一猜) make a guess;
    • ꜃ní shí꜄ ꜀chʻái[] [꜃]há (nei5 si3 caai1 haa5 你試猜一吓) just guess.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 你試猜一吓: Corrected há꜅ (haa6) to ꜃há (haa5).

踩 ꜂Chʻái (caai2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾜ + 8
Unicode code point
U+8E29
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
(Not present)
Conway's commentary
  • Here normalised to 踹; see 踹 caai2 for the canonical entry.
  • The character 踩 is not attested in the Middle Chinese rime books. Perhaps the pre-merger reading should be ꜂tsʻái instead of ꜂chʻái based on the initial of the phonetic 采, but the syllable tsʻái does not exist under any tone in Fan Wan.
  • For the purposes of Cantonese 踩 is indistiguishable from 踹. Note that the Mandarin readings are distinct.

踹 ꜂Chʻái (caai2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾜ + 9
Unicode code point
U+8E39
Alternative forms
  • 踩
Reading variations
  • cyun2 (expected from 市兖切 rising)
  • dyun3 (expected from 丁貫切)
  • jaai2 (vernacular)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 27 踹) 足踐踏也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 11 ꜂踹)

    Also read tün[꜄] (dyun3).

    • To stamp, to trample, to tread on with the heel;
    • to rase, to destroy;
    • the heel;
    • ꜂chʻái ꜃ün sok꜆ (caai2 jyun5 sok3 踹軟索) a treader on rope ladders—a thief;
    • ꜂pí ꜀sai ꜀kwá ꜁pʻí ꜃ní ꜂chʻái (bei2 sai1 gwaa1 pei4 nei5 caai2 畀西瓜皮你踹), let you step on a melon skin—trip you up;
    • ꜂chʻái pʻo꜄ ꜁ying ꜁pʻún (caai2 po3 jing4 pun4 踹破營盤), to remain victorious;
    • ꜂chʻái chü꜅ ꜃kʻü (caai2 zyu6 keoi5 踹住佢), step on it;
    • ꜂kí ꜁shí ꜁ngau ꜂chʻái kéuk꜆ (gei2 si4 ngau4 caai2 goek3 幾時牛踹腳) when is your birthday?
    • ꜂chʻái shat꜇ (caai2 sat6 踹實) to tread on firmly;
    • ꜂chʻái ꜀tang ꜀fá (caai2 dang1 faa1 踹燈花), to tread on lampwicks (a thief);
    • ꜂chʻái ꜁shing yuk꜇ tséung꜄ (caai2 sing4 juk6 zoeng3 踹成肉醬) trampled to death.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' Also read: Corrected ꜂tün (dyun2) to tün꜄ (dyun3).
  • See 踩 caai2 for commentary on 踩 versus 踹.

嘬 Chʻái꜄ (caai3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 12
Unicode code point
U+562C
Reading variations
  • saai3 (uncommon)
  • zyut3 (for vernacular "suck" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 28 嘬) 齧也一舉盡臠又捐其食曰嘬
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 11 嘬꜄)
    • To gnaw, to bite;
    • to eat rapidly;
    • to swallow, to bolt;
    • chʻái꜄ hüt꜆ (caai3 hyut3 嘬血) to suck blood;
    • chʻái꜄ ngít꜇ (caai3 ngit6 嘬齧) to eat with a noise.

    Also read shái꜄ (saai3).
