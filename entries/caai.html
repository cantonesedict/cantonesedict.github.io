差 ꜀Chʻái (caai1)

Radical + residual strokes
工 + 7
Unicode code point
U+5DEE
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 11 ꜀差)
    • To commission, to send on a message;
    • to manage, to act;
    • a messenger, an envoy;
    • a legate, a commissioner;
    • ꜀chʻái sz'꜄ (caai1 si3 差使) a bailiff;
    • ꜀chʻái yik꜇ (caai1 jik6 差役) a policeman, a lictor;
    • ꜀chʻái sz'꜅ (caai1 si6 差事) an errand;
    • kái꜄ ꜀chʻái (gaai3 caai1 解差), a criminal's guard;
    • ꜂fú ꜀chʻái (fu2 caai1 苦差), an unpleasant duty;
    • chʻut꜆ ꜀chʻái (ceot1 caai1 出差), sent as legate;
    • ꜂pʻáu ꜀king ꜀chʻái (paau2 ging1 caai1 跑京差), a courier with dispatches.
Conway's entry
  • 【差】 (caai1):
    1. An officer.
      • 差人 (caai1 jan4): "[Police] officer."
      • 差佬 (caai1 lou2): "Copper."
      • 差婆 (caai1 po4): "Copperess."
      • 當差 (dong1 caai1): "Serve as [a] copper."
      • 郵差 (jau4 caai1): "Post officer."

猜 ꜀Chʻái (caai1)

Radical + residual strokes
犬 + 8
Unicode code point
U+731C
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 11 ꜀猜)
    • To guess; to doubt, to conjecture;
    • to dislike;
    • to abhor;
    • to fear, to apprehend, lest;
    • ꜀chʻái yat꜆ kuk꜇ ꜀chung (caai1 jat1 guk6 zung1 猜一焗盅), to bet a glass of wine;
    • ꜀chʻái kwat꜆ ꜁pʻái (caai1 gwat1 paai4-2 猜骨牌), to guess on dominoes;
    • ꜀chʻái tok꜇ (caai1 dok6 猜度) an opinion or idea, to guess;
    • ꜀chʻái ꜁múi (caai1 mui4-2 猜枚) or ꜀chʻái ꜃má (caai1 maa5 猜馬), morra, to guess at the fingers—a game;
    • ꜀chʻái ꜁í (caai1 ji4 猜疑), to suspect, to doubt of;
    • ꜀chʻái ꜁ím (caai jim4 猜嫌), to suspect and dislike;
    • ꜀chʻái ꜀ngám (caai1 ngaam1 猜啱) guessed right;
    • ꜀chʻái yat꜆ ꜀chʻái (caai1 jat1 caai1 猜一猜) make a guess;
    • ꜃ní shí꜄ ꜀chʻái [꜃]há (nei5 si3 caai1 haa5 你試猜一吓) just guess.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 你試猜一吓: Corrected há꜅ (haa6) to ꜃há (haa5).

釵 ꜀Chʻái (caai1)

Radical + residual strokes
金 + 3
Unicode code point
U+91F5
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 11 ꜀釵)
    • A broad hair-pin;
    • met. females;
    • puk꜆ ꜀chʻái (buk1 caai1 卜釵), to divine by a hair-pin when a husband will return;
    • ꜁kwʻan ꜀chʻái (kwan4 caai1 群釵), a bevy of women;
    • ꜀king ꜀chʻái, (ging1 caai1 荊釵) a thorn hair-pin, met. poverty;
    • fung꜅ ꜀chʻái (fung6 caai1 鳳釵), an ornamental hair-pin with a phoenix.

꜁Chʻái (caai4 陽平)

  • 柴

柴 ꜁Chʻái (caai4)

Radical + residual strokes
木 + 5
Unicode code point
U+67F4
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 11 ꜁柴)
    • Firewood, faggots;
    • brushwood;
    • fuel;
    • to stop up;
    • yat꜆ ꜂pá ꜁chʻái (jat1 baa2 caai4 一把柴), a faggot of firewood;
    • ꜁chʻái ꜁tʻau (caai4 tau4 柴頭), half burned sticks;
    • ꜀kon ꜁chʻái pang꜅ ꜂fo (gon1 caai4 bang6 fo2 乾柴憑火), if dry wood is near the fire, [will it not catch]?
    • ꜁chʻái ꜀fá (caai4 faa1 柴花), kindlings;
    • ꜁chʻái ꜂fo (caai4 fo2 柴火), firewood;
    • pʻo꜄ ꜁chʻái (po3 caai4 破柴), to cleave wood;
    • ꜁chʻái ꜁m(u/n) (caai4 mun4 柴門), a cottage door;
    • ꜁pʻáu ꜁chʻái (paau4 caai4 刨柴), shavings;
    • ꜁chʻái ꜀san (caai4 san1 柴薪), faggots of wood.
