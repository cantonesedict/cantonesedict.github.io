增 ꜀Tsang (zang1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼟ + 12
Unicode code point
U+589E
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 29 增) 益也加也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 556 ꜀增)
    • To add, to double, to increase;
    • repeated, doubled;
    • ꜀ká ꜀tsang (gaa1 zang1 加增), to augment;
    • ꜀tsang ká꜄ (zang1 gaa3 增價) to raise the price;
    • ꜀tsang ꜁mái (zang1 maai4 增埋), to give a higher price, to throw in the difference.

憎 ꜀Tsang (zang1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼼ + 12
Unicode code point
U+618E
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 29 憎) 惡也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 556 ꜀憎)
    • To hate, to dislike, to abominate;
    • ꜂ho ꜀tsang (ho2 zang1 可憎), hateful;
    • tak꜆ ꜁yan ꜀tsang (dak1 jan4 zang1 得人憎), to be hated;
    • hat꜆ ꜁yan ꜀tsang (hat1 jan4 zang1 乞人憎), to act so as to be hated;
    • ꜃ngo ꜂hò ꜀tsang ꜃kʻü (ngo5 hou2 zang1 keoi5 我好憎佢), I perfectly detest him;
    • ꜃tsang hò꜄ pat꜆ ꜁tʻung (zang1 hou3 bat1 tung4 憎好不同), capricious, now hating and then loving.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【乞人憎】 (hat1 jan4 zang1):
    1. Despicable. (Literally, "begging others' hate".)
      • 真係乞人憎 (zan1 hai6 hat1 jan4 zang1): "[That] really is despicable."
