  • (Vol. 2 Image 29 筝) 樂器十二絃
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 20 ꜀箏)
    • A kind of harpsichord with 12 copper strings;
    • ꜀fung ꜀chang (fung1 zang1 風箏), glasses hung to jingle in the wind;
    • ꜂lá ꜀chang (laa2 zang1 揦箏), to thrum the guitar;
    • fong꜄ ꜀fung ꜀chang (fong3 fung1 zang1 放風箏), to fly a singing kite.
