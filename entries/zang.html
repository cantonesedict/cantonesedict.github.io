爭 ꜀Chang (zang1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽖ + 4
Unicode code point
U+722D
Reading variations
  • zaang1 (longer syllable)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 29 爭) 爭競爭闘
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 20 ꜀爭 ꜀Cháng (zaang1); here normalised to ꜀Chang (zang1) when appropriate)
    • To wrangle, to strive for precedence (Kangxi: 引也, 競也);
    • to contest, to litigate (Kangxi: 訟也);
    • (…).

    A colloquial word;

    • just, only, nearly, a little;
    • ꜀[chang] noi꜅ (zang1 noi6 爭奈) but still, howbeit, nevertheless;
    • ꜀cháng tám꜅ hí꜄ (zaang1 daam6 hei3 爭啖氣) unforgiving, choleric;
    • ꜀cháng ꜁lí ꜁hò (zaang1 lei4 hou4 爭釐毫), to quarrel upon trifles;
    • ꜀cháng mat꜆ ꜃yé (zaang1 mat1 je5 爭乜嘢), what are you quarreling about?
    • ꜀cháng tik꜆ ꜃shéung tong꜄ (zaang1 dik1 soeng5 dong3 爭滴上當) I came near being gulled;
    • ꜀[chang] tau꜄ (zang1 dau3 爭鬥) to fight, to fall to blows;
    • ꜀[chang] tsung꜅ (zang1 zung6 爭訟) to go to law;
    • ꜀cháng ꜀kung (zaang1 gung1 爭功), to emulate;
    • ꜀[chang] tüt[꜇] (zang1 dyut6 爭奪), to take by force;
    • ꜀[chang] chap꜆ (zang1 zap1 爭執) obstinate;
    • ꜀cháng ꜃yau hán꜅ (zaang1 jau5 haan6 爭有限) differs a little;
    • ꜀cháng tik꜆ chuk꜆ ꜂tò ꜃kʻü (zaang1 dik1 zuk1 dou3-2 keoi5 爭滴捉到佢), all but caught him;
    • ꜀[chang] áu꜄ (zang1 aau3 爭詏) to dispute;
    • ꜀cháng tak꜆ ꜀[to] (zaang1 dak1 do1 爭得多), very different; not enough;
    • ꜀cháng kau꜄ (zaang1 gau3 爭夠) I'm tired of striving, I'll contend no more.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan places 爭 under the short syllable ꜀chang (zang1); Williams places it under the longer ꜀cháng (zaang1).
  • The longer syllable ꜀cháng (zaang1) is preferred in the vernacular register, so normalisation to the shorter ꜀chang (zang1) is only appropriate in some instances.
  • Williams' "A colloquial word" etc.: Yes it is a vernacular word, but the meaning is to differ or to lack (see below), not "just", "only", etc.
  • Williams' 爭功: Not to emulate, but to claim merit.
  • Williams' 爭奪: Corrected tüt꜆ (dyut3) to tüt꜇ (dyut6).
  • Williams' 爭得多: Corrected ꜀tò (dou1) to ꜀to (do1).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【爭】 (zaang1):
    1. To differ by.
      • 爭好遠 (zaang1 hou2 jyun5): "Off by very far"
      • 爭少少 (zaang1 siu2 siu2): "Off by [a] little."
      • 爭啲 (zaang1 di1): "Off by [a] bit."
    2. To lack, to be missing, to be short.
      • 爭對筷子 (zaang1 deoi3 faai3 zi2): "Short [a] pair of chopsticks."
      • 爭一毫 (zaang1 jat1 hou4): "Ten cents short."
    3. To owe.
      • 爭住先 (zaang1 zyu6 sin1): "[I] owe [you] for now" (i.e. I'll pay you back later).
      • 爭佢錢 (zaang1 keoi5 cin4-2): "[I] owe him money."
  • 【爭在】 (zaang1 zoi6):
    1. To matter. Especially used in contrast to something that does not matter (or in contrast to something that does matter when used in the negative, 唔爭在).
      • 輸就輸硬、爭在輸幾多 (syu1 zau6 syu1 ngaang6, zaang1 zoi6 syu1 gei2 do1): "[Yes they've] lost for sure, [but what] matters [now is] how much [they] lose by."
      • 唔爭在遲多兩分鐘 (m4 zaang1 zoi6 ci4 do1 loeng5 fan1 zung1): "[It] won't matter [that we're] two more minutes late [on top of how late we are now]."
    2. To be lacking in, or to fall short in.
      • 平就平、爭在唔夠飽 (peng4 zau6 peng4, zaang1 zoi6 m4 gau3 baau2): "[Yes it's] cheap, [but it] falls short in [that it's] not adequately filling."

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 556 ꜀繒)

Conway's commentary
  • The reading ꜀tsang (zang1) is not unattested; 《集韻》 has the reading 咨騰切, explained 帛也.
  • Fan Wan erroneously has 繒 explained 魚[網] (綱 corrected to 網), and a strange character (like 曾, but with 丅 conjoined to the middle component between 八 above and 曰 below) explained 同上, here normalised to 罾.

贈 Tsang꜅ (zang6)

Radical + residual strokes
⾙ + 12
Unicode code point
U+8D08
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 32 贈) 送也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 557 贈꜅)
    • To present, to give to, to bestow on (Kangxi: 贈送也);
    • to confer, as by the crown;
    • to add to (Kangxi: 贈增也);
    • tsang꜅ sung꜄ (zang6 sung3 贈送) to give a present to;
    • kò꜄ tsang꜅ (gou3 zang6 誥贈) to honor an officer's parents for his merits;

    • chít꜆ ꜃lau tsang꜅ ꜁hang ꜁yan (zit3 lau5 zang6 hang4 jan4 折柳贈行人), to give a willow-twig at parting;
    • toi꜅ tsang꜅ (doi6 zang6 待贈) waiting for promotion, as expectant graduates do, at sixty.
