真 ꜀Chan (zan1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽬ + 5
Unicode code point
U+771F
Alternative forms
  • 眞
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 79 眞) 實也正也精也純也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 16 ꜀眞꜀真)
    • True, sincere, unfeigned, genuine, pure;
    • in fact, truly, indeed, no mistake;
    • a likeness;
    • actual, not secondary;
    • spiritual, subtle, pure, unmixed;
    • ꜀chan ꜁yan (zan1 jan4 真人), a sprite, a phantom of a man[;]
    • ꜀chan ko꜄ ꜃tsʻz' (zan1 go3 ci5 真個似) in fact, it was like;
    • ꜂sé ꜀chan (se2 zan1 寫真), to draw portraits;
    • ꜀chan hai꜅ (zan1 hai6 真係) it is so;
    • ꜀chan ꜀tsong ching꜄ [fán]꜅ (zan1 zong1 zing3 faan6-2 真贓正犯) this thing proves him a thief;
    • ꜁'m ꜀chí ꜀chan ꜂ká (m4 zi1 zan1 gaa2 唔知真假), I don't know whether it is so or not;
    • ꜀chan ꜀sam (zan1 sam1 真心), true-hearted;
    • ꜀chan fúi꜄ hí꜄ (zan1 fui3 hei3 真晦氣) low-spirited;
    • ꜀chan ching꜄ (zan1 zing3 真正) truly, indeed;
    • ꜀chan ꜂kong (zan1 gong2 真講), it ts really so.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 真人: The classical meaning of 真 is, Kangxi quotes, 《說文》仙人變形而登天也, "The immortal man changing form and ascending to Heaven". In modern non-Taoist usage this sense is now obsolete, and 真人 now simply means "real person".
  • Williams' 真贓正犯: Corrected fan꜅ (fan6) to fán꜅ (faan6).

震 Chan꜄ (zan3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾬ + 7
Unicode code point
U+9707
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 84 震) 卦名動也起也[懼]也威也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 17 震꜄)

    Interchanged with the last (振).

    • To quiver;
    • to shiver;
    • the 51st diagram, belongs to thunder;
    • to shake, as by thunder;
    • to quicken, as a fœtus (correctly ꜀shan (san1); see 娠 san1);
    • to awe, to impress;
    • thundering, terrible, as a sound;
    • dire, awful;
    • to intimidate;
    • to raise;
    • to alarm;
    • tí꜅ chan꜄ (dei6 zan3 地震) an earthquake;
    • chan꜄ nò꜅ (zan3 nou6 震怒) incensed;
    • ꜀wai chan꜄ (wai1 zan3 威震) to exhibit the majesty [of China]; (these square brackets appear in the original text)
    • chan꜄ ꜀[king] (zan3 ging1 震驚), terrified;
    • chan꜄ tung꜅ (zan3 dung6 震動) disturbed.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected ⿰亻瞿 to 懼.
  • Williams' 震驚: Corrected ꜀keng (geng1) to ꜀king (ging1).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【打冷震】 (daa2 laang5 zan3):
    1. To shiver.
    2. (slang) To have an orgasm.

陣 Chan꜅ (zan6)

Radical + residual strokes
⾩ + 7
Unicode code point
U+9663
Alternative forms
  • 陳
Reading variations
  • zan6-2 (for vernacular "later" sense 一陣)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 85 陣) 軍師行伍之列
  • (Vol. 1 Image 85 陳) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 17 陣꜅陳꜅)
    • A rank, a file of soldiers, a battalion;
    • an army;
    • to arrange, to place in ranks;
    • a little while, passing, transitory;
    • a battle;
    • to drill;
    • a mode of marshaling;
    • chan꜅ shéung꜅ (zan6 soeng6 陣上) in the fight;
    • yat꜆ chan꜅ chan꜅ ꜃[láng] (jat1 zan6 zan6 laang5 一陣陣冷), growing colder;
    • ꜂shai ꜃nü ꜁yan chan꜅ (sai2 neoi5 jan4-2 zan6 使女人陣) to send a woman to get it;
    • ꜁mai ꜁wan chan꜅ (mai4 wan4 zan6 迷魂陣) besotted in; silly;
    • ꜂pái chan꜅ (baai2 zan6 擺陣) to post troops;
    • ꜂kí ꜁tʻiú muk꜇ chan꜅ (gei2 tiu4 muk6 zan6 幾條木陣) several wooden sleepers (or supports);
    • chan꜅ shai꜄ (zan6 sai3 陣勢) the ranks; courageous;
    • yat꜆ chan꜅ ꜃ü (jat1 zan6 jyu5 一陣雨), a shower;
    • yat꜆ chan꜅ ꜀fung ꜃ü (jat1 zan6 fung1 jyu5 一陣風雨), a squall;
    • yat꜆ chan꜅ ꜁ming, yat꜆ chan꜅ ꜁'m ꜁ming (jat1 zan6 ming4 jat1 zan6 m4 ming4 一陣明一陣唔明), now you know, and then you do not know;
    • pái꜅ chan꜅ (baai6 zan6 敗陣) defeated the troops.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 一陣陣冷: Changed ꜃lang (lang5) to common reading ꜃láng (laang5).
  • Williams' 使女人陣: Literally, "employ [the] women's formation".
  • Williams' 陣勢: Figuratively, the situation of the battle.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【一陣】 (jat1 zan6):
    1. A moment or short while.
      • 等一陣啊 (dang2 jat1 zan6 a3): "Hold on [a] bit."
      • 瞓咗一陣 (fan3 zo2 jat1 zan6): "Slept [for a] bit."
  • 【一陣】 (jat1 zan6, jat1 zan6-2):
    1. Later, especially a short while later.
      • 一陣先吖 (jat1 zan6 sin1 a1): "[I'll see to it] later"
      • 一陣出去 (jat1 zan6-2 ceot1 heoi3): "[I'll] go out later."
    2. Marks the subjunctive mood ("what if" for an hypothetical future).
      • 一陣遲到咪弊 (jat1 zan6-2 ci4 dou3 mai6 bai6): "If [we] arrive late, [won't we be] stuffed?"
  • 【一陣間】 (jat1 zan6 gaan1):
    1. Synonym of 一陣 (in both the "moment" sense and the "later" / subjunctive marker senses).
  • 【陣間】 (zan6 gaan1):
    1. Short for 一陣間.
