幡 ꜀Fán (faan1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼱ + 12
Unicode code point
U+5E61
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 66 幡) 慢也又幡然攺
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 47 ꜀幡)
    • A banner, or streamer (Kangxi: 一曰幟也) with sentences, hung in temples or carried in processions;
    • a duster or napkin (Kangxi: 幡書兒拭觚布也);
    • to move;
    • ꜁chʻéung ꜀fán yat꜆ túi꜄ (coeng4 faan1 jat1 deoi3 長幡一對) a pair of streamers;
    • ꜀fán ꜁ín (faan1 jin4 幡然), forthwith.
  • (Page 47 ꜀旛; here corrected to 幡)
    • (…);
    • (…) ꜁wan ꜀fán (wan4 faan1 魂幡), a banner carried at funerals.

    Used for the preceding (幡).

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: The explanation 慢也 is erroneous. If it is a typo for 幔也, it is still not quite correct (a banner is not a curtain/screen).
  • Williams' "duster or napkin": Specifically for 觚 "wooden writing-slips". According to 段玉裁's annotations of 《說文》, 拭 ("wipe", which is not present as a headword) is a vulgar substitute for 飾 ("decorate"), and this sense should instead be understood as "decorative cloth".
  • Williams' "forthwith": Abrupt like an about-face (see supplemented sense below).
  • Williams' "a banner carried at funerals": Appears erroneously as 魂旛 under 旛.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. A reversal, an about-face (Kangxi: 幡反也). (Alternative form of 翻 (for the "reverse/flip" senses). See 翻 faan1.)

旛 ꜀Fán (faan1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽅ + 14
Unicode code point
U+65DB
Alternative forms
  • faan4 (alternative per 《廣韻》附[袁]切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 66 旛) 旗屬
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 47 ꜀旛)
    • A streamer (Kangxi: 幅胡也, 旛幡也);
    • ꜀tsʻing ꜀fán (cing1 faan1 青旛), or ꜁wan ꜀fán (wan4 faan1), a banner carried at funerals.

    Used for the preceding (幡).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 青旛: These are banners used in springtime rites, distinct from 魂幡 used in funerals, which should appear under 幡.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Banners or flags in general (Kangxi: 旌旗總名也).

番 ꜀Fán (faan1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽥ + 7
Unicode code point
U+756A
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 66 番) 夷狄之稱
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 47 ꜀番)
    • (…);
    • a time, a turn, a repetition of (Kangxi: 數也遞也);
    • to reckon, to change;
    • a tribe on the south, now applied to all foreigners;
    • ꜂kí ꜀fán (gei2 faan1 幾番), several times;

Conway's supplementary entry
  1. (mahjong) Points used to score a winning hand.
    • 三番起糊 (saam1 faan1 hei2 wu4-2): "Three points minimum [for a] win."
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【番子】 (faan1 zi2):
    1. (mahjong) The honours tiles (i.e. the unnumbered suit), consisting of the four winds (東南西北), the three duxes (中發白), and (optionally) the flower tiles.

繙 ꜀Fán (faan1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽷ + 12
Unicode code point
U+7E59
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 47 ꜀繙)
    • To spread out;
    • to agitate, or wave, as the wind does a flag (Kangxi: 𦆯繙風吹旗也);
    • (…).

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 翻. See 翻 faan1 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "To spread out": This is probably a rendering of Kangxi 《說文》寬也, which is a typo for 冕也, which perhaps should be 繙冤也 (neither of which I comprehend). In any case, this sense does not belong here, as the reading is ꜁fán (faan4) per 《廣韻》附[袁]切.
  • Williams' "to agitate, or wave, as the wind does": This is a misreading of Kangxi 𦆯繙風吹旗也. In, 𦆯繙 (same as 繽繙), 繙 means flag. It is 𦆯 (same as 繽, literally "disorderly") that supplies the meaning of "wave" or "flap".

翻 ꜀Fán (faan1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽻ + 12
Unicode code point
U+7FFB
Alternative forms
  • 反

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 66 翻) 翩翻羽飛貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 47 ꜀繙; here normalised to 翻)
    • (…);
    • to translate;
    • to explain;
    • ꜀fán yik꜇ (faan1 jik6 翻譯) to translate.
  • (Page 47 ꜀翻)
    • To fly (Kangxi: 飛也) to and fro;
    • to return, to change (Kangxi: 反覆也通作幡又通作反);
    • to revise a case;
    • ꜀fán fuk꜆ (faan1 fuk1 翻覆) changeable; vacillating;
    • ꜀fán ꜀[sháng] (faan1 saang1 翻生), resuscitated;
    • ꜀fán on꜄ (faan1 on3 翻案) to rejudge a case;
    • tiú꜅ ꜀fán ꜂sháng (diu6 faan1 saang2 調翻省); to to carry a case to the capital;
    • ꜀fán hü꜄ ꜀kwai (faan1 heoi3 gwai1 翻去歸); to go home;
    • fái꜄ ꜀fán ꜀kwai (faai3 faan1 gwai1 快翻歸) come home soon.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To reverse, to flip, to turn around.
