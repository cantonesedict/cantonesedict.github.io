招 ꜀Chiú (ziu1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 5
Unicode code point
U+62DB
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 2 招) 手呼也以言曰召以手曰招
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 24* ꜀招)
    • To beckon, to motion to with the hand, to hail;
    • to let people know;
    • to invite, to induce;
    • to raise, as troops;
    • to confess;
    • self-crimination;
    • to excite, to entangle;
    • a handbill, a sign-board;
    • a wave of the hand, a signal;
    • ꜀chiú ꜀fú (ziu1 fu1 招呼), to call and beckon;
    • ꜀chiú ꜁'m ꜀chiú (ziu1 m4 ziu1 招唔招), do you confess?
    • ꜀chiú tʻíp (ziu1 tip3-2 招貼), a handbill, a placard;
    • ꜀chiú ꜁pʻái (ziu1 paai4 招牌), a sign-board;
    • ꜀chiú ꜁long yap꜇ shé꜄ (ziu1 long4 jap6 se3 招郎入舍) to bring a son-in-law to one's house;
    • ꜀chiú shò꜄ (ziu1 sou3 招數) to own to the account, to become responsible for;
    • ꜀chiú ꜃yé hoi꜅ sz'꜅ (ziu1 je5 hoi6 si6 招惹害事) to involve one's business;
    • ꜀chiú ꜀ping (ziu1 bing1 招兵), to enlist soldiers;
    • ꜀chiú ying꜅ (ziu1 jing6 招認) or ꜀chiú tsúi꜅ (ziu1 zeoi6 招罪) to confess guilt;
    • ꜀chiú ká꜄ (ziu1 gaa3 招架) to ward off;
    • ꜀chiú ꜁tsʻoi ꜁tʻung ꜂tsz' (ziu1 coi4 tung4 zi2 招財童子) the god of wealth, worshiped at shop doors.

朝 ꜀Chiú (ziu1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽉ + 8
Unicode code point
U+671D
Alternative forms
  • 晁
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 2 朝) [旦]也早也又朝鮮國名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23* ꜀朝꜀晁)
    • Dawn, morning;
    • early;
    • ꜀chiú ꜃mán (ziu1 maan5 朝晚), morning and evening, early and late;
    • yat꜆ ꜀chiú (jat1 ziu1 一朝), in a morning, quickly, in a trice;
    • ꜀chiú ꜁tʻau ꜂tsò (ziu1 tau4 zou2 朝頭早), early in morning;
    • ꜀chiú fán꜅ (ziu1 faan6 朝飯) breakfast;
    • ꜀chiú ꜁shan (ziu1 san4 朝晨), morning time;
    • ꜀chiú ꜀chiú (ziu1 ziu1 朝朝) every morning;
    • ꜀Chiú ꜀[sín] kwok꜆ (ziu1 sin1 gwok3 朝鮮國) Corea;
    • yung꜅ ꜀chiú mí꜅ (jung6 ziu1 mei6 用朝未) have you breakfasted?
    • ꜃yau ꜀chiú ꜁mò ꜃mán (jau5 ziu1 mou4 maan5 有朝無晚), there is a morning but no evening, the day is very short;
    • tái꜅ pún꜄ ꜀chiú ꜁loi (daai6 bun3 ziu1 loi4 大半朝來), come before breakfast.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 且 to 旦.
  • Williams' 朝鮮國: Opinion is divided on whether 朝 in 朝鮮 should be read ꜀chiú (ziu1) or ꜀chʻiú (ciu4).
    • ꜀chiú (ziu1) is recorded in the dictionaries:
      《廣韻》陟遥切：朝：早也[…]又朝鮮國名[…]
      《康熙字典》朝：古文晁《唐韻》《廣韻》《集韻》《類篇》《韻會》𠀤陟遙切音昭[…]又朝鮮國名[…]
    • ꜀chʻiú (ciu4) is recorded in 《史記索隱》, specifically as a note under 朝鮮列傳第五十五:
      案朝音潮直驕反鮮音仙以有汕水故名也汕一音訕
    Corrected ꜀sin to ꜀sín.
  • Williams' 朝飯, 朝晨, 用朝未: Dated.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【朝頭早】 (ziu1 tau4 zou2):
    1. The morning.
      • 朝頭早沖涼 (ziu1 tau4 zou2 cung1 loeng4): "[I] shower [in] the morning."
  • 【朝早】 (ziu1 zou2):
    1. Morning.
      • 聽朝早 (ting1 ziu1 zou2): "Tomorrow morning."
      • 琴日朝早 (kam4 jat6 ziu1 zou2): "Yesterday morning".
      • 朝早八點 (ziu1 zou2 baat3 dim2): "Eight o'clock [in the] morning."
