處 ꜂Chʻü (cyu2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾌ + 5
Unicode code point
U+8655
Alternative forms
  • 処
  • 䖏
Reading variations
  • cyu5 (common in certain usages)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 46 處) 止居也又處置
  • (Vol. 1 Image 46 䖏) 同上
  • (Vol. 1 Image 46 処) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 32* ꜂處꜂処)
    • To dwell, to reside (Kangxi: 居也);
    • to be, to act, to occupy the place of;
    • while in;
    • to distinguish (Kangxi: 分別也), to manage, to judge, to decide on, to attend to, to do what is proper;
    • to place;
    • ꜂chʻü ꜃chʻü ꜂tsz' (cyu5 zi2 處子) a young lady;
    • ꜂chʻü chí꜅ ꜀tʻá (cyu2 zi6 taa1 處治他), do it for him, settle him, fix him;
    • ꜀séung ꜂chʻü ꜃chʻü (soeng1 cyu5 相處), familiar, friendly;
    • ꜂chʻü ꜃chʻü ꜀kwan ꜁shan (cyu5 gwan1 san4 處君臣), to be an officer;
    • ꜁nán ꜂chʻü (naan4 cyu2 難處), not easy to judge;
    • ꜂chʻü küt꜆ kʻüt꜆ (cyu2 kyut3 處決) execute him;
    • ꜂chʻü ꜃chʻü ꜀fú ꜃fú (cyu5 fu1 fu5 處夫婦) when married.
Conway's commentary
  • I have changed ꜂chʻü (cyu2) to the common reading ꜃chʻü (cyu5) when it seems appropriate (although I am not aware of any logic that can be used to determine when this is the case). From personal experience:
    • The classical reading ꜂chʻü (cyu2) seems mandatory in 處決, 處置.
    • The common reading ꜃chʻü (cyu5) seems preferred in 共處, 判處, 懲處, 獨處, 相處, 處分, 處境, 處女, 處斬, 處方, 處死, 處罰.
    • Either reading seems acceptable in 處世, 處事, 處理.
  • Williams' 處決: Changed küt꜆ (gyut3) to common reading kʻüt꜆ (kyut3).

處 Chʻü꜄ (cyu3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾌ + 5
Unicode code point
U+8655
Alternative forms
  • 処
Reading variations
  • syu3 (vernacular for "place" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 48 處) 處所
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33* 處꜄)
    • A place, a spot (Kangxi: 所也);
    • a circumstance, a condition;
    • added to nouns, it often forms the concrete, as ching꜄ kín꜄ ꜀tʻá ꜁ling chʻü꜄ (zing3 gin3 taa1 ling4 cyu3 正見它靈處) we now see its spirituality;
    • pít꜇ chʻü꜄ (bit6 cyu3 別處) elsewhere;
    • chʻü꜄ chʻü꜄ (cyu3 cyu3 處處) everywhere;
    • ko꜄ ꜂ko chʻü꜄ (go2 cyu3 嗰處) there;
    • ꜀pín chʻü꜄ (bin1 cyu3 邊處) where?
    • ꜂tün chʻü꜄ (dyun2 cyu3 短處) a failing;
    • ꜂hò chʻü꜄ (hou2 cyu3 好處) well off.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 嗰處: Changed ko꜄ (go3) to modern ꜂ko (go2).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【處】 (cyu3, syu3):
    1. Here.
      • 佢響處 (keoi5 hoeng2 cyu3): "He is here."
      • 唔喺處 (m4 hai2 syu3): "[It] isn't here."

紵 ꜃Chʻü (cyu5)

Radical + residual strokes
⽷ + 5
Unicode code point
U+7D35
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 47 紵) [絲]屬
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 32* ꜃紵)

    Contracted to ⿰糸⿳宀一　 (紵 with the last stroke unwritten), unless it is used for the private name of Hienfung (咸豐).

    • A coarse kind of hemp (Kangxi: 檾屬細者爲絟粗者爲紵, 紵亦麻也);
    • a coarse covering.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised 𮈔 to 絲.
  • Williams' "coarse": According to 段玉裁's annotations of 《說文》, 粗者爲紵 should be replaced with 布白而細曰紵.
  • Williams' "Contracted to ⿰糸⿳宀一　": Likely a mistake. The personal name of Emperor 咸豐 is 奕詝, not 奕紵.
