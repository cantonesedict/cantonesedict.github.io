處 ꜂Chʻü (cyu2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾌ + 5
Unicode code point
U+8655
Alternative forms
  • 処
  • 䖏
Reading variations
  • cyu5 (common in certain usages)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 46 處) 止居也又處置
  • (Vol. 1 Image 46 䖏) 同上
  • (Vol. 1 Image 46 処) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 32* ꜂處꜂処)
    • To dwell, to reside;
    • to be, to act, to occupy the place of;
    • while in;
    • to distinguish, to manage, to judge, to decide on, to attend to, to do what is proper;
    • to place;
    • [꜃]chʻü ꜂tsz' (cyu5 zi2 處子) a young lady;
    • ꜂chʻü chí꜅ ꜀tʻá (cyu2 zi6 taa1 處治他), do it for him, settle him, fix him;
    • ꜀séung [꜃]chʻü (soeng1 cyu5 相處), familiar, friendly;
    • [꜃]chʻü ꜀kwan ꜁shan (cyu5 gwan1 san4 處君臣), to be an officer;
    • ꜁nán ꜂chʻü (naan4 cyu2 難處), not easy to judge;
    • ꜂chʻü [kʻüt]꜆ (cyu2 kyut3 處決) execute him;
    • [꜃]chʻü ꜀fú ꜃fú (cyu5 fu1 fu5 處夫婦) when married.
Conway's commentary
  • I have changed ꜂chʻü (cyu2) to the common reading ꜃chʻü (cyu5) when it seems appropriate (although I am not aware of any logic that can be used to determine when this is the case). From past experience:
    • The classical reading ꜂chʻü (cyu2) seems mandatory in 處決, 處置.
    • The common reading ꜃chʻü (cyu5) seems preferred in 共處, 判處, 懲處, 獨處, 處分, 處境, 處女, 處斬, 處方, 處死, 處罰.
    • Either reading seems acceptable in 處世, 處事, 處理.
  • Williams' 處決: Changed Middle Chinese küt꜆ (gyut3) to common reading kʻüt꜆ (kyut3).

躇 ꜁Chʻü (cyu4)

Radical + residual strokes
⾜ + 12
Unicode code point
U+8E87
Han unification vigilance
May appear with 者 dotless (+12 assumed here) or dotted (+13), depending on the font.
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 45 躇) 躊躇猶[豫]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 32* ꜁躇)
    • At a loss, perplexed;
    • ꜁chʻau ꜁chʻü (cau4 cyu4 躊躇), embarrassed, hesitating, stopping to consider.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected illegible character to 豫.

紵 ꜃Chʻü (cyu5)

Radical + residual strokes
⽷ + 5
Unicode code point
U+7D35
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 47 紵) [絲]屬
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 32* ꜃紵)

    Contracted to ⿰糸⿳宀一　, unless it is used for the private name of Hienfung (咸豐).

    • A coarse kind of hemp;
    • a coarse covering.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised 𮈔 (⿰糸系) to 絲.
  • Williams' "Contracted to ⿰糸⿳宀一　": Probably a mistake. The personal name of 咸豐 is 奕詝, not 奕紵.
