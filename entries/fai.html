揮 ꜀Fai (fai1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 9
Unicode code point
U+63EE
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 27 揮) 指揮發揮又揮霍
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 43 ꜀揮)
    • To move, to shake (Kangxi: 振也動也);
    • to be agitated (Kangxi: 奮也);
    • to animate;
    • to sprinkle (Kangxi: 灑也, 揮湔也);
    • to scatter, to throw away;
    • ꜀fai ꜀chʻun (fai1 ceon1 揮春), to write new-year's inscriptions;
    • ꜀fai [꜁]hò (fai1 hou4 揮毫), to write;
    • ꜀fai ꜀kam ꜁ü ꜂tʻò (fai1 gam1 jyu4 tou2 揮金如土), to spend money like dirt;
    • ꜀fai fok꜆ (fai1 fok3 揮霍) extravagant;
    • ꜂chí ꜀fai (zi2 fai1 指揮), a major in a Manchu corps, to point with the hand;
    • ꜀fai ꜂shá (fai1 saa2 揮灑), to sprinkle, to spend fast;
    • ꜀fai tsau꜅ (fai1 zau6 揮袖) written.

    Also read ꜁wan (wan4), in the phrase ꜁wan ꜁lun (wan4 leon4 揮掄), entire, unbroken (Kangxi: 揮掄全而不破也).

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 揮毫: Corrected ꜀hò (hou1) to ꜁hò (hou4).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To direct, to command (Kangxi: 指揮也).
  2. To brandish.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【揮春】 (fai1 ceon1):
    1. New Year calligraphic banners.
      • 寫揮春 (se2 fai1 ceon1): "Write New Year banners."

輝 ꜀Fai (fai1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾞ + 8
Unicode code point
U+8F1D
Alternative forms
  • 暉
  • 煇
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 27 輝) 光輝
  • (Vol. 1 Image 27 煇) 火光也
  • (Vol. 1 Image 27 暉) 日光
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 43 ꜀輝)
    • Brilliant, refulgent, glorious (Kangxi: 光也火之光也), like the sun;
    • to glisten;
    • ꜀fai ꜁ín (fai1 jin4 輝然), spruced up, pleased;
    • ꜀fai ín꜅ (fai jin6 輝現) exceedingly glorious.
  • (Page 43 ꜀煇; here normalised to 輝)
    • Luminous, splendid (Kangxi 煇: 光也, 燿光也), like fire (Kangxi 煇: 火之光);
    • ꜀fai ꜀kwong (fai1 gwong1 輝光), lustrous, gorgeous, like many lamps;
    • ꜀fai ꜁wong (fai1 wong4 輝煌), illuminated.

麾 ꜀Fai (fai1)

Radical + residual strokes
⿇ + 4
Unicode code point
U+9EBE
Alternative forms
  • 戲
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 27 麾) [驅]也旌旗所以指揮
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 43 ꜀麾)
    • A signal or marking flag (Kangxi: 旌旗之屬所以指麾也, 或作戲, 戲大將之旗);
    • to make a signal, to motion to (Kangxi: 與撝同以手指麾也);
    • quick, hasty (Kangxi: 快也);
    • ꜀fai há꜅ (fai1 haa6 麾下) your honor! (used by soldiers.)
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected missing strokes in 驅.
