Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 29* ꜀諸)
    • To discriminate, to discuss;
    • not one alone, all, several, many, every;
    • in, respecting;
    • a pronoun at the end of a sentence, or a word implying doubt (contraction of 之乎);
    • a sign of the dative, upon, to, from, at (contraction of 之於);
    • it is often introduced to make rhythm;
    • ꜀shü ꜀chü ꜀shan (syu1 zyu1 san1 書諸紳), wrote it on his girdle;
    • ꜀chü ꜀shang (zyu1 sang1 諸生), siútsái (秀才) graduates;
    • ꜁yan ꜃yau ꜂shé ꜀chü (jan4 jau5 se2 zyu1 人有舍諸), will men reject him?
    • ꜀chü ꜁ü ꜂tsʻz' lui꜅ (zyu1 jyu4 ci2 leoi6-2 諸如此類) all are like this;
    • chü ꜁fán (zyu1 faan4 諸凡), all;
    • chü sz'꜅ ꜀to ꜁lò (zyu1 si6 do1 lou4 諸事多勞), I give you much trouble;
    • ꜀chü ꜁hau (zyu1 hau4 諸侯), feudal prince;
    • ꜀chü ꜀pún mat꜇ kín꜅ (zyu1 bun1 mat6 gin6-2 諸般物件) all sorts of things.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【諸事】 (zyu1 si6):
    1. Nosy or meddling.
      • 咪噉諸事啦 (mai5 gam3 zyu1 si6 laa1): "Don't [be] so nosy!"
