諸 ꜀Chü (zyu1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾔ + 8
Unicode code point
U+8AF8
Han unification vigilance
May appear with 者 dotless (+8 assumed here) or dotted (+9), depending on the font.
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 43 諸) 衆也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 29* ꜀諸)
    • To discriminate, to discuss (Kangxi: 辯也, 別異之辭);
    • not one alone, all, several, many, every (Kangxi: 非一也皆言也, 凡衆也);
    • in, respecting;
    • a pronoun at the end of a sentence, or a word implying doubt (contraction of 之乎) (Kangxi: 有諸疑辭);
    • a sign of the dative, upon, to, from, at (contraction of 之於) (Kangxi: 諸猶於也);
    • it is often introduced to make rhythm (Kangxi: 語助辭);
    • ꜀shü ꜀chü ꜀shan (syu1 zyu1 san1 書諸紳), wrote it on his girdle;
    • ꜀chü ꜀shang (zyu1 sang1 諸生), siútsái (秀才) graduates;
    • ꜁yan ꜃yau ꜂shé ꜀chü (jan4 jau5 se2 zyu1 人有舍諸), will men reject him?
    • ꜀chü ꜁ü ꜂tsʻz' lui꜅ (zyu1 jyu4 ci2 leoi6-2 諸如此類) all are like this;
    • chü ꜁fán (zyu1 faan4 諸凡), all;
    • chü sz'꜅ ꜀to ꜁lò (zyu1 si6 do1 lou4 諸事多勞), I give you much trouble;
    • ꜀chü ꜁hau (zyu1 hau4 諸侯), feudal prince;
    • ꜀chü ꜀pún mat꜇ kín꜅ (zyu1 bun1 mat6 gin6-2 諸般物件) all sorts of things.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "to discuss": This is a misreading of Kangxi 辯也. Here, 辯 is used for 辨.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Synonym of 之 (third-person object pronoun) (Kangxi: 諸之也).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【諸事】 (zyu1 si6):
    1. Nosy or meddling.
      • 咪咁諸事啦 (mai5 gam3 zyu1 si6 laa1): "Don't [be] so nosy!"

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 31* 註꜄)

    Used with the last two (著, 注).

    • To define, to explain, to illustrate;
    • to record, to write about;
    • a commentary, note, definition;
    • chü꜄ shik꜆ (zyu3 sik1 註釋) to explain;
    • lok꜇ chü꜄ ꜂kái (lok6 zyu3 gaai2 落註解), it needs a commentary;
    • chü꜄ í꜅ (zyu3 ji6 註義) or chü꜄ ꜂kái (zyu3 gaai2 註解), an explanation;
    • ꜂pò chü꜄ (bou2 zyu3 補註) to add to the comments.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected ⿰訁㔾 to 記.
  • Williams' "Used with the last two": I have not seen 著 used for 註 (or vice versa). Kangxi quotes 《毛詩序疏》註者著也, but this is an explanation, not an interchanging.

住 Chü꜅ (zyu6)

Radical + residual strokes
⼈ + 5
Unicode code point
U+4F4F
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 49 住) 居住
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 31* 住꜅)
    • To desist from, to cease, to stop;
    • to detain;
    • to dwell, to live in;
    • to endure;
    • to withstand;
    • to erect;
    • after another verb, it is often merely a sign of past time, or that the action has rested;
    • ꜀chá chü꜅ (zaa1 zyu6 揸住) held it;
    • hít꜆ chü꜅ (hit3 zyu6 歇住) wait a little;
    • ꜂shau chü꜅ lò꜅ ꜂hau (sau1 zyu6 lou6 hau2 守住路口), guard the passage;
    • chü꜅ pò꜅ (zyu6 bou6 住步) to stop walking;
    • chü꜅ ꜂shau (zyu6 sau2 住手), hold up work;
    • ꜃ní chü꜅ ꜀pín chʻü꜄ (nei5 zyu6 bin1 cyu3 你住邊處) where do you live?
    • ꜂yan pat꜆ chü꜅ (jan2 bat1 zyu6 忍不住) I can not bear it.
  • (Page 613 自꜅ Tsz'꜅ (zi6); here corrected to 住 Chü꜅ (zyu6))
    • (…);
    • ꜃mai [chü]꜅ (mai5 zyu6 咪住) stop, hold up!
    • ꜁'m ꜂ho hü꜄ [chü]꜅ (m4 hou2 heoi3 zyu6 唔好去住) don't go away, wait here a while;
    • (…).
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "after another verb": Not a sign of past time, nor that action has rested, but rather the continuous aspect, which marks that an action is continuing. See below.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【住】 (zyu6):
    1. Marker of the continuous aspect (ongoingness of an action).
      • 着住長褲 (zoek3 zyu6 coeng4 fu3): "Wearing long pants."
      • 食住先 (sik6 zyu6 sin1): "[Let's be] eating first [before the others arrive]."
      • 睇住佢 (tai2 zyu6 keoi5): "Watch [over] him."
      • 我揸住 (ngo5 zaa1 zyu6): "I'm holding [onto it]."
    2. Yet (for negative imperatives).
      • 咪行住 (mai5 haang4 zyu6): "Don't walk [off] yet!"
