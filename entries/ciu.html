超 ꜀Chʻiú (ciu1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾛ + 5
Unicode code point
U+8D85
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 2 超) 越也躍而過也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 25* ꜀超)
    • To leap over, to trip;
    • to hop across, to vault, to step over, to go before, to precede;
    • to excel, to surpass;
    • to promote;
    • to raise;
    • ꜀chʻiú [pat]꜇ (ciu1 baat6 超拔) to override an officer;
    • ꜀chʻiú ꜀yau (ciu1 jau1 超幽) or ꜀chʻiú tò꜅ (ciu1 dou6 超度) to release spirits from suffering;
    • ꜀chʻiú ꜂tang (ciu1 dang2 超等), to exceed in the bachelor's examination;
    • ꜀chʻiú ꜁kwʻan (ciu1 kwan4 超群), excelling;
    • ꜀chʻiú ꜀shang (ciu1 sang1 超生), to restore to health, to save from death;
    • ꜀chʻiú chʻéuk꜆ (ciu1 coek3 超卓) to surpass.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 超拔: Changed pát꜇ (baat6) to modern pat꜇ (bat6).
  • Williams' 超生: Also to be reincarnated.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【超】 (ciu1):
    1. To stare or glare.
      • 眼超超 (ngaan5 ciu1 ciu1): "Eyes staring."
    2. Sunglasses.
      • 黑超 (hak1 ciu1): "Sunnies."
    3. An interjection of dismissal or dissatisfaction.
      • 超、得兩個人 (ciu1, dak1 loeng5 go3 jan4): "Pff, only two people."

Radical + residual strokes

Fan Wan entry

  • (Page 581 ꜂俏 ꜂Tsʻiú (ciu2); here corrected to Tsʻiú꜄ (ciu3))

    • ꜂hò tsʻiú[꜄] túi꜄ ꜃ngán (hou2 ciu3 deoi3 ngaan5 好俏對眼), sparkling eyes;
    • tsʻiú[꜄] ꜀[sháng] (ciu3 saang1 俏生), like life, as puppets or images.

    • shap꜇ í꜅ ꜀shang tsʻiú꜄ (sap6 ji6 sang1 ciu3) the twelve animals which are thought to be like the twelve branches.

朝 ꜁Chʻiú (ciu4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽉ + 8
Unicode code point
U+671D
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 5 朝) 朝廷朝覲
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 25* ꜁朝)
    • A court;
    • the court, the palace;
    • to attend an audience, to go to court, to show fealty;
    • to have an audience, to hold a levee;
    • an audience;
    • a dynasty;
    • courtly, fashionable;
    • to visit a father or elder;
    • ꜁chʻiú ꜁tʻing (ciu4 ting4 朝廷), the court; his majesty;
    • ꜁chʻiú kín꜄ (ciu4 gin3 朝見) to see his majesty;
    • ꜃shéung ꜁chʻiú (soeng5 ciu4 上朝), to go up to court;
    • ꜁chʻiú teon꜅ ꜁chʻiú ꜀kong (zing2 deon6 ciu4 gong1 整頓朝綱) to strengthen the government;
    • ꜁chʻiú ꜀fán (ciu4 faan1 朝幡), to salute the banner, a religious practice;
    • shí꜅ ꜁chʻiú (si6 ciu4 侍朝), H. I. M. entering court;
    • ꜀tʻín ꜁chʻiú (tin1 ciu4 天朝), the celestial dynasty;
    • ꜁chʻiú kung꜄ (ciu4 gung3 朝貢) to hand up tribute;
    • ꜂chün ꜁chʻiú (zyun2 ciu4 轉朝), to change the dynasty;
    • yat꜆ ꜁chʻiú ꜀tʻín ꜂tsz' yat꜆ ꜁chʻiú ꜁shan (jat1 ciu4 tin1 zi2 jat1 ciu4 san4 一朝天子一朝臣), each monarch has his own set of ministers;
    • ꜁chʻiú ꜁fong (ciu4 fong4 朝房), an antechamber of the audience-room;
    • ꜁chʻiú fuk꜇ (ciu4 fuk6 朝服) court dress;
    • ꜁chʻiú ꜂háu (ciu4 haau2 朝考), examination for Hánlin (翰林).
