蝦 ꜀Há (haa1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾍ + 9
Unicode code point
U+8766
Alternative forms
  • 鰕
  • 虾 (Mainland simplified)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 74 蝦) 魚蝦
  • (Vol. 2 Image 74 鰕) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 67 ꜀蝦꜀鰕)
    • A shrimp, prawn, crawfish (Kangxi 蝦: 與鰕通 etc.);
    • ꜀há ꜀chʻun (haa1 ceon1 蝦膥), crab's eggs shrimp roe;
    • ꜀há ꜀chʻun cháp꜇ (haa1 ceon1 zaap6 蝦膥鈒) a skinflint;
    • ꜁lung ꜀há (lung4 haa1 龍蝦), the sea crawfish (Palinurus);
    • ꜁ngan ꜀há (ngan4 haa1 銀蝦), sea shrimps;
    • ꜁ming ꜀há (ming4 haa1 明蝦), large yellow prawns;
    • ꜀há kòm꜄ ꜂tsán (haa1 gam3 zaan2 蝦咁趲), to throwing the arms about passionately;
    • ꜀shang ꜀sháng ꜀há kòm꜄ tʻiú꜄ (saang1 haa1 gam3 tiu3 生蝦咁跳) lively, playful.

    (…)

    (Otherwise, a reading variation. See 蝦 haa4 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 生蝦咁跳: Changed ꜀shang (sang1) to vernacular reading ꜀sháng (saang1).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【蝦】 (haa1)
    1. To bully, to pick on.
      • 大蝦細 (daai6 haa1 sai3): "Elder picking on younger."
      • 唔准蝦人 (m4 zeon2 haa1 jan4): "No bullying others!"

The spelling 蝦 is arbitrary (but well established).

？瑕 ꜁Há (haa4)

Radical + residual strokes
⽟ + 9
Unicode code point
U+7455
Alternative forms
  • 瘕
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 74 瑕) 玉之[玷]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 67 ꜀？瘕 ꜀Há (haa1); here corrected to ꜁Há (haa4) and normalised to 瑕)
    • (…);
    • a scar blemish (Kangxi 瘕: 與瑕同);
    • (…).
  • (Page 67 ꜁？瑕)
    • A blemish in a gem (Kangxi 瑕: 玉玷也, 瑕玉之病也);
    • a crack (Kangxi 瑕: 瑕裂也), a flaw;
    • a fault (Kangxi 瑕: 過也), a bad habit;
    • (…), separated (Kangxi 瑕: 相閒曰瑕);
    • ꜃mí yuk꜇ ꜁mò ꜁há (mei5 juk6 mou4 haa4 美玉無瑕), a gem without a flaw, perfect;
    • ꜁há tsʻz'꜄ ꜀tsʻz' (haa4 ci1 瑕疵) a mistake, carelessness.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 遐. See 遐 haa4 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams misprints the headword 瑕 with an additional stroke in the middle component (equivalent to 𩙿 minus 亼); here corrected.
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 𪻕 to 玷.
  • Williams' "a crack": Metaphorically, a gap.
  • Williams' 瑕疵: Corrected tsʻz'꜄ (ci3) to ꜁tsʻz' (ci4) and normalised to ꜀tsʻz' (ci1).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Emptiness (Kangxi: 瑕謂虛脆也).
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【瑕】 (hae)
    • (noun-like) blemish, flaw

蝦 ꜁Há (haa4)

Radical + residual strokes
⾍ + 9
Unicode code point
U+8766
Alternative forms
  • 蛤
Reading variations
  • haa1 (common)
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 67 ꜀蝦꜀鰕)

    (…)

    The first character (蝦) is also read ꜁há (haa4), as ꜁há ꜁má (haa4 maa4 蝦蟆), a striped frog.

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 蝦蟆: Or, a toad.
  • The common reading of 蝦蟆 (usually spelt 蛤蟆) is haa1 mou1; see Kung Fu Hustle. The prescribed reading haa4 maa4 is almost never heard.
Conway's literary rendering
  • 【蝦】 (hae)
    • Used in 蝦蟆.
  • 【蝦蟆】 (hae mae)
    • (noun-like) frog, toad
