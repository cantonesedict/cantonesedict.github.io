囑 Chuk꜆ (zuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 21
Unicode code point
U+56D1
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 63 囑) 囑括又吩咐也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 35* 囑꜆)
    • To order, to bid, to enjoin (Kangxi: 付囑也, 託也);
    • chuk꜆ fú꜅ fú꜄ (zuk1 fu3 囑咐) to desire, to charge;
    • chuk꜆ ꜀shü (zuk1 syu1 囑書), a will;
    • chuk꜆ tʻok꜆ (zuk1 tok3 囑託) to commission;
    • ꜃mái chuk꜆ (maai5 zuk1 買囑) to bribe, to get evidence in one's favor.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 囑咐: Changed fú꜅ (fu6) to common reading fú꜄ (fu3).

  • (Page 35* 捉꜆)
    • To seize, to grasp, to gripe and hold (Kangxi: 搤也一曰握也);
    • to catch (Kangxi: 捕也);
    • chuk꜆ ꜁ná (zuk1 naa4 捉拿), to pursue, for the purpose of seizing;
    • chuk꜆ wok꜇ (zuk1 wok6 捉獲) to arrest a criminal;

    • chuk꜆ ꜃lò ꜂shü (zuk1 lou5 syu2 捉老鼠), to catch rats;
    • chuk꜆ ꜀tò (zuk1 dou1 捉刀), 'to grasp the knife,' to countenance or help one, to inform the government.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword ⿰木足 to 捉, and corrected 榒 to 搦.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【捉】 (zuk1):
    1. To play a board game.
      • 捉棋 (zuk1 kei4-2): "Play chess."
      • 捉圍棋 (zuk1 wai4 kei4-2): "Play surrounding-chess" (i.e. go).
      • 捉一鋪 (zuk1 jat1 pou1): "Play one game [of chess]."
      • 唔識捉 (m4 sik1 zuk1): "[I] don't know [how to] play."
  • 【捉伊人】 (zuk1 ji1 jan4-1):
    1. Hide and seek.
  • 【捉字蝨】 (zuk1 zi6 sat1):
    1. To nit-pick choice of words (or writing more generally).

    • tsuk꜇ tsʻü꜄ ꜂tsʻü (zuk6 ceoi2 續娶) or tsuk꜇ ꜁ín (zuk6 jyin4 續弦), to join the guitar string—to take a second wife remarry after becoming a widower;
    • luk꜇ tsuk꜇ ꜃yau ꜁lai (luk6 zuk6 jau5 lai4 陸續有嚟), coming one after another;
    • ꜁chʻün tsuk꜇ (cyun4 zuk6 傳續) to continue a family;
    • tsuk꜇ chü꜅ ꜃mí tsò꜅ (zuk6 zyu6 mei5 zou6 續住？做) to work by spells.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 續娶: Changed tsʻü꜄ (ceoi3) to common reading ꜂tsʻü (ceoi2).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To renew, to extend.
  2. To reprise.
