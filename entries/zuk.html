囑 Chuk꜆ (zuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 21
Unicode code point
U+56D1
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 63 囑) 囑括又吩咐也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 35* 囑꜆)
    • To order, to bid, to enjoin;
    • chuk꜆ fú[꜄] (zuk1 fu3 囑咐) to desire, to charge;
    • chuk꜆ ꜀shü (zuk1 syu1 囑書), a will;
    • chuk꜆ tʻok꜆ (zuk1 tok3 囑託) to commission;
    • ꜃mái chuk꜆ (maai5 zuk1 買囑) to bribe, to get evidence in one's favor.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 囑咐: Changed fú꜅ (fu6) to common reading fú꜄ (fu3).

捉 Chuk꜆ (zuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 7
Unicode code point
U+6349
Reading variations
  • zuk3 (uncommon)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 94 [捉] Chéuk꜆ (zoek3)) 捕也[搦]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 35* 捉꜆)
    • To seize, to grasp, to gripe and hold;
    • to catch;
    • chuk꜆ ꜁ná (zuk1 naa4 捉拿), to pursue, for the purpose of seizing;
    • chuk꜆ wok꜇ (zuk1 wok6 捉獲) to arrest a criminal;
    • chuk꜆ yung꜅ ꜁shan (zuk1 jung6 san4 捉用神), to divine;
    • chuk꜆ ꜃lò ꜂shü (zuk1 lou5 syu2 捉老鼠), to catch rats;
    • chuk꜆ ꜀tò (zuk1 dou1 捉刀), 'to grasp the knife,' to countenance or help one, to inform the government.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword ⿰木足 to 捉, and corrected 榒 to 搦.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【捉】 (zuk1):
    1. To play a board game.
      • 捉棋 (zuk1 kei4-2): "Play chess."
      • 捉圍棋 (zuk1 wai4 kei4-2): "Play surrounding-chess" (i.e. go).
      • 捉一鋪 (zuk1 jat1 pou1): "Play one game [of chess]."
      • 唔識捉 (m4 sik1 zuk1): "[I] don't know [how to] play."
  • 【捉伊人】 (zuk1 ji1 jan4-1):
    1. Hide and seek.

燭 Chuk꜆ (zuk1)

Unicode code point
U+71ED
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 63 燭) 火燭
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 35* 燭꜆)
    • A candle;
    • to illumine;
    • láp꜇ chuk꜆ (laap6 zuk1 蠟燭) a wax candle, a candle;

    • hon꜄ chuk꜆ (hon3 zuk1 看燭) show candles;
    • ꜃múi yat꜇ ꜂fo chuk꜆ ꜂ki ꜀to (mui5 jat6 fo2 zuk1 gei2 do1 每日火燭幾多), what are your daily expenses?

    • chuk꜆ ꜁tʻoi (zuk1 toi4 燭臺), a candlestick;
    • ꜁mò ꜁mí pat꜆ chuk꜆ (mou4 mei4 bat1 zuk1 無微不燭) lighted everywhere, to assist one's poor relations.
