竹 Chuk꜆ (zuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽵ + 0
Unicode code point
U+7AF9
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 63 竹) 竹木
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 34* 竹꜆)
    • The bamboo;
    • chuk꜆ ꜂kʻéung (zuk1 koeng2 竹蔃), whangees, bamboo roots;
    • chuk꜆ pò꜄ (zuk1 bou3 竹布) linen;
    • chuk꜆ ꜂sun (zuk1 seon2 竹筍), bamboo sprouts;
    • chuk꜆ tsít꜆ yéung꜅ (zuk1 zit3 joeng6 竹節樣) ridged;
    • chuk꜆ ꜁wong (zuk1 wong4 竹黃), tabasheer;
    • chuk꜆ shat꜇ (zuk1 sat6 竹實) seeds of the bamboo;
    • chuk꜆ pʻáu꜄ (zuk1 paau3 竹炮) a kind of large fireworks.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【竹花頭】 (zuk1 faa1 tau4-2):
    1. The character component 𥫗. (Literally, "bamboo flower atop".)

  • (Vol. 1 Image 63 竺) 天竺國

  • (Page 35* 竺꜆)
    • A country;
    • ꜀tʻín chuk꜆ kwok꜆ (tin1 zuk1 gwok3 天竺國) India, or the land where Budha was born.

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 64 筑) 擊筑
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 35* 筑꜆)
    • A sort of bamboo dulcimer with 13 cords, used in worship at the municipal temple.

築 Chuk꜆ (zuk1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽵ + 10
Unicode code point
U+7BC9
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 63 築) 擣也築室築城
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 34* 築꜆)
    • To make adobie walls;
    • to rear, to pile up earth;
    • to build, to erect;
    • chuk꜆ ꜁tsʻéung (zuk1 coeng4 築牆), to build a [wall];
    • chuk꜆ ꜃má ꜁tʻau (zuk1 maa5 tau4 築碼頭), to lay a jetty;
    • chuk꜆ ꜁chʻéung ꜂pò (zuk1 coeng4 bou2 築場圃), to lay out a kitchen-garden;
    • chuk꜆ ꜁tʻín ꜀kí (zuk1 tin4 gei1 築田基), to raise dikes in or between fields.
