Conway's Cantonese entry
  • гҖҗеҡ«гҖ‘ (can1)
    1. Suffixed after a verb to mark negative consequence.
      • еҶ·еҡ« (laang5 can1): "[I've] caught [a] cold."
      • ж’һеҡ« (zong6 can1): "[I've] bumped [something]."
      • ж•ҙеҡ« (zing2 can1): "[I've] hurt [myself]."
      • и·Ңеҡ« (dit3 can1): "[I've] had [a] fall."
    2. Suffixed after a verb to indicate "every time".
      • дёҠеҡ«йғҪжјҸ (soeng5 can1 dou1 lau6): "Every time [I] chow, [I] leak [good tiles]."
      • е°„еҡ«йғҪдёӯ (se6 can1 dou1 zung3): "Every time [he] shoots, [he's] on target."
      • йЈІеҡ«йғҪдҝӮз•¶жӯё (jam2 can1 dou1 hai6 dong1 gwai1): "Every time [she] drinks [medicine], it's dong quai."
