征 ꜀Ching (zing1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼻ + 5
Unicode code point
U+5F81
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 68 征) 往也取也伐也又正也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 18* ꜀征)
    • To go;
    • to take;
    • to collect taxes;
    • to go out to chastise, to reduce refractory states, to subjugate, to conquer;
    • to spy;
    • ꜀ching [fat]꜇ (zing1 fat6 征伐) to reduce to subjection;
    • ꜀ching ꜂tʻò (zing1 tou2 征討), to collect tribute by force;
    • ꜀ching ꜀fú (zing1 fu1 征夫), warriors;
    • ꜀ching ꜀shau (zing1 sau1 征收), to assess [taxes]; (these square brackets appear in the original text)
    • ꜀ching ꜀chü (zing1 zyu1 征誅), to exterminate;
    • chʻut꜆ ꜀ching (ceot1 zing1 出征), to go abroad on an expedition;
    • ꜀ching shui꜄ ꜁léung (zing1 seoi3 loeng4 征稅糧), to levy taxes;
    • ꜀ching ꜂tsiú (zing1 ziu2 征勦), to exterminate rebels.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 征討: More to subjugate than to collect tribute.
  • Williams' 征伐: Changed fát꜇ (faat6) to modern fat꜇ (fat6).

Radical + residual strokes

Unicode code point

Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 18* ꜀正)
    • The first;
    • ꜀ching üt꜇ (zing1 jyut6 正月) the first month of the year, January.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 正月: Usage to denote Western "January" is dated.
