拙 Chüt꜆ (zyut3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 5
Unicode code point
U+62D9
Reading variations
  • zyut6 (common)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 24 拙) 不巧也蠢拙
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 42 拙꜆)
    • Stupid, unskillful, unhandy, unapt (Kangxi: 不巧也);
    • ꜂chʻun chüt꜆ (ceon2 zyut3 蠢拙) stupid, inexpert;
    • chüt꜆ ꜀king (zyut3 ging1 拙荊), a 'dull thorn,' i. e. my wife;
    • chüt꜆ pat[꜆] (zyut3 bat1 拙筆) an unskillful penman;
    • chüt꜆ ꜂tsz' (zyut3 zi2 拙子) my son.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 拙筆: Corrected pat꜇ (bat6) to pat꜆ (bat1).

梲 Chüt꜆ (zyut3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽊ + 7
Unicode code point
U+68B2
Alternative forms
  • 棁
  • 棳
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 24 [梲]) 梁上短柱
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 42 梲꜆)
    • A joist to support a plate or girder (Kangxi: 同棳梁上楹也, 梲侏儒柱也梁上短柱);
    • a sort of king-post.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword 梚 to 梲, and fixed missing stroke in 短.

絀 Chüt꜆ (zyut3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽷ + 5
Unicode code point
U+7D40
Reading variations
  • fat1 (per 《洪武正韻牋》曲勿切 for "bent/withdrawn" sense)
  • zeot1 (per 《廣韻》竹律切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 86 絀 Fat꜆ (fat1)) 嬴絀猶屈伸也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 41 絀꜆ Chut꜆ (zeot1); here normalised to Chüt꜆ (zyut3))
    • Crimson (Kangxi: 絳也) silk;
    • to sew (Kangxi: 紩也);
    • to baste or sew badly;
    • ꜁ying [chüt꜆] (jing4 zyut3 嬴絀) to retire and come forth (Kangxi: 猶言屈伸也); unequal;
    • (…).
  • (Page 713 絀꜆ Chut꜆ (zeot1); here normalised to Chüt꜆ (zyut3))
    • This word also means obstructed, bent;
    • withdrawn, as money from circulation;
    • ꜁tsʻoi yung꜅ ꜀chí [chüt꜆] (coi4 jung6 zi1 zyut3 財用之絀) obstructions in the way of trade.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "to baste or sew badly": This is a misreading of Kangxi […]故作秫絀耳此蓋言其女工箴縷之麤拙也. Here, it is 秫 that supplies the meaning of "poor". The character 絀 simply means "sew" or "stitch".
  • Williams' 財用之絀: Not obstructed trade, but insufficient funds.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Insufficient.
