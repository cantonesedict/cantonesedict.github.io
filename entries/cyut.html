撮 Tsʻüt꜆ (cyut3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 12
Unicode code point
U+64AE
Reading variations
  • zyut3 (per 《廣韻》子括切 for "pull" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 24 [撮]) 兩指[蹙聚]而取曰[撮]又四圭曰[撮]
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 610 撮꜆)
    • To take a pinch with two or three fingers (Kangxi: 一曰兩指撮也, 三指取也);
    • a pinch, a pugil;
    • to take up a little;
    • to snatch, to bring together (Kangxi: 撮摠取也);
    • a little, a handfull;
    • a measure of 256 (some say 60) grains of millet (Kangxi: 四圭曰撮三指撮之也六十四黍爲圭);
    • brought together, selected (Kangxi: 蹙聚而捎取之也);
    • tsʻüt꜆ iú꜄ (cyut3 jiu3 撮要) a selection of what is important;
    • yat꜆ tsʻüt꜆ kòm꜄ ꜀to (jat1 cyut3 gam3 do1 一撮咁多), as much as a pinch, a little;
    • yat꜆ tsʻüt꜆ ꜂tʻò (jat1 cyut3 tou2 一撮土), a piece of land;
    • tsʻüt꜆ ꜁mái yat꜆ pò꜅ (cyut3 maai4 jat1 bou6 撮埋一部) make them all into one book;
    • tsʻüt꜆ ꜃shéung [꜂]kiú (cyut3 soeng5 giu6-2 撮上轎), to force one into a sedan;
    • tsʻüt꜆ hòp꜇ (cyut3 hap6 撮合) to make, an affiance, a partnership.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword ⿰扌⿳冖一取 to 撮, and fixed missing strokes in 蹙聚.
  • Williams' "(some say 60) grains": Per 《孫子算經》六粟為一圭十圭為一撮.
  • Williams' 撮上轎: Corrected ꜃kiú (giu6-5) to ꜂kiú (giu6-2).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To extract.
  2. To pull (Kangxi: 挽也).
