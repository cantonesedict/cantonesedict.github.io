卒 Tsʻüt꜆ (cyut3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼗ + 6
Unicode code point
U+5352
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 24 卒) 急也[速]也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 610 卒꜆)
    • An adverb, hastily, suddenly, abruptly (Kangxi: 急也, 匆遽之貌);
    • urgently;
    • tsʻüt꜆ kap꜆ (cyut3 gap1 卒急) in a hurry;
    • tsʻüt꜆ tsʻüt꜆ (cyut3 cyut3 卒卒) expeditiously;
    • tsʻüt꜆ ꜁ín ꜁loi tò꜄ (cyut3 jin4 loi4 dou3 卒然來到) he suddenly came upon one;
    • tsʻüt꜆ ꜁ín ꜀kán (cyut3 jin4 gaan1 卒然間), all in a hurry.

    (Alternative form of 猝 (for the "abruptly/hastily" senses). See 猝 cyut3.)

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 𬨮 to 速.

撮 Tsʻüt꜆ (cyut3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 12
Unicode code point
U+64AE
Reading variations
  • zyut3 (per 《廣韻》子括切 for "pull" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 24 [撮]) 兩指[蹙聚]而取曰[撮]又四圭曰[撮]
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 610 撮꜆)
    • To take a pinch with two or three fingers (Kangxi: 一曰兩指撮也, 三指取也);
    • a pinch, a pugil;
    • to take up a little;
    • to snatch, to bring together (Kangxi: 撮摠取也);
    • a little, a handfull;
    • a measure of 256 (some say 60) grains of millet (Kangxi: 四圭曰撮三指撮之也六十四黍爲圭);
    • brought together, selected (Kangxi: 蹙聚而捎取之也);
    • tsʻüt꜆ iú꜄ (cyut3 jiu3 撮要) a selection of what is important;
    • yat꜆ tsʻüt꜆ kòm꜄ ꜀to (jat1 cyut3 gam3 do1 一撮咁多), as much as a pinch, a little;
    • yat꜆ tsʻüt꜆ ꜂tʻò (jat1 cyut3 tou2 一撮土), a piece of land;
    • tsʻüt꜆ ꜁mái yat꜆ pò꜅ (cyut3 maai4 jat1 bou6 撮埋一部) make them all into one book;
    • tsʻüt꜆ ꜃shéung ꜃kiú ꜂kiú (cyut3 soeng5 giu6-2 撮上轎), to force one into a sedan;
    • tsʻüt꜆ hòp꜇ (cyut3 hap6 撮合) to make, an affiance, a partnership.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword ⿰扌⿳冖一取 to 撮, and fixed missing strokes in 蹙聚.
  • Williams' "(some say 60) grains": Per 《孫子算經》六粟為一圭十圭為一撮.
  • Williams' 撮上轎: Corrected ꜃kiú (giu6-5) to ꜂kiú (giu6-2).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To extract.
  2. To pull (Kangxi: 挽也).

猝 Tsʻüt꜆ (cyut3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽝ + 8
Unicode code point
U+731D
Alternative forms
  • 卒 (for "abruptly/hastily" senses)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 24 猝) 倉猝暴疾貌
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 610 猝꜆)

    Similar to the last (卒).

    • A dog rushing from a cover (Kangxi: 犬从艸暴出逐人也);
    • abrupt (Kangxi: 言倉卒[…]突也);
    • fierce, precipitate (Kangxi: 暴疾也);
    • ꜀tsʻong tsʻüt꜆ (cong1 cyut3 倉猝) impetuous; perplexed driven by many affairs.
