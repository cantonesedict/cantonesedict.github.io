啁 ꜀Cháu (zaau1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5541
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 41 啁) 啁嘐聲
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28)

    Interchanged with the last (嘲).

    • Chirping, bickering of birds;
    • ꜀cháu ꜀cháu ꜀sheng (zaau1 zaau1 seng1 啁啁聲), chirping of birds.

    Read ꜀tò; (see 啁 dou1).

Conway's commentary
  • This reading is used in 啁哳, 啁嘐.

嘲 ꜀Cháu (zaau1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 12
Unicode code point
U+5632
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 41 嘲) 以言相譏笑也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28)
    • To deride, to jeer at;
    • to ridicule;
    • raillery;
    • pák꜇ ꜀cháu (baak6 zaau1 白嘲), a pasquinade;
    • ꜀cháu siú꜄ (zaau1 siu3 嘲笑) to laugh at;
    • ꜀cháu má꜅ (zaau1 maa6 嘲罵) to jeer and rail at.
