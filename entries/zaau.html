啁 ꜀Cháu (zaau1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5541
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 41 啁) 啁嘐聲
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28)

    Interchanged with the last (嘲).

    • Chirping, bickering of birds;
    • ꜀cháu ꜀cháu ꜀sheng (zaau1 zaau1 seng1 啁啁聲), chirping of birds.

    Read ꜀tò; (see 啁 dou1).

Conway's commentary

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 12
Unicode code point
U+5632
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 41 嘲) 以言相譏笑也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 28)

    • to ridicule;

  • 找
  • 抓

找 ꜂Cháu (zaau2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 4
Unicode code point
U+627E
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 42 [找]) [找]尋[找]換
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 29 ꜂找)
    • To seek;
    • to make up the deficiency, to supply what is wanted;
    • to barter, to swap, to exchange;
    • ꜂cháu ꜃ní fúi꜄ hí꜄ (zaau2 nei5 fui3 hei3 找你晦氣) he's looking for you to scold you;
    • ꜂cháu ꜁tʻau lò꜅ (zaau2 tau4 lou6 找頭路) to look for work;
    • ꜂cháu shò꜄ ꜃mí (zaau2 sou3 mei5 找數尾), let us settle up that account;
    • ꜂cháu ꜁tsʻam (zaau2 cam4 找尋), to look for;
    • ꜂cháu ún꜅ (zaau2 wun6 找換) to change money or for money;
    • ꜂cháu ꜁tsʻín pʻò꜄ (zaau2 cin4-2 pou3 找錢舖) a money-changer's shop.

    Also read ꜁wá (waa4); same as 划; (see 划 waa4 for the canonical entry). (TODO)

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword ⿰扌弋 to 找.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【有找】 (jau5 zaau2):
    1. Suffixed after a monetary amount to indicate that the price is strictly lower. (Literally, "[and] there's change".)
      • 廿蚊有找 (jaa6 man1 jau5 zaau2): "[It's] twenty bucks [and] there's change."
  • 【找數】 (zaau2 sou3):
    1. To settle a bill or account.

Radical + residual strokes
⽖ + 0
Unicode code point

Fan Wan entry

  • 【狗爪頭】 (gau2 zaau2 tau4):
    1. The character component 爫. (Literally, "dog's paw atop".)

㷹 Cháu꜄ (zaau3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽕ + 12
Unicode code point
U+3DF9
Alternative forms
  • 𦻐
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 29 𦻐꜄ (⿱艹焯); here normalised to 㷹)
    • Blazing fire;
    • to fry;
    • to boil in fat;
    • ꜁yau cháu꜄ (jau4 zaau3 油㷹) boiled in fat;
    • cháu꜄ tò[꜄] tsʻui꜄ (zaa3 dou3 ceoi3 㷹到脆) fried crisp.
Conway's commentary
  • The deep-fry sense is very dated; 炸 zaa3 is now preferred.
  • Williams' 㷹到脆: Corrected ꜂tò (dou2) to tò꜄ (dou3).

    • A net, or basket to catch mud fish by covering them;
    • to catch or cover over;
    • to shade;
    • a shade, a cover, a protection from wind or dust;
    • ꜀tang cháu꜄ (dang1 zaau3 燈罩) a lamp-globe;
    • ꜀kún cháu꜄ (gun1 zaau3 棺罩) a network pall;

    • ꜀kai cháu꜄ (gai1 zaau3 雞罩) a hen basket;
    • [꜂]kiú cháu꜄ (giu6-2 zaau3 轎罩) a rain cover on a sedan.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised headword 𦋇 (罓卓) to 罩.
  • Williams entry: Normalised headword 𦋇 (罓卓) to 罩.
  • Williams' 轎罩: Corrected ꜃kiú (giu6-5) to ꜂kiú (giu6-2).
