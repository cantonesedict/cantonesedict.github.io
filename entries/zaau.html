找 ꜂Cháu (zaau2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 4
Unicode code point
U+627E
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 42 [找]) [找]尋[找]換
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 29 ꜂找)
    • To seek;
    • to make up the deficiency, to supply what is wanted;
    • to barter, to swap, to exchange;
    • ꜂cháu ꜃ní fúi꜄ hí꜄ (zaau2 nei5 fui3 hei3 找你晦氣) he's looking for you to scold you;
    • ꜂cháu ꜁tʻau lò꜅ (zaau2 tau4 lou6 找頭路) to look for work;
    • ꜂cháu shò꜄ ꜃mí (zaau2 sou3 mei5 找數尾), let us settle up that account;
    • ꜂cháu ꜁tsʻam (zaau2 cam4 找尋), to look for;
    • ꜂cháu ún꜅ (zaau2 wun6 找換) to change money or for money;
    • ꜂cháu ꜁tsʻín pʻò꜄ (zaau2 cin4-2 pou3 找錢舖) a money-changer's shop.

    Also read ꜁wá (waa4); same as 划; (see 划 waa4 for the canonical entry). (TODO)

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword ⿰扌弋 to 找.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【有找】 (jau5 zaau2):
    1. Suffixed after a monetary amount to indicate that the price is strictly lower. (Literally, "[and] there's change".)
      • 廿蚊有找 (jaa6 man1 jau5 zaau2): "[It's] twenty bucks [and] there's change."
  • 【找數】 (zaau2 sou3):
    1. To settle a bill or account.

    • Blazing fire;

櫂 Cháu꜅ (zaau6)

Radical + residual strokes
⽊ + 14
Unicode code point
U+6AC2
Alternative forms
  • 棹
  • 掉
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 42 棹 Cháu꜄ (zaau3)) 進船之器
  • (Vol. 2 Image 43 櫂 Cháu꜄ (zaau3)) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 29 櫂꜄棹꜄掉꜄ Cháu꜄ (zaau3); here corrected to Cháu꜅ (zaau6))
    • To row;
    • to shoot an arrow;
    • to throw away;
    • a long oar;
    • cháu[꜅] kéuk꜆ (zaau6 goek3 櫂腳) to limp, as the lame do;
    • cháu[꜅] chung꜄ (zaau6 zung3 櫂中) to hit the bull's-eye;
    • cháu[꜅] ꜂tséung (zaau6 zoeng2 櫂槳), to pull an oar;
    • cháu[꜅] ꜁mái ꜁tʻau (zaau6 maai4 tau4 櫂埋頭), to pull ashore;
    • cháu[꜅] kwo꜄ ꜂hoi (zaau6 gwo3 hoi2 櫂過海), to pull across the river;
    • chʻut꜆ lik꜇ cháu[꜅] (ceot1 lik6 zaau6 出力櫂) row harder;
    • cháu[꜅] chʻut꜆ ꜀kái (zaau6 ceot1 gaai1 櫂出街), throw it out in the street.

    The two last of these characters (棹, 掉) are improperly used in Canton for the first (櫂).
