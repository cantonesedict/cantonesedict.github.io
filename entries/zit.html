Alternative forms
  • 㘉
  • 折
  • 瀄 (for "squirt" senses)
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 22* 折꜆; here normalised to 擳)

    (…)

    A colloquial word.

    • To tickle;
    • to splash up, to spatter at;
    • to squirt at, as from a hose;
    • chít꜆ ꜂nan ꜀shan (zit1 nan2 san1 擳㤻身), splash me all over.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【擳】 (zit1):
    1. To tickle.
      • 怕擳 (paa3 zit1): "Ticklish."
    2. To squeeze, to pinch.
      • 擳暗瘡 (zit1 am3 cong1): "Pop [a] pimple."
      • 擳牙膏 (zit1 ngaa4 gou1): "Squeeze toothpaste" (used figuratively for slow progress in small amounts).
    3. A small amount.
      • 擳都唔笑 (zit1 dou1 m4 siu3): "Not [a] pinch of laughter."
    4. To squirt, to spray.
      • 擳到周圍都係 (zit1 dou3 zau1 wai4 dou1 hai6): "Squirted [it] all over [the place]."

折 Chít꜆ (zit3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 4
Unicode code point
U+6298
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 25 折) 損也耗也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 22* 折꜆)
    • To sunder, to snap in two, to break off (Kangxi: 拗折也);
    • to fold, to bend (Kangxi: 屈也);
    • to oppress, to repress (Kangxi: 挫也);
    • to reprehend (Kangxi: 直指人過失也);
    • to decide, to discriminate (Kangxi: 斷之也);
    • to deduct;
    • to stop (Kangxi: 止也), to injure;
    • to lose one's heir (Kangxi: 父喪子曰折);
    • an act of a play;
    • to exchange, to lose in trade;
    • to make amends for, to set over against;
    • to abase, to lower;
    • unmarried to die early, especially before marriage (Kangxi: 短折不祿也, 未婚曰折);
    • chít꜆ tsít꜆ há꜅ ꜀káu (zit3 zit3 haa6 gaau1 折節下交), to condescend to men of low estate;
    • chít꜆ yuk꜇ (zit3 juk6 折獄) to have a jail delivery make judgement in a litigation;
    • ꜃múi pʻat꜆ chít꜆ ꜁léung í꜅ shek꜇ (mui5 pat1 zit3 loeng4 ji6 sek6 每匹折量二石) each piece was reckoned as two stone of corn;
    • ꜁'m chít꜆ tak꜆ (m4 zit3 dak1 唔折得) cannot cheapen it; can't break it;
    • ꜂kí ꜀shing ꜁shing chít꜆ (gei2 sing4 zit3 幾成折) what discount will you make?
    • chít꜆ shik꜇ (zit3 sik6 折食) a composition in money for supplying viands;
    • chít꜆ kwai꜄ (zit3 gwai3 折桂) to pluck the cassia twig — to be a graduate;
    • chít꜆ hò꜄ ꜂kám tik꜆ (zit3 hou3 gaam2 dik1 折耗減滴) to discount a trifle; a little broken;
    • ꜂tá chít꜆ (daa2 zit3 打折) broken off;
    • chít꜆ ꜁mo (zit3 mo4 折磨), greatly afflicted;
    • ꜀tséung ꜀kung chít꜆ tsúi꜅ (zoeng1 gung1 zit3 zeoi6 將功折罪) to atone for error by meritorious conduct;
    • chít꜆ ꜂sün (zit3 syun2 折損), to break;
    • chít꜆ ká꜄ (zit3 gaa3 折價) to lower the price.

    (…)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 幾成折: Corrected ꜀shing (sing1) to ꜁shing (sing4).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To turn, to change direction (Kangxi: 曲也).
  2. To destroy (Kangxi: 毀也).
  3. A setback, a difficulty.
  4. To admire, to be convinced of.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【折】 (zit3):
    1. A discount.
      • 冇得折 (mou5 dak1 zit3): "No discount."
      • 折咗都係貴 (zit3 zo2 dou1 hai6 gwai3): "[Even] discounted it's still expensive."
      • 九折 (gau2 zit3): "Nine-[tenths] discount" (i.e. 10% off).
      • 七五折 (cat1 ng5 zit3): "Seven-[tenths]-five discount" (i.e. 25% off).

節 Tsít꜆ (zit3)

Radical + residual strokes
⽵ + 7
Unicode code point
U+7BC0
Han unification vigilance
May appear as either ⿱𥫗即 (+7 assumed here) or ⿱𥫗卽 (+9), depending on the font.
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 26 節) 制也儉也符節時節
  • (Vol. 1 Image 26 节) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 579 節꜆)
    • The joint of bamboos (Kangxi: 竹節也);
    • a joint, a knot, a node;
    • an article, a verse, a section of a chapter;
    • a limit, a term;
    • a holiday, a festival;
    • a time, a regular interval (Kangxi: 時節);
    • an emergency, the period or manner of doing a thing;
    • economy, temperance, moderation (Kangxi: 止也檢也制也 etc.);
    • chastity, virtue, in widows;
    • to limit a thing, to maintain, to restrain one's passions;
    • to mark, as music;
    • a classifier of joints, limbs, affairs;
    • tsít꜆ ꜂yam shik꜇ (zit3 jam2 sik6 節飲食) temperate in eating;
    • ꜀fan ꜃léung tsít꜆ (fan1 loeng5 zit3 分兩節) do it two ways, pay it at two times;
    • ꜁mò tsít꜆ chai꜄ (mou4 zit3 zai3 無節制) no limits to his outlay, careless;
    • ꜁fú tsít꜆ (fu4 zit3 符節) tally, passport, credentials (Kangxi: 符節所以示信);
    • tsít꜆ í꜅ (zit3 ji6 節義) chaste, in not remarrying;
    • shat꜆ tsít꜆ (sat1 zit3 失節) lost her virtue;
    • tsít꜆ ling꜅ (zit3 ling6 節令) the 24 solar terms;
    • tsò꜅ tsít꜆ (zou6 zit3 做節) to keep holiday;
    • pái꜄ tsít꜆ (baai3 zit3 拜節) holiday visits;
    • hí꜄ tsít꜆ (hei3 zit3 ？節) anger, feeling;
    • yat꜆ tsít꜆ sz'꜅ (jat1 zit3 si6 一節事) an affair;
    • ꜃mái ꜀kwán tsít꜆ (maai5 gwaan1 zit3 買關節) to get degrees by bribery, to bribe;
    • tsít꜆ tsau꜄ (zit3 zau3 節奏) to mark the time in music;
    • ꜀to ꜀shang ꜀chí tsít꜆ (do1 sang1 zi1 zit3 多生枝節) full of cares and business;
    • ꜃ná ꜁shí tsít꜆ (naa5 si4 zit3 那時節) on that occasion;
    • ꜀tʻín ꜀chung tsít꜆ (tin1 zung1 zit3 天中節) dragon-boat festival;
    • ꜀shau tsít꜆ chéung꜄ (sau1 zit3 zoeng3 收節賬) to collect bills at four terms;
    • tsít꜆ tsít꜆ ꜀kò (zit3 zit3 gou1 節節高), gradually rising in rank or wealth;
    • tsít꜆ kím꜄ kím꜅ (zit3 gim6 節儉) frugal, within the limits.
Conway's commentary

  • Williams' 節儉: Corrected kím꜄ (gim3) to kím꜅ (gim6).

截 Tsít꜇ (zit6)

Radical + residual strokes
⼽ + 10
Unicode code point
U+622A
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 27 截) 斷也止也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 580 截꜇)
    • To cut in pieces, to cut up;
    • to cut off (Kangxi: 斷也), to amputate;
    • to intercept, to cut off;
    • to obstruct;
    • to discriminate, to make nice distinctions;
    • a portion, a half;
    • ꜁lán tsít꜆ tsít꜇ (laan4 zit6 攔截) or tsít꜇ chü꜅ ꜃kʻü (zit6 zyu6 keoi5 截住佢), to stop, to arrest one's progress;
    • ꜀fan ꜃léung tsít꜇ (fan1 loeng5 zit6 分兩截) cut it in two;
    • tsít꜇ ꜀í (zit6 ji1 截衣), a robe whose body and skirt are of two colors;
    • tsít꜇ tün꜅ ꜃tʻün (zit6 tyun5 截斷) to cut apart.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "to intercept": E.g. 截的士, "stop a taxi".
  • Williams' "a portion": More generally, a classifier for segments.
  • Williams' 攔截: Corrected tsít꜆ (zit3) to tsít꜇ (zit6).
  • Williams' 截斷: Corrected tün꜅ (dyun6 去聲) to ꜃tʻün (tyun5 上聲).

捷 Tsít꜇ (zit6)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 8
Unicode code point
U+6377
Reading variations
  • zip6 (per 《廣韻》疾葉切)
  • zit3 (common in certain compounds, e.g. 快捷, 捷克)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 27 [捷]) 勝也敏疾也報勝曰[捷]報
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 580 捷꜇)
    • To obtain a victory;
    • successful, victorious (Kangxi: 尅也);
    • a victory (Kangxi: 勝也);
    • to complete (Kangxi: 成也);
    • to announce, as a victory or promotion (Kangxi: 報勝曰捷);
    • hastily, prompt, quick (Kangxi: 佽也疾也急也), as a herald;
    • joyful news;
    • kap꜆ tsít꜇ (gap1 zit6 急捷) nimble, fleet;
    • tsít꜇ king꜄ (zit6 ging3 捷徑) to take a short cut, a compendious way;
    • tsít꜇ pò꜄ (zit6 bou3 捷報) to hasten with good tidings;
    • ꜁lín tsít꜇ (lin4 zit6 連捷) to attain degrees at successive examinations;
    • fái꜄ tsít꜇ (faai3 zit6 快捷) quick.

    This is also read tsíp꜆ tsíp꜇ (zip6).

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised headword (with right component like 偼) to 捷.
  • Williams' "also read […]": Corrected tsíp꜆ (zip3) to tsíp꜇ (zip6).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. That obtained through warfare (Kangxi: 獵也軍獲得也).
