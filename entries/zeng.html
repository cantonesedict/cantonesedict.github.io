鄭 Cheng꜅ (zeng6)

Radical + residual strokes
⾢ + 12
Unicode code point
U+912D
Reading variations
  • zing6 (uncommon)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 74 鄭) 國名又鄭重
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 21* 鄭꜅ Chʻing꜅ (cing6); here corrected to Ching꜅ (zing6) and normalised to Cheng꜅ (zeng6))
    • A feudal state in the Chau (周) dynasty, now the prefecture of Káifung (開封) in Honán (河南);
    • it was first placed where the capital of Shensí (陝西) lies;
    • [cheng]꜅ chung꜅ (zeng6 zung6 鄭重) prudent, inflexible.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Removed extra character 又 at the end.
  • Williams erroneously puts 鄭 under aspirated chʻing꜅ (cing6).
  • Williams' 鄭重: In modern usage, only "solemn".
