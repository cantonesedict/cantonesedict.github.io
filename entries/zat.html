枳 Chat꜆ (zat1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽊ + 5
Unicode code point
U+67B3
Fan Wan entry
(Not present)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23 枳꜆)

    A colloquial word.

    • A cork, a plug, a stopple;
    • to cork, to fasten a stopper;
    • ꜂tsau chat꜆ (zau2 zat1 酒枳) a bottle cork;
    • chat꜆ shat꜇ ꜃kʻü (zat1 sat6 keoi5 枳實佢), cork it tightly.

  • 【枳】 (zat1):

    2. To block or stop using a cork or plug.

質 Chat꜆ (zat1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾙ + 8
Unicode code point
U+8CEA
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 86 質) 樸也又文質
  • (Vol. 1 Image 86 貭) 同質
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23 質꜆)
    • Substance, essence, nature, the material of (Kangxi: 質猶性也);
    • plain, unadorned (Kangxi: 樸也, 質猶本也);
    • sincere, honest (Kangxi: 實也, 質誠也);
    • to cross-examine, to confront (Kangxi: 問也定也);
    • to establish, to substantiate (Kangxi: 質體也);
    • opposite to, to appear in presence of (Kangxi: 質猶對也);
    • firm, as a texture;
    • a disposition, a habit;
    • a target (Kangxi: 的質也);
    • hí꜄ chat꜆ (hei3 zat1 氣質) the complexion; the elements of;
    • chat꜆ sun꜄ (zat seon3 質訊) to confront the accused and witnesses;
    • chat꜆ pʻok꜆ (zat1 pok3 質樸) plain;
    • chat꜆ tí꜅ (zat1 dei6-2 質地) temperament, constitution, talents;
    • ꜂pan chat꜆ (ban2 zat1 品質) the disposition.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【質】 (zat1):
    1. To stuff or pack tightly.
      • 質飽佢 (zat1 baau2 keoi5): "Stuff him full [with food]."
      • 質到爆 (zat1 dou3 baau3): "Packed till bursting."
    2. To query or interrogate in an adversarial manner.
      • 畀律師質 (bei2 leot6 si1 zat1): "Interrogated by [a] lawyer."

窒 Chat꜇ (zat6)

Radical + residual strokes
⽳ + 6
Unicode code point
U+7A92
Reading variations
  • zat1 (per 《廣韻》陟栗切)
  • zat6-2 (rising second in vernacular 窒窒地)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 87 窒) 窒塞不通也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23 窒꜇)
    • To close (Kangxi: 塞也), to stop up the mouth of;
    • to hamper[;]
    • the moon in opposition (Kangxi: 月在庚曰窒);
    • chat꜇ ꜂shau chat꜇ kéuk꜆ (zat6 sau2 zat6 goek3 窒手窒腳) impeded, things not to one's liking;
    • chat꜇ chü꜅ ꜃kʻü (zat6 zyu6 keoi5 窒住佢), stop it up tight;
    • chat꜇ ngoi꜅ (zat6 ngoi6 窒礙) difficulties.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【窒】 (zat6):
    1. To interrupt, especially by making an adversarial counterpoint or challenge.
      • 咪窒住嗮啦 (mai5 zat6 zyu6 saai3 laa1): "Stop interrupting!"
      • 窒到口啞啞 (zat6 dou3 hau2 aa2 aa2): "Challenged till speechless."
    2. To chide, especially as an interruption.
      • 窒我錯咗 (zat6 ngo5 co3 zo2): "Chided me [for] getting [it] wrong."
    3. To be intermittent and non-smooth in action or motion (stuttered speech, audio/video buffering, etc.).
      • 口窒窒 (hau2 zat6 zat6): "Stammering."
      • 影片窒吓窒吓 (jing2 pin3-2 zat6 haa5 zat6 haa5): "[The] video [playback is] stop-start."
