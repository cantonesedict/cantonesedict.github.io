只 Chat꜆ (zat1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 2
Unicode code point
U+53EA
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 86 只) 專也又語詞
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23 只꜆)
    • Only, merely;
    • but, however, yet;
    • chat꜆ ꜂hò ꜂kòm ché[꜄] (zat1 hou2 gam2 ze3 只好噉嗻), this way alone;
    • chat꜆ tak꜆ (zat1 dak1 只得) no otherwise, only could.

    Also read ꜂chí (zi2), in the same senses; and only ꜂chí (zi2), when it is used as a final particle. (See 只 zi2.)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 只好噉啫: Corrected ꜂ché (ze2) to ché꜄ (ze3).
  • In classical terms, the reading chat꜆ (zat1) for 只 is vulgar; Kangxi quotes 《五音集韻》之日切音質本之爾切無質音今讀若質俗音新增.
  • In modern terms, the reading chat꜆ (zat1) is obsolete.

質 Chat꜆ (zat1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾙ + 8
Unicode code point
U+8CEA
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 86 質) 樸也又文質
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23 質꜆)
    • Substance, essence, nature, the material of;
    • plain, unadorned;
    • sincere, honest;
    • to cross-examine, to confront;
    • to establish, to substantiate;
    • opposite to, to appear in presence of;
    • firm, as a texture;
    • a disposition, a habit;
    • a target;
    • hí꜄ chat꜆ (hei3 zat1 氣質) the complexion; the elements of;
    • chat꜆ sun꜄ (zat seon3 質訊) to confront the accused and witnesses;
    • chat꜆ pʻok꜆ (zat1 pok3 質樸) plain;
    • chat꜆ tí꜅ (zat1 dei6-2 質地) temperament, constitution, talents;
    • ꜂pan chat꜆ (ban2 zat1 品質) the disposition.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【質】 (zat1):
    1. To stuff or pack tightly.
      • 質飽佢 (zat1 baau2 keoi5): "Stuff him full [with food]."
      • 質到爆 (zat1 dou3 baau3): "Packed till bursting."
    2. To query or interrogate in an adversarial manner.
      • 畀律師質 (bei2 leot6 si1 zat1): "Interrogated by [a] lawyer."

[騭] Chat꜆ (zat1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾺ + 10
Unicode code point
U+9A2D
Alternative forms
  • 隲
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 86 隲) 陰隲品隲又定也成也[陞]也生也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23 [騭]꜆隲꜆)
    • A stallion;
    • to mount;
    • to ascend;
    • to raise, to promote;
    • to fix, to determine;
    • ꜀yam chat꜆ (jam1 zat1 陰騭) to think upon kindly, a secret benevolence;
    • tsik꜆ ꜀yam chat꜆ (zik1 jam1 zat1 積陰騭) to perform many good deeds.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 降 to 陞.
  • Williams prints 騭 as ⿰阝⿱步馬 rather than as ⿱陟馬.

郅 Chat꜇ (zat6)

Radical + residual strokes
⾢ + 6
Unicode code point
U+90C5
Reading variations
  • zat1 (expected from 之日切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 41 郅 Chí꜄ (zi3)) 世之隆也又極也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23 郅꜆ Chat꜆ (zat1); here normalised to Chat꜇ (zat6))
    • To ascend, to go up, as a hill;
    • flourishing, as an age;
    • very, exceedingly;
    • name of a city;
    • chat[꜇] ꜁lung (zat6 lung4 郅隆), very grand;
    • yuk꜆ chat[꜇] (juk1 zat6 郁郅) an ancient name of Ngánhwá hien (安化縣) in Kánsuh (甘肅).
