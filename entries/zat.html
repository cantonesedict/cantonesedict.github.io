Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 41 郅 Chí꜄ (zi3)) 世之隆也又極也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 23 郅꜆ Chat꜆ (zat1); here normalised to Chat꜇ (zat6))
    • To ascend, to go up, as a hill;
    • flourishing, as an age;
    • very, exceedingly;
    • name of a city;
    • chat[꜇] ꜁lung (zat6 lung4 郅隆), very grand;
    • yuk꜆ chat[꜇] (juk1 zat6 郁郅) an ancient name of Ngánhwá hien (安化縣) in Kánsuh (甘肅).
