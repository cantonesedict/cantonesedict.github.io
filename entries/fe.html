Williams (1856) pronunciation
  • Fé = *[feɪ]
Modern pronunciation
  • fe = [fɛː]
Conway's commentary
  • I have reluctantly assigned a Williams pronunciation of fé for modern fe1, but this is an anachronism, and perhaps inappropriate. Indeed in the original Williams entry for 炒, coffee is referred to as ꜀ká ꜀fí (gaa1 fei1), not ká꜄ ꜀fé (gaa3 fe1); see 炒 caau2.
