厄 Ak꜆ (ak1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼚ + 2
Unicode code point
U+5384
Alternative forms
  • 戹
Reading variations
  • aak1 (longer syllable)
  • ngak1 (hypercorrected initial)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 33 厄) 困窮也阻難也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 33 [戹]) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 3 厄꜆𢨪꜆)
    • (…);
    • small door in a house (Kangxi 厄: 同戹);
    • impeded, cramped;
    • to be ill-used, to be straitened;
    • ak꜆ ꜁kʻung (ak1 kung4 厄窮), poor;
    • ꜁ngai ak꜆ (ngai4 ak1 危厄) in danger.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected headword 卮 to 戹.
  • Most modern speakers prefer the longer syllable ák꜆ (aak1).
  • Williams erroneously has 𢨪 for an alternative form instead of 戹.

握 Ak꜆ (ak1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 9
Unicode code point
U+63E1
Reading variations
  • aak1 (longer syllable)
  • ngak1 (hypercorrected initial)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 32 握) 持也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 3 握꜆)
    • To grasp, to hold within the hand (Kangxi: 搤持也 etc.);
    • a little, as much as the palm can hold;
    • ak꜆ teng꜅ (ak1 deng6 握定) to grasp firmly;
    • ak꜆ ꜂shau (ak1 sau2 握手), to shake hands;
    • ak꜆ ꜁chʻí (ak1 ci4 握持), to hold in the hand;
    • ak꜆ iú꜄ (ak1 jiu3 握要) necessary.
Conway's commentary
  • Most modern speakers prefer the longer syllable ák꜆ (aak1).

渥 Ak꜆ (ak1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 9
Unicode code point
U+6E25
Reading variations
  • aak1 (longer syllable)
  • ngak1 (hypercorrected initial)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 32 渥) 厚也[沾]濡也洽也潤也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 3 渥꜆)
    • To moisten, to soak (Kangxi: 霑也);
    • to enrich with favors;
    • to fertilize;
    • to cover or daub thick (Kangxi: 厚漬也);
    • shining, rich;
    • ak꜆ ꜁mung (ak1 mung4 渥蒙), deeply grateful for;
    • ꜀yan ak꜆ (jan1 ak1 恩渥) imperial favor;
    • ak꜆ ꜀tán (ak1 daan1 渥丹), to make very red.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 沽 to 沾.
