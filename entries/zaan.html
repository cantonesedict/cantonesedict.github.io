琖 ꜂Chán (zaan2)

Radical + residual strokes
⽟ + 8
Unicode code point
U+7416
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 69 醆) 爵也夏曰醆殷曰[斝]周曰爵皆酒器也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 69 琖) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 ꜂琖)
    • A wine-cup, made deeper than the preceding (盞), of jade, or other valuable stone.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 牽 (but with top similar to 立) to 斝.

盞 ꜂Chán (zaan2)

Radical + residual strokes
⽫ + 8
Unicode code point
U+76DE
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 69 盞) 酒盞
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 ꜂盞)
    • A shallow cup for oil;
    • a wine saucer;
    • the classifier of lamps;
    • ꜀tang ꜂chán (dang1 zaan2 燈盞), a saucer for a night lamp;
    • yat꜆ ꜂chán ꜀tang (jat1 zaan2 dang1 一盞燈), a lamp.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【盞】 (zaan2):
    1. Classifier for lamps or lights.
      • 盞燈壞咗 (zaan2 dang1 waai6 zo2): "[The] light's broken."

Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 ꜂醆)
    • Muddy, unsettled spirits;
    • liquor beginning to clear;
    • a cup, a goblet.
