盞 ꜂Chán (zaan2)

Radical + residual strokes
⽫ + 8
Unicode code point
U+76DE
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 69 盞) 酒盞
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 ꜂盞)
    • A shallow cup for oil (Kangxi: 杯也, 酒盞最小杯也);
    • a wine saucer;
    • the classifier of lamps;
    • ꜀tang ꜂chán (dang1 zaan2 燈盞), a saucer for a night lamp;
    • yat꜆ ꜂chán ꜀tang (jat1 zaan2 dang1 一盞燈), a lamp.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 琖. See 琖 zaan2 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【盞】 (zaan2):
    1. Classifier for lamps or lights.
      • 盞燈壞咗 (zaan2 dang1 waai6 zo2): "[The] light's broken."
    2. Only, just (for emphasising that a negative outcome is highly likely).
      • 盞嘥氣 (zaan2 saai1 hei3): "[Talking to him] will just [be a] waste of breath."
      • 行得慢咪盞遲到 (haang4 dak1 maan6 mai6 zaan2 ci4 dou3): "[If we] walk slow then [we'll] only arrive late, [won't we]?"

趲 ꜂Tsán (zaan2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾛ + 19
Unicode code point
U+8DB2
Alternative forms
  • 䟎
Reading variations
  • zaan3 (per 《廣韻》則旰切 for "scatter" sense and 《集韻》則旰切 for "hasten" sense)
  • zaan6 (per 《廣韻》藏旱切 for "scatter" sense)
  • zou1 (per 《集韻》宗蘇切 for "run" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 69 趲) 催也促也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 555 ꜂䟎; here normalised to 趲)
    • To walk, to get on (Kangxi 趲: 走也);
    • to flee, to run away, to scatter, to put to flight (Kangxi 趲: 散走也);
    • to hasten, to urge, to quicken, to press (Kangxi: 逼使走也);
    • ꜂tsán pò꜅ (zaan2 bou6 趲步) to walk quicker;
    • ꜀tsʻúi ꜂tsán (ceoi1 zaan2 催趲), to urge on;
    • yat[꜇] yat꜇ ꜁loi ꜂tsán (jat6 jat6 loi4 zaan2 日日來趲), he comes urging me daily;
    • ꜂tsán ꜃kʻü ꜂hí ꜀shan (zaan2 keoi5 hei2 san1 趲佢起身), urge him to get out of bed.
Conway's commentary
  • The reading ꜂tsán (zaan2) is not unattested; Kangxi has 《集韻》[…]又子罕切 for the "run" sense. However, note that 《集韻》 actually has 子䍐切 for the "hasten" sense, explained 逼使赱也.
  • Williams' "To walk": More correctly, to run.
  • Williams' 日日來趲: Corrected yat꜆ (jat1) to yat꜇ (jat6).
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【走趲】 (zau2 zaan2):
    1. Wiggle room or leeway.
      • 冇得走趲 (mou5 dak1 zau2 zaan2): "There's no wiggle room."
      • 有得走趲 (jau5 dak1 zau2 zaan2): "There's wiggle room."

虥 Chán꜅ (zaan6)

Radical + residual strokes
⾌ + 10
Unicode code point
U+8665
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 71 虥) 虎淺毛
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 虥꜅)
    • A tiger cat (Kangxi: 貓也);
    • chán꜅ ꜁[miú] (zaan6 miu4 虥苗), a tiger shedding its hair.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 虥苗: Corrected ꜁mái (maai4) to ꜁miú (miu4). Note that 虥苗 is also spelt 虥貓, as the Middle Chinese reading of 貓 is homophonous with 苗 ꜁miú (miu4). In any case, Williams' ꜁mái (maai4) is a typo. Also "shedding its hair" is a misreading of Kangxi 虎竊毛謂之虦貓. Here, 竊 means "light-coloured".
