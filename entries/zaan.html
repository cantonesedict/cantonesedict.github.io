琖 ꜂Chán (zaan2)

Radical + residual strokes
⽟ + 8
Unicode code point
U+7416
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 69 醆) 爵也夏曰醆殷曰[斝]周曰爵皆酒器也
  • (Vol. 2 Image 69 琖) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 ꜂琖)
    • A wine-cup, made deeper than the preceding (盞), of jade, or other valuable stone.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 牽 (but with top similar to 立) to 斝.

盞 ꜂Chán (zaan2)

Radical + residual strokes
⽫ + 8
Unicode code point
U+76DE
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 69 盞) 酒盞
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 ꜂盞)
    • A shallow cup for oil;
    • a wine saucer;
    • the classifier of lamps;
    • ꜀tang ꜂chán (dang1 zaan2 燈盞), a saucer for a night lamp;
    • yat꜆ ꜂chán ꜀tang (jat1 zaan2 dang1 一盞燈), a lamp.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【盞】 (zaan2):
    1. Classifier for lamps or lights.
      • 盞燈壞咗 (zaan2 dang1 waai6 zo2): "[The] light's broken."

撰 Chán꜅ (zaan6)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 12
Unicode code point
U+64B0
Alternative forms
  • 譔
Reading variations
  • zaan3 (common)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 71 撰) 造也[脩]也述也具也翰林又修撰
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 撰꜅)
    • To regulate, to correct, to dispose in order;
    • to grasp;
    • to compose, to record;
    • a rule, a statute;
    • a received maxim;
    • a business, an act;
    • to edit, to revise books, to publish;
    • ꜀sau chán꜅ (sau1 zaan6 修撰) the first Hánlin (翰林) academician;
    • chán꜅ shut꜇ (zaan6 seot6 撰述), to narrate.

    Read ꜂sün (syun2); (see 撰 syun2).

    Read sün꜄ (syun3); (see 撰 syun3).

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 㫦 to 脩.

棧 Chán꜅ (zaan6)

Radical + residual strokes
⽊ + 8
Unicode code point
U+68E7
Reading variations
  • zaan6-2 (vernacular rising-tone noun)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 71 棧) 閣木爲棚曰棧四川有棧道
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 棧꜅)
    • A covered loft;
    • palisades;
    • a scaffold;
    • a covered, planked road cut out of the side of hills;
    • a hearse, a workshop;
    • a warehouse;
    • a storehouse, a etable, a pen, a sheepcote;
    • a wooden bridge or pathway;
    • chán꜅ ꜁fong (zaan6-2 fong4 棧房), a warehouse, a packhouse;
    • chán꜅ ꜀tsò (zaan6-2 zou1 棧租), storage charges;
    • [꜀máu] chán꜅ (maau1 zaan6-2 貓棧) a kennel for cats.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 貓棧: Changed Middle Chinese ꜁miú (miu4) to common reading ꜀máu (maau1).

綻 Chán꜅ (zaan6)

Radical + residual strokes
⽷ + 8
Unicode code point
U+7DBB
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 71 綻) 衣縫裂也事不完全曰破綻
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 綻꜅)
    • A rent seam;
    • to rip, to tear, to rend;
    • a hint, inkling;
    • pʻo꜄ chán꜅ (po3 zaan6 破綻) the affair is known, to hint a matter;
    • ꜂tá tak꜆ ꜁pʻí ꜀hoi yuk꜇ chán꜅ (daa2 dak1 pei4 hoi1 juk6 zaan6 打得皮開肉綻) beaten till the flesh was laid bare;
    • chán꜅ lít꜇ (zaan6 lit6 綻裂) rip the seams.

虥 Chán꜅ (zaan6)

Radical + residual strokes
⾌ + 10
Unicode code point
U+8665
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 71 虥) 虎淺毛
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 19 虥꜅)
    • A tiger cat;
    • chán꜅ ꜁[miú] (zaan6 miu4 虥苗), a tiger shedding its hair.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 虥苗: Corrected ꜁mái (maai4) to ꜁miú (miu4). Note that 虥苗 is also spelt 虥貓, as the Middle Chinese reading of 貓 is homophonous with 苗 ꜁miú (miu4). In any case, Williams' ꜁mái (maai4) is a typo.
