張 ꜀Chéung (zoeng1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼸ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5F35
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 89 張) 開也設山？也又宿名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 ꜀張)
    • To draw a bow;
    • to extend, to stretch, to open;
    • to draw up, as a list;
    • to increase;
    • to boast;
    • to state, to proclaim, to publish;
    • a classifier of things spread out, as a table, chair, bed, paper, &c.;
    • ꜀chéung kwá꜄ (zoeng1 gwaa3 張掛) to hang out, as festoons;
    • ꜀hoi ꜀chéung (hoi1 zoeng1 開張), to open quick or strongly;
    • ꜀san ꜀hoi ꜀chéung (san1 hoi1 zoeng1 新開張), to open a new shop;
    • ꜀chéung tái꜅ (zoeng1 daai6 張大) to make much of, boasting;
    • ꜀pʻò ꜀chéung ꜁wá lai꜅ (pou1 zoeng1 waa4 lai6 鋪張華麗) to make a great show;
    • ꜀kwái ꜀chéung (gwaai1 zoeng1 乖張), crossgrained;
    • ꜀chéung ꜁yéung (zoeng1 joeng4 張揚) to make known;
    • ꜀chéung ꜀hoi ꜂hau_ (zoeng1 hoi1 hau2 張開口), open your mouth;
    • ꜂chü ꜀chéung (zyu2 zoeng1 主張), to direct, to lord over;
    • yat꜆ ꜀chéung ꜁mún ꜁lim (jat1 zoeng1 mun4 lim4-2 一張門簾), a door-curtain.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: The unidentified character is ⿵𡗜弓, where 𡗜 is ⿻大丷.
  • Williams' 主張: Dated; 主張 now means to assert or hold a position or opinion.

章 ꜀Chéung (zoeng1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾳ + 2
Unicode code point
U+7AE0
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 89 章) 文章采章章明表章
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 ꜀章)
    • A piece of music;
    • a chapter, a section;
    • a composition;
    • a statement;
    • rules, laws, regulations;
    • clear, beautiful, manifest;
    • a display;
    • a grove;
    • a metonic cycle;
    • ꜂tsʻoi ꜀chéung (coi2 zoeng1 彩章), variegated;
    • hín꜄ ꜀chéung (hin3 zoeng1 憲章), magistrate's orders;
    • ꜀chéung kü꜄ (zoeng1 geoi3 章句) sections and paragraphs;
    • shò꜄ ꜀chéung (sou3 zoeng1 數章), many papers or statements;
    • ꜀chéung ꜁chʻing (zoeng1 cing4 章程), regulations, rule of conduct;
    • ꜁wong ꜀chéung (wong4 zoeng1 王章), laws of the land;
    • ꜁tʻò ꜀chéung (tou4 zoeng1 圖章) or yan꜄ ꜀chéung (jan3 zoeng1 印章), a seal.
