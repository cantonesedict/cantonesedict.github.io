將 ꜀Tséung (zoeng1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼨ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5C07
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 89 將) 事之未必就時也又奉也送也又干將劍名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 565 ꜀將)
    • To take, to hold in the hand (Kangxi: 扶持也);
    • sign of the future, shall, will, about to go (Kangxi: 將甫始之辭);
    • sometimes a form of the accusative;
    • then, soon, presently;
    • to help, to accommodate (Kangxi: 助也);
    • to use, to avail of, to go with, to escort, to accompany (Kangxi: 送也);
    • great (Kangxi: 大也), stout;
    • long (Kangxi: 長也), large;
    • to receive and act upon, endued with (Kangxi: 承也奉也行也);
    • to act, to perform, to implement (Kangxi: 將行也);
    • to progress, to increase, to approach, to advance (Kangxi: 進也) towards a mark;
    • to follow (Kangxi: 從也隨也);
    • going, passing, as time (Kangxi: 去也 etc.);
    • the side (Kangxi: 側也);
    • ꜀tséung ꜁loi (zoeng1 loi4 將來) presently;
    • ꜀tséung ꜂sz' (zoeng1 si2 將死) dying;
    • ꜀tséung kan꜅ án꜄ chau꜄ (zoeng1 gan6 aan3 zau3 將近晏晝) it is nearly noon;
    • ꜀tséung ꜂ho chʻut꜆ (zoeng1 ho2 ceot1 將可出) then he can go;
    • ꜀tséung ꜀kwan (zoeng1 gwan1 將軍), a commander-in-chief, all of whom are Manchus; met. the best, the principal;
    • ꜀tséung ꜁ü ꜀chí ꜁ho (zoeng1 jyu4 zi1 ho4 將如之何), how shall we then act?
    • ꜀tséung ꜁loi ꜀chʻung ꜀kung (zoeng1 loi4 cung1 gung1 將來充公), to confiscate effects;
    • ꜀tséung ꜀kam pok꜆ ꜂pò (zoeng1 gam1 bok3 bou2 將金博寶), to value a rarity with gold;
    • ꜀tséung ꜂pún ꜁kʻau lí꜅ (zoeng1 bun2 kau4 lei6 將本求利) to make money on a capital;
    • ꜀shü ꜀tséung (syu1 zoeng1 輸將), to aid the government;
    • ꜀tséung ꜁iú ꜁ín ꜁chʻün pò꜄ (zoeng1 jiu4 jin4 cyun4 bou3 將謠言傳布) to avail of strange words to make it known.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 將軍: In the non-Manchu context, a general; in Chinese chess, to check.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Or (Kangxi: 抑然之辭).
  2. And, also (Kangxi: 且也).
  3. To provide for, to support (Kangxi: 養也).
  4. Strong, vigorous (Kangxi: 壯也).
  5. To recuperate.
  6. (Chinese chess) Short for 將軍 (to check).

張 ꜀Chéung (zoeng1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼸ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5F35
Reading variations
  • zoeng3 (per Kangxi 知亮切 for "boast" sense)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 89 張) 開也設山？也又宿名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 ꜀張)
    • To draw a bow (Kangxi: 施弓[弦]也);
    • to extend, to stretch, to open (Kangxi: 開也);
    • to draw up, as a list;
    • to increase;
    • to boast (Kangxi: 自侈大也);
    • to state, to proclaim, to publish;
    • a classifier of things spread out, as a table, chair, bed, paper, &c.;
    • ꜀chéung kwá꜄ (zoeng1 gwaa3 張掛) to hang out, as festoons;
    • ꜀hoi ꜀chéung (hoi1 zoeng1 開張), to open quick or strongly;
    • ꜀san ꜀hoi ꜀chéung (san1 hoi1 zoeng1 新開張), to open a new shop;
    • ꜀chéung tái꜅ (zoeng1 daai6 張大) to make much of, boasting;
    • ꜀pʻò ꜀chéung ꜁wá lai꜅ (pou1 zoeng1 waa4 lai6 鋪張華麗) to make a great show;
    • ꜀kwái ꜀chéung (gwaai1 zoeng1 乖張), crossgrained;
    • ꜀chéung ꜁yéung (zoeng1 joeng4 張揚) to make known;
    • ꜀chéung ꜀hoi ꜂hau (zoeng1 hoi1 hau2 張開口), open your mouth;
    • ꜂chü ꜀chéung (zyu2 zoeng1 主張), to direct, to lord over;
    • yat꜆ ꜀chéung ꜁mún ꜁lim (jat1 zoeng1 mun4 lim4-2 一張門簾), a door-curtain.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: The unidentified character is ⿵𡗜弓, where 𡗜 is ⿻大丷.
  • Williams' 主張: Dated; 主張 now means to assert or hold a position or opinion.
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Great (Kangxi: 大也).
  2. To spread (Kangxi: 施也).
  3. To set out, to arrange (Kangxi: 設也).
  4. To string (a string instrument) (Kangxi: 施[絃]曰張).
  5. To spread a net to catch animals (Kangxi: 羅取鳥獸曰張).
  6. A surname (Kangxi: 姓).
  7. A position, an opinion.
  8. To look.
Conway's Cantonese entry
  • 【張】 (zoeng1)
    1. (mahjong) Classifier for (revealed) tiles. Also spelt 章.
      • 九張 (gau2 zoeng1): "Nine tiles [revealed]" (three melds).
      • 十二張 (sap6 ji6 zoeng1): "Twelve tiles [revealed]" (four melds).
