張 ꜀Chéung (zoeng1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼸ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5F35
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 89 張) 開也設山？也又宿名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33 ꜀張)
    • To draw a bow;
    • to extend, to stretch, to open;
    • to draw up, as a list;
    • to increase;
    • to boast;
    • to state, to proclaim, to publish;
    • a classifier of things spread out, as a table, chair, bed, paper, &c.;
    • ꜀chéung kwá꜄ (zoeng1 gwaa3 張掛) to hang out, as festoons;
    • ꜀hoi ꜀chéung (hoi1 zoeng1 開張), to open quick or strongly;
    • ꜀san ꜀hoi ꜀chéung (san1 hoi1 zoeng1 新開張), to open a new shop;
    • ꜀chéung tái꜅ (zoeng1 daai6 張大) to make much of, boasting;
    • ꜀pʻò ꜀chéung ꜁wá lai꜅ (pou1 zoeng1 waa4 lai6 鋪張華麗) to make a great show;
    • ꜀kwái ꜀chéung (gwaai1 zoeng1 乖張), crossgrained;
    • ꜀chéung ꜁yéung (zoeng1 joeng4 張揚) to make known;
    • ꜀chéung ꜀hoi ꜂hau_ (zoeng1 hoi1 hau2 張開口), open your mouth;
    • ꜂chü ꜀chéung (zyu2 zoeng1 主張), to direct, to lord over;
    • yat꜆ ꜀chéung ꜁mún ꜁lim (jat1 zoeng1 mun4 lim4-2 一張門簾), a door-curtain.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: The unidentified character is ⿵𡗜弓, where 𡗜 is ⿻大丷.
  • Williams' 主張: Dated; 主張 now means to assert or hold a position or opinion.

掌 ꜂Chéung (zoeng2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼿ + 8
Unicode code point
U+638C
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 91 掌) 手掌又主也掌握掌[管]又鞅掌失容也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 34 ꜂掌)
    • The palm of the hand;
    • the paw or sole of animals;
    • a webbed foot;
    • to grasp;
    • to rule, to control;
    • a control, a jurisdiction;
    • ꜂shau ꜂chéung (sau2 zoeng2 手掌), the palm;
    • ꜂tá yat꜆ ꜀pá ꜂chéung (daa2 jat1 baa1 zoeng2 打一巴掌), a slap of the hand;
    • ꜂chéung ꜂kún ꜀kung ꜁yan (zoeng2 gun2 gung1 jan4 掌管工人), to oversee workmen;
    • hòp꜇ ꜂chéung (hap6 zoeng2 合掌), to clasp the hands, as in prayer;
    • ꜂chéung ak꜆ ꜀ping ꜁kʻün (zoeng2 ak1 bing1 kyun4 掌握兵權), to command the forces;
    • ꜂chéung káu꜄ (zoeng2 gaau3 掌教) to teach [graduates]; (these square brackets appear in the original text)
    • hon꜄ ꜂shau ꜂chéung (hon3 sau2 zoeng2 看手掌), to examine the hand, palmistry;
    • pʻák꜆ ꜂chéung (paak3 zoeng2 拍掌), to clap the hands;
    • ꜂chéung kwai꜅ (zoeng2 gwai6-2 掌櫃) a bookkeeper, a salesman;
    • í꜅ ꜁ü ꜂fán ꜂chéung (ji6 jyu4 faan2 zoeng2 易如反掌), as easy as to turn the hand over.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised 𬋩 (⿱爫⿱冖㠯) to 管.

長 ꜂Chéung (zoeng2)

Radical + residual strokes
⾧ + 0
Unicode code point
U+9577
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 91 長) 尊也大也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 34 ꜂長)
    • Old, senior, superior, greater;
    • to excel, to increase;
    • to grow, to extend, to advance;
    • to prosper;
    • to elevate, to think highly of;
    • an elder, a superior;
    • ꜂pò ꜂chéung (bou2 zoeng2 保長), a constable;
    • ꜀ká ꜂chéung (gaa1 zoeng2 家長), a paterfamilias;
    • ꜃ngo ꜂kau ꜂chéung ꜃ní (ngo5 gau2 zoeng2 nei5 我久長你), I'm somewhat older than you;
    • kwai꜄ ꜀kang ꜀to ꜂chéung (gwai3 gang1 do1 zoeng2 貴庚多長), how old are you?
    • ꜂fo ꜂chéung (fo2 zoeng2 伙長), mates in a ship;
    • ꜂chéung tái꜅ (zoeng2 daai6 長大) to grow older;
    • ꜂chéung ꜂tsz' (zoeng2 zi2 長子) or ꜂chéung ꜁fong (zoeng2 fong4 長房), the eldest son;
    • ꜂chéung ꜃lò (zoeng2 lou5 長老), a Budhist priest;
    • ꜀tsün ꜂chéung (zyun1 zoeng2 尊長) or ꜂chéung ꜂ché (zoeng2 ze2 長者), an old man;
    • ꜁nín ꜂chéung (nin4 zoeng2 年長), old, older;
    • pat꜆ ꜂chéung tsun꜄ (bat1 zoeng2 zeon3 不長進) untrustworthy.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 長房: More the eldest son and his household, than simply the eldest son.
  • Williams' 不長進: Not untrustworthy, but unimproving, or not progressing.

帳 Chéung꜄ (zoeng3)

Radical + residual strokes
⼱ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5E33
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 92 帳) 帷幕也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 34 帳꜄)
    • A curtain, a screen;
    • a tent;
    • a house;
    • to calculate;
    • to spread;
    • a reason, a plan;
    • an account, for which the next is used;
    • pò꜄ chéung꜄ (bou3 zoeng3 布帳) an awning;
    • chéung꜄ ꜁lím (zoeng3 lim4-2 帳簾), a hanging door-screen;
    • chʻít꜆ chéung꜄ (cit3 zoeng3 設帳) to pitch one's tent, to become a teacher;
    • ꜀man chéung꜄ (man1 zoeng3 蚊帳) a bed-curtain;
    • wan꜅ chéung꜄ (wan6 zoeng3 混帳) unreasonable, incoherent;
    • ꜁pʻing chéung꜄ (ping4 zoeng3 屏帳) a movable screen of silk;
    • shau꜅ chéung꜄ (sau6 zoeng3 壽帳) a scroll given to old people.

賬 Chéung꜄ (zoeng3)

Radical + residual strokes
⾙ + 8
Unicode code point
U+8CEC
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 92 賬) 賬目
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 34 賬꜄)

    An unauthorized character.

    • A debt, a claim, an account, a charge;
    • to reckon, to sum up;
    • to calculate[;]
    • ꜁'m sün꜄ chéung꜄ (m4 syun4 zoeng3 唔算賬) do not put it to my account, it's none of my business;
    • ngan꜅ chéung꜄ (ngan6 zoeng3 韌賬) a tough account, a refused bill;
    • chéung꜄ muk꜇ (zoeng3 muk6 賬目) accounts;
    • ꜀shau chéung꜄ (sau1 zoeng3 收賬) to receive money on account;
    • chéung꜄ ꜀tán (zoeng3 daan1 賬單), a bill;
    • ꜀chui chéung꜄ (zeoi1 zoeng3 追賬) to dun;
    • chéung꜄ ꜁fong (zoeng3 fong4 賬房), a counting-room;
    • hau꜅ chéung꜄ (hau6 zoeng3 候賬) waiting for a settlement;
    • sün꜄ chéung꜄ (syun3 zoeng3 算賬) to reckon accounts;
    • chéung꜄ ꜂pò (zoeng3 bou6-2 賬簿), an account book;
    • túi꜄ chéung꜄ (deoi3 zoeng3 對賬) to balance accounts by offsetting[;]
    • ꜀tsʻing chéung꜄ (cing1 zoeng3 清賬) or ꜁wán chéung꜄ (waan4 zoeng3 還賬) to pay off an account;
    • hím꜄ chéung꜄ (him3 zoeng3 欠賬) to owe.

    A colloquial word.

    • A time, an occasion;
    • ꜁lai kwo꜄ yat꜆ chéung꜄ (lai4 gwo3 jat1 zoeng3) I have been here once[.]
Conway's commentary
  • Williams erroneously puts the "account" sense examples under "A colloquial word"; I have fixed this by moving them under "An unauthorized character".
