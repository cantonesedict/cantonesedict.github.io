婚 ꜀Fan (fan1)

Radical + residual strokes
⼥ + 8
Unicode code point
U+5A5A
Alternative forms
  • 㛰
  • 昏
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 80 婚) 婚姻
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 45 ꜀婚)
    • Marriage (Kangxi: 婚姻嫁也);
    • to take a wife;
    • ꜀fan ꜀yan (fan1 jan1 婚姻), nuptials;
    • ꜁mái ꜀fan (maai4 fan1 埋婚), to complete a marriage;
    • [꜁]lín ꜀fan (lin4 fan1 連婚) (Kangxi: 婚嫁之婚親曰連婚), to marry relatives;
    • ꜀fan pʻúi꜄ (fan1 pui3 婚配) to contract a marriage.
Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 婚姻: Better rendered "marriage".
  • Williams' 連婚: Corrected ꜀lín (lin1) to ꜁lín (lin4). Not to marry relatives, but relatives by marriage (i.e. in-laws).

昏 ꜀Fan (fan1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽇ + 4
Unicode code point
U+660F
Alternative forms
  • 昬
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 80 昏) 日暮也暗也昧也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 45 ꜀昏)
    • The sun setting (Kangxi: 日冥也);
    • twilight;
    • dusk (Kangxi: 昏代也, 代明也, 日入後二刻半爲昏昏來則明往故云代明);
    • obscure, confused, dull (Kangxi: 昏亂也);
    • in disorder;
    • to oblige to do;
    • ꜁wong ꜀fan (wong4 fan1 黃昏), dusk;
    • ꜀fan òm꜄ (fan1 am3 昏暗) dark, dull;
    • ꜀fan lün꜅ (fan1 lyun6 昏亂) confused;
    • ꜀fan múi꜅ (fan1 mui6 昏昧) unintelligible;
    • ꜀fan yé꜅ (fan1 je6 昏夜) night;
    • ꜀fan ꜀fá (fan1 faa1 昏花), motes in the eye.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 婚. See 婚 fan1 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "twilight": Specifically, dusk.
  • Williams' 昏夜: Better rendered "gloomy night".
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. To endeavour, to make an effort (Kangxi: 昏彊也).
  2. Not yet named (Kangxi: 未名曰昏).
  3. To lose consciousness.

熏 ꜀Fan (fan1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽕ + 10
Unicode code point
U+718F
Alternative forms
  • 燻 (for smoke-related senses)
  • 薰
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 80 熏) 烟也又[熱]氣蒸也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 45 ꜀熏)
    • Vapor, exhalations, steam (Kangxi: 火氣盛貌);
    • smoke rising (Kangxi: 火煙上出也);
    • to smoke, to fumigate, to heat (Kangxi: 熱也), to parch (Kangxi: 熏灼也);
    • to offend;
    • to becloud;
    • (…);
    • ꜀fan ꜀fung (fan1 fung1 熏風), warm wind (Kangxi: 東南曰熏風);
    • (…);
    • ꜀fan ꜂fo ꜂tʻúi (fan1 fo2 teoi2 熏火腿), to smoke hams;
    • ꜀fan ꜀fan (fan1 fan1 熏熏), uneasy, fidgetty;
    • ꜀fan ꜀kon (fan1 gon1 熏乾), to dry at the fire;
    • ꜀fan chek[꜆] (fan1 zek3 熏炙) to cauterize.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 曛. See 曛 fan1 for the canonical entry.)

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 醺. See 醺 fan1 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised 𤍠 to 熱.
  • Williams' "uneasy, fidgetty": This is a rendering of Kangxi 熏熏坐不安之貌, which is one of the explanations for 《詩大雅》公尸來止熏熏, but not a very good one, in my opinion. The other explanations (熏熏和悅也 and 熏說文作醺醉也) are better.
  • Williams' 熏炙: Corrected chek꜇ (zek6) to chek꜆ (zek3).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Amiable and cheerful (Kangxi: 熏熏和悅也).
  2. Warm.
