[勳] ꜀Fan (fan1)

Alternative forms

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 80 [勳]) 功勞也
  • (Vol. 1 Image 80 勛) 同上
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 45 ꜀[勳])
    • Loyal merit (Kangxi: 能成王功也 etc.), meritorious;
    • to spend strength for one's king;
    • ꜀fan ꜁lò (fan1 lou4 勳勞), merit obtained in serving the state;
    • ꜀fan ꜁shan (fan1 san4 勳臣), a patriotic statesman.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised headword ⿺熏力 to 勳.
  • Williams entry: Normalised headword ⿺熏力 to 勳.

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' "Twilight": Specifically, dusk.
  • Williams' 山涵曛映: Corrected ꜂yéung (joeng2) to ꜂ying (jing2).

熏 ꜀Fan (fan1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽕ + 10
Unicode code point
U+718F
Alternative forms
  • 燻 (for smoke-related senses)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 80 熏) 烟也又[熱]氣蒸也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 45 ꜀熏)
    • Vapor, exhalations, steam (Kangxi: 火氣盛貌);
    • smoke rising (Kangxi: 火煙上出也);
    • to smoke, to fumigate, to heat (Kangxi: 熱也), to parch (Kangxi: 熏灼也);
    • to offend;
    • to becloud;
    • (…);
    • ꜀fan ꜀fung (fan1 fung1 熏風), warm wind (Kangxi: 東南曰熏風);
    • (…);
    • ꜀fan ꜂fo ꜂tʻúi (fan1 fo2 teoi2 熏火腿), to smoke hams;
    • ꜀fan ꜀fan (fan1 fan1 熏熏), uneasy, fidgetty;
    • ꜀fan ꜀kon (fan1 gon1 熏乾), to dry at the fire;
    • ꜀fan chek[꜆] (fan1 zek3 熏炙) to cauterize.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 曛. See 曛 fan1 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Normalised 𤍠 to 熱.
  • Williams' "uneasy, fidgetty": This is a rendering of Kangxi 熏熏坐不安之貌, which is one of the explanations for 《詩大雅》公尸來止熏熏 (not a very good one, in my opinion).
  • Williams' 熏炙: Corrected chek꜇ (zek6) to chek꜆ (zek3).
Conway's supplementary entry
  1. Amiable and cheerful (Kangxi: 熏熏和悅也).
