沮 ꜀Tsü (zeoi1)

Radical + residual strokes
⽔ + 5
Unicode code point
U+6CAE
Reading variations
  • ceoi1 (per 《廣韻》七余切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 44 沮) 水名
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 594 ꜀沮)
    • The name of an affluent of the Yángtsz' kiáng (揚子江) (Kangxi: 水名, 水出漢中房陵東入江) west of [Kingchau] fú (荊州府) in Húpeh (湖北);
    • an ancient district now the extreme southwest of Shensí (陝西).
Conway's commentary
  • The reading ꜀tsü (zeoi1) is not unattested; 《集韻》 has the reading 子余切, explained 水名說文出漢中房陵東入江.
  • Williams considers rising-tone ꜂tsü (zeoi2) an acceptable alternative reading for the name sense. This is incorrect.
  • Williams' 荊州府: Corrected "K'ingchau" to unaspirated "Kingchau".

追 ꜀Chui (zeoi1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾡ + 6
Unicode code point
U+8FFD
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 55 追) 追述追隨又追也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33* ꜀追)
    • To pursue (Kangxi: 逐也), to follow after (Kangxi: 隨也);
    • to come up with (Kangxi: 送也), to overtake, to catch (Kangxi: 逮也);
    • to sue;
    • to seek out, to trace back (Kangxi: 凡上遡已往曰追);
    • to reflect on, to look back on, retrospective;
    • to expel;
    • ꜀chui ꜂kon (zeoi1 gon2 追趕), to chase;
    • ꜀chui tò꜄ (zeoi2 dou3-2 追到) to catch, to reach;
    • ꜀chui fúi꜄ (zeoi1 fui3 追悔) to feel remorse;
    • ꜀chui hü꜄ (zeoi1 heoi3 追去) to drive out;
    • ꜀chui kau꜄ (zeoi1 gau3 追究) to investigate thoroughly;
    • ꜁ngan pat꜆ ꜀chui ꜁úi (ngan4 bat1 zeoi1 wui4 銀不追回), the money cannot be refunded;
    • ꜀chui ꜃kʻü ꜀fán ꜁lai (zeoi1 keoi5 faan1 lai4 追佢返嚟), hurry him back here;
    • ꜀chui ꜁'m ꜃shéung lok꜆ (zeoi1 m4 soeng5 lok3 追唔上佢) could not catch him;
    • ꜀chui ꜂tʻò (zeoi1 tou2 追討), to dun;
    • ꜀chui ꜀fung (zeoi1 fung1 追封), posthumous honors;
    • ꜀chui ním꜅ (zeoi1 nim6 追念) to recollect.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: 追也 is self-referential.
  • Williams' "to sue": In the sense of seeking by request.
  • Williams' "to expel": This is a misreading of Kangxi 逐也.

咀 ꜂Tsü (zeoi2)

Radical + residual strokes
⼝ + 5
Unicode code point
U+5480
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 46 咀) 咀嚼
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 595 ꜂咀)
    • To taste (Kangxi: 含味也);
    • to chew, to bite in order to taste (Kangxi: 咀嚼);
    • ꜂tsü tséuk[꜆] ꜃há (zeoi2 zoek3 haa5 咀嚼吓), take a bite of it;
    • ꜁hòm ꜀ying ꜂tsü [꜁]wá (ham4 jing1 zyu2 waa1 含英咀華), to learn and relish the beauties of a style.

    (Otherwise, an alternative form of 嘴. See 嘴 zeoi2 for the canonical entry.)

Conway's commentary
  • Williams' 咀嚼吓: Changed tséuk꜇ (zoek6) to common reading tséuk꜆ (zoek3).
  • Williams' 含英咀華: Corrected ꜀wá (waa1) to ꜁wá (waa4).
  • It is only in modern Cantonese that 咀 ꜂tsü and 嘴 ꜂tsui are homophonous (both zeoi2), and that 咀 is an alternative form of 嘴.

Radical + residual strokes
⼌ + 10
Unicode code point
U+6700
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 57 最) 殿最前曰最後曰殿
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 597 最꜄)
    • To come together, to assemble (Kangxi: 聚也);
    • an adjective denoting the most important, the highest degree of (Kangxi: 極也, 最爲第一);

    • tsui꜄ pín꜅ tong꜄ (zeoi3 bin6 dong3 最便當) the most convenient, very handy.

  • Williams' "very, extremely, exceedingly": These should instead be rendered "most", as 最 is superlative.
