Reading variations

Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 1 Image 44 疽) [癰]疽
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 594 ꜀疽)
    • A deep-seated ulcer;
    • a carbuncle or anthrax;
    • ꜀yung ꜀tsü (jung1 zeoi1 癰疽), an old ulcer;

  • (Page 594 ꜀睢)

    (Reading variation (for "glare/gaze-up" senses). See 睢 fai1 for the canonical entry.)

    Read ꜀sui (seoi1);

    • (See 睢 fai1 for "self-contented" sense);

Conway's commentary
  • The reading ꜀tsü (zeoi1) for 睢 is not attested in the Middle Chinese rime books. It is possible that Williams has mistaken it for 雎, which is what is present in Fan Wan (see 雎 zeoi1).

追 ꜀Chui (zeoi1)

Radical + residual strokes
⾡ + 6
Unicode code point
U+8FFD
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 55 追) 追述追隨又追也
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33* ꜀追)
    • To pursue, to follow after;
    • to come up with, to overtake, to catch;
    • to sue;
    • to seek out, to trace back;
    • to reflect on, to look back on, retrospective;
    • to expel;
    • ꜀chui ꜂kon (zeoi1 gon2 追趕), to chase;
    • ꜀chui tò꜄ (zeoi2 dou3-2 追到) to catch, to reach;
    • ꜀chui fúi꜄ (zeoi1 fui3 追悔) to feel remorse;
    • ꜀chui hü꜄ (zeoi1 heoi3 追去) to drive out;
    • ꜀chui kau꜄ (zeoi1 gau3 追究) to investigate thoroughly;
    • ꜁ngan pat꜆ ꜀chui ꜁úi (ngan4 bat1 zeoi1 wui4 銀不追回), the money cannot be refunded;
    • ꜀chui ꜃kʻü ꜀fán ꜁lai (zeoi1 keoi5 faan1 lai4 追佢返嚟), hurry him back here;
    • ꜀chui ꜁'m ꜃shéung lok꜆ (zeoi1 m4 soeng5 lok3 追唔上佢) could not catch him;
    • ꜀chui ꜂tʻò (zeoi1 tou2 追討), to dun;
    • ꜀chui ꜀fung (zeoi1 fung1 追封), posthumous honors;
    • ꜀chui ním꜅ (zeoi1 nim6 追念) to recollect.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: 追也 is self-referential.

Radical + residual strokes

U+9310
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 55 錐) 鎗劍之屬
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 33* ꜀錐)
    • An awl, a borer;
    • a sharp pointed tool;
    • the apex, the point;
    • to bore;
    • trifling;
    • ꜀chui ꜀tò mút꜇ (zeoi1 dou1 mut6 錐刀末) a small matter;
    • ꜁mò ꜀chui (mou4 zeoi1 毛錐), a pencil;
    • ꜀chui ꜀lung (zeoi1 lung4-1 錐窿), to bore a hole;
    • ꜁pʻan ꜁mò lap꜇ ꜀chui ꜀chí tí꜅ (pan4 mou4 lap6 zeoi1 zi1 dei6 貧無立錐之地) miserably poor, not a place to stick an awl.

  • (Page 33* 墜꜅隊꜅)
    • To fall, to tumble over;

贅 Chui꜅ (zeoi6)

Radical + residual strokes
⾙ + 11
Unicode code point
U+8D05
Reading variations
  • zeoi3 (per 《廣韻》之芮切)
Fan Wan entry
  • (Vol. 2 Image 57 贅) 謂以[物]質錢也又男附女家謂之贅壻
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 34* 贅꜅)
    • To pledge, to pawn;
    • connected with, hanging to, an excrescence, a useless appendage;
    • a blemish;
    • unsuitable;
    • tautology, verbiage;
    • a postscript;
    • yap꜇ chui꜅ (jap6 zeoi6 入贅) to live in a wife's family;
    • chui꜅ lui꜅ (zeoi6 leoi6 贅累) reiterated, verbose;
    • chui꜅ ꜁ín (zeoi6 jin4 贅言), to repeat;
    • chui꜅ kʻap꜇ (zeoi6 kap6 贅及) a postscript.
Conway's commentary
  • Fan Wan entry: Corrected 𢪱 (⿰扌勿) to 物.
