꜀Áu (aau1 陰平)

  • 坳

坳 ꜀Áu (aau1)

Radical + residual strokes
土 + 5
Unicode code point
U+5773
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 ꜀坳)
    • (For the literary "uneven ground" senses, the expected reflex is aau1 from 《廣韻》於交切, but the common reading is aau3 (attested in Kangxi, quoting 《集韻》於敎切); see 坳 aau3 for the canonical entry.)

    A colloquial word.

    • Poor, destitute;
    • ꜀áu ai꜄ (aau1 ai3 坳屭) in want.

Áu꜄ (aau3 陰去)

  • 坳

坳 Áu꜄ (aau3)

Radical + residual strokes
土 + 5
Unicode code point
U+5773
Reading variations
  • aau1 (for literary "uneven ground" senses, expected from 《廣韻》於交切)
Williams (1856) entry
  • (Page 6 ꜀坳 ꜀Áu (aau1); here normalised to Áu꜄ (aau3))
    • A furrow, a hollow;
    • an unevenness in the ground;
    • undulations;
    • rolling, as land.
  • (Page 7 坳꜄)

    A colloquial word;

    • a turn, a corner;
    • ꜂chün áu꜄ (zyun2 aau3 轉坳) turn the corner.
Conway's commentary
  • The reading aau3 is not unattested; Kangxi quotes 《集韻》於敎切音拗.
